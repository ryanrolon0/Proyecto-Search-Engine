Nancy is warned not to go to Scotland, but she ignores the warning. She and her friends, Bess and George visit Nancy's great-grandmother at an estate in the Scottish Highlands. The Drew family's heirloom, a set of bagpipes is missing, and Nancy is determined to find it. While there, Nancy becomes involved in the mystery of missing flocks of sheep and a mysterious bagpiper has been spotted. Clues leading to a discovery in an old caste and a prehistoric fortress lead to the mystery's solution.
