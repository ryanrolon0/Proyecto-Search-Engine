The plot of the novel centers around the dredge, an abandoned gold dredge in the town, Skeleton Creek. The dredge is haunted by the ghost of Old Joe Bush. Two youths in the town, Ryan McCray and Sarah Fincher, investigate the dredge and its ghost. A video series also goes along with the novel, which show trips to the dredge and ghost sightings. The books has three sequels, Ghost in the Machine, The Crossbones, and The Raven.
