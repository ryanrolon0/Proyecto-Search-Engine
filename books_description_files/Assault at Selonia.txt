Han Solo is a prisoner on his home planet of Corellia. His captor is his evil cousin, Thrackan Sal-Solo. The man plans to restore the imperial empire and bring himself great personal power. Han joins with a female alien named Dracmus against Sal-Solo's corrupt Human League. Han Solo wishes to escape to Selonia to warn his friends but he is not even sure he can trust Dracmus. Meanwhile, forces conspire to take control of Centerpoint Station, an ancient artifact/world with the ability to destroy solar systems.
