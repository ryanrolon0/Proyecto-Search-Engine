In a previous volume Dr Tachyon had found his personality placed in the body of a pregnant teenage girl by his evil grandson Blaise. Blaise flees with Tachyon's body to Tachyon's home planet Takis. Determined to recover his body Tachyon and his friends Popinjay and Cap'n Tripps purchase travel on an alien spaceship and head to Takis. While there they have to fend off an attack by an alien race, in the course of which one of Cap'n Tripps' personas is killed.
