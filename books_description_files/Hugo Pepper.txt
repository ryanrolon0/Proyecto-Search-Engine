This novel is set in the same world as Fergus Crane and Corby Flood. It stars Hugo Pepper, a young boy who was raised by reindeer herders after his parents were eaten by polar bears. When Hugo discovers that his parents' sled has a very special compass which can be set to 'Home', he sets off to find where his true home is.As he does this, he unravels the mystery of 'The Firefly Quarterly's' Institute disaster and where the treasure of his Great great grandmother , Brimstone Kate...
