The Path Ahead

 Uncover the universal human experience of being on a path to dreams, through the story of Marty Headd, a three-year starter on the Syracuse University Orangemen's basketball team. He dropped out of college in the early '80s, played ball in Europe, and returned home to realize his path and dreams were behind him.
