Regretful Decision

 Unhappy with her marriage and having an affair, Kandience Perez-Jackson makes the decision of contacting a hit man called, 'The Cracker' to put a contract out on her husband's life! Carrying out the deed of service, 'The Cracker' forces Kandience into a mind numbing spiraling environment of Psychological terror because of her REGRETFUL DECISION of putting a contract on her husband's life. Kandience Perez-Jackson is a 28 year old accountant from Chicago who has the financial ability to do anything she wants. She has been unhappily married for 8 years to her husband Tariq Jackson. Due to the fact that Kandience is unhappy with her marriage, she is a having a love affair with her co-partner at their accounting firm. When ever Kandience and her co-partner become intimate, Kandience complains that she hates her husband and wishes that her husband would disappear forever. Tariq naively has no indication that his wife is having an affair?? He is a handicapped man that gave up on life after a tragic accident. After listening to many years of Kandience's negative remarks about Tariq, Kandience's co-partner gives her a pager number for a hit man called 'The Cracker'. Kandience's co-partner states that if Tariq is killed then he would be unable to take half her financial earnings and that they can finally get married. After days of pondering, Kandience becomes pushed into contacting 'The Cracker' as a solution to end her misery because of Tariq's constant failures and negative outbursts. Carrying out the deed of service, 'The Cracker' forces Kandience into a mind numbing spiraling environment of PSYCHOLOGICAL TERROR because of her REGRETFUL DECISION of putting a contract on her husband's life.
