Tex Ralph's Westerns #2

 Aviator hero Ted Warren returns from fighting in WWI only to find everyone thinks he was killed in the war. The villain, Kent, intercepted Ted's mail, rustled his family's cattle, took over the home ranch, and is making inroads with Ted's girlfriend. Kent's gang tries to kill Ted and Kent shoots Ted's father, leaving him for dead. Ted and his dad plan on the father appearing as a ghost to scare a confession from the bad man. ...
