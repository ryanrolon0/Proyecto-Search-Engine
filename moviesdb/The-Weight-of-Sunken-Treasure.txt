The Weight of Sunken Treasure

 Years ago Michael Dooley watched from afar as, on the day of his elaborate proposal of marriage, his fiancE Mary was abducted by an unseen stranger. The police questioned whether there was a third party at all. But Michael never gave up, still poring over the case, obsessed by his Mary's disappearance, struggling seemingly to the verge of madness. When fresh evidence is discovered, a chance for Michael to let go of the past emerges.
