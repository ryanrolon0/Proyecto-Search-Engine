Soliloquy

 Christian has returned home after his established poet sister passes away. While the two never had a close relationship, Christian discovers that a confidential poem has been addressed to him in his sister's will. Upon reading it, he discovers cryptic instructions resembling a scavenger hunt. With the help of his friend, Tom, the two set out to solve the mystery she left behind, one that will slowly change the way Christian thinks about his sister forever.
