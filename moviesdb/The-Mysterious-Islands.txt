The Mysterious Islands

 Journey to the Galapagos Islands with Christian creationists and scientists. Follow 16-year-old Joshua Phillips, his father, and Dr. John Morris as they reveal Darwin's errors and prove that the creatures on this remote island chain actually support the biblical creation account. Your whole family will enjoy this beautifully filmed documentary featuring white-tip sharks, lava fields, salt-spitting marine iguanas, blue-footed boobies, flightless cormorants, and many others.
