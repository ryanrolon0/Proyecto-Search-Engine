The Justin Junk Man

 A documentary about the life and times of Dennis Johnson Jr. or better known as simply 'the junk man' the film tells of how this man came to be the self appointed 'captain of the dumpster divers' after escaping a very dramatic childhood in the ghettos of fort worth Texas Dennis is now living in the small town of Justin Texas. Starting his own junk hauling business he has written two books and created his own post cards for the town. A sort of local celebrity around town everyone knows the junk man in Justin. The townsfolk will tell you just how full of life and charismatic he his, once you meet him you will not disagree.
