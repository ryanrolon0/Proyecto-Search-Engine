The End of Winter

 "The End of Winter" recounts the emotional journey of Billie, a young woman struggling to reconcile her grief after the untimely death of her father. When Billie, her sister Ella, and boyfriend Jackson, visit the apartment where their father once lived to retrieve his belongings, questions are raised about how he lived his life. With their exquisite sorrow ever present, Ella must find the strength to help Billie decide whether or not she will let the past dictate her future.
