Shadows in the Garden

 Eric Gollinger, Diana Spitzer, and Andy Kumpon star in this unusual micro-budget mood-piece in which the fate of a rainy town rests in the hands, the claws, of a monster. But the real star is the monster itself, who must fit the clues together in time to prevent the next victim -- his own beloved! A Shadow has come to the coastal town of Cthulhu Gardens. A butcher dubbed "Cutthroat Cthulhu", who leaves decapitated corpses in his wake -- including, it is feared, the town's own "Sheriff". Yet there are whispers of a new Shadow, a monstrous Other, a thing sharing the Garden's own likeness ....
