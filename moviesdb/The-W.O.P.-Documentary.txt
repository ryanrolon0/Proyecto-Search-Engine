The W.O.P. Documentary

 Tiffany Lauder, Candi Oppenheimer and Katherine Vanderbilt grew up in wealthy families on Manhattan's Upper East Side. Fed up with being labeled "privileged," "sheltered" and "white" by most of society, they sought refuge in Gangsta Rap and formed W.O.P. (Without Pimps). Their first album "All Hands on Dick released their second album "Et tu, Hoochie?" along with the music video for the song "My Otha Ride (Is Your Man)".
