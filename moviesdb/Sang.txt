Sang

 The Protagonist kills herself upon finding out that she was not the person she thought herself to be. After death, The Protagonist finds ahead of her three paths. At the end of the first path, she meets The One who Listens and at the end of the second path, she meets The One who Feels. Despite the dissuasion The Protagonist in the end chooses to take the third path, where there was no one who listens nor feels. What was at the end of the third path? Why did she choose that path?
