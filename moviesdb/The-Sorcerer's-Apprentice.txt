The Sorcerer's Apprentice

 Become The Sorcerer's Apprentice in the Nintendo DS game based on the blockbuster Disney/Bruckheimer summer 2010 film. Transport into a world filled with fantastical quests and magical combat as you fight alongside Balthazar Blake and the forces of good against the evil Morgana and her minions, bent on world domination. Join a fight thousands of years in the making as you become the ultimate Sorcerer's Apprentice!
