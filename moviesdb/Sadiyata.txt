Sadiyata

 The action takes place in 1884. Bulgaria is still divided into the legally autonomous Principality of Bulgaria and Eastern Roumelia which, in spite of its administrative autonomy, remains under the sovereignty of the Ottoman Empire. A new judge arrives in a small town in an outlying region of the Principality. A gang supported by a wealthy landlord is roaming the area. The judge has to deal with gang all by himself because the people are too scared to help him. In late 19th century, a new judge arrives in a Bulgarian town, Soon thereafter, four of his friends also come in with the knowledge that a golden treasure, once belonging to a Turkish ruler, is buried in the vicinity. They need the gold in order to arm the fighters for the Union of Bulgaria. But other people are also in search of treasure.
