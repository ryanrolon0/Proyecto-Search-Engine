The Dark Side of Love

 Two brothers on different life paths reunite to mourn the death of their mother. Julian, who's been his mother's caretaker for years, must now confront his brother Michael who's been a drug addict and has now reached rock bottom. Both brothers will be tested as they deal with each others prejudice and rage. Two brothers, estranged from each other, must deal with the death of their mother. Michael, is a cocaine addict who has distanced himself from his mother & brother Julian because of his brother's homosexuality. Julian, a restaurant waiter, has just accidentally won an auction for charity. His winnings is the handsome, wealthy eligible straight bachelor Steven. The winning item (Steven) was meant for a lucky lady who would spend five full days with this eligible bachelor in the name of charity. Both Julian and Steven are trying to figure out what to do with each other for the next three days as the death of Julian and Michael's mother will force both to deal with their prejudices as they make final arrangements for their mother's funeral.
