Vampire Vermont

 It's been over a year since Maggie's brother Dave and his friend Summers died in France under mysterious circumstances. But when Summers appears alive in Vermont, all sorts of strange things begin to happen to Maggie and her friends. Maggie must survive the oddest night in her life, as she struggles to find out who is dead, who is a vampire, who is a fetish freak, and who is simply....strange.
