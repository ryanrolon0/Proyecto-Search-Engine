Sailing with a Song

 This animated, audience-participation sing-along musical short features 'The King's Men' (qv) sings three of the most popular sea-oriented songs of all time..."My Bonnie Lies Over the Ocean," "Row, Row, Row Your Boat," and "By the Sea" It is animated with cartoon figures and lyrics-on-the-screen in order for the audience to join in on the chorus of each song.
