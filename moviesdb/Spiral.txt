Spiral

 Provincetown, Massachusetts is rocked when a local fisherman is found dead, impaled on a tree. Captain Joe Bradford and his First Mate Bobby Atkins top the suspects list, as they are the dead man's rival fishermen in these waters. To prove their innocence, Joe and Bobby search for the real killer, only to discover, it is not of this world.
