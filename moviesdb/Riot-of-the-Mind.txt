Riot of the Mind

 Anchored in a "direct from the headlines" story, we follow the developments of two police officers that are tried for murder when they mistakenly shoot and kill an innocent eleven year old African American boy and the racial violence / moral dilemma that ensues when the officers are set free. A documentary style film based around the fictional story of two Caucasian police officers that are tried for murder after mistakenly killing a African American 11 year old boy and the violence that ensues in various communities when the officers are set free.
