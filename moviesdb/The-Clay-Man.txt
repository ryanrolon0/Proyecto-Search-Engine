The Clay Man

 Jobless, hopeless and freshly boyfriend-less, Leslie is in the midst of a serious mid-twenties crisis. But when she stumbles upon a children's fairy tale about an ancient Rabbi who made a magical man out of clay, Leslie decides to take control of her life she might as well make one. Unfortunately, Leslie never stops to consider what will happen if the recipe actually works pound naked clay man running around her apartment... and more importantly what will he do to her?
