The Colonel

 As a US Major faces a gritty and long inquiry into his career during the second world war, his carefully spun web of deceit, lies and intrigue slowly comes undone. The mysterious death of a former colleague he recruited is only a pale shade compared to the allegations of doctoring and falsification he is charged with. But as his past is recalled and the facts of what actually happened are pieced together it becomes clear that this meeting may be more than just an inquiry.
