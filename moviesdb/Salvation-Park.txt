Salvation Park

 Salvation Park is a 16mm Crime story short film, starring Juan Riedinger and Kate Isaac. Carter, a man on his 30s, comes out from jail. Meredith, his girlfriend in her early 20,s awaits him to start a new life together. Unfortunately Lagardo, a powerful boss with whom Carter used to work, thinks Carter stole from him a lot of money from a robbery loot and hit it before being captured. Carter however swears that he never had the money. To complicate the situation, detective Sullivan, corrupted and ambitious, wants to lay hold of the missing loot too.
