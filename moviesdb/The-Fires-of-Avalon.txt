The Fires of Avalon

 Young, in love and thieves, Max and Abigail caught stealing and she is sentenced to death. He promises he won't commit suicide, but hopes for death as a soldier, daily. Unknown to Max, hoping to die in war, Abigail is spared by the King Edward, and becomes queen. From these distance and unexpected places, their paths cross, brought together by a series of unlikely events; will they rekindle their long lost love?
