The Harmion Tale

 An ex-captain of the Harmion Council must go on an epic journey to the other side of the world to retrieve a healing plant cut from the legendary Maldus Tree. Tritan (Alastair Sill) discovers on the way that he is not only fighting for his son's survival, but that he has entered a journey of deep realization, lost deep in the lands of Orldin. Whether or not he can save his son, he must first save himself...
