Taxi to L.A.

 Taxi to L.A. which looks at the sense of sight, is a road-movie about Samantha, an impetuous MontrEal socialite who embarks on a cross-country journey to Los Angeles. Grief-stricken after her fiance calls off their wedding, Sam impulsively hires Jack, a taxi driver with voyeuristic tendencies, to drive her from MontrEal to L.A. Can a relationship between them last beyond their eight-day taxi ride to L.A.?
