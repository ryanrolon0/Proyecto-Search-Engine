Saru gecchu

 When a skilled professor invents the world's first time machine, he is eager to show his young friends. During a demonstration, a group of rogue apes begin to cause chaos, leaving everything in pieces. Under order of the evil monkey mastermind Specter, they disband throughout time, eager to change the past in their leader's favor. Only teen-hero Spike can capture the monkeys, save his brainwashed friend, rescue the professor and finally - overthrow Specter in a corrupted and dark future!
