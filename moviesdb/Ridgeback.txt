Ridgeback

 Ridgeback is the story of Ezekiel Mann and his encounters with his own dark freak show beast and even the woman of his dreams to stay away and avoid the monster, Ezekiel is determined to keep his show and livelihood, 'Zeke's Freaks' up and running in the face of a force that is more dark and powerful than even he realizes. Disaster strikes, and Zeke is soon himself the one in danger, being hunted in the Appalachian mountains by the very beast he once so cherished. He now must choose to run and escape his problem before the sun sets, or face the problem he himself created.
