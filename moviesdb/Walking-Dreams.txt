Walking Dreams

 Walking dreams is a film about the famous American painter David Garibaldi. The film shows a 4 year time span following the artist from London England all the way back to his hometown Sacramento and everywhere else in between. The painters feelings on life are to live your life with passion and purpose, with that said the film shows small stories of interactions with people from all walks of life, truly being inspired by the artist. The film itself is truly an inspiration for people who know David, and especially if you don't know him, his presence can light up a room.
