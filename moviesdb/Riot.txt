Riot

 Blue Sea Lake, circa 1950. Times were a little simpler back then; but even though there wasn't much in the means of electricity it still was a favorite summer retreat for all of the cottagers in the inlet of Orlo. Much has changed in the 60 years since these videos were taken, most notably the once undisturbed 'Back 40' is now a network of trails linked together by footprints, vehicle parts and of course, the unmistakable tread of dirt bike tires...
