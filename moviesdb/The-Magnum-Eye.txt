The Magnum Eye

 The Magnum Eye is a series of eighteen 10-12 minute documentaries, shot and directed on small format video by renowned Magnum Photo Agency photographers. Some of the participants were Gilles Peress, Eugene Richards, Bruce Davidson, Susan Mieselas, Eli Reed and Eliot Erwitt. The subjects ranged from Detroit street gangs, to Famous Gleason's Gym in Brooklyn, to sidewalk Santas Clauses in New York. Kiki Miyaki produced this special series or TV Tokyo. The series was honored at the New York Film Festival's video series in 1994.
