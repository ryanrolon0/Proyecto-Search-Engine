Visions of Paradise

 "Where the Road Ends" is a powerful, engaging and inspiring biopic of one of America's most loved and celebrated literary geniuses, Jack Kerouac and his great friendship with beat generation icon Neal Cassaday. The film chronicles their first meeting and Cassady's inspiration on Kerouac's writing culminating in the breakthrough novels On the Road and Visions of Neal. The success of On the Road changes both men's lives forever and drives a wedge between one of the greatest literary friendships of a generation.
