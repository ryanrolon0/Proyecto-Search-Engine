The Making of 'Winnie the Pooh'

 Narrated by Corey Burton, this made-for-video documentary treats animation fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Winnie the Pooh, Disney's adaptation of A.A. Milne's classic children's story about a young boy's stuffed bear and the adventures he gets himself into. Includes archived footage of the voice actors and teams of Disney animators hard at work on bringing this classic favorite to life.
