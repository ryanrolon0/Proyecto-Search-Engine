Schöne Lügen

 Kim Joosten is young, attractive, and happily married. Her career as a successful interiour decorator is just about to pick up, when a terrible accident leaves her mentally and physically scarred. Soon she discovers that her life wasn't as good as she thought is trying to keep Kim in the background so that customers won't see her face, and her husband has lost his job and is cheating on her. Only Andreas, a young man she met in the hospital, can help her get her life back...
