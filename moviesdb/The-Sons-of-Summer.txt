The Sons of Summer

 Based on True Events. Former county league baseball star turned Marine, James Dalton lies in a blast hole with his brother Jacob in Vietnam, staring death in the face. In an act of faith and luck he lives, but the effects of his survival are shown in the years ahead... Years later, veteran James moves his family to the mining town of Rupland where baseball is that of a religion. His three sons intertwined with the game and the town's growing problems that tear them apart. The father of a true ballplayer, a rebel and a storyteller, James battles to keep his boys together through Rupland's hardships of alcohol and drug abuse, death, decline and war. From Little League to the town's famous "Summer League", the Dalton boys grow to follow different paths in life that change the course of their family forever. The Sons of Summer tells the story of a family, baseball and the American dream's glory and demise in the last gasp of small town America.
