Saga of a Crew 2008 Special Edition

 The Earth 3rd Expeditionary Fleet in search of new resources suffers a mishap and the Commanding Admiral must dispatch a hastily assembled 3 man team on a desperate mission to an uncharted region of space to obtain a vitally needed material for the ship's generators. The 3 man crew aboard 2 spacecraft crash land on a desolate planet, where they struggle to repair the damaged spacecraft and complete their mission before time and sanity run out.
