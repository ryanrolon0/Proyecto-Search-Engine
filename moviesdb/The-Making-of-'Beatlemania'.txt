The Making of 'Beatlemania'

 During the late 70's, there was a brief resurgence of nostalgia for the 60's tunes of the Beatles. Since the fab four themselves had no interest in getting back together, a stage production called Beatlemania toured around the country with four actor/singers performing the Beatles' greatest hits. This TV movie takes a behind-the-scenes look at the work that went into bringing the Beatles back to life.
