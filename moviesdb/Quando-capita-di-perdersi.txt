Quando capita di perdersi

 It happens, sometimes, that a place finds you, even though you are not looking for any place specifically - and then, when you have found it, you cannot do without it. This was the case with Elisa, when Sadurano found her. She got lost while she was wandering along the snake-like route leading to Castrocaro. At Sadurano, on the top of a hill in Romagna, a group of mad people cultivate the earth. By taking care of the land and the animals, they learn to take care of themselves. Elisa decided to stay on and help them
