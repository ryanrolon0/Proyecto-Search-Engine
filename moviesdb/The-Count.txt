The Count

 The story starts off in a remote farm house situated in the small town of Dimboola in Western Victoria. Chris Finiot accompanied by family and friends celebrate Amy's 25th Birthday. Soon after the night ends the couple share some time together only to hear a knock at the door. The couple are forced into a situation where they need to defend themselves from 2 crazed men, Marko and a serial killer by the name of Jericho. The couple are caught and the games begin, Jericho introduces his victims to his world of sick torment and brutality by playing a game called, "The Count" which is a rule designed to enforce control.
