Taivaankulkijat

 Tomi, a 26 years old guy, lives alone in a big city, working in a warehouse. His life is simple and the future seems clear until the day of his death. What is life and what it should be? During the film Tomi meets different kinds of people and talks with them about life. In The Past, he has taken a course on Parachuting and all the people he meets, are involved in parachuting in a way or another.
