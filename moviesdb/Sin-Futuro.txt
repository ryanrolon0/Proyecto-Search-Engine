Sin Futuro

 Don Felipe Durán is an honorable retired Colonel of Cavalry and a cultivated 51-year old man, with aristocratic appearance and manners. As a Master of Arms, he is devoted to teach unselfishly his knowledge and values to young people. Lucía, 24 years old and a beautiful literature student, is his fencing apprentice. She looks up to him for his wisdom. A strong empathy arises in between the two of them. They discuss about the fundamental values of present and freedom. Don Felipe is invited to Lucía's prom party and, there, they confess their love to each other. A fatal event drastically changes their lives and their promising future is taken away from their hands.
