The Forever Tree

 Tawni Bennett didn't start working at the Chicago Defender to sell ads. So, when her opportunity to score a front page scoop comes in the form of a clue to the legendary "forever tree", she jumps full body into the adventure. Tawni teams up with a wise old apothecary and a two-bit criminal to follow the clues, all the while being pursued by a sinister pharmaceutical scientist bent on finding the tree for his employer Lotus Labs and making millions. At the end, they discover that the forever tree has lead them to something none of them could have ever expected.
