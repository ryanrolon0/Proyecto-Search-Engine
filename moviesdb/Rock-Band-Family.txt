Rock Band Family

 A day in the life of northern Indiana family that loves, lives, and plays music together. Mom and dad Jeremy and Bobbi are in a rock band called Gilt 4. Sons Skyler and Gunner are in a rock band called Weeks Later. Jeremy also runs an Internet radio station. They all still have normal lives, working, going to school, and just being regular people while living their dreams of rock stardom. On this day Skyler has to go for a driving test.
