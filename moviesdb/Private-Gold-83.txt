Private Gold 83

 The few remaining Jodi are in hiding, training new padawans in their secret base. While the Jodi concentrate on defeating Seperatist groups, the evil Seth have been plotting a return to power, this time by using traitors within the Jodi temple. Sonnen Lightside, now a full Jodi knight, becomes suspicious when master Traley Sommerset starts acting uncharacteristically short tempered. It seems that for the Jodi, no one can be trusted anymore.
