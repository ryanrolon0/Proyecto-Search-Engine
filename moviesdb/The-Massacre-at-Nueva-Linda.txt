The Massacre at Nueva Linda

 The Massacre at Nueva Linda, focuses on the squatter community in Guatemala. The community was protesting the kidnapping of their leader Hector Reyes. On August 2004, they were violently evicted and massacred by the Guatemalan government in conjunction with private secruity and police. The film also pays special attention to the emerging role of the army in police operations, echoing past human rights abuses.
