Tu pasado me condena

 A woman flees terrified from a celebration dominated by drugs, the alcohol and sex. She only wears a cute set of white underclothes and an elegant mink coat. Confused, she takes a car mistaken. The storm and the narcotics make the rest. The first accident is foreseeable, the second in the beginning gives foot of the plot of "Tu pasado me condena". Based on a original work of the spanish romantics bestseller writer Corín Tellado.
