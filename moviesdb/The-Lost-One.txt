The Lost One

 In this moving psychological drama, we meet Bill, an old man who wants desperately to reconnect with his estranged son, but horrifying visions and inexplicable nightmares haunt him endlessly. Bill, with the help of his wife, must unravel the mystery behind the nightmare or risk losing his son and forever remain 'The Lost One'.
