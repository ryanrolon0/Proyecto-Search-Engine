The Puzzle Within

 Alice has a gift, she can decrypt complex algorithms at a glance. As Alice becomes more aware of her abilities, her father and teacher begin to show more interest in her, whist her mother moves further and further away. Trapped between an abusive family and the manipulative approaches by her teacher, Alice delves deep with her own passion of cryptography and studies the secrets that lay dormant deep inside, in a last attempt to save her own troubled life and shield her siblings from living the same horrific experiences.
