The Kidnapping of Demi Morgan

 When a woman refuses to move from the home she inherited from her parents, despite it's location in a seedy part of town, her husband hires a thug to terrorize her, by breaking into her house and placing her in numerous stringent bondage positions. When her husband comes to her "rescue" she tearfully agrees to leave the family manse and move to a "safer" area. In the second segment, a famous model visits a gal pal from the old days and comes to grief when her friends sleezeball boyfriend decides to kidnap the women and hold them for a million dollar ransom. The ladies are tightly bound and gagged and struggle desperately to escape their awful fate.
