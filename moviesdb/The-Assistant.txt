The Assistant

 Eunice is struggling to realize her dream of becoming an actress. Unfortunately, the harsh economic realities of day-to-day life, like paying the rent, are getting in the way. In an act of sheer desperation, Eunice answers a classified ad in the newspaper to assist Enzo the Amazing, in his magic act. Eunice soon realizes there's more to assisting a magician than merely smiling and waving at the audience. Especially when you're working with an old hack like Enzo. During the interview Eunice receives an important phone call that will change everything. The big question is can Eunice finally pull herself together?
