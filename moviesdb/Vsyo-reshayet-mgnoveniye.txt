Vsyo reshayet mgnoveniye

 A story of a Russian girl Nadya Privalova, a record-braking swimmer. She grew up by the sea, and learned swimming with a dolphin named "Fregat". Her first coach encouraged Nadya, and she took swimming professionally. She could beat the world record during her practice routine. But in public she suffered a classic "stage fright" syndrome, and she could not win any international competition. Now her participation in the Olympic games is at stake... . Can she overcome..?
