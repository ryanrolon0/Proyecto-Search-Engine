Sugar and Shit

 Karl, a prison inmate in isolation forms a relationship with a fly inside his cell; here he sits in solitude talking about his life, building a lifelike relationship with the insect who is his only companion. He confesses his pursuit of an under aged girl, which he regarded as an innocent loving relationship, where he was in absolute control when the control is taken from him, he snaps in a violent frenzy one is then shown flash backs of how this relationship ended as a sexual bloodbath. His release from prison is to be the following day however having acknowledged his horrific crime, he ends his life, rather than face the world, his family and himself as a convicted pedophile killer.
