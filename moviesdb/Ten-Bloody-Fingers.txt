Ten Bloody Fingers

 On his way from LA to Reno, Bruce's car breaks down on the open highway in the middle of the night. He's forced to stay in a deserted motel room. This is where the nightmare begins. Bruce is subjected to a ritualistic countdown by a known serial killer. Bruce must retrace a scary story told the night before to save his life. Once the countdown begins, it can't be stopped, or can it?
