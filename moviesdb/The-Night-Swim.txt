The Night Swim

His daughters, Alice and Sophia, have problems of their own her husband Sam, while Sophia is returning home after a sixteen-year absence. Arthur's brother-in-law Donald, tries to guide things, but with his daughters in tow, Lee and Charlotte, that becomes difficult cousin Will ; Charlotte, is distracted by items such as dolls and her husband, Grant, a real estate agent who seeks to find clients. Noah, the nurse who cared for Arthur's wife through her final days comes to pay his respects. But is that all he is there for?
