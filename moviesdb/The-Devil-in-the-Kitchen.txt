The Devil in the Kitchen

 CHEF Herbert and Ann are kindred spirits. They share the same insatiable appetite for fine cuisine and fine wine. Together they have created a haven for gourmets and gourmands, owning their dream restaurant in Silverlake, Los Angeles, where they bask in the fruits of their labor. Naturally running such a fine restaurant does not go unnoticed, so of course the food critics have ascended to sample this very delicate cuisine. MR SPAGAT, the most famous and infamous critic of them all, renowned for exploiting his power to equally praise a restaurant and degrade a restaurant in the same sentence. A little sales tactic? Hence, he finds the liver too fatty, a little too tasteless, has potential but yet a little too bland for his tastes. He decides to strip a star from Chef Herbert, costing them dearly. A year has passed and Mr.Spagat has returned to review the restaurant once more. This time Chef Herbert and Ann are ready for him having cooked up a surprising recipe, one that will tickle the taste buds, tantalize the pallet and keep you wanting more!
