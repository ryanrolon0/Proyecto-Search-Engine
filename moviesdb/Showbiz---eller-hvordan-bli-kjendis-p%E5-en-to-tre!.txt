Showbiz - eller hvordan bli kjendis på en-to-tre!

 A young boy named Espen Spølekladd is leaving home. He starts to work at a jazz cafe named "Balla Jazzhus". But his evil brother is trying to make everything bad for him. A young boy named Espen Spølekladden is leaving home, with only one dream; to meat Erna-Werna, the host of a synth-pop show on Norwegian TV. Together with his brothers, Per and Pål, he starts working at a jazz club named "Balla Jazzhus". As it happens, the boss at Balla Jazzhus is Erna-Werna's father, and he tells Espen the horrible truth spell of fabricated synth-music. In order to save Erna-Werna and bring her back to the Jazz club, Espen starts his own pop-career, under the name "Rabagast".
