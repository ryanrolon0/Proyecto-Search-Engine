The I Do Diaries

 When a young woman accepts a surprise proposal on television, she thinks her ship has come in. But only after the diamond is sparkling on her finger does she learn that her ship is about to set sail... literally! Blushing bride Shellia has just six hours to arrange her dream wedding, from picking the flowers and choosing the dress to designing a cake and rehearsing the couple's first dance. With the nuptials scheduled to take place aboard a chartered luxury yacht, she can't afford to miss the boat!With all the drama of a soap opera, this real-life "Instant Wedding" special, hosted by soap queen Lisa Rinna, gives you behind-the-scenes access to this couple's under-the-gun wedding planning and their gorgeous celebration.
