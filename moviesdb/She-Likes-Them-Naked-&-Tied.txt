She Likes Them Naked & Tied

 Sharon is teasing her bound and gagged friend Catalina when a door-to-door saleswoman knocks at her door. Sharon lures the lovely salesman into her house than pounces, stripping, binding and silencing the unlucky woman. Catalina resents the attention Sharon lavishes on the unfortunate saleslady, so when she slips out of her ropes she binds the Veronica even tighter. Sharon isn't amused and soon both submissives are roped together for additional torment.
