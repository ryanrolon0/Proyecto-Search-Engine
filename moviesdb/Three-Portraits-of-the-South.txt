Three Portraits of the South

 How does someone who grows up in a culture they don't fit into, survive within that culture? Three Portraits is a deeply personal insight into the lives and attitudes of three real-life southern characters. English filmmaker, Liz McGinnes, scratches beneath the surface of Southern stereotypes to reveal the personalities behind three distinctly different individuals. How have their lives been impacted and how have they responded to the day-to-day reality of the issues characterizing the American Southern Story From the unique perspective of an outsider Three Portraits can expose a universal truth make us stronger.
