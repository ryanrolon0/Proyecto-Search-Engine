The Dominguez

 The Dominguez is a documentary that reveals the story of one family who helped shape the history of Los Angeles and California. Since 1784 to the present, the Dominguez family has been part of the most important chapters in the history of Los Angeles and California. Dynamic and full of rich historical facts, the documentary combines colorful interviews with historians, community leaders, and Dominguez family descendants. The Dominguez legacy remains strong today and proves that if an individual can affect the course of history, we all can do something to make this world a better place.
