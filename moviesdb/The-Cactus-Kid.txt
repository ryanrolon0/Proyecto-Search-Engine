The Cactus Kid

 After Jack and Jimmy get paid for delivering horses, Jimmy rides off to pay the hands. Plug Perkins gets Cheyenne to kill him but Cheyenne leaves a plate from his harmonica at the scene. Jack finds the plate and catches up with Cheyenne just as Perkins is about to shoot him. After saving Cheyenne's life, he has a plan that will trick Cheyenne into a confession. Perrin and his partner get paid a big sum of money at the end of their cattle drive. Shortly thereafter, the partner is found with a knife in his back and Perrin is blamed for the murder.
