Scale Ella

 When the evil Scaleo escapes from prison, he causes havoc by scaling things out of proportion. The people cry for Scale Ella to make things right, using the power of 'mathematics'. Can our heroine Scale Ella put a stop to his evil deeds by using Scale Factor? Follow this entertaining educational animation for sixth and seventh graders as Scale Ella attempts to solve the problems created by Scaleo and put him away for good!
