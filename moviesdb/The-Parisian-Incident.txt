The Parisian Incident

 Based on a true story of two friends that went to Paris, France only to find a "hell on earth" experience waiting for them! Scott is a music producer trying to make a living selling his music over sea's to different record companies. He brings his long time friend, Issic to chill out with him around Europe as he works. As they are headed back to their hotel late one night on the train, three thugs pray on these two unaware Americans, leading them into an incident they will never forget!
