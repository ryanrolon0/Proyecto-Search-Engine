Sanam Ni Shodhma

 Love fantasy about two young men, Naval and Mavji, who dream of their ideal love. Naval's fantasy draws upon the 'Laila Majnu' story while Mavji's is from the 'Arabian Nights'. Trying to realize their dreams, Mavji falls for a woman he sees in a horse-drawn carriage. Naval meets Dolar, the daughter of Seth Kapurchand, who is trying to escape from an oppressive home....
