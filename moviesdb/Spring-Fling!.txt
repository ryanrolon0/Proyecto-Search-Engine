Spring Fling!

 Teacher, Linda Hayden has taken her all male class on an excursion in Los Angeles. Problems begin when the bus driver gets them lost and the hotel they were supposed to stay at loses their reservations. After driving around they come to an empty hotel. It also being Spring break the boys plan on having a great time. Linda, plans on keeping the boys at arm length. Perhaps, Linda is in for a little Spring fling when the hotel owner is old boyfriend, George.
