The 5,000 Fingers of Dr. T.

 Bart has only one enemy in the world Dr. T has a mad plan to force 500 young boys to practice at his magnificent piano 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Bart is the only hope to save these boys from being enslaved. Fantastic sets, screenplay, and even song lyrics were provided by 'Dr. Seuss' (qv). Features the only piano academy ever known to be equipped with cells and surrounded by an electric fence.
