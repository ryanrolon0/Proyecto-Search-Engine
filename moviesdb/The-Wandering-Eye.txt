The Wandering Eye

 A re-imagining of a 1954 German TV pro gramme called 'Das Wandernde Auge' (The Wandering Eye) commissioned as part of month long festival called 'Wir sehen uns morgen wieder' (see you tomorrow) for the FFT Theatre in Dusseldorf, Germany. This short film features the journey of the film-maker Oliver Clark from a local beauty spot though Crouch End to his family home in north London.
