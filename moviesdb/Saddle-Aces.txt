Saddle Aces

 Prisoners Steve and Montana escape the law by jumping from a moving train. Hiding in Jane Langton's wagon thay are soon caught up in her struggle with land grabber Sutton. Sutton makes her a prisoner and delivers her across the border. Steve rescues her and returns to face Sutton while Montana uses his safe cracking talent to retrieve her deeds from his safe. A convicted murderer and a safecracker pull off a daring train escape while handcuffed together. They later show up at a ranch where they help a young woman being swindled by a large cattle company. Steve Brandt (Rex Bell) and his young pal Montana Nick Sabot (Buzz Barton) are on their way to prison after being framed and unjustly convicted. They escape from the train and take refuge at the ranch of June Langton (Ruth Mix.) Pete Sutton (Stanley Blystone) has discovered that June's father never registered their land and is trying to take it away from her. Steve and Montana help June retain her land, and they are also assured of a new trial on evidence that Sutton was behind their original conviction.
