The Glove

 Gabe thought it was a toy, an innocent device which could improve his score at computer games, but, in reality, it was a five-fingered fiend from Hell! It was The Glove sulfurous pits of Hell and bent on destroying the souls of all who touch it. Unsuspectingly let loose, the world's most evil computer device wrecks carnage and terror in this "gripping" FX-fest as the hero risks soul and sanity to end its diabolical designs before "game over". Based on GameSpy's Daily Victim columns by Dave "Fargo" Kosak, this short spoof of B-grade horror films and computer-game culture grabs straight for the funny bone. When a computer gamer comes into possession of the Devil's own game-device, a blood-sucking Glove bent on destroying the soul of anyone who uses it and spreading Evil in the game world, only carnage and terror can ensue. Gabe, the gamer-hero, must battle the seductive power of the murderous Glove and save the world from the satanic machinations of the most evil computer interface outside Redmond. A "gripping" short spoof of B-grade horror flicks and gamer-culture legend, based on GameSpy's Daily Victim columns by Dave "Fargo" Kosak.
