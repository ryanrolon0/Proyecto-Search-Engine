Race tegen de klok

 In "Race tegen de klok"(Race against time) we follow the two young men Leon and Ginger. They start to work as financial advisors at a big insurance company. Ginger has great ambitions and even sponsors the local dart club to get new clients. In a nice new suit he's driving through the region to sell more and more insurances. Ginger's colleague Leon has just started the job and must do some exams before he is able to do the same job as Ginger. Leon's plan is to get a contract as soon as possible and he hopes to live happily ever after together with his girlfriend and his little dog. But Leon's dream won't come true...In the meantime Ginger has much success and is choosing a faster lease-car. But then Leon makes a big decision in his life...
