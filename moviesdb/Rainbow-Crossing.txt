Rainbow Crossing

 RAINBOW CROSSING - Out & About with Brewer & Berg in Hawai'i. Along the way they meet up with Pila of Hawai'i and experience real Hawaiian magic through his exquisite chanting. Each place they visit and the journeys in between spark fresh conversations on topics ranging from ancient Hawaiian Gods, to men's issues, the environment and philosophy leading into dimensions rarely seen on a screen, where myth and reality blend on the island where the veil is thinnest
