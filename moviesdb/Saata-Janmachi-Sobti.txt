Saata Janmachi Sobti

 Pramila lives a wealthy lifestyle along with her widower dad, Raosaheb Jadhav, and is a strong believer in women's liberation. After a heated debate, she accepts a challenge thrown at her by Bangaon-based wealthy Mohan Mohite, gets married to him, and moves in his house. Mohan will soon realize that Pramila will still continue to have her way and will not even permit him to touch her, leave along be intimate, be bold, different and outgoing, scandalize his name and end up changing his lifestyle forever.
