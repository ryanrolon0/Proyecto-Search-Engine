Thrillogy of Terror

 Three short films. Film number one is titled TRIP. JESSE CONHER - a player with the ladies - will say anything to trick a girl into giving him a treat. Jesse gets just what he deserves with a surprise slipped into his cocktail. He's going on a sudden TRIP - but it's a one-way ticket. Film number two is REPRISAL. We follow the journey of Wilson Beckford as he uncovers the terrifying truth about the demise of his father. When no one else is left to set things right; There is REPRISAL. In the third film, Ezra Carlisle's wishes come true when she finds her DREAMHOUSE.
