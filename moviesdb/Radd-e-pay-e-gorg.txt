Radd-e-pay-e-gorg

 Reza and Sadegh-Khan are two old friends who are engaged in illegal activities. on the first day after his wedding, Reza is arrested carrying the dead body of one of his friends. He is sentenced to jail. Years later, he is freed, lives in a little town. A letter arrives from Sadegh that invites him to his Son's wedding and asks Reza for doing him a favour. But in Tehran, Reza finds out this is just a trap and a plan for his elimiantion as the only witness of Sadegh's dark past......
