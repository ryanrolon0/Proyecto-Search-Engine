Visionary

 Laura Michael's is a private investigator with no time for her family or friends. Until a head injury leaves her with the ability to communicate with the dead. A power that puts her in touch with Kate Meer, the most recent victim of a vicious serial killer known as "The Reaper". As the only living person that knows his identity, Laura sets out to try and stop the slaughter. But as the supernatural world becomes aware of her gift, she becomes more and more distracted by the various requests made of her by the ghosts of her town. And "The Reaper" is fast becoming aware of her existence...
