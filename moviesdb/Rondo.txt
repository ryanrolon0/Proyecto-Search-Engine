Rondo

 Simon Miller is 12 years old. Although he is born Jewish, Simon has no religious beliefs. During World War 2, he becomes a refugee in England, along with his grandfather Abraham, a very religious and intolerant man. As they go from one refugee camp to another, they learn about the Holocaust and the death of their loved ones. At first they do not believe what they hear... Thrown into this unbearable reality, the two will learn to love each other.
