The Marlboro Marine

 Prior to Marine Corporal Blake Miller's returning from the war in Iraq, by sheer chance, a photo of his face was plastered around the world and dubbed "The Marlboro Marine", instantly catapulting him to iconic stature. He returns home a reluctant hero, struggling with severe Post Traumatic Stress Disorder and forms an unlikely bond with the photographer, who with a click of his camera shutter, changed his life forever.
