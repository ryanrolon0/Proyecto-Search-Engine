Snakes and Ladders

 A wry story of female friendship street performers, find the ups and downs of life and love amid the pubs and music scene of modern day Dublin. Two best friends, Jean and Kate, share a flat in contemporary Dublin. They also share a thirst for success as flamboyant street performers trying to break into the cabaret scene. Their increasingly complicated private lives momentarily takes a toll on their friendship until events take on a new path...
