Visual Purple

 Take a journey into Jack's mind, where he retells his traumatic childhood and confesses his sins to his psychiatrist Grace Sommers. He is forced to relive his tortured past, near death experience and his struggles growing up on the city streets where he meets his mentor Father-G, a blind man who tries to set Jack on the right path through chess and philosophy. Full of suspense and false realities, can Jack overcome his hunger for revenge against his father Edward.
