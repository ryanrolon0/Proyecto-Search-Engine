RealVision

 RealVision is a timely narrative exploring the implications of seemingly armless mass-marketed consume technology. It focuses on camera phones and their potential uses and misuses. The video deals with issues of voyeurism, surveillance, and privacy in the age of digital reproduction. It serves ti illustrate that human beings will always find new, innovative, and often unanticipated ways to use technology far beyond the practices depicted on the manufacturer's' label and advertised in their propaganda
