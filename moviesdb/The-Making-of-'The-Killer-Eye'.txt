The Making of 'The Killer Eye'

 This documentary treats movie fans to a behnd-the-scenes look at the making of The Killer Eye, a B-grade horror film about a mad scientist who unwittlingly unleashes a giant murdering eyeball on the unsuspecting public. Includes interviews with the cast and crew, who share some of the experiences they had in making the film and discuss some of the special efforts that went into it.
