Svidanie v teatre pepla

 Filmmakers alert us that nuclear war has been actually going on for decades is the guise of nuclear tests. Numerous nuclear tests caused severe nuclear pollution across the former Soviet Union and especially in Northern Russia. The Novaya Zemlya test site was used for the largest thermonuclear explosions ever. People, animals, plants, and land has been exposed to extremely high levels of radiation. The world is in danger.
