The North Cape

 The North Cape - Home of the Midnight Sun and the Northern Light brings you on a journey through the four distinguished seasons of the northernmost point of continental Europe. The film explores this arctic latitude's rugged, yet sublime physical elements and compelling natural phenomenon as well as the inhabitants who endure them. Illuminated by the vibrant midnight sun in the summer and the mysterious northern light in the winter, the North Cape's unique landscape is full of intense contrasts and alluring scenery captured in a breathtaking panoramic image.
