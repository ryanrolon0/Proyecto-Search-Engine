The Warning

 Jane Hampton is an experienced investigative psychiatrist and is working on a case that, all being well, should lead to recognition and instant promotion. The study is of murderer, David Catcher, who has confessed to the crimes. But Jane, to the annoyance of the impatient police and hostility of the stubborn David Catcher, believes that his guilt is not of the murders... Her quest to determine his guilt sheds an uncomfortable light on his past...
