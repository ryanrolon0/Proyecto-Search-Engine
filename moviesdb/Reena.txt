Reena

 Going out that fateful evening was the last of many bad decisions Reena had made in her short life, the end of a domino effect that had started a year and a half earlier when, out of desperation to escape another school year with the same crowd who had bullied her all through elementary school, Reena told the first of many lies. "Reena" seeks to uncover the mind of the bullied through the journey of Reena Virk and the lengths she went to ensure she wouldn't be bullied again .
