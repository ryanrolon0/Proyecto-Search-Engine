Selected Shorts #3

 This third short film compilation (which also tours cinemas) focuses on the best Flemish shorts of the last year (2005) and contains Happiness, The Sunflyers, De Laatste Zomer (The Last Summer), Dag Opa (Goodbye Granddad), Ureca, The One Thing To Do, Alice ou la vie en noir et blanc (Alice, or life in black and white), Het einde van de rit (The end of the ride), Forever, Meander, On a Lead, Angst.
