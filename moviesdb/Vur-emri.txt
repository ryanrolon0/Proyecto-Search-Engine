Vur emri

 Rich and beautiful Gul fancies their driver Ali. Ali also fancies her but worries about the difference of their social status. Gul's father wants her to marry Turgut, who is in fact after their fortune. Turgut gets Ali fired, they have a row and Ali's man gets stabbed. Ali gets the blame and is arrested. Ali wants to take revenge, he marries Gul after his release. Turgut kills the factory guardian and puts the blame on Ali, Ali gets arrested, sentenced to death. The truth is revealed, the police arrests Turgut and his men. Ali who escaped from prison surrenders, happy days are about to come for the two lovers.
