Skeletons in the Closet

 In this suspense-filled horror, laced with Delta Blues, Roger Bankman comes home from college to find that his whole town is being butchered by a dark force claiming its victims from out of their own closets. When the murders hit close to home, Roger and his friends begin investigating, hoping to find closure. Instead they find that in the sleepy little southern town of Redwater, everyone has a few skeletons in their closets. The body count grows higher and higher with every lie that is told. Unless you're a saint, you'll want to padlock all the closets in your home after watching this spine-tingling supernatural thriller.
