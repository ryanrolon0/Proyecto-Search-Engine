QuE Despelote! La película

 After several years of being at the top of radio stations programs, Rocky, Billy and Tony are not so down to earth like they used to be. From having a private massage, sushi at the studio and fancy cars suddenly, they have gone to being unemployed average men. This creates a big dilemma since all they know is to be broadcasters and now they have to find out how to make a living and face the economic crisis in Puerto Rico.
