The Double Cross

 Jack DeForest is a down on his luck Private Eye. His business is treading water, but one evening Jack gets a phone call that changes all that. Jack is secretly hired by crooked business mogul Bruce Markham to find his kidnapped brother. Jack takes the job and partners up with his informant to find Markham's missing brother. The case soon begins to unravel and reveal double dealings, greed and murder. Jack finds himself stuck in the middle hoping not only to collect the cash promised, but also to get out of the case with his life.
