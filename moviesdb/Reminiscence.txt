Reminiscence

 Jessica MacKenzie loves her adopted mother Mary and is grieved by her passing. However, questions about her past and her biological parents send her on a quest for answers back home. As she searches, she unlocks secrets from her past and discovers shocking truths about her real parents, about Mary, and about Mary's husband, Donald. Scared to learn more, but needing answers, Jessica keeps searching, strangely guided by happenings in the old house.
