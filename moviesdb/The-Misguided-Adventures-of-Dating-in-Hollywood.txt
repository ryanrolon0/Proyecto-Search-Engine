The Misguided Adventures of Dating in Hollywood

 OCEAN (a struggling cartoonist), RICK (an unskilled actor) and CHRIS (a celibate personal trainer) find in LA, it is hard to draw the line between work and pleasure. After being dropped by his longtime, actress girlfriend ZORA, Ocean shuns all relationships, becoming a serial dater until he falls for his newly appointed writing partner TROI. Rick dates an older casting agent in hopes of a job, and Chris can't seem to keep his hands off his client, the spiritual and gorgeous PASQUAL. The film presents a hilarious and touching real life look at three friends in LA. It's a hip comedy done in the style of "Swingers" and "The Player" with a diverse cast. Hollywood, where rich producers rule, Gold digging women lurk, the average Joe gets no respect and you're only as popular as your last movie. Here is where three friends embark on a quest to have fun and chase their dreams. Along the way they find that dating in Hollywood is no easy task, especially when you have no fame and fortune to call your own. A funny look at dating in Hollywood when you're a struggling cartoonist, an unskilled actor and a celibate personal trainer.
