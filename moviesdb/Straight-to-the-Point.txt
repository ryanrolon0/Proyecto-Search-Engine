Straight to the Point

 Straight to the Point (SttP) highlights the journey of gay men from varied professional backgrounds who reveal details about how and why they got into the sex-crazed, methamphetamine lifestyle. This film delves into how losing everything forced them to kick their active addictions and ultimately come out the other side better persons. SttP also offers professional opinions from medical, social and religious experts on what drives men in the gay community to crystal meth and what can be done to stop this and promote healing. While crystal meth is considered an epidemic among many demographics, this documentary promotes recovery and growth particularly in the gay male culture.
