Three Men in a Boat

 'Griff Rhys Jones' (qv), 'Rory McGrath' (qv), 'Dara O'Briain' (qv) and 'Loli (II)' (qv) the dog set out from Kingston to Oxford across the Thames in a wooden skiff, attempting to re-trace the journey of 'Jerome K. Jerome' (qv) in his classic 1889 book "Three Men in a Boat" and having some adventures of their own along the way, including entering a race after rowing into the middle of the Wallingford Regatta.
