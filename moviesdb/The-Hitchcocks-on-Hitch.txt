The Hitchcocks on Hitch

 This short offers director 'Alfred Hitchcock (I)' (qv)'s three granddaughters the chance to share their fond remembrances of their granddad, as well as 'Patricia Hitchcock' (qv)'s memories of her father. Featuring vintage home movies, family photos, and even some vacation snapshots, this featurette delivers a heartfelt look into facets of Hitchcock's personality and family life rarely seen by the public.
