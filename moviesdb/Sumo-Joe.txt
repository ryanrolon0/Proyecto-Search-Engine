Sumo Joe

 Nominated Best Comedy, Best Screenplay - Comedy, Best Original Score, and Best Makeup at the 168 Film Festival (2010). 'Sumo Joe' is about a little boy (Sawyer Peace) struggles to achieve his dream of becoming a sumo wrestler (Dan Kalbfleisch)and follow in his recently deceased father's footsteps as his grieving mother (Jenn Gotzon) turns from faith to New Age religion.
