Rook

 From a simple traffic stop, a mysterious, unidentified man is brought in for investigation by seasoned interrogation specialist Thomas Pearl. Pearl quickly realizes this suspect is unlike any he has ever attempted to crack, and is propelled into a battle of wits which slowly unravel the layers of Pearl's own dark past. This suspect, this John Doe, defies logic with his knowledge of Pearl's dark secrets, and despite Pearl's own shrewdness and foresight, threatens to destroy him completely. Everyone has their demons, but this night, for Thomas Pearl - they've come to collect.
