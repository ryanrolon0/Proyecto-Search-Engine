The Amazing Adventures of Little Batman

 Based on the 1966 Batman TV series, this fan film has a very young Bruce Wayne taking on the famous, pint-sized villains of Gotham City. After escaping Gotham Penitentiary, the criminals put their latest plans in action to take over total control of the entire world. It is up to Batman and Robin to put an end to their evil plans. Almost the entire cast is made up of children.
