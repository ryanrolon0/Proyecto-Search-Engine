The Ballad of Letter Foldin' Joe

 Letter Foldin' Joe is the best employee in his dreadful corporate office. Joe has a special gift, and that's to fold letters faster than anyone had ever seen or known before. When Joe arrives to work one day, he discovers he's been replaced by a machine. To save his job and the woman he lusts for, Wendy Sue the office receptionist, Joe battles the machine to a letter foldin' duel.
