Wanted in Rome

 Jacqueline, a music composer is forced to leave her apartment and struggles to find a new home. Through Wanted in Rome, a magazine for foreigners, she finally meets Louise and has to move in her house. after breaking up with Pietro, her boyfriend, she does not know how to move her piano there. At her critical moment, Louise's son Luca, a stranger to Jacqueline, comes to her aid and offers to help Jacqueline move her piano in a magic night of Rome. In the morning, Jacqueline realizes that who Luka is and what he represents and what has really happened. She will remember this when finally practicing for the opening of her grand concerto.
