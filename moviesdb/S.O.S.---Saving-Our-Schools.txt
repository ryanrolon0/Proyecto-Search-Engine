S.O.S. - Saving Our Schools

 In 2010 when 14 area Chicago Public Schools are listed for Closure, Consolidation or turn-arounds, one school, Simon Guggenheim and one neighborhood, Englewood, decides to "fight back!" Led by Jonathan Jackson, son of former Presidential nominee, Rev Jesse Jackson and writer/producer Josef Cannon (former student of Guggenheim) they set out on this crusade to rescue an institution that meant so much to so many! This is their story. Executive Producers Ken Streutker & Kathy Brayton. / Associate Producers Jesse Jackson Sr.
