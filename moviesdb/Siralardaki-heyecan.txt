 Bulent is poor high school student who earns his living By singing in weddings. His classmates always tease him and play tricks on him. They make him fall in love with their classmate Gulsen. Because of this love, Bulent's scholastic performance decreases. One day he realizes their plot about Gulsen and ends up in great dismay. He leaves school and starts to work as a singer in a club. On the other hand, Gulsen is much regretful about what she has done. She goes to Bulent, asks for his forgiveness and tell him that she too is in love with him. They decide to get married. Although, Gulsen's father is against this marriage, they do so. But one day Gulsen turns out to have leukemia and she soon dies. Bulent is all alone now.
