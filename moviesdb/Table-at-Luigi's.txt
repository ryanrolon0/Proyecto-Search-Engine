Table at Luigi's

 A romantic comedy produced by the Feature Filmmaking class at the University of Central Arkansas, 'Table at Luigi's' is a modern-day fairy tale about David, 'Chef' to his friends, who creates living dreams for people through his cooking. Chef has created a small, safe world for his customers inside his restaurant 'Luigi's,' until the night Emily walks in, and Chef begins to realize that the dreams he's been creating have locked him in his own dream world. Can Chef leave the world he's created? Does he even want to?
