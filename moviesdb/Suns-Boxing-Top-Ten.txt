Suns Boxing Top Ten

 THE SUN presents this program, a compilation of archival footage arranged into a series of "Ten Best" lists. Divided into five categories, this program presents some of boxing most exciting moments presented in an format that allows for easy access to each segment. The five lists are 10 Greatest KO's, Top 10 Comebacks, Top Ten Big Finishes, Top Ten Fights, and Top Ten Fighters.
