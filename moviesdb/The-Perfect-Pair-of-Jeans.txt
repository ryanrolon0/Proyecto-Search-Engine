The Perfect Pair of Jeans

 Those looking for love oftentimes look in the wrong places. Whether in church, social engagements or even the college classroom, for some, love never comes easily. In this short film, a young woman overwhelmed with the cards she has been dealt in lovers lane, decides she may need to give up on the unforeseen notion. Little did she know, she was looking for love in all of the wrong places and soon her patience would lead her to the perfect pair of jeans.
