Tinok Lefi Hazmana

 'Baby on Order' examines the one subject that will influence our lives more and more in the near future - cracking the human Genome and its profound consequences. The next generation can be born perfect, genetically speaking. It is possible to envision a world without disease or without slight flaws such as baldness or the need for glasses. What are the advantages and disadvantages of such a situation? What constitutes the red line beyond which it is no longer worth being born?
