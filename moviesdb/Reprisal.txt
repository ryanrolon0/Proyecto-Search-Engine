Reprisal

 Wes Walling's life has been leading up to this moment. After years of searching for the man who betrayed his father and left him to be killed by the police, Wes has finally tracked him down. With a lifetime of anger that's been festering inside him, Wes shows up at the man's doorstep with his plan of revenge. What happens inside the home becomes a test of survival for both men.
