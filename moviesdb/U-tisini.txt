U tisini

 A year has passed since Daniel died. Who's Daniel?, ask the four of his best friends during dinner. The wake was held in the fanciest restaurant in town. Everything else is irrelevant. While they're sitting over dinner things from the past start to come out. There is an enormous gap of misunderstanding and fake friendship between them. After a year, the only thing they have in common is a girl. And boredom. Great boredom. There is only one thought in their minds - to get away. From this world, this life, themselves...
