The Hunters of Xuan Wu

 The Hunters Of Xuan Wu centers around two young men in an unspecified place in history. Their task is to hunt the mysterious Xuan Wu, a mythical beast that patrols the valley they call home. Whilst goofing off one afternoon, Xuan Wu emerges from the jungle into their clearing and the two are immediately thrust into an epic battle that will test their wits and strength to the limit.
