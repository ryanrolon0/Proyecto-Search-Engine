The Golden Girls

 This 90-minute special aired on the Lifetime network in June 2003 served as a reunion show for 3 of the 4 Golden Girls. This show has clips from the girls' favorite moments from the show, as well as some never-before-seen bloopers and outtakes. In addition, the special contains the Girls' favorite music numbers as well as interviews with the Girls, executive producers Paul Junger Witt and Tony Thomas, and creator and writer Susan Harris. When Lifetime aired this special, it earned the network an all-time high rating.
