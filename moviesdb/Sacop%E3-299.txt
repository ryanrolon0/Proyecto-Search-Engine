Sacopã 299

 A strange message is heard being recorded in the answering machine at Leonardo's apartment after a night of doubtful perspectives and uncertain visions. Nothing seems unusual, except for the fact it mentions Sofia, who apparently left with him, and has now disappeared. Leonardo now has to vividly relive the uncertain and vague moments of a twisted night that lead up to present. Facts and ideas seen and relived through the eyes of not only Leonardo, but Sofia herself in a unique time sequence. One night, Two stories, two tellers and no certain facts eluded by the curtains of a bizarre Christmas eve celebration at the infamous Street Sacopã, #299.
