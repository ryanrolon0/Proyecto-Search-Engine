Tears of Happiness

 Raffi and Silva are a married couple with a son. After struggling to make ends meet, the couple have a fight. Silva and the baby son runaway leaving Raffi behind. Raffi is heartbroken and struggles with his dream to be a musician. After some time, he gets the big break and goes from an Armenian singing idol into a worldwide superstar while Silva sends the son to the orphanage while she works as a maid. Even though Raffi's got the money, fame and a Hollywood girlfriend, he's still misses the two people that are his life back in the old country
