The Blazing Guns

 Gunther 'Gunn' Allcock, Richard 'The Ram' Raminov, Tony 'The Money' Miranda and John 'The Hammer' Hammond are The Blazing Guns. The self titled, greatest band you've never heard of. Join them on the road to their latest gig as they candidly discuss life, love and their creative process on the cusp of becoming music legends. Hear insights into what makes the band and their music great from journalists, fellow musicians, fans and those closest to the band. Feel the exciting atmosphere as an all access insider with the band as they play a top London venue. A satire about the current music industry's reliance on style over substance. A band who have grand ambitions but do not rehearse together. See them play a live gig in front of a real East London audience of hardened rockers as well as their reactions to the biggest band since (sliced) Bread.
