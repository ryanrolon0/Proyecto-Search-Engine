The Santa Chronicles

 The Santa Chronicles is a chronicle of Santa's history and that of his Merry Elves. The first episode introduces audience to Santa's Toy Shoppe and his Merry Elves. Episode Two delves into Santa's and the elves' history. Episode Three Santa and his elves must fight a force that threatens to end their magic forever. In episode 1 of The Santa Chronicles, SANTA TAKES A HOLIDAY, Santa finds himself distressed, depressed and disillusioned by the modern world and new technology. With Christmas nearing, everything that could go wrong, does go wrong and his Toy Shoppe machinery explodes after installing a new computer and robotic toy assembly line. Overwhelmed, Santa decides to retire or quit, he doesn't know which, and heads for Hawaii, for his first vacation ever. The change of scenery does little for his grumpy mood, until a boy and his family and friends, befriend Santa and remind him of the spirit of living and giving with their Aloha. Santa's elfin friends also come to his aid, when they, for the first time ever, fly his sleigh to Hawaii. Together, they help make toys for the millions of girls and millions of boys throughout the world.
