Romeo and Juliet in Stanley Park

 In Vancouver's luscious Stanley Park, a heartbroken Romeo finds hope when she spots the woman of her dreams, Juliet. There's just one problem - Paris, Juliet's long-term boyfriend is about to propose. Jumping in to save the day, Mercutio distracts Paris, leaving Romeo free to court her love. A whirlwind of passion and drama ensues in this charming tale of forbidden love.
