Seekers

 Matthew Lordes (15) is crippled and lives his life in a wheelchair. He is a consumate artist and draws the visions of a man stalking and killing, like a vampire hunting for its prey. In the real world Adrian is a psychotic killer being hunted by NYPD detective John Merrit. Matt helps John find a capture Adrian but not before being tempted by what he belives to be immortality.
