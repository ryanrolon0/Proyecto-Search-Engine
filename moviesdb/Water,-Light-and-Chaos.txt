Water, Light and Chaos

 This video explores, in depth, one of artist Juan Geuer's (1917-2009) most acclaimed installations, 'WiS' (Water in Suspense), in which a single water droplet and an orange laser beam create a powerful visual experience that changes as the water drop forms and then falls away, in a darkened room.
