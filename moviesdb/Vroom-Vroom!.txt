Vroom-Vroom!

 Hungry for love, misfit Zoe, hits rock bottom when her boyfriend dumps her before their long-awaited cross-country motorcycle adventure. With nothing left to lose, she loads her gear into her car and hits the road solo. When her radiator bursts in the middle of the desert, clueless Zoe is captivated by Lola, the zany head of an International School where learning the lessons of crime-fighting is a labor of love. Zoe must decide if she's been captured or rescued by Lola and ultimately how to rescue herself.
