The Enemies Club

 A movie star decides she needs a larger office, her personal assistant and a sinister woman posing as a real estate agent lure her into an empty building and kidnap her. The two red-headed villainesses aren't out for money - they plan to bind and gag the movie star and publish photographs of the sex symbol taken while she was tied and helpless. Three bondage vignettes featuring buxom models tied in a variety of positions conclude the video.
