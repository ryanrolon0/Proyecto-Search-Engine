Punctum

 'Punctum' - 'A photograph's punctum is that accident that which pricks me, but also bruises me, is poignant to me.' (Barthes 1981 short modest film through sound and image explores how the prosaic photographic snapshot and the singing voices of people passed can not only bruise the 'maker, but provide a poignancy that is perhaps more universal. The film was the filmmaker's first solo production and it sought to explore the combined potency of the photographic and the cinematic.
