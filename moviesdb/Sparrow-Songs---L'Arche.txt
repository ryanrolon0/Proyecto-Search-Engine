Sparrow Songs - L'Arche

 Sparrow Songs is a project in which filmmaker Alex Jablonski and cinematographer Michael Totten make and exhibit one short documentary per month, every month for one year. Episode 6 - L'Arche Forty years ago philosopher Jean Vanier founded L'Arche, a community of people of faith living and worshipping alongside developmentally handicapped adults. There are currently 137 of these communities around the world.
