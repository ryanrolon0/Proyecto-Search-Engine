Seven Sins and Other Tales

 In the Harmony Concepts universe, the seven deadly sins lead to bondage the first vignette, a writer and her maid swap interludes of bondage; in the second, an angry defendant discovers what a "gag order" really means; in the third, an art critic suffers the wrath of an artist she panned; and in the fourth, a genie and a sorceress match magical powers.
