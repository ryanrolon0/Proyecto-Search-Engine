Roberto and the Robot

 The power of rock and roll will be put to the test. Kendra is a young woman, paying the price for an adolescence of rebellion and rock 'n' roll. Taking on the alias of Roberto, she makes her way back to the underground subculture to pursue her passion of music. When her gang of rebellious outcasts find themselves with an experimental Robot, they hatch a scheme to go legit and make it big.
