This Is Encino

 It's hard to set up a big score at the last minute. But that's what Rocco is trying to do. Little does he know that everything will go wrong from the get-go. From the enlistment of his distrustful associate Seymour who insists on bugging him about his ex-girlfriend Kim, to his cousin Maynard, who suffers from bouts of diarrhea and fits of ADHD, he'll be lucky to survive the night. Then again, the guys may not survive each other.
