The Marshal of Windy Hollow

 The Marshal (Sunset Carson) calls in the head of the Texas Rangers (Ken Maynard) to track down the disappearance of a entire Wagon Train. Ken Maynard (Texas Ranger) finds out that the outlaws were robbing the Settlers, stealing the Gold from the Wagons and dumping the Wagons with the Settlers into the Canyon River. The Texas Rangers trapped the Outlaws in the Canyon, some were blown up in there Canyon hide-out and others were arrested by Marshal Sunset Carson and brought into the town of Windy Hollow for trial.
