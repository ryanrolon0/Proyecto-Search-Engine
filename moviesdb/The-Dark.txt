The Dark

 Sylvia wants what every young woman wants, to find true love and live happily ever after. When she meets Jack, a true gentleman who sweeps her off her feet, Sylvia believes that he might just be the man of her dreams. However, Jack is not who he seems to be and his intentions towards Sylvia are harmful to say the least. What Jack doesn't realize is that Sylvia has a dark side unlike anything that he ever expected.
