Reality Horror Night

 Americas top Reality Stars from Biggest Loser, Rock of Love, I Love Money, The Mole, Survivor, Howard TV, Next Top Model, Amazing Race, I Wanna Work for Diddy, and Fit Club are invited to compete for a Million Dollar Prize on America's next great Xmas Reality Show. Little do the celebrities know as they are voted off one at a time, hour by hour, they lose their lives. Is the million worth dying for? Which star gets it next? Which Reality Star bites the dust?
