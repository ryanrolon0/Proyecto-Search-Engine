Sterren Stralen Overal

 Piet Amstel is a struggling taxi driver in the post-war Netherlands. His car is beyond repair, still operational though, but customers aren't lining up to take his old cab. He doesn't have the money to buy a new one and Amstel fears the next car inspection. The future of his children also worries him. Will they be able to find a house in the crowded Netherlands? The situation is giving him nightmares. Then his wife comes with a solution possibilities, the more they get the impression that his is the right step to take. There's just one problem though. Daughter Willie has fallen in love with a man, Jim.
