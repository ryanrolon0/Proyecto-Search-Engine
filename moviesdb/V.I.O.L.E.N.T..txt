V.I.O.L.E.N.T.

 Originally written in 1995 (WGA/W) V.I.O.L.E.N.T. follows 7 moralistic violent vignettes that intertwine throughout an event filled evening. Each letter is a word, each word is a story... An inside look at normal human beings who are faced with circumstances within or beyond their control and the decisions they make.
