Râkî

 This short film was made for a special TV program on a popular actor Masahiro Motoki (meeting the acclaimed director Jun Ichikawa). They were interested in working each other although they thought their style wouldn't work well. It turned out to be a splendid piece, portrayed ordinary people's lives, through Isamu's eyes, a sub manager of a family restaurant (diner), including his'. They went on to make a feature film 'Tokiwa-so no seishun' (1996).
