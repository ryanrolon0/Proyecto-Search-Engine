The Lost and Found

 Sam is a tortured alcoholic who is on the fighting the urge to drink the glass in front of him. He imagines himself in the forest, squaring off against the the part of himself with these desires. Soon he is on the hunt for it, his inner demon, the one trying to convince him to give in. But he is over powered and remembers how he drove away the woman he loved with his alcoholism. Then, in a final explosion of will, Sam overcomes his destructive desires... for now.
