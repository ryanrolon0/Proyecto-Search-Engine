The Worriers

 In 1979 the Warriors fought their way home, facing New York City's most notorious gangs. In 2011 the Warriors will be battling across Los Angeles, facing LA's worst gangs and the curse of the Hollywood remake. But in 2010, an intrepid gang of poets attempted to pass through the dark woods of the poetic underbelly of the third largest city of the Pacific Northwest, Portland Oregon. The Worriers. This is their story.
