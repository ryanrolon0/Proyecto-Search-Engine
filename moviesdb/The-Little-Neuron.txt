The Little Neuron

 A fable explaining Parkinson's Disease created by two people with Parkinson's. Focusing on the main character, a small cell in the brain called 'The Little Neuron', the writer Dr. Dave Heydrick explains how Parkinson's affects the body and all that he does as a doctor to treat his own case. Illustrated and animated by Peter Dunlap-Shohl, a cartoonist with Parkinson's Disease.
