Strangled!

 Three stories from the original STRANGLED tape and the revised version. "Car Trouble", which was the original segment, stars two hapless motorists who go to the wrong house for help and end up the prisoners of a psychotic killer! "Secret Agent Murders"  - Two secret agents learn a deadly secret about their boss and are targeted for murder. But in order to throw suspicion away from himself, he has his associate make it look like the work of the serial killer, the stocking strangler. In "George's Daydreams", George plans to murder three women after they humiliate him at a bar. He goes after each one, strangling two of them, and drowning the third.
