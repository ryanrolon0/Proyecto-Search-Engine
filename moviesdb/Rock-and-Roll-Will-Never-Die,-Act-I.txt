Rock and Roll Will Never Die, Act I

 It's Left Behind meets Night of the Living Dead meets Two Lane Blacktop -- a recovering addict falls off the wagon after his band loses their drummer in The Rapture. As his inner demons surface, so do the undead. Regardless, the band soldiers onward on a cross-country rock n roll tour through post-apocalyptic America. Kyle is a recovering addict pushed off the wagon after his band loses their drummer in the rapture. HIs inner demons resurface, and so do the undead. He's attacked by zombies shortly before leaving out for a rock n roll tour across post-apocalyptic America with his best friend Joe.
