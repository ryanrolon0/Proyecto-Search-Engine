The Teen Choice Awards 2008

 Teens choose their favorites in Movies, Music, TV, Fashion, Sports and more in this annual awards show, which airs on Fox. 2008 winners included host Miley Cyrus, The Jonas Brothers, Gossip Girl and its cast, Will Smith, David Beckham, Adam Sandler, Channing Tatum, Hayden Panettiere, Ryan Sheckler and Steve Carell.
