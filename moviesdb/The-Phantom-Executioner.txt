The Phantom Executioner

 In the future, a corrupt prison system capitalizes on the endangerment of its inmates by means of televised execution. Nathan Reeves, a prisoner who must survive against an other-worldly opponent known as The Phantom Executioner, places his fate in the hands of a handicapped, retired boxer who is haunted by his own past. The night of the execution, Nathan realizes that the freedom he has been fighting for, is what he should be running from...
