The Chad Effect

 Chads took their toll on the 2000 Presidential election in more ways than one. Reverberations from the controversy were felt around the world, but none felt it more than King Patsá and Queen Norjean from the small country of Mulvany. Who could have guessed that their family vacation to the Sunshine State would end so dramatically? With the media's spotlight focused on the turmoil surrounding the tabulation of voting ballots, the kidnapping of their daughter, Princess Elvia, went largely unnoticed - until now.
