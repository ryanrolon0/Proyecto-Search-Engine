The Bourne Conspiracy

 You, the player, are Jason Bourne. You are a deadly weapon . . . on the run. Play and progress through a storyline set loosely around "The Bourne Identity" film/book. Find out who you are and why Treadstone wants you ! Along the way, you'll face deadly foes, corrupt agents, assassins like you, foreign police, terrorists as well as a deadly secret as you engage in deadly combat !
