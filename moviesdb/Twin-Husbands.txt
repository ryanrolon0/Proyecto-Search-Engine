Twin Husbands

 When Leon tells his wife Dorothy that he has to go out of town on business, Dorothy's friend warns her that Leon seems to be away a lot of the time. Dorothy thus decides to go to the beauty parlor to get a new look. Meanwhile, at the airport Leon sees his twin brother Richard, who has just arrived in town. While Leon is out of town, Dorothy and Richard run into each other. By the time that Leon gets back from his trip, he finds himself in the middle of a series of misunderstandings.
