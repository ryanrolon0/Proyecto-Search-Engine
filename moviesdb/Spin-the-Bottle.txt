Spin the Bottle

 Sam thinks he has the answer - find the girl of his dreams and his problems are solved. His roommate Cole, still spinning from a break-up with Devon, doesn't believe in true love. Their best friend Zach does - especially if it involves Hannah. - but does she feel the same way? Over a beautiful spring weekend in New York City, these three young men find themselves face to face with their true loves. The beautiful and exotic Eva literally "steps" into Sam's life, Cole encounters Devon and they reconsider their break up and Zach summons the courage to confess his true feelings to Hannah. Will these bumbling heroes find true love before Sunday night? Spin the bottle to find out.
