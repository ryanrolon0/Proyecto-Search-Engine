Rest in Peace, Cuervo Jones

 Cuervo Jones is a low-level gangster who dreams of becoming a rap star. But when a drug lord's cocaine is stolen, all signs point to Cuervo and a bounty is put on his head. Now, he's dodging both sides of the law as he seeks out a motley crew of friends to help him survive. When his best friend is killed with a bullet meant for him, Cuervo Jones decides to fight back. He and his homies must discover who framed him, and fast.
