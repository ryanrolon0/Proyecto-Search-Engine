The Ecstasy Note

 The Ecstasy Note is an absurd dark comedy that follows a frantic month in the life of a middle-aged nobody named Henford Phelps (Mark Camacho). In a moment of rage, Henford's wife unwittingly discovers within him a mysterious talent. As public demand for his "ecstasy note" grows out of control, Henford is drawn into the center of a rising maelstrom of addiction and chaos that threatens to tear apart his town and his life.
