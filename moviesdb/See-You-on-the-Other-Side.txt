See You on the Other Side

 Inspired by a true story, 'See You on the Other Side,'introduces us to Zoey Meola - a vibrant, beautiful, young woman celebrating her 23rd birthday. With great friends and a boyfriend who adores her, life couldn't get much better. There's just one problem tragic turn, one that will alter her existence forever... To help Zoey accept her fate and maneuver through this new world is a sometimes wacky, but always endearing, cast of spirits - Tiny, a gentle giant, Sucio Al, an enlightened bum, and a spunky surfer type named Dana. 'See You on the Other Side' promises to be as entertaining as it is poignant and thought provoking. An off-beat comedy and a heart breaking drama, the film touches on questions of faith, love and life after death.
