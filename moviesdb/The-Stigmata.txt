The Stigmata

 Nine-year-old Desiree is blind and needs a real miracle. Instead, she suffers a stigmata bodily marks resembling the wounds of the crucified Christ. Her Muslim father, agnostic mother and Baptist grandmother are divided about how to handle the situation as they are about their own beliefs. When a Catholic priest is sent to study the case, Desiree becomes the coveted prize in a twisted play for power.
