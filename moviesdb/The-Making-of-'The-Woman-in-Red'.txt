The Making of 'The Woman in Red'

 This made-for-tv documentary gives comedy fans a look behind-the-scenes at the making of The Woman in Red, about a shy, quiet man who's desire for a little adventure in his life is answered with far more than he bargained for. Also included are interviews with stars Gene Wilder and Charles Grodin, along with the rest of the cast and crew, who all share some of their experiences from working on the film and discuss the special efforts that went into bringing the project to completion.
