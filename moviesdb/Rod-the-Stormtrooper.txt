Rod the Stormtrooper

 For years a battle has been fought between two relentless enemies. The great battle of sci-fi superiority. At the front lines of this battle is Rod Panaflex, a 4th level Imperial Stormtrooper who is training to serve at the Emperor's side. Rod is out to defeat a man who was once a friend, but now his arch nemesis, Patrick Fitzpatrick. The Trekkie's will stop at nothing to make sure the once-powerful Stormtrooper Core never rises again. However, the Trekkie's and the Stormtroopers do not know what is happening behind their backs...
