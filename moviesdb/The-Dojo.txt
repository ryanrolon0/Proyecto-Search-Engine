The Dojo

 Fly-on-the wall, reality program centering around the 'goings-on' at a new martial arts academy. Featuring, Scott "TNT" Tannenbaum, founder of The North American Federation of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (NAFBJJ), former pro cage fighter, and certified black belt instructor under Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu world champion Rigan Machado. Scott trains his team to become Mixed Martial Artists, by preparing them for competitions, tournaments, and cage fights.
