The Making of 'The Mean Season'

 This documentary treats movie fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of The Mean Season, about a crime reporter who finds himself targeted by a glory-seeking murderer. Includes interviews with stars Kurt Russel, Andy Garcia and Mariel Hemingway, as well as the rest of the cast and crew, who share some of their experiences with working on the movie, and discuss some of the efforts that it required.
