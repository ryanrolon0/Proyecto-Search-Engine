The Ghost

 It is a fantasy/mystery story about a farmer and his wife who adopts two coloured girls when their mother dies. When the farmer's brother is crippled in a very mysterious way, one of the twins challenges God to make him walk again. This leads to some strange things happening on the farm. It is a fantasy/mystery story about a farmer and his wife who adopts two coloured girls when their mother dies. When their white uncle, played by Kevin Otto, is crippled in a very mysterious way, one of the twins challenges God to make him walk or she'll never talk to Him again. This leads to strange things beginning to happen on the farm. In the end God sends his angel(s) down to earth to make the little girl's wish come true.
