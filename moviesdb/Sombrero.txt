Sombrero

 Writers use 'teller', an automatic typewriter machine, kind of computer, so as to write stories. All is one of them, but he's not the one. He's about to be fired by his editor in chief because he's not selling a word. He got one last chance. but his 'teller' is obsolete and he got no money to boost the machine. One of his friends Gil, who is a good 'seller', is about to go on holidays. Al would like to borrow Gil's teller. but Gil doesnt want. Between despair and anger, he got to find out a solution.
