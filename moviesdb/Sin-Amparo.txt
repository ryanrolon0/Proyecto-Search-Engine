Sin Amparo

 Rodrigo Castillo is very sad finds some fan's flowers on Amparo's grave. But when he finds that those flowers come from Armando Lascar, a friend of them, he doesn't get angry. What he doesn't know, is that Armando was the Amparo's lover. This is when it start to be shown some Rodrigo's and Armando's flashbacks, and Amparo's two lovers join their memories to rebuild their lives.
