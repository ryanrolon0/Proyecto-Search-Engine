The City for God's Sake

 This 3 part , 150 minute video documentary series explored how leaders and groups affected societal and spiritual change in major cities across the world, including Mexico City, London, Chicago, Nairobi and Delhi. Hosted by Dr. Raymond Bakke, a leader in urban research. Produced by the MARC division of relief & development agency World Vision International for worldwide vhs sales this series played in USA and Internationally in the 1980's.
