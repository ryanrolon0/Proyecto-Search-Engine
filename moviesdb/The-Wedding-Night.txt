The Wedding Night

 A story about a young couple embarking on the greatest adventure of their life, marriage. Unfortunately for them, their hotel does not share their excitement and does near nothing to make sure their hitch goes without a hitch. From an under prepared honeymoon dinner to the two slacker bellhops trying to make a quick buck, this couple's night goes from bad to worse at the fastest speed possible. Will love prevail? Will they tip the bellhops?
