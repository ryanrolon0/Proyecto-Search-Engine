The Road Ahead

 Summer 1993. A boy works on isolated country roads, saving money to buy a car and visit the girl he loves. Their late night phone conversations inspire him until eventually he finds himself driving down the road towards freedom and love. But for the boy and his girlfriend, the road is long. After one night together, the boy finds himself back where he started - working on isolated country roads, saving money to buy a car and visit the girl he loves.
