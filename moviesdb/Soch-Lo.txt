Soch Lo

 The film takes off from the point where a wounded man is left to die in the desert. He survives the ordeal but loses his Identity. He then resorts to robbing solitary car drivers traversing on this highway. He becomes a Thug and finds an abandoned shed as his hideaway, where three troubled individuals Harry, Toto and Pali find him. They take him to their home in a nearby village and name his Baba, the beleaguered stowaway makes a deal with Harry that he will fight for his property seized by a local brute and in return Harry would help him find his real identity. When Harry is not of any help, Pali steps in. Baba and sympathetic Pali embarks on a very long journey to solve his mystery. Flashes of his wife, visions of being stabbed and then betrayed come rushing to his mind. His relentless search leads to something more diabolic than his expectations. After losing his memory, unable to walk, a male struggles to survive in the desert. He does find water, and after no vehicle stops to assist him, he creates a road-block and robs motorists. Then 3 individuals, Harry, Totto, and Pali, arrive and decide to use his muscle-power to fight the Kullu family over land-takeover issues, and name him Baba Adam. After a physical confrontation, Baba overcomes his adversaries but is grievously injured but recalls being on a honey-moon with his wife. With the assistance of Pali, he to sets out to clear the mystery behind his memory loss, his identity, and the whereabouts of his wife.
