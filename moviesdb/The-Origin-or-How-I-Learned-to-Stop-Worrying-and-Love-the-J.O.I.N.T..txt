The Origin or How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the J.O.I.N.T.

 The Origin or How I Learned to Stop and Love the J.O.I.N.T. is the story of young James Bong's induction into J.O.I.N.T., a secret agency dedicated to protecting stoners. Here he learns the truth about his father and his own ultimate destiny. Follow his adventures as he investigates local dealer Chet Rok's foray into the oregano market, flirts his way to a 4 Sensimilia and spars with saucy German teacher Claudia Borutadef.
