The Inlaws and the Outlaws

 Jeff Parise and Cree Summer are in love. It's Christmas and time to meet the parents. Their families could not be more different. Both sets of parents have been married for over 30 years. Through a series of compelling interviews, home movies and photographs. We gradually come to know both uniquely individual families on a profound level. This documentary covers it all. Love, life, music, dance, art, food, laughter, spirituality, family and more. It shows us that love don't care where you come from.
