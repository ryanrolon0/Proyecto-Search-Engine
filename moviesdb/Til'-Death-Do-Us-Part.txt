Til' Death Do Us Part

 Anthony Charles is just an ordinary, hard-working man at the prime of his life. When he begins to tire easily and lose focus, he calls his old friend -- a local doctor. His diagnosis is severe. With two weeks to live -- and two weeks until he marries the woman of his dreams, will he be able to make the right decision? A story about choice and consequence, Til' Death Do Us Part is a film that everyone can relate to.
