Sexina

 SEXINA television aren't Sexina's only talents. The pop sensation secretly moonlights as a Private Investigator resolute in fighting corruption. When the leading scientific mind in humanoid robotics (and a former child pop star) is kidnapped, Sexina is hired to get him back. The investigation leads her to the mega-hit record label Glitz Records and its mysterious owner, known only as "the Boss". Coincidentally, Glitz Records has just unveiled its latest sensation; a can't-miss boy band that promises to top the pop charts forever! As Sexina grow closer to the truth, the reigning queen of pop must contend with assassins, ninjas, dangerous thugs, mysterious informants, a wild grizzly bear, and of course, take some time out for a little clothes shopping. In the end, Sexina is in for an unbelievable climactic battle of the bands like no other!
