Torchy Runs for Mayor

 Torchy Blane is investigating the political career of mayor Dr. Dolan, who has installed a corrupt leadership. She demands a new election, and after the opposition's candidate is murdered, she decides to run for mayor. Investigative newspaper-reporter "Torchy" Blane uncovers evidence of graft, corruption and murder against an underworld boss who has control of a big city's politics. After a political opponent of the machine is murdered while running for Mayor of the city, "Torchy" decides to put her own name on the ballot.
