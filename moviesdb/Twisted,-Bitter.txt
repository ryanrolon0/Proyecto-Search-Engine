Twisted, Bitter

 When filmmaker Ruchir Chowdhury's 'Caffeine Encounters' is selected at the Madrid International Film Festival, he decides to fly down his principal crew at his own expense. But on the way to the airport, he finds the most important thing missing - the film cans. Soon they realise that the cans have not just been misplaced but stolen, and someone is about to lead them on a wild goose chase if they want to recover their precious reels...
