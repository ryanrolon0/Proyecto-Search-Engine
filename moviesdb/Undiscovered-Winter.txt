Undiscovered Winter

 The famed Black Hills of South Dakota remains relatively undiscovered yet quoted as one of the '50 Wonders of the World'. Four years ago our family who grew up in the Black Hills and loves extreme winter sports set out to create a film to show the rest of the world the magic, mystery, and 'coolness' of the Black Hills; yet, the real irony in making the film was discovering the 'coolness' ourselves.
