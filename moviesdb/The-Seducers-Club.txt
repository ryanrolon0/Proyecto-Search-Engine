The Seducers Club

 Sal and Bobby, two college-aged charmers who have only one thing on their minds. After a number of failed attempts at losing their virginities, Sal goes to extreme measures to get what he wants. Friendships, reputations and lives are destroyed in this dark tale of depravity. The film features original music by Allan Vest and stars Sean Thomasson, Dallos Paz, Chanel Roady, Rebecca Cox, Graham Lee Brewer, Todd Staggs, Don Russell, Nehemiah Alexander, Hannah Wolff, Rhiannan Hilliard and Mickey Reece.
