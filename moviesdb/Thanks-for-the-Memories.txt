Thanks for the Memories

 In this supernatural thriller, a young woman is poisoned to death by her fiance after he discovers that she's learned he murdered his business partner. In purgatory, she is deemed wrongly-killed, granted a new lease on life, stripped of selected memories, and issued the body of a freshly deceased young lady to awaken in and inhabit. Flashing forward, she encounters her former fiance, and their uncanny chemistry results in them falling in love. Unfortunately, certain memories begin to surface in the young lady. She recalls first, that her new love murdered his partner and the revelation threatens their happiness. However, it's the next memory to surface that she deems unforgivable.
