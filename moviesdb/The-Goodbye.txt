The Goodbye

 In the face of loss there is still hope. This true story follows Sandra's last night in the hospital. Sandra has a loving husband, a beautiful son, an alcoholic mother, and a caring pastor. How do you say goodbye? Sandra's deep-seated faith transcends her circumstances and she finds the strength to not only say goodbye, but to encourage her husband to move forward. This film is dedicated to women of faith everywhere battling with or knowing someone battling with breast cancer.
