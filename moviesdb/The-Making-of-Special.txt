The Making of Special

 This made-for-tv documentary treats adventure fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea, an Australian-made adaption of Jules Verne's classic novel. Includes interviews with star Michael Caine, along with the rest of the cast and crew of the film, who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it to completion.
