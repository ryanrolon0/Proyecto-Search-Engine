Super Mehra Bros.

 AJ Mehra is an uptight young Indian-American professional, whose stoner little brother drops out of college and comes to stay with him. Reluctant at first to let him stay, he eventually enlists his little brother's help to win back his ex-girlfriend. Through their ensuing adventures, the once estranged brothers become increasingly close until finally, AJ comes to the heartbreaking revelation as to why his brother arrived on his doorstep.
