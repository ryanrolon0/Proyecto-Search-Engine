The Stiller & Meara Show

 Jerry is the deputy mayor of New York while his wife, Anne, is an actress whose only role come in TV commercials. After 30 years of marriage, they're still going strong even thought they're three kids haven't lived up to their expectations. There's Daniel, who dropped out of Harvard Law to play his sax on the street; Krissy, a shrink who takes her own advice a little to seriously; and Max, a never-ending troublemaker. In the first episode for this proposed series, Jerry and Anne try to plan a nice quiet celebration for their wedding anniversary. Hilarity ensues.
