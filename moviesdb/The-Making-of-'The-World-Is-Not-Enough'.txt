The Making of 'The World Is Not Enough'

 This made-for-video documentary treats James Bond fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of The World is Not Enough, in which 007 must protect a wealthy and beautiful heiress from an insane hitman who thinks only of revenge. Features interviews with the cast and crew of the film who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it all together.
