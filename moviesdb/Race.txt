Race

 Kira, an Asian-American woman, and Luke, an African-American man, work together as Vice Presidents of a Research firm. When their Caucasian boss, Jack, announces to them that one of them will be promoted to become Senior Vice President based on the presentation that needs to take place the following morning, Luke & Kira suddenly become at odds with each other. They let everything -- including the issue of race -- get in their way. It takes most of a fraught and bloody night for Kira and Luke to come to a realization....
