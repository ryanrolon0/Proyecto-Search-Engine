The Thief

 Cat burglar Samantha Reeves accepts an offer from the mysterious crime lord known as the Emperor to steal a necklace from Eddie Smooth, a half-assed criminal with a pain-in the ass mistress. In planning and executing the plan to take the necklace, the thief becomes emotionally connected to an oblivious young man who she's using as her alibi and begins to reconsider her line of work.
