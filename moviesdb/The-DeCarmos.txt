The DeCarmos

 The DeCarmos are known as the most celebrated troup of acrobats and equilibrists in Europe at the present day. In this subject they have executed some of their cleverest feats, such as somersaults, dangerous double somersaults, clever foot work, etc., etc., making in all a most startling exhibition and one that is bound to please all kinds of audiences. If you are looking for a subject that is different from the average motion picture, something that is sure to please, then this is the film you want.
