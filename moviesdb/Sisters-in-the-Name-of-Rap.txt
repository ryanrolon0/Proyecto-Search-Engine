Sisters in the Name of Rap

 Hosted by Dee Barnes of Fox TV's ''Pump It Up'', with acts ranging from established artists like Queen LaTifah, Salt-N-Pepa and MC Lyte to newcomers like Tam Tam. A 75-minute extravaganza of live performances taped at the Ritz in New York, this revue preaches self-reliance and demanding respect from guys, even if that means as one rapper says ''kicking them in the balls.''
