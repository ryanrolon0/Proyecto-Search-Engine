The Last Good Man

 Affairs at Jack's Lounge are never what they seem, especially with the unexpected arrival of Audrey (Margaret Gabriel), a strikingly independent female patron. When young bartender Sam (Paul Rodrigues) falls in love with this mysterious beauty, he uncovers a web of lies and deceit that he must struggle to overcome if their relationship is to survive.
