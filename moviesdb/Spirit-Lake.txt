Spirit Lake

 Through a startling discovery on an ice fishing expedition, traveling copy machine salesman Wayne Speedstock begins an unexpected self investigation. Wayne finds that he is not the man he thought he was and becomes raveled in a darker mystery that involves his girlfriend who he neglects. Through a series of revelation's brought about from the mystery at Spirit Lake, Wayne finds himself on a quest to find his soul, win back his girlfriend and live a life of fulfillment.
