Scandal in the Mansion

 In a rich mansion, French maid Mimi finds her employer Mr. Buttons dead. She joins the butler and German housekeeper Bridgett in bed and reveals the news. Police Inspector Armstrong and detective Miss Walston arrive to investigate. The widow Mrs. Buttons seduces the inspector while The butler keeps pinching Miss Walston's buttocks. The Swedish chef Inga seduces Inspector Armstrong by demonstrating her actions with Mr. Buttons. Miss Walston then seduces the butler. Inspector Armstrong soon joins them. The female staff comfort each other. Case closes as a stereotyped gay coroner finds a suicide note. The maid and housekeeper celebrate with the butler, while Mrs. Buttons celebrates with the gardener.
