The Motivator

 From food stamps, government cheese and powdered milk to CEO of a multimillion dollar company, International Motivational Speaker Victor Antonio G.'s story is one of triumph in the face of tremendous adversity. "The Motivator" is a voyeuristic look at the business of professional speaking from one of the industry's most honest and entertaining personalities. Victor shares candid observations on the self-improvement industry as he travels to a national conference to deliver the keynote address.
