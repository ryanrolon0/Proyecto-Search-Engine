Promise Not to Tell

 When Allan Hadfield was convicted of four first degree felony counts of child sexual abuse of his ten year old son and twelve year old daughter the small town of Lehi, UT could not believe it. Allegations of satanic ritual abuse had been made for more than three years against more than 60 adults in this community. Nationally acclaimed psychologists, social workers, legal scholars look at the problem of social policy when children are the only witness to a crime against themselves.
