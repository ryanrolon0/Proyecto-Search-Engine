Puppy Bowl VII

 Back for its seventh consecutive year, the Puppy Bowl brings viewers a lineup of adoptable puppies tackling and chasing each other inside Animal Planet Stadium. Special features include the water bowl cam, tail-gating fans, a blimp piloted by hamsters, the all-kitten halftime show, and for first time, a puppy P.O.V. from special "Puppy Cams."
