The Van

 Infamous TV host Colin Malone (Colin After Dark) stars as Rusty Guzner, a serial killer who possesses a van with his demonic soul. Three horror stories trace the van's bizarre journey from Rusty's capture and death to it's new owners cost and Paul, a handicapped dishwasher willing to run over anyone who gets in the way of his van. The bodies pile up as the new owners discover they're riding shotgun with evil. Three stories. Two owners. One demonic van. Take the ride!
