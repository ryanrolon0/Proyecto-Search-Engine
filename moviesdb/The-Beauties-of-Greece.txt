The Beauties of Greece

 The Beauties of Greece is a fascinating look at the countless beauties of the Greek Islands from Crete to thrace and from Rhodes to Corfu. The astonishing golden blue beaches to the picturesque villages and traditions of everday life amid these ancient cultures. Paul Pissanos takes you, the viewer on a tour over thousands of yesrs in a journey that unveils the endless diversity of beauties in a land blessed by the gods...
