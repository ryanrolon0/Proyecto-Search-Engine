Saravann

 Saravann is a young man with dreams to reach the top. One day he decided to drop everything and head over to New York City to pursue a career as movie actor and model. The journey to success is not an easy one, Saravann realizing from his friend Merlin that he has succeeded already....??? Saravann shares his thoughts and emotions in his diary and with Merlin of the present moment, the now, success and matters of most importance in this world and the next. That is until...... Saravann is forced to reveal his true ability and his life's calling.
