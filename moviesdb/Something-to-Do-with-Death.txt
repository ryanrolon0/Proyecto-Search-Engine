Something to Do with Death

 Part three in a documentary trilogy about the making of C'era una volta il West. The first part of Something to Do with Death focuses on the music of composer Ennio Morricone. The music actually was written before the shooting even began. The documentary continues with the political views of director Sergio Leone. Did C'era una volta il West have a political message? The film concludes with a view about the importance and influence of the film on the world then and now.
