Tender Rage

 When Mickey arrives uninvited at Richard and Julia's country home, they are abruptly forced to fight and fear for their lives and the safety of their eight year old son Liam. It is quick to show that Mickey's mind is not as stable as one would hope. Mickey struggles to keep himself together and hide his pent up emotions. However he gradually splits further and further at the seams as his troubled mind forces itself deeper into the equation, teaching us more about his hard, tormented past as we go. Richard must stay strong if he is to save his family, but nothing in life is that straightforward. Who is in the right and who is in the wrong?
