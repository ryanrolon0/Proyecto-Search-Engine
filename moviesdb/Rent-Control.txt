Rent Control

 Lucy dreams of becoming a painter. But ever since her live-in boyfriend became her ex-boyfriend, she's lost her inspiration. And she's stuck living with the jerk until she can find a cheap place. So when Lucy finds her dream apartment for a dream price, she takes it. While she waits to sign the papers, the current tenant's phone rings. The machine picks up. The caller She stares at the blinking light. She looks around the incredible apartment. She hits delete. With the wedding 24 hours away, Lucy scrambles to convince the groom to get married. But along the way, she falls for him instead. Will Lucy win the apartment or the man of her dreams?
