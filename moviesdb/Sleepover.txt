Sleepover

 On the last day of 8th grade before their freshman year in high school, Julie has a slumber party with 3 best friends As a trio, they end up having the adventure of their lives. To win the high school lunch spot by the fountains, they have to win a all-night scavenger hunt against their popular girl rivals. They need a car to get to the places that they need to get everything on the scavenger hunt list. Yancy has a little tiny car in her garage. Her dad taught her how to drive it, so they use the tiny car. In order to win they sneak into clubs, they don't listen to Julie's mom's rules about leaving the house, and Julie's first kiss, anything is possible at Julie's sleepover.
