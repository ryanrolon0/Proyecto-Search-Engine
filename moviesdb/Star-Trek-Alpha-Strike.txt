Star Trek Alpha Strike

 A fan film based upon the idea that Gene Roddenberry's 1964's Star Trek's 'the Cage' pilot is still the apex of star trek(before Kirk was captain or Spock was first officer and Captain Pike commanded the Enterprise), and our heroes are aboard the sister ship of the Enterprise, the USS Alpha Strike, getting into strange and weird adventures! Filmed in glorious black and white...star trek alpha strike is bare knuckles sci-fi excitement! Written and directed by Jerry Williams....with horror host Spooky D, Leslie Rogers, Chad Hundley, Claude Miles, Steven Guynn, and scream queen Heather Price star in this not for profit fan film of the star trek universe.
