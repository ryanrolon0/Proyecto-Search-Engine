The Chocolate Farmer

 The Chocolate Farmer is the story of Eladio Pop, a man who is so far behind the times, he may actually be ahead. A contemporary cacao farmer and descendant of the ancient Maya, Eladio represents the ambitions and dilemmas amongst the farmers of a progressive co-operative in southern Belize. Contained inside Eladio's family, within a small community, is a story that underscores the increasing dis-connect with a direct relationship with nature and offers an interesting alternative to all global or all local.
