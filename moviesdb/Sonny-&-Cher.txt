Sonny & Cher

 A rare and nostalgic look at the couple that defines the 70s is now on DVD. Complete with the hair-styles, bell-nottoms and sideburns, this classic-to-be showcases the couple in the happiest of times. Sonny & Cher joke, sing and dance their way through the program, leaving a lasting impression of Cher's magnificent voice, charisma and beauty as well as Sonny's songwriting.
