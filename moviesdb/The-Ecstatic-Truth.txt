The Ecstatic Truth

 A film about film making. The life story of a legendary German filmmaker. He ventures to the most treacherous and unforgiving corners of the world, all in the name of cinema. Now the filmmaker must embark on a journey to find true justice and balance through the art of cinema. Together we will discover the deepest essentials that define us as human beings. Searching for the truth, THE ECSTATIC TRUTH.
