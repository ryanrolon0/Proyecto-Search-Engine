Scenes from a Campaign

 Congresswoman Lusi Lion champions 'New Politics' in her quest to become the mayor of the city of Lenape, New York. As the days wind down towards the election, the conflicts in her campaign bring into question the 'newness' of her political call, and we watch as her advisors, Joan Quinn, Vince Romani and Joseph 'Fitz' Fitzpatrick, battle for the soul of her candidacy. Local reporter Tricia Woodward is there as the drama unfolds.
