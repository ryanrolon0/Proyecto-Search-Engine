Praise the Dead

 When Gabriel, a charming serial killer, meets Victor, a somber and masochistic gay man, on the street one day, they develop a symbiotic, destructive relationship that haunts both their dreams and reality. As they realize that they have an unusual bond, they seek comfort in each other while exploiting the other's weaknesses. But when they decide to take a trip into the woods, the true nature of their relationship is revealed leading the way to pain and madness.
