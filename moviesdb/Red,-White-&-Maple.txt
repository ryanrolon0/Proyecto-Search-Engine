Red, White & Maple

 This film chronicles the saga of urban music in Canada. The documentary is composed of raw and insightful interviews from producers, managers industry executives, singers, mc's and dj's along with riveting performances by Canada's best hip-hop, r&b, reggae, gospel, and soul artists. This film dives into the intricate world of the music business and it exposes the struggles and triumphs of Canada's uniquely incomparably talent base. Issues surrounding artist development, sales, record companies, commercial radio, and economic infrastructure within the urban music industry in Canada are explored in a vibrant, uncompromising, and authoritative manner.
