Syphon Filter

 Gabe Logan is now in charge of the Agency and it's about time too. The Syphon Filter virus is radiply spreading all over the world, and Gabe must hire new recruits to eliminate the terrorists responsible for launching the outbreaks. Gabe also has to locate Mara Aramov, who's vital to preventing World War III. The only way to stop the virus forever is to locate the mysterious person behind the conspiracy from the beginning.
