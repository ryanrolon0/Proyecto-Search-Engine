The Great 80's TV Flashback

 "The Great 80's TV Flashback" takes a nostalgic look back at some of the decade's most beloved television shows. "The Great 80's TV Flashback" appears on the following DVD's Season 4, Miami Vice Season 3, Gimmie a Break Season 1, The A-Team Season 4, Kate and Ally Season 1, Simon & Simon Season 1, Murder She Wrote Season 3, Quantum Leap Season 4, and Knight Rider Season 4.
