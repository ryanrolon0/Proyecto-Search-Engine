Restless

 After days and days of sleep deprivation, the mind can turn on itself. Delusions come as surely as reality. When pushed to the absolute limit, what could you be capable of? College Sophomore, Leah, is forced to consider this very question. When her insomnia becomes too much for her to handle, things could become dicey for her younger sister, Gina. This short satirizes the thriller genre, guaranteeing a laugh-out-loud experience.
