Screen Snapshots Series 16, No. 8

 Stars attend a barbecue at the Racquet Club; Frank Morgan's new business enterprise, The Trading Post, a country store in modern style, is seen. The annual Academy Awards are presented Paul Muni, George Jessel, upstaging Bette Davis who was to have been the official presenter, to Luise Rainer; Frank Capra unveils the Irving Thalberg Memorial Award; Norma Shearer makes her first public appearance since Thalberg's death six months earlier, on the arm of Louis B. Mayer.
