Restless Spirits

 Rowdy ropin' granny Toni Savoie-German finds Zen in the art of Rodeo. Toni Savoie has always had a passion for horses and riding. Now in her late 50's Toni is on the rodeo circuit as a break away calf-roper. Toni's uniqueness is in living life on her own terms. The beautiful backdrop of this film, and of Toni's life, is the rural Ontario landscape and small town rodeo culture.
