Reluctant Hero

 A normal day on tour for Reluctant Hero takes a turn for the worst when their van is swallowed by a bright flash of light. They soon realize they're lost in time. Before they can get their bearings, the band is staring down the barrel of Vincent's gun; an evil tyrant with a taste for blood and a hate for music. In a nonstop action packed adventure, Reluctant Hero must run, fight, and rock to save the town and protect what they love!
