The Aquifer

 By the mid 21st century, diminishing and polluted water resources will represent a serious threat to life on earth. The world's biggest reservoir of water lies under the ground in Brazil stop at nothing to protect it. This will be the beginning of a dirty war. While ex-CIA agent John Strudge reveals a political conspiracy in southern Brazil, he saves the beautiful landlady Stella Cortes from the cruel means of their opponents.
