The Pearce Sisters is a tale of love, loneliness, guts, gore, nudity, violence, smoking and cups of tea. An amusingly bleak hearted tale of two weather lashed old spinsters. Lol and Edna Pearce live on a remote and austere strip of coast. Taking shelter in a beaten driftwood shack they scrape out a miserable existence from the sea. When the sea offers up a handsome, but half drowned man, they do their best to resuscitate him. Its not until he fails to show the proper gratitude that things start to go, unfortunately, somewhat awry. The Pearce Sisters is a tale of love, loneliness, guts, gore, nudity, violence, smoking and cups of tea.
