Trails of the Wild

 An agent tracking down a man who disappeared in the mysterious "Ghost Mountain" area discovers discovers the hideout of a gang of murderous outlaws. Mountie Jim McKenna and partner Windy are sent to look for missing mining Engineer Madison. When they are accused of murder, they get unexpected help from two Mounties working in disguise. This lets them find Madison but learn his note on the claim he found must be paid by midnight. While McKenna heads for the bank to get the money, henchman place themselves to make sure he doesn't return.
