Vixen Highway 2006

 Vixen Highway 2006 and 70's exploitation pictures & a re-imaging, re-regurgitation and semi-sequel to John Ervin's original 'Vixen Highway'; by Canadian schlock-meister Faustian-tale, which takes place in Minnesota where the ailing, egotistical and sexually perverted rock-star Bobby Barzell (who sold his soul for wealth and rock and roll stardom) awaits a liver transplant from a psychotic fugitive of the law; as a vengeful, demonic alien tries to collect a past debt. This multi-genre feature is chock-full of Blood, Breasts, Bullets and Beasts ... an interstate, interplanetary menagerie of bad-assery! Killer fugitives crack zombie & a demonic Star Beast wreak havoc all over Mid-West America. Hero Detective Divine Otaku has to investigate; but, will the sexy hunter become the hunted? Vixen Highway 2006 Detective Divine Otaku's horrific, gut-wrenching, psychedelic interstate Mid-West U.S.A. "Star Beast / Cocaine Zombie Ritual Murder Case" & fugitive "Vixen Gang Investigation" !....Will the Hunter become the Hunted?
