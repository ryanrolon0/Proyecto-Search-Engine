Service Men

 Jason Nicholls returns in the sequel to Service Man, where he must face a tougher opponent in a new fight challenge. For his 1st military posting, Jason is posted to an RAF Air-Station. Whilst there he meets his Father who he has not spoken to since he was five and their difficult relationship must develop. Jason must also help his friend Kerry who is in trouble with the law and beat his fearsome new opponent Captain Rick in the All Arms Boxing Tournanment. However, Jason must do all this and recover from a sinister bout of food poisoning. Will he recover in time and how badly will his fight training suffer?
