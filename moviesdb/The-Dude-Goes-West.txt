The Dude Goes West

 Daniel Bone leaves his Bowery shop in New York City to seek his fortune in the American West, and, on the train, meets Liza Crockett, also heading west, where her father had been murdered after discovering a gold mine. The Peco Kid tries to steal Liz's map to the mine, but is accidentally foiled by Dan'l. Liz and Dan'l part, after a quarrel, but meet again in Arsenic City where the mine is located.
