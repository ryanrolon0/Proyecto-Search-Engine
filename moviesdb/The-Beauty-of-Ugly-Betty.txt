The Beauty of Ugly Betty

 The Beauty of Ugly Betty is your guide to the most popular new show around - ABC's "Ugly Betty". Get to know Betty Suarez, the fashion-challenged girl from Queens with the heart of gold, who is trying to fit in at a Manhattan fashion magazine. Her focus on internal beauty has struck a chord with America, and her "Seven Secrets to True Beauty" are here just in time to help you ring in the New Year. Join the cast and the experts at People Magazine and get caught up - plus take a peek at what lies ahead for "Ugly Betty".
