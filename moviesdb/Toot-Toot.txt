Toot Toot

 At six years old, bespectacled and liquorice-chewing Stan (Nicholas Evans) is determined to have the best Christmas ever. Awake the early hours of Christmas morning, Stan can't help but take a peek at the presents. When he discovers they aren't anything like the pictures on the box, he must go back to the drawing board of his epic plan. While his determined approach to fulfill his Christmas wish may get him into a whole lot of trouble, fear not; Stan understands the true spirit of Christmas!
