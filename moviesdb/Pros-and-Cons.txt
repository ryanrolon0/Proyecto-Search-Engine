Pros and Cons

 Jack Snow is a family man obsessed with winning his high stakes fantasy football league. He's a huge fan of fantasy football and professional football. After his wife forbids him to play, Jack's friends convince him to play one more time, but he keeps it secret from his wife and son. He must take home the league championship in order to win the $60,000 payout in order to save his family. But at what cost? The surprise ending will have many viewers asking is Jack now living the good life or has he thrown it all away? We'll let you decide
