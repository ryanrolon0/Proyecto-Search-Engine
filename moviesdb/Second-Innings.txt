Second Innings

 A Group of Senior Citizens come together to participate in forth coming fashion show for senior citizens. WIthin a short period of time they become very close friends of each other. During this process they lose one of their dearest friend due to faulty system. the tragedy ruins their peaceful life & turn it into sorrow & grief. At that point they realize that whatever they have seen & shuttered in their first phase of life termed as first inning. Hence they decide that this is the exact time to fight against this faulty & shrewd system, making their second inning an extra ordinary event. They revolt against the existing system with their astuteness & intelligence making their second inning a remarkable event.
