Pool Sharks

 This is Fields' first movie. a one-reeler (10-15 minutes depending on frames per second), it stars Fields performing the pool game routine which he had performed often in vaudeville. Two ardent suitors for the hand a pretty young woman carry their fight from an picnic to a test of skill in an indoor pool hall. After a series of unbelievable trick shots, the fight degenerates into billiard throwing, which involves an innocent bystander.
