Survival Tactics

 Released early from jail, street musician, Jet (Wire MC) attempts to reunite with his girlfriend, emerging young dancer, Rosa (Demi Sorono). While he was in lockup, Jet's best mate Fury (Morganics) has slipped into homelessness and drug addiction, scoring from Jet's rival, DJ Rich (Bboy Jay). Now their rivalry comes to a head when he discovers that Rose has been rehearsing with DJ Rich to host his upcoming all star Hip Hop jam.
