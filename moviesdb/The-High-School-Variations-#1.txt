The High School Variations #1

 High School Variations follows the story of Mike, a 17 year old kid navigating his way through a maze of events in school. Through the day, Mike seeks advice in friends and neighbors, all in his quest to avoid responsibilities in class and with his parents. The story escalates when Mike chooses to confront his difficulties, learning that the best way to overcome what tests you is to stand up to it face to face.
