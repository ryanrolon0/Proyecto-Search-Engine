W

 On the planet Earth water supplies are running out in 2019. The governments support space missions in order to look for alternative planets.After years of searching a planet is found. A spaceship that is able to move a limited number of humans on this planet is built .Every nation of the world claim to be saved, and so a new war breaks out on Earth. The war and the total absence of water on the planet will cause the extinction of the human race.
