Brian Epstein was the flamboyant, brilliant, but troubled entertainment industry visionary best-known for discovering and managing the Beatles. The Fifth Beatle is a non-documentary feature film that covers the last 6 years of his life. His story unfolds via a mixture of history, fantasy, allegory, dream sequences, and hallucinations.
