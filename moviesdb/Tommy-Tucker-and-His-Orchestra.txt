Tommy Tucker and His Orchestra

 This "Name Band" musical short features the band of Tommy Tucker, which opens with "Colonel from Kentucky." Vocalist Don Brown sings "All I Do is Dream of You" and Marilyn Hare sings "For Him No Love", while the band plats for both. The band also plays "Singin' in the Rain", "I Never Knew" and "Give Out." Universal production number 2307.
