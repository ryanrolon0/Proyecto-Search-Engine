RU-486

 The dilemma faced by one woman over whether or not to abort the baby she carries inside while the world around falls apart. Meena Patel (Zina Badran) leaves her home and boyfriend, spending the week preceding her abortion with a student whom used to be besotted with her. She hopes he still is, for he is only one left to turn to. The only one who could help her keep the baby.
