Tempo of Tomorrow

 A Paramount Headliner featuring the music of Richard Himbler and His Orchesta. Baritone Stuart Allen sings "The Prom Waltz," written by Leo Robin and Ralpg Rainger, and he also sings "Alone at the Station." Patricia Gilmore does "Listen to My Heart," and the short ends with Himbler's orchestra swinging on "Gettin' Off."
