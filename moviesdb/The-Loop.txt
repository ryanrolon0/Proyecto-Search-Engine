The Loop

 A topical personal drama that plunges headlong into a nightmarish world that revolves around a deprived 17-year-old boy - Lox - dealing with the ever-rising violent youth culture in London. As Lox drifts around the concrete plains of his estate, he talks candidly to camera sharing with the viewer in-depth details of his life. He talks about peer pressure, family issues, violence, sex, education, death - all whilst trying to accommodate the rage growing inside him as another day slips away. Through Lox's raging words it's possible to get a glimpse of his vulnerability. And there is a glimmer of hope, as Lox shares his thoughts on a brighter future and on trying to break his way out of the loop.
