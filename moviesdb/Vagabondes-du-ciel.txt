Vagabondes du ciel

 This documentary explores the history of comets, from the Roman Empire's times until the Renaissance. During this travel along the centuries the men will change their point of view about the comets, believing what they was disaster's symbols or a joke of the gods. More later the Holy Church considerate it as messengers of God, and finally they taken as fortune's symbols and change's signs. A travel awesome by the stars in company of the wanderers of the sky.
