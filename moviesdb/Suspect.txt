Suspect

 Scott Ferguson is a dedicated cop who knows right from wrong and something is definitely wrong with his latest case. After interrogating the suspect with his partner, Emily Keane, Scottie is not convinced that he has heard the truth. As he works late into the night he is visited by a Special Investigations Agent, Mark Halliday, who claims he wants to help with the case. But as the night wears on and tiredness sets in, Scottie struggles to separate dream from reality and friend from foe. The question is; does he still know his right from wrong?
