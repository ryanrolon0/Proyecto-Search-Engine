The Legend of the Gray Man

 Islanders on Pawley's Island, South Carolina today still stand by its' Legend of The Gray Man and his existence. Dating back to the 1800's his sightings by residents still exist today. This tiny island and its' residents still hold true to this incredible legend, which has built a strong tourist trade that draws international visitors yearly. The Legend of the Gray Man is not only a tourist attraction, but also based on numerous witnesses that have claimed The Gray Man actually saved their lives and warned those on the island prior to a hurricane - is it fable or true?
