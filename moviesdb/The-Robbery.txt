The Robbery

 They say that crime doesn't pay... The town of Fannington in Central Australia is gripped by 'The Robbery'. A thief breaks into the local millionaire's estate and steals many of her valuables - most importantly her prized possession 'Pom-Pom' a spoilt Cavalier King Charles Spaniel. This is all over the local news. What will become of Pom-Pom and the thief? Find the answers in 'The Robbery' a short film made for Trop Jr Film Festival.
