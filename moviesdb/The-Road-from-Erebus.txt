The Road from Erebus

 A young man's obsession with a mysterious woman unleashes the psychotic rage of her abusive ex-lover/foster father and the seductive manipulation of her jealous alter ego. The woman's shocking secret involves them all in a desperate confrontation that takes place as much inside their own minds as in the physical world, where they must fight for their lives. Barry Atman just spent the best night of his life with a sexy, mysterious woman named Helen. This morning she disappeared. Barry has to know why. But his obsessive search to find her unleashes the psychotic rage of her abusive ex-lover and the seductive manipulation of her jealous twin sister. Helen's shocking secret brings Barry into a desperate confrontation that takes place as much inside his and Helen's own minds as in the physical world, where they must fight for their lives.
