The Kamoro

 The Kamoro A Documentary by Kalman N. Muller The film follows the traditional Kamoro, who's life-style fits perfectly with the bountiful resources that nature provides for them, such as fishing, collecting shellfish, hunting for monitor lizards, gathering the slimy tambelo, and sago production among lots more.
