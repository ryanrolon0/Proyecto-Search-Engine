Que Lejos Estoy

 How has one song about missing one's home taken over the hearts of many Spanish-speaking immigrants and ex-patriats across the globe? The film explores how 'Cancion Mixteca', written by a student in Mexico in 1934, has captured the imagination of people from all walks of life, who sing the song and tell their stories.
