Toros y toreros

 Short documentary about bullfighting made of archive images featuring Ricardo Torres 'Bombita', Vicente Pastor, Marcial Lalanda, 'Parrita', 'El Andaluz', Pepe Martín Vázquez, JosE Miguel Dominguín, Domingo Ortega, Carlos Arruza, 'Manolete', Conchita Cintrón, Álvaro Domecq and Juan Belmonte. Images show man-bullfighting and horseman-bullfighting.
