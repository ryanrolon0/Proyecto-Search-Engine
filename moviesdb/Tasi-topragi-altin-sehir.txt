Tasi topragi altin sehir

 Okkes comes to Istanbul from a small village with his wife Fatma, son Mehmet and brother Cemal to Istanbul. They buy a tractor and work for paying its installments. Fatma works as a maid for a bar woman which bothers Okkes a lot. Mehmet sells illegal cigarettes and gets arrested. Okkes is fired and he starts selling fruits. In the meantime the tractor's price increases. Cemal's boss and is a smuggler and Cemal is shot at a police operation. Okkes's tractor gets stolen and Fatma leaves with another man. Okkes is the only one in the family, who could stay firm in Istanbul, but understands that the life in the biff city is not easy.
