Veja Esta Canaão

 Four love stories which portray a wide gamut of emotions and desires against the backdrop of Rio de Janeiro. "Elephant's Stomp" highway patrol officer who is infatuated with a mulatto dancer at a local nightclub. "Drao" in their marriage. "You Are Beautiful" amidst their personal misery. "Samba of the Great Passion" who is charmed and captivated by the voice of a female singer coming from a building across the street. Characters in search of love in order to escape from the boredom, loneliness and helplessness of their lives.
