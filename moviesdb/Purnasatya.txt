Purnasatya

 Novelist Amrit Manohar gets invited by Patang Rao, the Sarpanch of Gandhi Nagar, to write a fact-based account of this ideal community. However, upon arrival itself, Amrit gets assaulted while trying to help a woman to retrieve water from the village well. He gets medical treatment from Dr. Ashmita and finally gets to meet the Sarpanch. Amrit does not know that he will be lured into the personal lives of the villagers.
