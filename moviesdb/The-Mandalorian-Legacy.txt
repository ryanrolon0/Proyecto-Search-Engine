The Mandalorian Legacy

 A Star Wars film about the originations of the infamous bounty hunter, Boba Fett. It tells the story of how he becomes the character that is well known from the Original Star Wars Trilogy. In the film, Boba undergoes physical and emotional hardships that mold him into the hardened and ruthless bounty hunter that he is famous for. Along with Boba Fett's origination story, there are many characters that are recognizable from the Star Wars films and a few surprises that will excite any fan.
