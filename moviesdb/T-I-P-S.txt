T I P S

 Bellman Raymond Beck wants to become Manager at Canyon View Resort. He is married to a wealthy resort owner's daughter, but wants to climb the ladder of success on his own, without any help. Raymond is forced to do petty little unimportant, non-bellman related tasks at the resort. Merrilee Kauphman pays Raymond for sex and Raymond learns Merrilee has been a Swinger with his in-laws. Raymond wants Merrilee out of the Resort. He asks his father-in-law to make him Manager and to take Merrilee off his hands.
