Sencillamente Guty

 "Sencillamente Guty" is a documentary film that was made upon the 100th anniversary of the amazing, emblematic composer of the Yucatecan music, Augusto Cárdenas Pinelo, "simply" called Guty. It was made in the city of MErida, Yucatán, and it gathered together people from different generations who expressed their recollections and their unbridled admiration for Guty, such as the famed historian Dr. Silvio Zavala, as well as musicians and music investigators. The delight with history, the music, the loving anecdotes as well as the legend are the true leading characters of this documentary.
