Roundhay Hall

 A very subtle character driven piece following two men Sebastian and Ethan. It shows how they find solitude and happiness in one anothers company over the course of one evening when meeting at a murder mystery dinner. It is here that they discover they are not alone when facing the pressures that society and everyday life place on them. It's not long after making this revelation, both men realize they must go back to their everyday lives and individually seek out the next step. This is a story of friendship, self discovery and romance. It shows the cross over between heterosexuality and homosexuality and touches upon the difficulties that some young couples face. It can be interpreted in many different ways by many different audiences.
