Smash!

 The summer holidays financial dire straits, the two best friends decide to try and save it from disaster. Their team mate Sarah, quickly changes clubs and they find her on the opposing side. After many unsuccessful attempts to earn money for the salvation of the club, they enter the beach volleyball competition. Soon a whole new set of problems exist and the girls seek the help of former coach who accepts their plea. Will Linda and Ema prepare themselves in time for the most important tournament in their history?
