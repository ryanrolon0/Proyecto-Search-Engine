Shut Your Blog Off

 Shut Your Blog Off This drama, suspense is about a news reporter trying to standout and make a name for himself. As he takes on his first assignment, he uncovers an insurance scandal that is sure to win him the Investigation Award. Wanting to shed some light on the issue, he begins posting his findings on his blog. But, not everyone wants the truth to be revealed and they will go to lengths to keep him quiet.
