Sev spitak

 'Black & White' is a story about seven women living in a remote mountain village during the war, waiting for their husbands. Their children don't survive the hunger and cold. Their husbands perish on the battlefields. Now, the war is over and only one man comes back to this village of widows. But life and love are unbearable, poisoned by death and solitude. In a remote, mountainous village seven women wait for the arrival of their husbands from the war. Their children are starving. The wives soon get the death notices of their husbands. All the women try to resist the cruelty of destiny and nature.
