Razmataz

 Razmataz is a story about the meeting between old Europe and young black music, and the meeting takes place in the city that produces the most resonance to that event respresented in the story by a series of symbolic characters bourgeoisie, the expressionist artist from Berlin, the italian man-of the world, the parisian Haute-couturier, the sporty englishman, the lady detective-story writer.. The work in its finished form involves a mixture of different techniques effects, drawings and soundtrack with original music and songs (including 8 brand new paolo conte tracks never released before).
