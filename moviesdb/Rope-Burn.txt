Rope Burn

 The show is dangerous and exhilarating and Charlie, the circus rigger, is responsible for the safety of the trapeze artists;her girlfriend Simone and co-performer Bella. Moments before the curtain goes up, Simone discovers Charlie cheating on her with Bella. Amid the tensions of jealousy and deceit, Charlie has to stop the pair from performing their risky double act before something goes horribly wrong.
