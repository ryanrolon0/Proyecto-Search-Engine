Vengeance

 Lucious Malone, a successful hit-man for a Detroit mob family, is set up for the annihilation of his entire gang. He then teams up with his ex girlfriend Christine, to find the person responsible for the mass assassination. As they dig deeper and deeper into Detroit's seedy underbelly while trying to find the real killer, their investigation leads them into dead end after dead end as more and more people want them dead and the death toll keeps piling.
