Soul of a Juggernaut

 Two boy create what they belive to be an fictional comic book character, until the truth of the monster reveals itself.The ancient demon becomes real and murders one of the boys. leaving the last boy running for his life,with no one to turn to or they will next. A young man by the name of Nate Mathews has had a normal childhood until today ....... his best friend has died, Which he believers has been murdered by an imaginary beast that they created.The beast is only known to them as the Juggernaut. A ancient demon born to do one thing, enslave the souls of all mankind. The evil spirit is now after Nate and will not stop until it has consumed him. No one else can ever know or they will be next. As the guilt and anxiety of Anthony death builds in Nate, he falls into a deep depression. Nate then makes the deadly mistake of telling his new female friend of the demon, opening her up to the beasts wrath.
