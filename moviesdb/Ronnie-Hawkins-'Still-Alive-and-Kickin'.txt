Ronnie Hawkins 'Still Alive and Kickin'

 Toronto, Ontario (July 29, 2004) - To his friends, he's Mr. Dynamo. To his doctors, he's a miracle. To the rest of the world, he's The Hawk, the Arkansas-born King of Rockabilly who made Canada his home and his stage for more than four decades. He's still rocking the house today 70 against all odds. On Friday, August 20 at 9 premieres Ronnie Hawkins documentary.
