The Artist's Passion

 Danny is an artist that has finally agreed to sketch his college girlfriend, Brooke. However, as she poses, his questions turn into an interrogation, and a secret is pulled from her that pushes him to thoughts of murder, suicide, or both. The tensions winds to a climactic, surprising resolution. 16 year old Nick Rua, wrote, directed, and edited the film, including composing and recording the haunting original score.
