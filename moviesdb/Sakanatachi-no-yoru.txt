Sakanatachi no yoru

 Mr. and Mrs. Sasaki are struggling to make a living as a leather worker. One day, when the husband takes a walk alone at night, he finds an old man lying helpless on the street. As a kind man, he takes the old man to his house, and offers him food and a night's lodging. Next day the old man, getting back on his feet again, is willing to help them by bringing their products to their customer's place. The husband is sure that he is a good old man, but the wife keeps doubtful eyes on his strange behavior...
