The Scent of Harvest

 An independent feature film concerning solitude among America's younger generations. The film blends the humor of different cultural perspectives on human relations with the universality of solitude and the need for family and friends. The result is a moving tale of a lonely young professional woman who is befriended by an elderly foreign man. Natasha is a successful young acupuncturist. She runs her own clinic and is loved by her patients. But her personal life is lonely and empty, a fact that becomes painfully clear when her boyfriend coldly rejects her. Reeling from the pain and driving through tears, she collides with a large truck. This collision begins an unexpected friendship that opens her eyes to a part of life she has been missing.
