The Adventures of Wild Kat Jones

 Kat Jones is not your average teenager, she and her father, Peter, make their home on a mysterious island where he, a renowned paleontologist, is hoping to recover a complete T-Rex fossil for the Smithsonian Museum. When she uncovers a plot to steal the last T-Rex bone, Kat relies on help from her best friend and a few wild animals to save not only her dad but also herself.
