Trafficking

 Serge Grimbert wakes up to find himself in an unknown place. He soon finds out that he is in a shack in the middle of the desert. Shook up by a spell of amnesia and a wound to the head, he tries to remember what happened. Inez, a woman who is nursing him back to health tells him that she found him on the road with his film gear scattered all around. Serge slowly begins to remember why he came to the desert child trafficking. As Serge slowly recovers his memory, he discovers that it was no accident that he was lying in the desert left for dead.
