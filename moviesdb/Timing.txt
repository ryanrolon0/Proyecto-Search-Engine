Timing

 In a city orchestrated by the ticking of a clock, only a dreamer can fight time's inhuman pace. He escapes in nighttime fantasy to find the mysterious woman he longs for, but when he comes close, time intrudes and forces him into the drone of his daily routine. But today will be different encounter and the sound of a familiar laugh leave him wondering could this be the girl from his dreams? Is she the way out? Only time will tell.
