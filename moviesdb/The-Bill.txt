The Bill

 Feature-length version of the popular TV series, sees the police officers of Sun Hill, South London involved in a murder mystery after the seemingly random shooting of an innocent woman on the street. Originally shown in three episodes - "Fire" (transmitted October 6th 1995); "All Tucked Up" (transmitted October 10th 1995) and "Bait" (transmitted October 12th 1995) PC Loxton and WPC Ackland are caught up in an accident where a respected member of the community is shot by a sniper. But who was the intended target?
