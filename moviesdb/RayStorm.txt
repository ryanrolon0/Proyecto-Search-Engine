RayStorm

 In a future date of Earth, space travel and colonization becomes a major part of life. The main colonies however are under strict military jurisdiction which has complaints and grievances from the colonists that go unheard. This results in a wide-scale rebellion that stretches to the colonized planet Secilia. The rebellion is so successful that Earth is forced to surrender and the forces further plot to destroy the Earth. It is at that time a special type of fighter ship is created and deployed in a secret operation to destroy the colony rebellion completely.
