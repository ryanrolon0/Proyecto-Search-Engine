Sí, se puede

 "Chivas USA an MLS expansion team. With intimate candor from its protagonists, this journey takes us through the ups and downs of pre-season, teamwork, injuries, and most importantly, the coming together of American and Mexican players to speak one language buying furniture and learning a new language, these professional "migrant" players never expected to go through what one day will be remembered as the birth of the first ever Mexican Team in the MLS.
