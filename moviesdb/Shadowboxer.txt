Shadowboxer

 Twenty-three-year-old Dylan Walsh (Jonathan Pretty) is back in his little hometown of Kelowna, British Columbia, after a college life full of sex, booze and partying. But now that he's back and seeing all his old high school friends again, he realizes they've all grown up and left him behind. Even his best friend Stan (Scott Lawler) has started to distance himself. But Dylan refuses to let Stan go-until Stan's deepest secret is revealed. Then Dylan must make an inevitable choice; the result of which will make him gain-or lose-everything.
