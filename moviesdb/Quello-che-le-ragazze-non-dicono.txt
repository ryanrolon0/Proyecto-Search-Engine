Quello che le ragazze non dicono

 Francesca, Paola, Laura and Alicia are four friends that fall in love with the same man, a musician called Gigi.Alicia is a woman that fears compromising in her relationships, Laura is an overprotected and dreaming girl,Paola is a lawyer who seeks true love, and Francesca is a spectacular dancer in a TV Show, independant and sexy.
