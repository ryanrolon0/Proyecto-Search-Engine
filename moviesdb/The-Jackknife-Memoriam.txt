The Jackknife Memoriam

 Faced with his ailing, absentee father and his own paternal and marital shortcomings, James life is turned upside down on this trip to Echo Lake. This triptych mid-American family drama follows Jim, James and Jack Jensen through the birth and death of a misguided paternal legacy; literally left in the hands of an 8-year-old boy. Part One 1st of 3 parts of this feature-length film. James confronts his father and his estranged wife Abby. What he learns on Echo Lake will have an affect on the rest of his life.
