The Serial Killer and Vampire Guardian Angel Diaries

 The Serial Killer and Vampire Guardian Angel Diaries (TM) are the thoughts of Lia Scott Price's Serial Killer and Vampire Guardian AngelTM Characters who answer the prayers of the despairing who pray specifically to them by putting them out of their misery. A Guardian Angel is a supernatural, unseen being. So, do you really know who you're praying for help to? And what they are really thinking?
