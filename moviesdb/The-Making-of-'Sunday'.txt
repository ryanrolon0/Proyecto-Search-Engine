The Making of 'Sunday'

 Behind the scenes on-location documentary about the making of _Sunday Too Far Away (1975)_ (qv), the first feature length movie produced by the South Australian Film Corporation. Features locations such as Port Augusta, the Port Augusta Town Hall and Carriewerloo Station, all in South Australia, Australia. Features on the set filming footage and interviews with director 'Ken Hannam' (qv) and star 'Jack Thompson (I)' (qv).
