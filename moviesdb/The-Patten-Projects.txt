The Patten Projects

 Twenty-three short moving image projects that span a broad range of imaginative thoughts and ideas. These projects represent a significant portion of Grant Patten's creative output during his time as a university undergrad from 2003 to 2008. 1 envisioning the mundane imaginatively; 2 through color and shape; 3 need for a visual impetus in accurate recollections; 4 Paint/Scratch Version two; 5 The Cowardly Camera - the personality of the camera. 7 Challenge - aiding in the treatment of a widespread ailment,  visual illiteracy; 8 perspectives on the same event; 9 and the attempt to overcome it; 10 atmosphere; 11 Young Man - the exploration of a space and the potential discoveries therein; 13 the artificiality of reality; 15 mystery of disease; 16 17 A Study of Wind Behavior - the discovery of a pattern within nature; 19 Visual Study - the analysis of imagery and the benefits thereof; 20 Interactive Cinema - a call to eradicate the passivity of the medium; 21 The (Rough) Date of My Death - the use of aleatory techniques to predict mortality; 22 reality into a movie reality; 23 the personification and characterization of architecture.
