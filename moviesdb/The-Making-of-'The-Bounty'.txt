The Making of 'The Bounty'

 This documentary treats movie fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of The Bounty, about the investigation into the mutiny that occured on the HMS Bounty under the command of Captain Bligh. Included are interviews with stars Mel Gibson and Daniel Day-Lewis, as well as the rest of the cast and crew who share their experiences from making the film, as well as disscuss the efforts that went into it.
