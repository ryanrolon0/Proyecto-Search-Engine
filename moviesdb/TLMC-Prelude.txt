TLMC Prelude

 TLMC is a sentinel soldier from the Chronodeon, a misterious corner of the universe where video-roaming is made possible through captured human memories. On a mission to find information about soap bubbles, a system error finds him trapped inside specific memories belonging to the inhabitants of Victoria in a 2010 time co-ordinate. He is gradually exposed to human activity, but more importantly, exposed to the Tekenus. A tribe of unusual men of inordinary faculty with whom he finds possible to communicate...
