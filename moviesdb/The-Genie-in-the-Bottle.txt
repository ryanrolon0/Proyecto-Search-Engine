The Genie in the Bottle

 The Genie in the Bottle is the animated version of the beautifully illustrated children's story by Professor Hugh Montgomery who together with the film's director, Dr Jack Kreindler, run Project Genie - a new initiative putting primary school children and teachers in charge of Climate Change. The film, narrated by actor Tom Baker, tells the story of an old man explaining the fate of a once beautiful planet to his grandchild.
