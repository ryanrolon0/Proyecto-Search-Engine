Tight Genes

 Tight Genes - A reporter risks it all vs. a huge pharmaceutical company (Rated - R) A screenplay based on the novel "TIGHT GENES" by David E. Flake Producer is giving a sweet assignment to redeem his career. All he has to do is write an article about VitaChrome, a new product to hit the market and the big drug company behind it. The assignment turns into an unwanted clandestine investigation as he follows a string of clues that leads from the streets of LA to a secret lab in Malaysia. Sam struggles with his morals, then his life as big corporate money tries to silence the truth.
