Shrove Tuesday

 Follow a day in the life of religious spruikers, Nathaniel and Frederick, as they ply their trade through a block of apartments. With little success trying to talk residents into following the lord they are forced to apply the rules of trade which invariably lead to their own downfall. Follow a day in the life of telemarketers, Dimitri and Eric, as they deal with the daily rituals of putting callers on hold for no real reason. However Dimitri has plans to leave the trade and convinces Eric to join him. unfortunately. the story are linked strangely through phone callers on hold and an opportunist known as Sven, the cleaner.
