Undercover Angels

 Three beautiful young women have just graduated as private investigators and are about to embark on their first 'undercover' assignments under the watchful eye of the master PI, Charles Rahim. They all want to be taken on in Charles' elite PI firm and begin new lives in this world of mystery and danger. But do they have what it takes? Can they handle the pressure? Will they ultimately succeed and become Charles' Undercover Angels?
