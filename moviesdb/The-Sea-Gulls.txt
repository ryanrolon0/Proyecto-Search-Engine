The Sea Gulls

 Samsara, means transmigration and recurrence in Buddhism. This is a mystery drama and surrealistic story. Alex and his wife Jane are sunbathing on the beach. Jane complains he's incompetent. Meanwhile, seagulls are calling and flying over them. Jane is attracted by the seabirds and starts chasing after them. Jan's cell phone rings and wakes up Alex. He thinks Jane is missing, and then, a policeman and a young lady comes sequentially. Alex is attracted by the sea gulls and chase them with the young lady. After Jane comes back, she begins to find Alex. Everything is a continuous circle, like Samsara.
