Soldier of Fortune

 Our story focuses on the struggle of 1st Lieutenant Ayman Habid Thuliam, a Middle Eastern American who was raised in the practice and belief of Islam, as he lives his life in a Middle Eastern POW camp. As a way to cope and remain connected with the world and people he left behind, Ayman begins to write down his life story. Regrets, ambitions, and thoughts of a young military man drive the story as the viewer realizes that we are more similar than different. Everyone fears, everyone loves, and everyone wants to live.
