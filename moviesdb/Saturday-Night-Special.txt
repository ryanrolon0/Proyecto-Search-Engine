Saturday Night Special

 A large group of criminals gather at an abandoned strip club to celebrate the release of an associate from prison. After waiting for hours things start taking a surprising turn into a new direction. What seemed to start as light entertainment escalades into a violent, bloody, downward spiral, revealing the whole gathering as an entirely different setup.
