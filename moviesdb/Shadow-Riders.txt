Shadow Riders

 When Elliot O'Connell learns that his father, a North West Mounted Police constable, has been murdered in cold blood by the vicious Carter Gang, something inside him snaps. Elliot becomes obsessed, compelled to meet his father's killers face-to-face. The film follows the events of that fateful night when Elliot's bloody past finally catches up to him. He finds himself -through his own quest for vengeance- in a position similar to his father's, confronting powers he never imagined possible.
