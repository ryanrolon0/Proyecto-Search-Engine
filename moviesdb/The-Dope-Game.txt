The Dope Game

 Two hired killers for a ruthless drug lord, fed up with killing, decide to jump into the Dope Game. On a seemingly simple trafficking job, the two killers fly to Texas. The job worth of heroin from a Mexican trafficker and transport it back to California safely. It sounded simple, but in the Dope Game, things are easier said than done. Will they succeed, or is this a game they never should have played?
