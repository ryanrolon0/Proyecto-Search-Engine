The Witchdoctor

 The film revolves around Mr Omau who has not been feeling well and decides to get a H.I.V test done at one of the many free voluntary counseling and testing centers in Nairobi Kenya. The results confirm that indeed Mr Omau is HIV Positive. With the news, his world begins to crumble. Despite the counseling centers advice that he can live a normal healthy life with H.I.V Mr Omau decides to seek an alternative cure from a witchdoctor... The film though a 5 min comedy seeks to Educate as well as celebrate the role of traditional healers in spreading the message that one can live a long and healthy life with H.I.V.
