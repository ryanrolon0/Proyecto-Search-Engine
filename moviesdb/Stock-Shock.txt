Stock Shock

 Buckle up and hold on to your wallet as we follow the wild ups and downs of the American stock market through the eyes of stunned Sirius investors. Sirius Satellite Radio's commercial free music and blockbuster talent seemed to make it a sure-fire investment. After buying out their only competition in 2008 the stock was poised to go through the roof, but something went horribly wrong. Was it the bad economy? Mismanagement? Or something even more sinister?
