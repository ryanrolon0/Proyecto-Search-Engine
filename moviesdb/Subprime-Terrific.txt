Subprime Terrific

 After losing his house to the bank and his wallet to bad luck Jeff struggles to buy his daughter a pink bike for her birthday. Unable to find anyone willing to accept his check, Jeff must break into his foreclosed home and reclaim an old big screen if he wants to raise the money. Haunted by old creditors, and inner demons, Jeff struggles to deliver on his simple promise; caught up in the bleak realities of the financial crisis.
