The Pardon

 Toni Jo Henry is arrested and accused of murdering a man on Valentines Day 1940, following an ill-conceived plan to rob a bank to help pay for an appeal of her husband's murder conviction. With the help of two inexperienced tax attorneys, Toni Jo fights for her life in a system stacked against her. Because of her beauty, Toni Jo instantly becomes a notorious celebrity, drawing unparalleled media attention. 'The Pardon' is the incredible true story of Toni Jo Henry, a story of love and despair, of hope and redemption, found in the only woman ever to be electrocuted by the State of Louisiana. The incredible true story of Toni Jo Henry, a product of childhood abuse and neglect, who briefly discovers love and happiness that soon turns to despair after she is accused of murder and faces electrocution in two highly publicized and sensational murder trials.
