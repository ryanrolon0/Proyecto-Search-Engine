Sat.land

 The film combines satellite images from different parts of the world into one simulated topography. It shows a smooth, animated flight over conflict areas (Afghanistan, Iraq) and topical cities (Falludja, Cairo, Los Angeles) and develops from a "devine" to a "military" perspective. Whilst the sound scape intensifies, aircrafts are crossing, and cars drive by - far below on roads and highways. But in the midst of Los Angeles something unexpected happens...
