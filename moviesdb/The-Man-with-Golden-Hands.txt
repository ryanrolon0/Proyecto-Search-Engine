The Man with Golden Hands

 It's sometime in the not too distant future, and Dr. Iscariot, a hugely successful gynecologist, known within the medical community and by his more than 5,000 patients as THE MAN WITH GOLDEN HANDS, is suddenly interrupted by an unscheduled patient that walks into his office unannounced, and walks out 8 minutes later leaving the doctor teetering on the edge of insanity.
