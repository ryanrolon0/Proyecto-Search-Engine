View of the Spider

 A mockumentary short revolving around Dawn, a pro-female boxer, living a one track life, obsessed by Sylvester Stallone, determined to live her life as she wants and win the title. Those around her have a different view. She still lives with her Mother, who's scheming to get Dawn out of her house, joining Dawn to a dating agency. Mother is determined Dawn can find true love but is she too busy thinking of the next fight? Does she have what it takes? Is self belief all you need? How far would you go to live the dream?
