The Platoon of Power Squadron, Part IV

 The 4th episode of a web-serial with 27,000 subscribers and consistent viewership of over 50,000 views for each episode. Episode 4--Do you really need a secret identity if you can't find crime to fight? Can you actually choose how people see you? What if the most proactive part of you broke free and didn't want anything to do with you anymore? Trying to live up to your potential during your twenties is hard...having super powers just makes it harder.
