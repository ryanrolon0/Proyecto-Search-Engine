Supermodel Showdown

 The "On location DVD" will take you on an intense two week journey into the heart of the Costa Rican jungle with models that have appeared in Maxim, Playboy, FHM, Stuff and countless others. Hosted by the vivacious Nadia Dawn and led by a fearless director D.J. Perry and an adventuress photographer G.W. Burns. The commando style film crew has photographed and filmed a group of brave sexy exotic supermodels. Watch the stars of this 13 episode reality show series "Supermodel Showdown - Costa Rica" heat up the already unbearable hot and humid jungle. Your heart will pound as you bear witness to these girls as they pose be of breathtaking waterfalls upon pristine beaches and amongst the colorful jungle foliage. This Central American "Eden" will provide the ultimate backdrop as primal beauty becomes one with some of world's most refined beauties to ever grace a DVD. Get ready for Supermodel Showdown "On Location DVD" Costa Rica.
