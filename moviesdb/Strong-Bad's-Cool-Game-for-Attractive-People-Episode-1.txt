Strong Bad's Cool Game for Attractive People Episode 1

 It was another ordinary day at the Strong house for Strong Bad. Checking E-Mails, playing video games, pulling off pranks. As well as cooking up a scheme in winning the "Tri-Annual Race to the End of the Race". Only thing in the way is his slow thinking advisory find a way to get the disgraced athlete back on the A-list and out of his own house.
