Ruk tee ror koi

 Sangchan is a girl working in a factory who falls in love with an attractive guy named Rawee. As Rawee has to go study abroad, he keeps a promise of coming back to see Sangchan on October, 8th of each year. While Rawee is away, Sangchan who anticipates to see him has a chance to befriend to Lim, another guy who admires her. Now it is the test of love, faith, and vow that Sangchan has to make through. Sangchan is a factory worker who falls in love with an attractive guy named Rawee. As Rawee has to go study abroad, he promises to return home to see Sangchan on October 8th of each year. While Rawee is away, Sangchan befriends Lim, another guy who admires her. Sangchan and Rawee meet again several times over the years, with the turbulent politics of Thailand in the 1970s and 1980s always close by in the background.
