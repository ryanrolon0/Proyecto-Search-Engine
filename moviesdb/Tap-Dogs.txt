Tap Dogs

 Tap Dogs, the all-male Australian dance troupe, have taken the world by storm becoming an international sensation with their sell out tour... leaving packed audiences gasping and howling for more! Tap Dogs put power, humour, high velocity and raw energy into tap. This is the show that hypnotises audiences with the thunder of synchronised feet. Six Aussie guys stomp their way through a dynamic succession of pulsating routines. This is testosterone tap!
