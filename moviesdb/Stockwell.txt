Stockwell

 Dramatised account of the events leading up to the death of Jean Charles de Menezes, a Brazilian electrician in London. On 22 July, fifteen days after the 7 July terrorist bombings in London, police have a block of flats under surveillance where they believe suspects in a set of attempted bombings the previous day are living. As Menezes leaves his flat to go to work, he is mistaken for one of the suspects, and followed by police as he boards a bus to Brixton and then on to Stockwell. As Menezes boards a train at Stockwell tube station, he is confronted and shot dead by armed police officers.
