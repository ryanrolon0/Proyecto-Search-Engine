Sexual Confessions

 Director Ted W. Crestview takes four scintillating Seduction Cinema sinners, then proceeds to get them to confess and undress at the drop of a "tell me all about it" from sexy shrink Katie. Ruby digs Darian, Darian desires Kelli, and Kelli wants them all, Dr. Katie included, and when the smoke clears, you can bet this quartet of sexually charged young women has set psychiatry back about 100 years!
