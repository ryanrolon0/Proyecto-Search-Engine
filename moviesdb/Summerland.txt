Summerland

 When Hector 'Creeper' Molina, a 25-year old gang member obtains his release from a youth correctional facility, following a nine-year stint, he returns home only to find himself imprisoned by his environment. The choice remains, continue down the path of destruction or risk his life to save himself. Forced to work in a convalescent home as part of his probation, he befriends an older Armenian man of similar circumstance. 'Summerland' brings these two unique individuals together for a common goal. Freedom.
