Takav ti je zivot

 That's life tells a story about Jo sip, a young man who had to come to Zagreb because of the war in Bosnia right after he had finished elementary school. In a new environment he has to fight to make it and with time he slowly gives up on his dreams. He starts working on a construction site where he meets Jojo. A latent conflict occurs on a working day lunch break, during which Jojo slowly takes Josip on a trip to his past.
