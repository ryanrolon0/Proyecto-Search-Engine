This Is an Adventure

 During production on the film _Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou, The (2004)_(qv), documentary filmmakers followed the cast and crew of a film which depicts other documentary filmmakers who follow animal life. In this film, we get a first hand look at the sets and come very close to many of the cast and crew members at work, especially 'Bill Murray'(qv) and 'Wes Anderson'(qv).
