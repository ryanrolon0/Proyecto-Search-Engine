Vardi

 Lalchand (Kader Khan) is arrested by Inspector Verma (Vinod Mehra) and vows to avenge this. He does so by abducting his only son, and later informing Verma that he has killed his son, in the process of apprehending Lalchand, Havaldar Bhagwan Singh (Dharmendra) is killed by saving Verma's life. Verma and his wife Shanti (Anjana Mumtaz) bring up Bhagwan's son, Ajay, as their own. Years later Ajay (Sunny Deol) has grown up to be a police inspector as well, Verma is now the Commissioner. Together they band together to stop Lalchand, who is now a crimelord of international standards, from carrying on terrorist activity in the country; and also try and locate Verma's son, who they find out is still alive, but leads a life of crime. A notorious bandit, Lalchand kidnapped Inspector Verma's son, Anil at a very young age and kills one of his accomplices, Inspector Havaldar Bhagwan Singh, and later informs Verma that he has killed his son too. Verma and his wife Shanti brings up Bhagwan son. Years later the boy became a cop like his dad, Inspector Ajay Kumar Singh, Verma is now the police commissioner while Lalchand is now a king-pin of the underworld criminal activities. On an encounter with Lalchand's henchman, Rudra, Inspector Ajay comes to know that Verma's son, Anil, now known as Jai/Munna is alive but is a crook who will do anything to make a quick buck. Ajay now swears to unite Verma with his son and to bring Lalchand justice.
