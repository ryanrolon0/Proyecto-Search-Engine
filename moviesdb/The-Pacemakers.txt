The Pacemakers

 A series of twelve 2-reel comedies, each story complete in itself and each title a play on words modelled after a popular feature film of the era Welcome Granger; (2) He Who Gets Rapped; (3) Merton of the Goofies; (4) The Great Decide; (5) The Fast Male; (6) The Covered Flagon; (7) Madam Sans Gin; (8) Three Bases East; (9) The Merry Kiddo; (10) What Price Gloria?; (11) Don Coo-Coo; (12) Miss Me Again.
