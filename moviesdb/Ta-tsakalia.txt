Ta tsakalia

 Panos is a young and handsome man who only cares about motorcycles, discos, girls. When he realizes that he cannot go on living in poverty, he decides to rob his own father (Spiros Kalogirou- a highly respected Greek actor) along with 2 friends of his. A couple of days ago Fanis (Stamatis Gardelis- an "icon" of the 80s Greek cinema) dies.
