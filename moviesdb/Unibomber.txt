Unibomber

 We've all been there. The feeling inside where you feel like you have to go to the bathroom but at the same time, don't. Our lead has experience with that, and after his coworker introduces him to 'LiquidGo' to relieve some stress caused by their upcoming presentation, he uses the drink to get over that feeling instead. What follows is an embattled quest to find the perfect bathroom with maximum privacy so he can be free from his torture.
