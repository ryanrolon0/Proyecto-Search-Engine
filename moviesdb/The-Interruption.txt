The Interruption

 Before Marten Fisher has a chance to commit suicide, she's interrupted by a phone call from her friend Robin. Robin begs Marten to come over and stop her boyfriend Frank, a man who can't control his temper, and someone Marten's had to deal with before. But things take a turn for the worst when Marten finally confronts Frank, and she must make a decision that will change the course of her life.
