The Art of the Deal

 "The Art of the Deal" tells the heart-warming story which many children face today. Eight-year old Ethan, only child to single-mother, Trish, escapes his loneliness through his love for military aircraft. Oblivious to her son's craving for her love and attention, Trish is more focused on climbing the corporate ladder. In a series of events, it is Ethan who demonstrates the skill of savvy negotiating.
