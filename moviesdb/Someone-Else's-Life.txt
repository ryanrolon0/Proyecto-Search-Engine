Someone Else's Life

 Someone Else's Life brings awareness to pediatric congenital heart defects and the advances being made to heal and prevent them. The complex case of Selma Wood sets the backdrop for the emotional moments, gripping visuals and compelling information that the film relates. The collective stories of heart patients and their families are combined with the wisdom of and progress made by an array of leading medical professionals and related organizations across the United States. By sharing the lives of affected children and looking into the future of pediatric cardiac medicine, the film reminds us what it means to stand-in-the-gap for someone else's life.
