Refract

 Neil wakes up on a strange train without his belongings or any knowledge of where he is. He scans his surroundings and approaches a junkie sitting across from him to find some answers. Thus begins an interesting journey for Neil, who finds a eerie connection to the three people he encounters on the train - the junkie, a young child, and an old man - in the process revealing the dark secrets of his past and inner turmoil. After each character exits the train, Neil is left alone for the first time to make a vital decision.
