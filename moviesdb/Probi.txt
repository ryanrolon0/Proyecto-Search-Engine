Probi

 Life through the prism of love. Four young people caught in their private cathartic moments discovering true meaning of love. Poet worships love, desperately trying to keep it alive. Athlete plunges into the darkness of his soul, just to uncover his heart. Journalist, who lives and breathes love, is lost, speechless without it. Dancer, a free spirit, is in search of true language of love. When their paths cross...
