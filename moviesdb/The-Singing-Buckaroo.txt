The Singing Buckaroo

 Barbara Evans has $25,000 and Gifford is after it. When his henchman fail to get it he refuses to pay them. They then decide to double cross him and get the money for themselves. Gordon is trying to protect Barbara and he must not only take care of the two henchmen, but also Gifford and his phony Sheriff. Gifford, a crooked stock broker, is about to abscond with $25,000 of bank money, but Barbara Evans and her father take it until the situation can be resolved. Barbara escapes with the money and is being pursued by Gifford's henchmen when she is rescued by Grant Gordon. Gifford holds Mr. Evans prisoner and demands the money in exchange for his freedom, but Grant and his expert archer Indian friend Maneeto, rescue Evans and capture Gifford and his gang.
