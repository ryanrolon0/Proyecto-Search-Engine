Reactionary

 With the continuing fight in war torn Afghanistan, two US Soldier friends Gonzalez and Regan face a new threat each day in the most dangerous land in the world. During an operation to extricate insurgents from a civilian town, Regan makes a crucial knee jerk reaction that will change the dynamics of the two friends relationship forever. Now faced with a dilemma of morality in men, Gonzalez confronts his close friend and his own worse fears.
