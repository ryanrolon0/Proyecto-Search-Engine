Tell Me If It Hurts

 Luncheon at a respectable restaurant. A diner damages a tooth. In intense pain, he telephones his dentist's surgery. He rushes there by taxi, as fast as the London traffic will permit. Arriving at the surgery, he is left in pain in the waiting room. He is treated with extensive drill work, he suffers, he recuperates. The operation to save the tooth has failed. Work must begin again, suffering recommences...
