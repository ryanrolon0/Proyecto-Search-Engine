Story About a Witch

 In a town near the North Pole, Andrew Stitch and his teammates are being terrorized by an uncommon witch. When young Stitch's baseball jersey is stolen, he knows the Witch is to blame. Unable to play baseball without his assigned jersey, Andrew and his team solicit the power of a young goth girl to help catch the witch. What Andrew finds is the unraveling of an old Christmas tale, a tale which hits a little too close to home
