The Biggest Wish

 Zoran Loshich, Serbian refugee from Knin, Croatia, has been in wheelchair since he was 6 years old. Wounded from the back by 75 years old retired army officer, disgruntled by kids playing soccer under his window. Film also deals with the war and the exodus of the 450.000 Croatian Serbs in 1995 after the "operation storm" by the Croatian Army.
