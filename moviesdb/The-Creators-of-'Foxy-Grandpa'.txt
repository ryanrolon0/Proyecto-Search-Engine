The Creators of 'Foxy Grandpa'

 The "Foxy Grandpa" series consists of eight pictures, illustrating scenes and incidents from Wm. A. Brady's musical production "Foxy Grandpa." This picture shows the various people who have made the character famous. Carl E. Schultz (Bunny), the artist; Joseph Hart as "Foxy Grandpa;" Carrie DeMar as "Polly;" and the two mischievous boys "Chub" and "Bunt."
