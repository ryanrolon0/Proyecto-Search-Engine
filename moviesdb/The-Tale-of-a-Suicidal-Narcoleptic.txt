The Tale of a Suicidal Narcoleptic

 A romantic comedy based on the loneliness of life, and the joy of love. Set around Josh a 20's something retail manger, whose ambition has faded along with his drudging hope for tomorrow. When the only answer left is to end it all, Josh finds solace in Lindsey, the suicidal hot-line 1 am shift. Josh soon discovers that the one girl who can change it all is a hooker.
