Sicilia

 The image of Sicily, in the world, is often related to chronicles of blood, danger and fear but if we take a glance beyond that, we find out that Sicily has being for centuries a melting pot of culture that made this region the first successful example of synergy among different civilizations. This documentary brings you into the uniqueness of the historical journey and the intensity of the present and takes you on a personal journey to discover an unusual horizon both timeless and universal.
