The Empty Space in Between

 When Cassandra, who exists in a seemingly perfect yet empty marriage, finds herself alone in the Bel Air mansion a mysterious phone starts to call from somewhere in the house. When she finds her old cell phone and a photo album tucked away in a box she is brought back to the memories of the love of her life, Isaak. The phone wont stop ringing and when she finally hears the voice of Isaak she decides to return to a passionate yet destructive past.
