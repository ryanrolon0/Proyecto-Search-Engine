The Sheepish Wolf

 Married for only three-and-a-half weeks, Harry Von Zell is anxious to get home to his new bride but his boss sends him to entertain a client in hopes of winning a big radio contract. Once he gets there, he learns the woman has a jealous husband, and when the latter finds Von Zell in her room havoc breaks out, and even more havoc ensues when Harry's wife shows up.
