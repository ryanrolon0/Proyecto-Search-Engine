Split Decision

 The painful and inspiring true story of middle-heavyweight and cruiser-weight world champion boxer Bobby Czyz. Set in 1970's New Jersey, this dramatic biographical short-subject depicts how a promising first-born son lives for the love of his family. With their encouragement, he turns to boxing to win respect. Then boxing empowers Bobby to try something daring. Can he protect his family from the disturbing abuse they have suffered at the hands of a tyrannical sociopath? To succeed, Bobby may have to betray the most important person in his life... his own father.
