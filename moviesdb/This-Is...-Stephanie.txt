This Is... Stephanie

 The sequel to the 2009 comedy short This is..Amir. This time around, 17-year-old Stephanie is the main character, and plans to hang out with her best friend Nicolette for day. But Nicolette blows her off, Stephanie's day gets more weirder and worse as she prepares to carry on her day without her best friend. Stephanie finds out that her day is just not gonna get any easier...it's just gonna get more epic.
