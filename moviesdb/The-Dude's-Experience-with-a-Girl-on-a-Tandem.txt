The Dude's Experience with a Girl on a Tandem

 "A number of bathing girls are shown in the surf frolicking, and one of them is on shore with a tandem bicycle. She persuades a swell young man, who is in his regulation street attire, to take a ride with her on the double seated machine, and having him once mounted she directs it into the water, where he is seized by the other girls and thoroughly ducked."
