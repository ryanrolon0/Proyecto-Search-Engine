Storm Surfers, Dangerous Banks

 Two Aussie legends of big-wave surfing - Ross Clarke-Jones and Tom Carroll - as they risk life and limb to hunt the biggest waves of the Australian 2008 winter season, including a never-before ridden wave called Dangerous Banks. In a combination of sport, adventure and science, Ross and Tom team with leading surf-forecaster and meteorologist Ben Matson to track and chase the largest storms in the Southern Ocean.
