The Importance of Blind Dating

 How hard is it to find your soul mate? A pair of Internet romancers end up at the wrong table for a hilarious mess of a first meeting. Stephen Tobolowsky portrays "Patrick", an omniscient flower vendor, who manipulates destiny to drive four unsuspecting strangers together. Mistaken identities,a mix-up with a certain red carnation, and a popular book by Oscar Wilde all conspire to prove that "the course of true love never did run smooth."
