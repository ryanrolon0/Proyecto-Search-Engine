The Sage Ewing Show

 Sage Ewing lives life from one moment to the next. He feels that his reality is the norm for everyone else. There's nothing that Mr. Ewing likes more than meeting new people. Especially people that society regards as important. Sage never misses an opportunity to interact with doctors, lawyers and corporate executives. He finds them fascinating. In addition, Sage also likes a challenge. He loves the challenge of staying healthy. He loves the challenge of trying to get an advance on a lawsuit which has yet to come before a judge. Furthermore, Sage likes the challenge of trying to get high-powered corporate job although he has no qualifications for it. The Sage Ewing Show is about a man who is completely out of touch with reality.
