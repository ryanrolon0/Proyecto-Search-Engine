Self Inflicted Freedom

 After 20 years on the police force, Officer Tina Jackson encounters a case that shakes her to her core. As she hunts down an elusive child molester she grapples with emerging and painful memories. Can she continue to be an upstanding, law abiding, and objective police officer under these extreme circumstances? Or will she throw away her many years of exemplary service and numerous awards for a moment of revenge? Officer TJ falls back on her trusted community allies and spirituality for support. While Chanting, Therapy and Drumming for Healing have always worked in the past, Officer TJ now faces a new demon. As the situation reaches an explosive ending, who will remain standing?
