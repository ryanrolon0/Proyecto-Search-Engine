The Luckiest Girl on the Lower East Side

 The Luckiest Girl on the Lower East Side is a film that blends honesty and artistic interpretation through colors, edits, and sound to create a more genuine experience. This slice of life tale doesn't have pretension or overly sentimental & commercialized views. The story takes place in modern day New York City and revolves around Sarina, a young French-Algerian woman who finds herself at the crossroads..fighting for her life. With lovely shots and a lot of honest details in location and characters that anyone who has ever lived in a big city can relate to. The film features music from NYC experimental/punk legend SUICIDE, and upcoming bands such as AVA LUNA, Ultra Violent Lights, hip-hop artists POETIC REPUBLIC, and jazz musician GREG DIAMOND.
