Pozdniye svidaniya

 Pozdniye svidaniya (aka...'Late Rendezvous') is a story of Lena, a career woman , who is in search for a perfect man. Lena finds herself in a midlife crisis, when her successful career does not bring her the fullness of life anymore. Her loving father gives her support and comfort, but Lena wants a real husband and a family, just like most of her friends. So she starts dating men, and finally ends up being torn between two good choices...
