Running Home

 On shaky ground in a new relationship, Lee Delano takes an impromptu vacation from her closeted girlfriend to re-connect with her HIV positive, estranged gay brother Sebastian. Lee's unexpected arrival into Sebastian's life fuels his life partner Mitchel's insecurity that Sebastian's biological family may appear at any moment to make a claim on their estate. As Sebastian's health deteriorates Mitchel's bad behavior escalates while Lee attempts to make peace with her brother.
