Understanding High Blood Pressure

 Understand the causes of high blood pressure, how it harms you and what treatments are available. Discover the lifestyle changes you can make to improve your health. State of the art animation explains the complex interactions involved in high blood pressure and how treatments control the condition. Personal patient stories also counter-balance the on-stage presentation by world experts on Hypertension. This documentary (Understanding High Blood Pressure, The Full Story) was made for patients with the condition - a tool to avoid heart attacks and strokes.
