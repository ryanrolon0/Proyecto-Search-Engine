Rescuing Oedipus Rex

 When a Greek chorus bursts in Super-Therapist Bertold Krasenstein's office, Krasenstein is faced with his greatest challenge ever. To save Oedipus Rex from Oedipus' terrible fate. And so, Krasenstein travels back in time, into Sophocle's play to rescue Oedipus Rex from Oedipus' dysfunctional mother issues.
