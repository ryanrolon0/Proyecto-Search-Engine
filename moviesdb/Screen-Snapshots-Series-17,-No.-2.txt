Screen Snapshots Series 17, No. 2

 John King, Dick Powell and Binnie Barnes are seen fishing at the Rainbow Angling Club; Mary Pickford and Charles 'Buddy' Rogers disembark from ship in Hawaii; Tom Brown and Paul Kelly are seen building their new houses; Anita Louise, Marjorie Gateson, Suzanne Kaaren, Irene Hervey, Helen Ferguson and Douglass Montgomery are spotted frolicking in the swank Sunset Plaza swimming pool.
