The Young and the Wrestling 2

 Wildman Danny is reviving his wrestling achievements on television with his lover Penelope, when he receives a challenge from an Arab sheik, who arrives escorted by two harem girls as bodyguards. While the woman champion Lynn Ferrari trains a male pair of European wrestlers, Wildman also trains the harem girls in all-in wrestling. The action heat soon leads to sex. The main match is interfered with by the referee, none other than a masked Captain America.
