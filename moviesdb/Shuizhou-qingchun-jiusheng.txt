Shuizhou qingchun jiusheng

 On a winter's day on a beach, the only people left are a stubborn lifeguard, an old man who is waiting to hunt the next shark, and a kid who wishes to spot a dolphin. All waiting on the beach for their dreams to come true. But it all changes when three young girls arrive. Thinking that it is their playground, never expecting that it will be their world and school ground...
