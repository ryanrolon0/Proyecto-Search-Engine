Quando as Mulheres Querem Provas

 The womanizer Bira travels from Rio de Janeiro to Vila Velha to spend vacation in the Hostess Hotel that belongs to the family of his friend SErgio. In the hotel, Bira befriends the Italian Stefano, who is facing a problem with the relationship with his wife Marta, and Bira advises him to keep distance from Marta for a couple of days. When Bira sees the guest Verônica in the hotel lobby, SErgio explains to him that the sophisticated woman is the faithful wife of Dr.Sampaio and he would have no chance to flirt with her. However, Bira invites Stefano to go with him to Vitória in his car since he intends to meet Verônica that is shopping with her driver Jorge. When Verônica asks Jorge to blow a speck of dust in her eye, Bira believes they are kissing each other and he tells Stefano. However, the maid Penha sees the two men in the car from such angle that she believes they are kissing each other. Penha spreads the rumor in the hotel and Verônica and other local women decide to have sex with Bira to "heal" him.
