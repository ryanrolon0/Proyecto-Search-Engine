Skeletal Dreams

 Ever have a dream about someone or something that you have encountered in the waking hour, but in a different context? Ever have a dream about what the future holds for you and it becomes true? She did... Skeletal Dreams is a film about a misunderstood woman who dreams about various situations that have occurred in the waking hour, but in a different context, a very different context. These dreams also reveal specific segments of her life that become horrifying precognitions of what awaits her escape from this dream world. Drake Films in association with Davon E. Johnson Filmworks creates a wonderful adaptation from an original stage play by Kimma Drake that has you on the edge of your seat to see what this woman is going to encounter next. Skeletal Dreams introduces Alexandria St. Aubin, Alex DelVecchio and many others to twist and turn you through a dream sequence you will never forget. Skeletal Dreams, the past, future or just the truth!
