Shafted

 When femme lesbian Lindsay Spherros finds that her ex-lover has taken all of her sex toys after their break-up, she is outraged. Lindsay seeks solace in her friends, only to find that it is commonly accepted that femmes get "the shaft" in terms of sexual property. The personal and political collide as Lindsay channels her heartbreak and rage in a one-woman journey for justice. She decides to seek legal action in getting her personal property back. Fueled by righteousness and heartbreak, her quest becomes an obsession drawing Lindsay outside the law.
