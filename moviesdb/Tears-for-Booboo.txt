Tears for Booboo

 The Booboo Show is a self help public access TV show. It is the most popular TV show of its kind, giving people a way to cope with life's problems, and the human condition. The method used is crying. Everyone cries for it, except one woman, Wendy. As Wendy realizes she is missing out on this phenomenon, she goes straight to the source, searching for Booboo. Meanwhile Booboo's biggest fan, Chuck, is on a mission of his own, a deadly one that he's been waiting to accomplish for a very long time.
