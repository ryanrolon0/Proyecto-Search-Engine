Unconditional Love

 A film that focuses on the intense struggle of a young Christian man whose sister is abducted by dangerous criminals. In his struggle, this man and his friends turn to the aid of two former soldiers in a bid to find the girl. However, in the face of such pain, the young man is intent in following the ways of his faith and example of Jesus Christ creating a moral dilemma for him and for the team of people searching for the girl. During this struggle, the captors of the frightened teenager are on a mission to become the most powerful criminal organization in the world...and nothing and no-one will get in their way!
