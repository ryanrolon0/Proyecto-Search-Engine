The Road to Ironman

 The Road to Ironman is the personal tale of 7 triathletes as they make the journey towards the sports ultimate goal, the Ironman. Our tale took a sudden, serious turn, when one of the seven athletes was struck by a car while training. This intimate portrayal of a tragic accident and how it affects this small group of family and friends becomes an inspirational tear jerker as we follow one amazing man towards recovery and the six remaining athletes as they push towards their goal, now with new purpose.
