Secrets to Love

 Director Tracie Donahue sets out on a journey to find answers about what it takes to have a fabulous relationship and a lasting marriage. She interviews three couples of various backgrounds and bona fide experts on love. Also Actress Robin Givens and Author John Gray give you an exclusive look into their relationships and what worked or didn't work in their lives. There are new found, well researched secrets to love - do you want to know the secrets?
