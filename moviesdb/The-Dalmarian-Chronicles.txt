The Dalmarian Chronicles

 "The Dalmarian Chronicles" is an action packed Sci-Fi adventure set on a planet populated by living machines! Believed to be the lone survivors of a forgotten war, these intelligent machines are put to the test when their creators resurface decades later. The robots must choose between serving their former masters or protecting their own independence. Utilizing state of the art computer animation, "Chronicles" delivers epic space battles, imaginative characters, exciting plot twists and a sense of humor that make it a must see for any Sci-Fi or animation fan.
