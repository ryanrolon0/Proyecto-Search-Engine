Water Wings

 An improvised drama about a young man addicted to drugs who loses his fiancE and goes for a weekend to visit his childhood girlfriend to reboot his life. Peter just got out of drug rehab and is back living with his fiancE. He relapses and she kicks him out. Peter contacts his childhood girlfriend and she invites him to visit her for the weekend. When he arrives and sees her it's as if the proverbial rainstorm begins to clear. As they traverse the landscape of sunny San Diego and rediscover their friendship, Peter begins to heal from the wreckage of his past.
