The Making of 'Total Recall'

 This made-for-tv documentary treats science fiction and action fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Total Recall, Paul Verhoeven's film about an ordinary man who suddenly discovers he has memories of being part of an interplanetary resistance movement. Includes interviews with stars Arnold Schwarzenegger and Sharon Stone, along with the rest of the cast and crew of the film, who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it to completion.
