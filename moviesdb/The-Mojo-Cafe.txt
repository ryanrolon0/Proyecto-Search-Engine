The Mojo Cafe

 Reporter, Mike Lyttle publishes his first book on paranormals and extraterestials. At his book signing, a beautiful, mysterious woman invites Mike to what is supposed a romantic dinner. Unbeknownst to Mike, it is actually a test of his investigative abillities. Flunk this test and Mike will recieve paranormal help to insure his rise to the top of news brodcasting. Pass it and Mike will never be seen again.
