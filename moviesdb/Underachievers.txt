Underachievers

 Drew, Brandon, and Jake have been friends since high school and have always had a love for comic books. They were supposed to go to top universities and accomplish great things. They had no rival other than Michael. Now in his mid-twenties, Drew flunked out of school and has been working in a coffee shop for the past five years. However, a chance run in with Lauren, his ex-girlfriend, convinces Drew he needs to get Lauren back. Can Drew get Lauren back and top Michael?
