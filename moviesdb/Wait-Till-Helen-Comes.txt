Wait Till Helen Comes

 Twelve year old Molly is the eldest of three children in a blended family that moves from Baltimore into a house converted from a church in the Maryland countryside. The tormented ghost of a little girl comes to haunt Molly, but forms an intimate though dangerous friendship with her seven year old stepsister Heather.
