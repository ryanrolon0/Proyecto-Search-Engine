Speaking Out

 SPEAKING OUT shows the personal story of a survivor. Holly was kidnapped and brutally insulted by a Cab Driver. But she didn't let this traumatic event crush her soul. Holly speaks out about her sexual assault and how she deals with it. She helps the police to catch the criminal, and takes the case to trial. She soon learns the legal system doesn't support cases like hers. The trial ends with a hung jury. Holly finds her own way of dealing with the trauma.
