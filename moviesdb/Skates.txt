Skates

 A beautiful girl, Sati, from Iceland who is infatuated with a local Ice Skating Champion comes to India. Sati's father wants her to get married to an Indian boy but finds out that the man chosen by her father is gay. Sati meets a new guy, Vijay. Sati's uncle is a film maker and he would love to make a film on sport. So she comes up with an idea for her uncle to make a film on Ice Skating. Her Uncle likes this idea but needs to know who will act as the lead character... And so she takes Vijay from Mumbai to Iceland but discovers more than she bargained for.
