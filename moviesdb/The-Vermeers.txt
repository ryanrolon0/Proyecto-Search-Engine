The Vermeers

 The film is a visual exploration of Johannes Vermeer's (1632-1675) paintings. Constructed as a promo and motivated by the contemporary glance, this film challenges Vermeer's paintings with the pulse of the present beyond language and narrative. It offers an innovative palette of digital technology to bring the essence, mystery and beauty of Vermeer's paintings. 'The Vermeers' visions an experience of Jan Vermeer's paintings (1632-1675) through the contemporary digital elastic palette. It is an attempt to merge content of the art-film with the structure of the one of the most popular and cool visual forms of our time that is the film-trailer. The desire is to contemplate on Vermeer's paintings from a different and unexpected POV being loyal to the source but true to the technological evolution.
