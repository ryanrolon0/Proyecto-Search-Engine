Run for the Shadows

 Logan Graves is a kleptomaniac door-to-door salesman. Stealing from his potential customers is the only thing keeping him sane...well, almost sane. Enter Mila Cross. Logan falls for this eccentric beauty and his bad luck begins to change...until he finds a corpse floating in the pool of a house on his sales route. After Logan disposes of the body, many of it's severed parts begin popping up in unexpected places around him. He quickly starts to lose touch with reality and his life spirals downward into a mixture of hallucinations and dream states. Will Logan Prove to himself that he's capable of overcoming his own nightmares as well as the ones around him?
