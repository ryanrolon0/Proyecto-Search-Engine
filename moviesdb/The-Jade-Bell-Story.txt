The Jade Bell Story

 The story of one man's quest to get his life back after overdosing from a near-lethal injection of heroin and cocaine. This mute, blind and disabled man is now facing the demons of his past by traveling to schools and prisons telling his gritty and powerful story firsthand, while also working with musicians to produce an album that promotes creativity over drug use. By saving lives, Jade believes that one day he might get his own life back.
