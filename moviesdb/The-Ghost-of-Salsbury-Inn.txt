The Ghost of Salsbury Inn

 The story of a man who has heard the frightening tale of the beautiful young maiden who has tragically committed suicide because of the untimely death of her one true love. Mad with grief, she was completely naked when she died. Rumors say that her ghost appears to visitors in death as in life, young, beautiful and exposed. Our hero has stayed the night in the inn, but only to see if the rumors are true.
