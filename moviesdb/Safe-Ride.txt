Safe Ride

 Sixteen year old Jake McAlester just got his license. Ready to hit the open road, his mom Tess has a different agenda in mind. Tess, the family matriarch, knows that her teen son will quickly become an adult driver. She knows that a license is just the starting point to her son Jake's driving experience. But what's Tess' plan to making sure her son's ready for the highway of life?
