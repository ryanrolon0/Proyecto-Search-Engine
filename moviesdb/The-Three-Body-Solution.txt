The Three-Body Solution

 As with any body in motion, Prof. Delia Howell seems destined to stay in motion future as a rising star in theoretical physics is nearly guaranteed. But the upward trajectory of her career is jeopardized when a handsome young adjunct claims to have solved the 'three-body problem' and is added to the shortlist for the Newton Medal. Will Delia be able to disprove her rival's theory, or will a third 'body' lead her in an entirely unexpected direction?
