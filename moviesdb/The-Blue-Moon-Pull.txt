The Blue Moon Pull

 A story about a telephone internet technical assistant, Henry, who is stuck is a monotonous and stress-driven work environment.  He unsticks himself from his dead-end job by a single phone call from a stranger at his office desk.   This call is by a beautiful young lady from Australia.  They spark and intimate relationship through a telephone conversation.  Before Henry can get more information about his new found love, he will be interrupted by his overbearing boss.  On top of him not getting her number, Henry get fired. He must gather himself together so he can find his new found love.
