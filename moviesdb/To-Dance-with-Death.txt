To Dance with Death

 A young woman returns home from the hospital following a suicide attempt. Once there she realizes that almost everything is not exactly right and she is being followed by a mysterious woman in black. What follows is a tug of war for her soul between good and evil and a meditation on mortality. After returning home from the hospital a young woman who had attempted suicide finds that her world is almost completely alien to her and that she is being watched by a mysterious woman in black who may wish her harm. In addition her parents don't seem to remember her suicide attempt and her friends are all treating her oddly. The Dark Woman enters her dreams and shows her many eerie and frightening visions of her life and possibly her death.
