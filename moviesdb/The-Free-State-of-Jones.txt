The Free State of Jones

 In the Civil War South, a group of Confederate deserters fight to defend their freedom at any cost. When the band captures a Confederate prisoner, tensions mount and the true meaning of their cause is brought into question. Set among the piney woods of Mississippi, this is a glimpse at the untold story of a rebellion within a rebellion, of a war within a war.
