R.A.H.M.A.N.

 Malaysia's first Prime Minister, Tunku Abdul Rahman is such a respected man that his name RAHMAN has been turned into a prophecy in determining all future Prime Ministers of the country; R - Rahman, A - Abdul Razak, H - Hussein Onn, M - Mahathir Mohamed, A - Abdullah Badawi. While N has yet to come, many predict that it will be the current Deputy Prime Minister, Najib Razak. How true is this prophecy? And what is in store for the country after R.A.H.M.A.N. runs its course? This documentary is a snapshot of the lives of several Malaysians during the 2004 General Elections.
