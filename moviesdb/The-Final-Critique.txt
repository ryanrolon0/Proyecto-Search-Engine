The Final Critique

 Karolina, a high-school student, is applying for multimedia university programs. Pressured by the opposing expectations of her father and her teacher, she goes through a creative crisis. After discovering controversial modern artists such as Marcel Duchamp and Andy Warhol, Karolina produces alarming art work. She is sent for a psychological treatment. Is she insane? Has she crossed the 'common-sense' boundaries? What is the line between art and reality?
