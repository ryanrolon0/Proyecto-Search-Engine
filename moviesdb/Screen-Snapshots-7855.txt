Screen Snapshots 7855

 Ralp Staub invites George Burns over to the Columbia screen-room, Staub dubs him the Guest Master of Ceremonies, and proceeds to unreel off-camera film shots of many Hollywood luminaries of the day, including Burn's pal Jack Benny. Others seen include gossip-columnist Louella Parsons, actresses Loretta Young and Irene Dunne, cowboy star Allan Lane, and radio-television host Garry Moore.
