Running in Traffic

 Running in Traffic is a modern day drama that examines the lives of two central characters who attempt to bring the balance back into their lives after their respective loss. It is examination of the nature of coincidence and how we are all connected in this life in ways that we might never know the truth about. In the early hours of a winter morning, Joe and Kayla are moments away from an emotionally traumatic experience that will change their lives forever. As they attempt to rebuild their lives, an unsympathetic world watches over their quest of hope for a new beginning. So begins the journey that leads these two souls towards a heart rendering conclusion, of hope and of love.
