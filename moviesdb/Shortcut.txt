Shortcut

 Shortcut is a Hindi and English thriller set in London in 2010. The film aims to explore the lives of a British Asian professional, Vinny and his immigrant European Indian housemate and consequent love interest, Neelam. The film deals with the pressures faced by young professional of modern day life and it's consequent effect on relationships and self confidence. Shortcut is a Hindi/English thriller set in London in 2010. The film aims to explore the lives of a British Asian professional and ('Vinny') and his immigrant European house-mate and love interest ('Neelam'). The film deals with the pressures faced by young professionals of modern day life and it's consequent affect on relationships and self-confidence. Shortcut is extremely unique in style as it aims to cross between both Western and Eastern audiences in an native manner. The film has been Directed by Western Director, Natasha Barber, although Shortcut maintains a very strong Eastern flavour and introduces actors Armaan Kirmani (Vinny) and Kiran Kalotia (Neelam) both of whom are of both Western and Asian backgrounds. There is also a special appearance in the film by western actress Jessica Lawless (Monica).
