The Wonders of Alaska

 Out-of-this-world Alaska movie with stunning footage, educational narration and enjoyable music. Featuring Alaska's magnificence, beauty and pristine nature, The Wonders of Alaska is a pure delight for all ages. Go on an incredible journey and visit humpback whales, black bears, sea otters, birds, harbor seals, mountain goats, killer whales and salmon. Observe Alaska's magnificent waterfalls, gigantic icebergs and watch huge pieces of ice crash into the ocean. The calving glaciers in this movie are astonishing. Some even say, "Unbelievable!" This DVD is loaded with amazing Alaska footage of glaciers and animals. Visit, learn and enjoy The Wonders of Alaska.
