The Virgin and the Demon

 On the morning of her 15th Birthday Jacquline Lepre (Estefania Crespo) receives word from her father (John Zambito), a figure from her dark past and is unable to handle or confront his return. Later that evening while her mother (Julie Kendall) is at work, she leads herself into a night of drinking, self-mutilation and distrucive behavior. This is her last chance to wake herself up before her alter ego begins to fully take over. In the morning she has no recollection of what happened the night before. What or who would end all her deep pain and suffering? Her enemy is here to save her. But,despite her unconditional love for him, Jacqueline learns she is the only one who can save herself.
