Safe

 Can you let in love if you lock away your past? It tells the tale of Mia, who is 31 and eternally single. Yet Mia can never get past the second date because she is too scared to answer any questions about her past. Then Mia discovers the Secrets Safe. The Secrets Safe 'listens' to Mia's secrets and then encases them in a cement block, to be kept silent forever. Will the Safe free Mia from her secrets? Or shut her further in? A story about trust, love and letting go of your past.
