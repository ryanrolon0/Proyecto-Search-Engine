Quest for Humanity

 Quinn Becket is a science teacher who is bored with his job and thinks his life is boring. As a result, he takes his family, friends, and girlfriend for granted. Quinn has the heart of an adventurer and he spends his free time dreaming about exploring the galaxy and discovering the unknown. He will soon get more than he bargains for when he meets an android named Quest who claims that he is sent to Earth to learn about humanity. Quinn's mundane life suddenly takes a surprising turn as he is put into the action with the fate of humanity hanging in the balance.
