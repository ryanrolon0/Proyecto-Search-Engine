The Alien Paradox

 A behind-the-scenes look at dancer/performance artist Paradox Pollack's work on JJ Abrams' STAR TREK. Pollack worked closely with the creature design and special effects make-up teams to create realistic alien movements & coached actors how to incorporate them into their performances. "The Alien Paradox" is one of several alien-themed special features on the STAR TREK 3-Disc Special Edition Blu-ray.
