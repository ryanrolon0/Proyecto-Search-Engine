The Lovely Bones

 A 14-year-old girl in suburban 1970's Pennsylvania is murdered by her neighbor. She tells the story from the place between Heaven and Earth, showing the lives of the people around her and how they have changed all while attempting to get someone to find her lost body. Pedophile serial killer rapes and murders multiple children and escapes justice while the murdered children play happily in heaven and watch their families destroy themselves as they attempt to deal with the tragedy of their children's untimely deaths.
