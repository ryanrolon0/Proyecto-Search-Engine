The Lady and the Taxi Driver

 Lilly, a female cab driver and costume designer for show girls on the side, goes through her days thinking she is just that, a cabbie, until Divine Intervention breaks down, one cab after another, and places her in spots where she picks up people who really need to be heard and loved. Then her own secret fantasies and nightmares challenge her own destiny, until she meets one Lady, Rose Marie,whom, she helps to heal her, then in return, gets a pair of wings.
