Trouble at Hand

 Trouble At Hand is an educational short film for the 88-Crime Program of the Pima County (AZ) Attorney's Office, about a middle school student, Gina, struggling with the decision of how to act upon another student's threat on a teacher's life. Gina wrestles with the fear of being wrong and the danger of not reporting the threat, as she struggles with the toughest and most important decision of her life.
