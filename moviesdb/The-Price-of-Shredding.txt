The Price of Shredding

 An arrogant teenager named James who is desperate to become an amazing guitar player. After being threatened by his fellow band member to be kick out if his skills do not improve, James runs into a strange man on his way home that makes him an offer he can not refuse. The mysterious man offers to make his dream of being a great guitarist come true. After some bizarre occurrences James suspects that he may have just made a deal with the devil himself.
