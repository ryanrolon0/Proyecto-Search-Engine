Soccer Stories

 Laura is a young girl from Astoria (Queens). She lives with the dream of becoming a good soccer player, but her personality does not help, since she is too hot blood and can't control it. Plus, she still suffer the break up with her ex boyfriend, Rick, who likes to abuse psychologically on her. The real challenger for Laura comes the day she makes a bet with Rick, with very much to lose, for a soccer game three against three. Only with the help of her friends Robert and Riccardo, and specially with her new mentor, Paolo Nero, ex Liverpool soccer player, who also needs the help of Laura to fix the memories of the mistakes of the past, Laura will be able to become a real sportier and win the game.
