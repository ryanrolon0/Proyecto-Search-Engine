Tom and Huck

 A mischievous young boy, Tom Sawyer ('Jonathan Taylor Thomas' (qv)),witnesses a murder by the deadly Injun Joe. Tom becomes friends with Huckleberry Finn ('Brad Renfro' (qv)), a boy with no future and no family. Tom has to choose between honoring a friendship or honoring an oath because the town alcoholic is accused of the murder. Tom and Huck go through several adventures trying to retrieve evidence. Tom Sawyer and his best "friend" Huck go on many adventures throughout the town of Hannibal, Missouri. Finished by him winning the heart of young Becky Thatcher. Some of the adventures include witnessing a murder, getting lost in a cave, and almost getting killed. Based on the book, Tom Sawyer, by Mark Twain.
