The Hope Within

 The film sees an overriding storyline in which Michael Wallace soon becomes the new target for his employers. Having worked for a man named Fraser Morris, the relationship between the two men shatters and Morris arranges for Michael to be eliminated. However, with the help of his best friend Steven Platt, Michael escapes and begins to speak with Christians from a church in Aberdeen. During these events, a Christian woman and journalist named Sarah Moir is reporting on criminal activity in the city after her own flat had been broken into. Ironically, it was Michael Wallace who had broken into their place as part of his work for Morris. As Sarah continues to uncover the truth behind this break-in and others she realizes that there is more to these events than meets the eye. Eventually, the two story lines interlink and add a meeting with Christians one night he commits his life to Jesus Christ and is saved. During these events, Fraser Morris and his organization are hot on Michael's heels and Michael still faces the serious dangers of his past. And adding to this, Michael's psychotic cousin named Fred Rousseau has escaped from a mental institution and is on the rampage. With no agenda and no loyalties, he creates unprecedented chaos and mayhem in the city...and Michael Wallace is one man that faces this dangerous criminal also. As a result, Michael faces dangerous criminals that want to take his life, an uncontrollable psychopath who seems to have a vested interest in him, leaving him with nowhere to turn except an inner hope...The Hope Within.
