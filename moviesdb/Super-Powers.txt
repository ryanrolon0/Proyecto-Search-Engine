Super Powers

 Uncovering on the moon the Tower of Darkness, secret base of the New God Darkseid, Lord of Apokolips, and his minions Steppenwolf and Mantis, astronauts are captured and the Super Powers team are called to rescue them. Superman, Green Lantern, Flash, Red Tornado, Firestorm, and Doctor Fate must do battle with the energy vampire Mantis as the moon hurtles towards the Earth.
