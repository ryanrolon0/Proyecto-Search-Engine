The Boy Who Got Too Much

 This is a short film about a physically challenged young boy from a very middle class background. He has been living his life in depression due to his handicap condition until one day, when he discovers the truth about life & realizes his true strength. From that day onwards he understands that although he is incomplete but, still has a valuable existence in the world. This is a work of fiction, telling truth about life. It has just one character in it & is a silent film.
