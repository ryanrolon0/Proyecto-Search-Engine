The Riot

 What would you do if you were desperate to survive? How far would you go? Three years from now, the United States has found itself in another great depression. On the cusp of disaster, the desperate people abandon their humanity to form a vicious mob with no remorse and no mercy. Amongst the chaos is the story of four friends. On this day, these four will have to unite in a way they never thought possible. To survive the devastating riot outbreak in their city, Andrew, Caleb, Jerrick, and Ors will fight to protect their lives and their home. And as the riots continue, they begin to realize that surviving is only the beginning.
