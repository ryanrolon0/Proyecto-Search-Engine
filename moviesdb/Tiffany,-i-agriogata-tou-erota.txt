Tiffany, i agriogata tou erota

 Jenny is sent to the Greecian Islands by her magazine in order to find next month's cover girl. While on an island she meets Nikos and the 2 share an intimate day and night together. Nikos' girlfriend Tiffani threatens to leave him the next day unless he promises to stay monogamous. Even though Nikos loves Tiffani he enjoys sex with multiple partners and decides it would be better if Tiffani met Jenny. When they first meet they are instantly attracted to each other. From that point on Jenny will lead Nikos and Tiffani on a journey of carnal delights.
