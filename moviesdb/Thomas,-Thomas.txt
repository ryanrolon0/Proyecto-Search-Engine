Thomas, Thomas

 Wolfgang Weber, archivist of the city of Castrop-Rauxel, makes an astonishing discovery while receiving treatment at Thomas Vallomtharayil's Ayurvedic clinic. 150 years earlier, another Thomas was at work where the clinic stands today This film accompanies Wolfgang Weber in a documentary style through his research into the astounding parallels between the lives of the two Thomas', which leads him to a striking conclusion the reincarnation of Thomas Mulvany.
