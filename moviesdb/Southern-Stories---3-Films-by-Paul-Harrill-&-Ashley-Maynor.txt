Southern Stories - 3 Films by Paul Harrill & Ashley Maynor

 Southern Stories brings together three short films by filmmakers Paul Harrill and Ashley Maynor. The program includes the Sundance award-winning short film, 'Gina, An Actress, Age 29,' the ITVS co-production, 'Quick Feet, Soft Hands,' and the Southern Humanities Media Fund documentary, 'For Memories' Sake.'
