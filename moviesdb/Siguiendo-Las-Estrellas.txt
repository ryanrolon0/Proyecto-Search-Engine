Siguiendo Las Estrellas

 Tutu, an indigenous Kuna, works without any success as a fashion designer in Panama City. Her father went to die in his hometown on the Caribbean island Ukub Seni. Tutu follows him without knowing that he already died. On the island she begins to isolate herself by using IPod, Cellphone and TV. She does not understand Kuna and hates all indigenous residents and their primitive lifestyle. Just for fun she seduces IGUA, the fiancee of her cousin OLEANDA. Now the complete village is against her. As she urgently needs help from the Kuna for a huge order to design clothes, the drama starts.
