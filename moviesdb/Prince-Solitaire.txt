Prince Solitaire

 Prince Solitaire longs for a bride, but all the maidens in the land have been taken. Solitaire takes drastic measures and gets the help of a magic mirror. The mirror tells him of a Princess, locked in a tower far away. Solitaire sets out to rescue his bride, but fate is not on his side. A naked dwarf, a perverted wizard and a prostitute all fall into the mix. This isn't your average fairytale...this is Prince Solitaire. Prince Solitaire is a lonely prince looking for a bride in a land where all the princesse's are taken. After years of longing he buys himself a magic mirror to ask it for help. The mirror sends him on a thousand mile journey to find the girl of his dreams.
