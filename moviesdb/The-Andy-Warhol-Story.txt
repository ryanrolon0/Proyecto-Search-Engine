The Andy Warhol Story

 Andy Warhol, played by Rene Ricard, invites a friend (Edie Sedgwick) over to his apartment one evening to discuss his career. As they talk, the truth about how Andy uses and then throws people away comes out. The woman (Edie Sedgwick) begins to come undone and reveals to Andy how he ruined her life with drugs and false promises of fame.
