Pray for Light

 When his wife Danielle is reported missing, Jeremy Sykes ignores the warnings of local police and ventures into the deep Carolina wilderness to look for her with the help of his brother-in-law and a mysterious stranger. They quickly discover that this is no ordinary woods but a terrifying haunting ground ruled by a malicious entity and its damned minions. Even as Jeremy faces impossible and deadly odds odds, Danielle struggles to save her own immortal soul.
