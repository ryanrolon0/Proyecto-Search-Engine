Va-Room Service

 Wilbur the Wanted is a dog falsely accused of drowning Moe Hare, the Bent-Eared Rabbit. But Moe Hare is still alive; and Wilbur must evade Detective Seamus until he can prove it. While on the run, Wilbur checks into the Potted Palms Hotel. Little does he know that Moe Hare is working there as a bellboy
