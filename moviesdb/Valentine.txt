Valentine

 A series of bizarre murders have recently taken place in a suburban town, having no leads with the exception that each victim is an ex-boyfriend to one Brianna Valentine... who is currently missing. With 48-hours almost behind them, Lt. Detective Brian Lucas & Officer Jerry Newing dig further as they interrogate an Eric Bates, hoping to refine their path towards the killer.
