The Amazing Kreskin

 From the legendary Friar's Club in New York City, The Amazing Kreskin shows you how to duplicate many of the astounding effects he has spent decades perfecting for his television and stage appearances. Learn how to make a table levitate & how to mysteriously prevent a grown man from getting out of a chair. Kreskin reveals the secret of finding a chosen person in a crowd & and how to use a special pendulum as a lie detector and predictor of the future. In addition to learning seldom revealed secrets, see The Amazing Kreskin at his best as he shows why he has been called the world's greatest mentalist.
