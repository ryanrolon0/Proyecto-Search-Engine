The Life of James C. Holmes

 The Life of James C. Holmes is a documentary film about an 83-year-old visually impaired man. With only ten percent vision, James raised five children with the help of his blind wife Naomi. James and Naomi supported their family by playing music on the streets of St. Louis and Chicago, selling goods at taverns and working in factories. It is a story about blindness, struggles, family, relationships, love and music.
