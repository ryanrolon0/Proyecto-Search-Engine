Sofia for Now

 Sofia For Now is the coming-of-age story of a young Latina girl named Sofia. After a tragic car accident Sofia Peña has never been the same. Her life has been one small tragedy after the next. When she discovers that her mother may still be alive, Sofia buys a one-way ticket to Pittsburgh, PA. The 16-year-old is determined to find the last family she has left. With the help of new friends, her quest for the true meaning of family is no longer an impossible task. On her journey she finds more than she could ever imagine
