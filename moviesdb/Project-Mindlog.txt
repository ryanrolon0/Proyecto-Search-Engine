Project Mindlog

 The Student Gary Miller just lost his job again. Since he is running out of money and his rent is due, he decides to make some quick cash by signing up for an experiment in a University sleep lab. A decision he may live to regret. The experiment is starting to affect his entire life. Did the scientists lie to him about their true intentions? The boundaries between dream and reality start to blur. Gary's life is turning into a nightmare with no chance of waking up.
