Showville

 What if 15 minutes of fame are not enough? Where can you go? The town of Showville is here to answer your prayers. A world where you can be famous for just being yourself. But you better know the tour guides because they are your ticket to fame. In Showville Wendell J. Wheadon is the best tour guide ever. He is living the dream only to realize too late that another shares the same dream. When his world collapses around him, he has only one choice...to become the Tour Guide Killer. He will make Showville remember where it came from and how empty being a celebrity can be. But is it enough to change the inevitable.
