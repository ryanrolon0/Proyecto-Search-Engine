Um Varão Entre as Mulheres

 Comedy in five segments, all with the same actor, Jorge Dória. Dr. Hugo meets his new maid, a voluptuous woman, who seduces him right away. The problem is his wife, who is about to arrive at any moment. In the second, playboy Bob hires a call-girl, by phone. But a beautiful woman selling lingerie knocks at his door, instead. The third story introduces Roberto Prada, a famous TV star, in his first day at his new apartment. He is visited by a neighbor, who asks him to make a phone call, using his telephone. In the fourth, a student pays a visit to her teacher. She wants to have sex with him, in exchange for the right answers for an upcoming test. He tries to resist, but since the temptation is too enticing, he asks God for help. In the last episode, Sandra, a lonely woman, is visited by a book seller. She devises the opportunity for some love making, but the man has eaten something that made him sick.
