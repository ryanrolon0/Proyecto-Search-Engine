The Mountain Top

 The Mountain Top shows the journey of people in the civil right movement in the words through historical document. It purpose is tell the struggle from free slave to American citizen in the eyes of the people who lived it. 'The Mountain Top', which focuses on the impact of the American Civil Rights Movement through its greatest leaders. It includes interviews and speeches from Martin Luther King Jr.and Malcolm X, as well as images of the KKK and darker sides of the movement. It is a tribute to all who fought and died for American justice.
