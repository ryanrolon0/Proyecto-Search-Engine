The Kidnapping of Holly Manning

 A beautiful brunette is kidnapped the day after her graduation by a shady business associate of her uncle's. The abductor demands the evidence his colleague has amassed that could lead to his prosecution and conviction. While he waits for the material, he binds, gags and fondles his shapely captive. As the captive fights the tears welling in her eyes, she wonders if her older relative doesn't have a trick up his sleeve. In other plot line, another busty babe plots with a ruthless crook to nab her sexy sister. Little does she realize, but her "partner" plans to take her captive as well.
