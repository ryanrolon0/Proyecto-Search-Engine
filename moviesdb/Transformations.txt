Transformations

 A fateful encounter between three friends and a suburban teen leads to a revenge-fueled scheme to oust a menacing local drug dealer. But as the scheme unfolds, their plans go awry, and things grow more intense and violent with every murder the friends leave in their wake. Wrapped up in a world of sex, drugs, and violence, they must choose whether to embrace their vile surroundings, or to rise above it all.
