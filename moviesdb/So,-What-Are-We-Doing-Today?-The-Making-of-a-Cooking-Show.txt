So, What Are We Doing Today? The Making of a Cooking Show

 'So, What Are We Doing Today documentary about 'Drinking Chefs,' a short-format cooking series created by and starring Marla Graves and Megann Rogala, and filmed and edited by David Graves. The series is a cooking show based on the girls' comedic interactions as they have a few drinks while preparing both complicated and simple meals. The documentary follows them as they film episodes, talk about creating the recipes, and drink and cook! As the show is fun and lighthearted, the documentary following them takes a fun look at all the silliness and work that goes into making a weekly comedic cooking show.
