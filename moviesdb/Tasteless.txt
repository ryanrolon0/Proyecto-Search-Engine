Tasteless

 Senta brings her new husband of three months, Kyle, to the monthly dinner party among dear friends, held at the house of one of the high tech scientist. The rumors run. Surprise and curiosity ripples when they find that Senta's old boyfriend that she dumped three months ago, has brought his new significant other. Senta is surprise, but Kyle is furious when he learns that Senta was dating a...homosexual. Kyle tries to relieve his anxiety in the most dastardly way, only to find himself entangle in his own trap. Get ready for the laughs when Kyle tries to undo his deeds.
