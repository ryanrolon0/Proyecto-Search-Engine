The Rules of Golf

 Tony, aged 14, dodges the paramilitaries in Belfast to pursue his dangerous love of stealing and driving cars. While out with his mates playing golf, he observes the ancient and modern rules of the game the cost, never get into the passenger seat if you're the driver, and should an accident with an automobile or a baseball bat with nails in it get you down, you're still the hero with the balls to flirt with a pretty nurse, even though you're 4 years short of coming-of-age!
