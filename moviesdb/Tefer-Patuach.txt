Tefer Patuach

 Roi and Noa are a young Israeli couple, living together and in love. Roi wants more from this relationship and wants her to be the 'perfect' spouse. Following a Serious fight, and after an interesting class, Roi finds a 'surgical' solution to his wish. But soon he realizes that not all that glitters is gold, the grass is not always greener, and there are some things that should remain 'wishful thinking'. A romantic horror comedy.
