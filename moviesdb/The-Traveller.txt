The Traveller

 Aaron is The Traveller - an attractive, mysterious guy who arrives in a new city and into the lives of two fellow travellers. Anna and Michael, a couple looking for a break from one another, invite Aaron to join them on their exploration of London. Attracted to Aaron, Anna leads the interaction in an attempt to impress him and to find out more about this cagey addition to their day. The couple later insist that Aaron join them for a drink, a decision they may grow to regret. A possible revelation by Aaron threatens the very basis of Anna and Michael's relationship and leaves us wondering... Who is the traveller and what has he seen that they could not
