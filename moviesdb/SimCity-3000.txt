SimCity 3000

 Get ready for the power trip of a lifetime. You're in charge of building an entire city from the ground up, and you'll use your power as mayor to manage everything from budgets to bulldozers, taxes to tornadoes - all with more control than ever before. Actual landscapes and landmarks let you re-create the world's greatest cities. Save your city from destruction or savage it will all-new disasters. Trade freely with neighboring cities or swindle them mercilessly. Consult expert advisors to help with those difficult decisions.
