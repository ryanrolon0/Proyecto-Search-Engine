The Fan

 Movie actress Audra is tied to her bed by her girlfriend, Ocea. Unfortunately for the ladies, instead of Audra's boyfriend, a muscular burglar enters their house. He quickly overpowers the slender Ocea, leaving her bound and helpless and goes upstairs to gather up the ladies' goodies. Stumbling upon the equally helpless actress, the burglar confesses to be her biggest fan. Video includes a solo tale starring the cute Celeste.
