Ramírez

 Ramirez is an attractive lonesome 25 year old that leads a creepy double life. He is believed to be an artistic photographer. At night he slowly turns into a cold blooded serial killer. Although a love affair with Maria would be his only salvation. Despite being a complete perfectionist, Ramirez will one day make a mistake. Ramírez is a young man dedicated to seducing young women and who will soon find himself dragged into a spiral of money, sex, drugs and mafias. Shot digitally, Ramírez is a precise and gloomy portrait of a serial killer. Absolutely original and captivating.
