Prodigal Son

 The story of gangster's son Joe, who is brought back from the dead by mysterious corporation Prodigal Inc after an untimely death. But Joe's mother Eileen instantly recognizes that her son is no longer himself. Once a brutal enforcer, he's now a lost boy, afraid of his own shadow. While Joe's father Denny forces his son to face off against psychotic teenage assassin The Boy, Eileen plots an even more sinister solution.
