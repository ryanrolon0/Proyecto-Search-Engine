The Jason Doornick Documentary

 Amidst the tumbleweed and green hills of Kent, CT lives Jason Doornick speed metal guitarist, skateboarder, connoisseur of foods high in vinegar, thrift store bargain hunter, medieval swordsman, and Metallica enthusiast who drives a pale green car missing an unusual number of parts. The Jason Doornick Documentary captures all of these unique traits and more, giving viewers a vivid caricature of Jason and an opportunity to see why what he does makes him so entertaining.
