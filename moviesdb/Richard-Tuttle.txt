Richard Tuttle

 Richard Tuttle is an unconventional artist. Many of Tuttle's works are small pieces made of unusual materials, such as wire, or string, and many are both sculptures and paintings. Utilizing a wide variety of materials and possessing a unique sensibility, Tuttle produces works which confound art critics and museumgoers. Richard Tuttle Tuttle's work and critical response to it. The film examines Tuttle's art and its impact in several locations with collectors, and in Miami Beach, where Tuttle was chosen to create a rather large piece of public art.
