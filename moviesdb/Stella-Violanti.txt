Stella Violanti

 Stella is the daughter of a banker from Zakynthos and is in love with a poor telegraph operator. Her father wants to marry her off to a rich man, but Stella refuses to comply. Her father locks her up in the attic where she stays isolated. When she finds out that the telegraph operator only wanted her for her money and that he married the daughter of a nobleman, Stella Violanti collapses physically and psychologically and dies.
