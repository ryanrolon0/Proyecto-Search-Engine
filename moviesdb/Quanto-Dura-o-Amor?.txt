Quanto Dura o Amor?

 Marina, a young woman from Sao Paulo's country side, goes to the capital to follow her dream of becoming a great actress. An old downtown building called "Jaqueline Apartment" is her new home. In the struggle to succeed in the big city, she finds in her neighbors a new true family with whom she creates a strong bond. The aspirant actress Marina leaves the countryside and her boyfriend Caio for an audition for "Uncle Vanya" in São Paulo. She shares the rent of the apartment of Caio's friend, the lawyer Suzana, in Avenida Paulista and befriends the lonely writer Jay Machado in the playground. Marina goes to the concert of the bisexual singer Justine in a bar nearby her home. On the next day, they have one night stand and Marina has an infatuation for Justine. Meanwhile Suzana's dates her colleague Gil that falls in love with her. The needy Jay wants a solid relationship with the whore Michelle but the slut is only interested on the payment in exchange for sexual relations. A couple of days later, Suzana discloses her past to Caio and her revelation affects their romance. Marina learns how Justine is connected to Nuno. Jay is rejected by Michelle and her pimp.
