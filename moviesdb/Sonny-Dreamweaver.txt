Sonny Dreamweaver

 When Sunny's life is crumbling before him, his girl leaves him, he parties too much, and his Hollywood rap career is fizzling out. He decides to search on the internet for answers. Eventually he finds...GOD's website. He finally gets the one chance to really prove himself to someone who he thinks matters. Will Sunny get his life back together, without getting to wasted? Will he fulfill his life goals and find happiness or get stoned? Come find out!
