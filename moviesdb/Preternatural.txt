Preternatural

 Ava, a young working woman, fears that she has distanced her boyfriend by overworking. One evening, while trying to surprise him she discovers the infuriating truth. Ava cannot fathom that this is just the beginning of an increasingly unnatural circumstance. As she recalls her recently bizarre life, Ava explains how she was twisted through a dark patch where her lifestyle, perspective, and ideals were changed forever.
