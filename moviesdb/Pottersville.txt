Pottersville

 Jill Bailey has just entered the adult world. Upon graduating college, Jill struggles to find her niche when her parents shock her by deciding to charge her rent to live in her old room. Jill enters into a questionable business arrangement and ultimately loses everything she owns - only then realizing none of it was worth owning to begin with. By the end of Pottersville, Jill runs away from home, sells her soul, gets crucified, and learns the true meaning of Christmas.
