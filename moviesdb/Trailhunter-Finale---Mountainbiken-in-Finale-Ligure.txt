Trailhunter Finale - Mountainbiken in Finale Ligure

 Trailhunter Finale is a documentary of the most beautiful mountainbike trails in the surroundings of Finale Ligure. The area is well-known for great single trails with stunning views and a climate, which provides the opportunity to go biking and rock climbing all year long. The Mediterranean Sea, old towers and forts represent one of the most beautiful landscapes of Italy. The local mountainbike scene constantly makes an effort to build new trails for freeriders and bikers and the annual "24 h of Finale" race attracts riders from all over Europe.
