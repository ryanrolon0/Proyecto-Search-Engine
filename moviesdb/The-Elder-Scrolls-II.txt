The Elder Scrolls II

 The Emperor of Tamriel asks you to investigate the strange case. Lysandus, the King of Daggerfall, died on the field of battle, but his spirit did not rest, and still haunts Daggerfall city. Also he wants you to retrieve and destroy the his letter to the queen of Daggerfall. He sent it several years ago, but it never arrived. You board the ship, and incredibly ferocious storm throws you to some cavern with sealed exit and corridor leading into the depth. Find your way out and try to do as your Emperor orders.
