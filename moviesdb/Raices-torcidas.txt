Raices torcidas

 Rigo is a law student in Los Angeles, CA. He thinks he has the perfect family but his father hides a secret that has haunted him for many years. Rigo's mother is having an affair with one of the doctors at the hospital where she works. And his little sister is not the perfect high school student he thought she was. Rigo wishes to become an immigration lawyer so he can help his dad return to see his parents in Mexico. His dreams comes to an end when his father dies leaving an unfinished business. He decides to go to Mexico in search of his grandparents to let them know that their son has died. With the help of his best friend Fer, Rigo hits the road without knowing what he's really getting into. They'll meet a lot of people that are going to be key in finding their roots while other people are going to make Rigo's plans harder to achieve.
