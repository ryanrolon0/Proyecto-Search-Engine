Valathottu Thirinjal Nalamathe Veedu

 A happy go lucky dude Aji (Jishnu) is forced to take up his brother's job after his father's death. The job is that of breaking old building and selling the crap. Years ago his father had taken up a contract of demolishing Aditya Varma's (Narendra Prasad) house. Advance for the job has also been taken. If Aji completes this work he will get two lakhs, which will be helpful for him to conduct his sister's wedding. Varma's daughter Sangeeta (Bhavana), fights Aji saying that the contract was a fake one created by her cousin. What begins as fight ends as love.
