Sharzadeh Scampolo

 The hope and glory of a better period in the life of Scampi Nater, aka Shahrzadeh Scampolo ends up in a non edited storyline - a romantic story of a picnic together with the Prince of Persia by moonlight in the desert close to Teheran. But that's just one half of the narration, the specific end wasn't expected at all...
