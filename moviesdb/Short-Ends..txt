Short Ends.

 After four grueling years of study at the venerable lib-arts Keckler Institute, wannabee documentarian Daniel Litman (Sage Price, Nate and Natalie) is still struggling to define himself as an artist. When the opportunity to take part in an elite, invitation-only cinematic intensive arises, Litman doesn't hesitate to sign up with the intention of making a film from the class experience.
