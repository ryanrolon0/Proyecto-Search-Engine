The Making of Bounce

 Bounce is an innovative documentary commissioned by Hampshire Dance and The Institute of Sound & Vibration Research at Southampton University and explores how scientific concepts can be explained through dance. This groundbreaking film uses a combination of live footage and animation to document the journey of Hampshire Youth Dance Company as they create a powerful piece of dance using the concepts of sound and vibration. The film features hand-drawn illustrations by Emily Harper and Motion Graphics by Simon Byrnes.
