The Lost Art

 Filmmaker Blue Kraning explores the mystical origins of Tai Chi by taking up self study and poring over the ancient poems and texts that are all that remain of the ancient Tai Chi teachings and after seven years of daily practice in his backyard--breaks through into a full supernatural experience far beyond his wildest imaginings, emerging with a new style of Tai Chi that involves total body control from an external force of unknown origin. A documentary.
