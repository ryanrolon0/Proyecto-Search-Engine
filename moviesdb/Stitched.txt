Stitched

 Short Stitched is a brief version of a documentary called Stitched. The film follows three controversial art quilters as they prepare to compete in the nation's largest quilt show, a fast-paced event that attracts more than 50,000 devoted artists annually. Short Stitched focuses on Randall Cook, a quilter who often depicts male nudes in his artwork. That's created quite a stir in the quilting world. An article about his piece created international headlines.
