Secrets of a Hollywood Supermadam

 For 13 years, "Babydol" Gison owned and operated an Escort Empire that serviced the rich and famous. She was eventually convicted and spent 3 years in the most severe prison in California, Chowchilla; a level 4 Maximum Security prison and the only women's prison that houses Death Row inmates. Overcoming huge obstacles, "Babydol" takes us on a journey from the highest highs to near death experiences. This is her story.
