Requital

 Wilson Beckford visits home after many years away, only to find that his mother is extremely distraught from the untimely demise of his father. That night, Wilson is haunted by the ghost of his father Morgan Beckford, who beseeches him to solve the horrific issues surrounding his death. Only when dark deeds are brought to the light, and injustices are made right, can Morgan's soul finally be at rest. Wilson embarks on a detective's journey, uncovering clues based on the cryptic visions that he has received. After several discoveries, Wilson is lead into the very trap that has destroyed so many others, and he must tap into all of his resourcefulness to survive.
