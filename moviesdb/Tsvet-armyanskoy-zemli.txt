Tsvet armyanskoy zemli

 In his debut 'Mikhail Vartanov' (qv) presents the ancient and modern art of Armenia through post-impressionist painter Martiros Saryan's silent commentary of gestures. Biblical landscapes, the ruins of temples, frescos, cross-stones, contemporary sculptures of Chakmakchian, the first appearance on film of iconic modernist painter Minas and his paintings, as well as the world famous behind-the-scenes episodes of Sergei Parajanov at work on the landmark film "Sayat Nova". Banned in Soviet Union, the film placed 'Mikhail Vartanov' (qv) on KGB's blacklist (1967 - 1990s) and though some of the footage reappeared in his poetic masterpiece _Parajanov the archives - only 1 frame recently surfaced.
