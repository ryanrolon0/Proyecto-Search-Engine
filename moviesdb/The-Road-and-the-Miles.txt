The Road and the Miles

 A Man returns to his home town of Dundee, on the East Coast of Scotland after having spent several years away, working in London. On his return, he quickly realizes that a lot of his old friends have 'moved on'. Along the way he becomes entangled in a Bizarre Love Triangle!
