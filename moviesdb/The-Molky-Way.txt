The Molky Way

 Mrs. Molky is a 73 year old Iranian woman, and a widow now for 14 years. She lives alone in a humble house in the small town of Baragún, Iran. Molky wanted to come with us to Isfahan, with the excuse of visiting some relatives which she had not seen in over 20 years. Without knowing what would happen and why, I began to film her reactions, words, her looks, her desire for travel...
