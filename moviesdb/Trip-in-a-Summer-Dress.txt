Trip in a Summer Dress

 Meet a strong-willed mother and her children Matthew, the son. They figure in a story that explores age-old, classic conflicts steadfast about how she feels Sara Jean, age 15, should live her life. Over the course of five years Sara Jean struggles to find the courage to confront her mother. Oddly, as she embarks on a journey of the heart, Sara Jean finds strength through the prodding of a precocious five year old little girl, Scarlette. Emboldened by their mutual bond they proceed to face their mothers together, and Sara Jean finally dares to speak her truths. Will it reap the reward she so desperately seeks?
