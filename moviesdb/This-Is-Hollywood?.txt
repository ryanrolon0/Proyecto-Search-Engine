This Is Hollywood?

 Johan Karrento and Marc Svahnström are two Finnish film makers. After two long years they finished their first feature script. Instead of shooting it in Finland, they get caught up in a crazy dream right away! Let's shoot the film there!" They've heard the stories about Matt Damon, Ben Affleck and Sylvester Stallone. Why should Johan's and Marc's destiny be any different? This is Hollywood? Is a journey through the murky waters of Hollywood.
