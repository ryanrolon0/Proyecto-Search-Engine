Sayulita

 GEO (Greek) and GRACE (Korean) loved each other in college at a semester aboard in London. 10 years later GEO is getting a divorce, quitting his high paying job that he hates, and selling his ocean view house in Laguna Beach to find his passion. GEO and GRACE meet by chance a a coffee shop in Laguna Beach.. GRACE is getting married in 12 weeks to a rich man in Seoul. They spend the day together. The film is about longing, second chances, and taking chances.. 32 yr old man living in Laguna CA gets a divorce and decides to sell the home, quit the high paying job he hates, and find his passion. By chance he runs into a Korean woman at his favorite coffee shop in Laguna. He hasn't seen her since he fell in love with her in college in London. She is getting married in 12 weeks to a e wealthy man in Korea. Both of them are at cross roads in their lives. It all happens at the Orange Inn.
