Success Story

 Josh isn't happy to discover old friend Darren Murphy at the door of his crappy apartment at 4AM, but things only get worse when Darren reveals he's sold a screenplay for 1.2 million dollars and has come back home to brag about it. Rife with jealousy and years of unresolved tension, what follows is a dangerous war for the upper hand, as both men wrestle to claim or crush a story of success.
