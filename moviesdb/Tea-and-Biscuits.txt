Tea and Biscuits

 A teenage girl, visits her loving father's grave each year to remember and reconnect with him through a ritual. Little does the she realize that the dead aren't quite dead. They see us, they feel us, they love us and equally miss us. 'Tea and Biscuits' is a cute and emotional journey of a teenage girl into her fading childhood memories, and of a ritual that now connects her to her father ever after he is long gone. Or is he?
