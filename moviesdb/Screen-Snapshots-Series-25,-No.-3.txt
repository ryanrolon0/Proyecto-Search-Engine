Screen Snapshots Series 25, No. 3

 A Hollywood fashion show, the singing of Robert Mitchell's Boy Choir and shots from Leo Carrillo's annual rodeo are blended together in this edition of Screen Snapshots (production number 7853.)Live (for then) and archive footage featuring Columbia starlet Dusty Anderson, and Jinx Falkenburg, Shirley Ross, Jane Withers and Michael O'Shea is used.
