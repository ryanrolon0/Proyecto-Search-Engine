REveille-toi Sonia

 « Wake Up Sonia » is the story of a struggling little girl, constantly looking for the attention of her father. Sonia takes us with her on a quest to find her father's love, with the help of Missy, her favorite toy. For Sonia, everything is clear loves his wife too much and has an interesting way of showing it. We plunge into Sonia's fascinating comprehension and interpretation of the adult world surrounding her.
