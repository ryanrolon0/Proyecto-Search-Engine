The Blood Pact

 This made-for-DVD documentary details the work that went into the production of Blade II, Wesley Snipes' sequel to his hit vampire movie. Action and monster-movie fans are treated to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of this film about a half-human, half-vampire hunter, as well as to interviews with the director, cast members and crew, who give their insights into the efforts that were needed to bring this project to completion.
