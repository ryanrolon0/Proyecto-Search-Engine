The Senior Zone

 Sydney, a kid who wrapped up in her teenage lifestyle, wakes up to find that she has entered a dimension where young adults are out of fashion and seniors are the 'cool' ones. In this upside down world, Sydney discovers just how frustrating it is to be discriminated against because of her age. Sydney learns the heartbreak of living in a world of criticism and strict supervision. In the end, she realizes that seniors should not be stereotyped and that they really do have fun!
