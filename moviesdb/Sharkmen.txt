Sharkmen

 Sharkmen is a gritty hard-edged look at the world of organised crime. Merciless killing is the main ingredient for the everyday hitman; but none make it look so easy than an infamous group who has been dubbed the Sharkmen.The Sharkmen are underground group of warhorses, who formed a loyal bond among one another. Now becoming the cause of envy and fear throughout Queensland. This world takes a turn for the worst when one of the Sharkmen is murdered. The group sets off on a rampage to find those responsible and won't stop till they're satisfied.
