Resurrected

 Lucas is a man with a dark past, who gave up that life to marry the beautiful, love of his life Lily. After being married for five years and giving birth to their daughter Jessica, there picture perfect life is violently torn apart. Lucas seeks revenge for the wrongs that have been done to him and his family, at the same time tearing apart the system he once helped to set in place. Everyone, including the cops, are forced to choose a side with the odds mounting more and more against Lucas. For a man that has nothing to left to lose, does it matter how many are against him.
