The Making of 'Lost World'

 This documentary treats film fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Lost World, the sequel to the hit dinosaur movie Jurassic Park. Includes footage of the filming process, as well as interviews with director Steven Spielberg and other members of the cast and crew, who give their insights into what it was like working together on this project, and the efforts it took to bring the film to completion.
