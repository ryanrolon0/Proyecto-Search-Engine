Anthony Block is a doctoral student on the cusp of getting his PhD when he learns he must demonstrate proficiency in a foreign language. Recalling his painful past experience in Hebrew school, he opts to test in Biblical Hebrew. But as he finds himself unable to pass the exam, he sees his future slipping away, and contemplates cheating, since he will never have to deal with the ancient language again in his profession as a scholar.
