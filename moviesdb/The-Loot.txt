The Loot

 "The Loot" was a short film created for the Imagine Cup 2006 world festival, and is an exploration of the theme "Imagine a world where technology enables us to live healthier lives." In the film, two sisters are searching for hidden treasure. As they embark on their quest the older sister must constantly rely on the younger because of poor eyesight. The girls find the treasure, but the older sister finds a pair of glasses which gives her the ability to be self-sufficient and therefore become a healthier person.
