Project 2

 A man is beaten and broken in a warehouse somewhere in the city. With his strength and will for life low, he must muster of the energy to find the man who's death will save his life. This is a story of 2 men forced into a game of life or death by a secret government department designed to find and train the worlds best assassins. When Chris Turner (Adam Booth) is kidnapped by a mysterious organization he is forced to make a terrible decision packed thriller he tries to hunt down James Seguin (Jay Melnychuk), a known junkie, in order to save his own life. Follow Chris as he fights to make his own fate, all the while trying to discover the true motives of this organization that hides in the shadows.
