Screen Snapshots 1856

 The sixth entry in the Screen Smapshots series filmed in Technicolor. Shown having a good time at Hollywood's Mocambo Club are film stars Tab Hunter and Rock Hudson. Also seen are Bob Hope, Ed and Keenan Whyyn, Natalie Wood, Corrine Calvet, Pat O'Brien and many other. This is a Screen Snapshots rarity in that it has no stock or archive footage...shot live at the moment.
