Through a Dark Wood

 Adam, a young man who after granted visions of the underworld is never the same. Now to save his own soul, Adam must use these visions to help the people he knows. However, the road to salvation is wrought with many obstacles, and Adam soon finds himself in a struggle between right and wrong, all the while running from his own guilt over the death of his brother. Now he must decide for himself how he will keep his soul from ending up in the hell of his own dreams.
