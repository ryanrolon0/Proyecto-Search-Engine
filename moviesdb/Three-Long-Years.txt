Three Long Years

 After suddenly being dumped by the live-in girlfriend that he adores, Jude Donnelly hits the road to seek solace from his overwhelming pain. But instead of finding solace, Jude instead experiences three more years of excruciating suffering. After being dumped by his girlfriend Agnes, Jude Donnelly hits the road in search of solace from his pain. But instead of solace, Jude experiences three years of endless suffering. Then Jude meets Stephen, a gay nurse who's as lonely as he is.
