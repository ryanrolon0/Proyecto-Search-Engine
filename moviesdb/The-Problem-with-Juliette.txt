The Problem with Juliette

 After literally interpreting a sexual education assignment Juliette finds herself an expecting young mother. With the slow-witted father's untimely passing, coupled with no family to support her, she realizes she must face motherhood alone. It turns out her son is more cheeky and sneaky then most. Juliette devises a plan to give her son away, only her mischievous son has plans of his own.
