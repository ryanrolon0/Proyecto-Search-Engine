The River Wolves

 In a drama of treachery, intrigue and love beside the dark waters of the Thames, two men come together in the sinister shadows of Dockland. The paths of the international criminal and a fighting sea-captain, who rules his ship with an iron-hand, had crossed before, but this time there was to be a final reckoning in which only one man would emerge victorious in a winner-take-all battle.
