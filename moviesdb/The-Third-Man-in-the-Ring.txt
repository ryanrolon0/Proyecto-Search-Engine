The Third Man in the Ring

 The story inspired by the life of a celebrated professional prize fight referee, a member of the boxing hall of fame, a bookmaker, a mob associate,a bon-vivant, and a memorable New York character. This story also encompasses a love-hate relationship between the Third Man in the Ring and his wife. Our hero is betrayed by mob treachery when he refuses to influence the outcome of a fight. This heart-rending tale will provide insight into the world of boxing in the 30's, 40's, and 50's, bootlegging, Prohibition, the Underworld's Overlord of Boxing, mob treachery, mommy-dearest relationships, and child abuse. This is a New York story that must be told.
