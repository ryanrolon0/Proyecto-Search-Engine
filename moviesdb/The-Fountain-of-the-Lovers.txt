The Fountain of the Lovers

 The story of Alice, a young American, and her first trip to Paris. She grew up with her favorite book, Camille in Paris, which talks about this mysterious and romantic place called the Fountain of the Lovers. One day when she is ready, she decides to go on a quest to find it. When she gets to Paris, Alice is like Alice in Wonderland, in a world so different from hers. Through this film made entirely of 35mm film stills pictures, we follow Alice, on her quest, as she discovers the eternal city of Paris, his people, and its beauty.
