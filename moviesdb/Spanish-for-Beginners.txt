Spanish for Beginners

 Children explore the world of the body and play. Accompanied once again by their young friends from Spanish for Beginners familiar puppet pals, children will learn the parts of the body, clothing, numbers, and more! Kids won't be able to resist dancing and singing along to this Spanish DVD's toe-tapping, hand-clapping, maraca-slapping new songs, including the favorite Spanish folksong, Los Ninos Cuando Bailan, new twists on favorite children's songs Cabeza, Hombros and Ojos, Orejas and the irresistible toe-tapper Los Deditos. This Spanish DVD continues to build children's Spanish vocabulary, through skits, dance, Spanish songs, and play. Children learn the Spanish words for the parts of the body, clothing, and numbers. The program continues to reinforce Spanish words learned in Whistlefritz's Spanish for Beginners Translation Guide provides an easy reference for parents and teachers who are first-time learners of Spanish.
