Walking on My Fingertips

 The music industry is in need of a change. Major record labels are merging or collapsing and the new wave of digital technology has made it possible for the average person to create a professional sounding product. Filmmaker Scott Fine traveled to different corners of the world in search of musicians who will start the beginning of a new musical era. Follow Scott as he gets the true insight on how artists feel about the current state of the music industry, and how major labels can cope with the change.
