Respect the Producer

 It's all about the hustle. This film will paint an intimate portrait of famed hip-hop producer Dame Grease. Fully submersed in the day-to-day of a successful producer, viewers will have the opportunity to observe the hustle one takes to empower themselves to a higher platform. By featuring interviews with some of Grease's most prominent collaborators, we will learn first hand; struggle is just a small part of the game.
