Quadrum

 Quadrum is an enthralling story that explores the boundaries of moral character in a psychological journey about Wes, a suffering insomniac struggling to accept his fate. His untimely demise has left a restless soul holding on to a collection of past memories, searching for the truth. Will Wes find eternal peace or will his soul wander forever searching for the answer? Blending surreality and past visions, the film is spun in the manner of a poetic painted landscape that touches themes of life, trust, death the afterlife and the power of dreams.
