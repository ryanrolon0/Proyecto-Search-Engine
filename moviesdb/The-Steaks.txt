The Steaks

 Four randomly selected students from a Montreal University are chosen for SCRU 690's campus radio contest, 'Steakout'. The contestant who can live inside a 1996 Jeep Grand Cherokee the longest, keeps the car. However, the station gets more than they bargained for when the contestants begin to find solace in their new sanctuary, and the contest becomes a cult hit on the campus Four Montreal college students are plucked from their everyday lives to live inside a Jeep Grand Cherokee for a campus radio competition. Savoring both sanctuary and a newfound fame, the contestants undergo a variety of transforming experiences, as the weeks slowly become months...
