The Trespassers

 Five attractive young Angelinos in search of the perfect view, stumble across something horrible when they trespass in a beautiful Hollywood Hills home. It's a question of survival as they grapple with fear, lust and demons on the prowl and inside of themselves? The Trespassers takes you on a shocking thrill ride with some light moments and some steamy interaction. You'll think twice before you ever trespass again.
