The Centre Line

 The Center Line follows the story of a mother and her two kids who decide to stop at a hotel one night to break up their long journey. What follows are experiences that forces the mother to distinguish what is illusion and what is reality. The motel takes on a life of its own as it haunts the mother with her internal conflict and personal demons. Part human drama, part suspense thriller, The Center Line will leave the audience thinking about how our past affects us all in different ways and how the fight inside can be the hardest to win.
