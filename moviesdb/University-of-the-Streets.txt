University of the Streets

 It's an ordinary afternoon in the lower east side of Manhattan for Martial Arts Grand Master Ro and his retired friend Leon until a woman's purse is stolen and the culprit is revealed to be a former karate student of Ro's. Returning the purse becomes a difficult task when the woman cannot be located, leading the unlikely trio into a bizarre series of events that forces them to work together in this action-packed grind-house comedy.
