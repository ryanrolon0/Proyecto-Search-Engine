The Last Smoke

 Tough financial times has Omar fantasizing about his life with different women. However, reality seems to creep into his fantasies turning them into nightmares. As he smokes away each fantasy gone awry, he reaches for his last cigarette and decides whether to continue living in a fantasy world or face reality. 'The Last Smoke,' is a look into the psyche of male fantasies from the perspective of an Arab-American man in financial distress.
