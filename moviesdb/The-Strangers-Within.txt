The Strangers Within

 In the summer of 2009, a group of filmmakers traveled through Romania performing clown theater to various Gypsy communities largest minority and historically Europe's most excluded ethnic group. Using this medium as a means to reach the reality behind a culture which has evoked such strong stereotypes throughout Europe, the film discovered many different personal stories and experiences, and above all a strong sense of a fiercely proud culture whose highest virtue is dignity.
