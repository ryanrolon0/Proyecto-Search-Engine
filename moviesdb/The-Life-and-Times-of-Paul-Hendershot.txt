The Life and Times of Paul Hendershot

 Paul Hendershot was at the top of his game. He had it all, a successful talk show, a beautiful, loving wife and a nice house. However, following the premiere of another talk show, Paul's life begins to change. His ratings drop, his wife leaves him and he becomes an alcoholic. Desperate to turn his life around, Paul decides to pull one last ditch stunt to make a name for himself, televised suicide.
