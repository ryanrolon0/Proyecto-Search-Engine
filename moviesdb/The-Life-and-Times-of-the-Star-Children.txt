The Life and Times of the Star Children

 After the passing of his beloved grandmother and the break up from his adored girlfriend; Joseph McConnell goes to Europe in search of answers to his many misfortunes. With the only friend he had left, his camera, Joseph documents his life in this psychological study of himself and humanity at large. In his climb out of hell, Joseph realizes that life is not what it seems. Finding truth around each corner in his recent awakening, Joseph urges, displays and explains that all men and women around the world can rise above and fulfill their dreams.
