To See If I'm Smiling

 The story of women who served in the occupied territories. Tamar Yarom studied film at the London Film School and psychology at the Hebrew University. Her diploma films were screened at festivals around the world. Her documentary credits include "Laundry" and "Witches." "Sob Sister," Tamar Yarom's drama debut, won the Best Drama Award at the 2002 Haifa Festival. Selected filmography
