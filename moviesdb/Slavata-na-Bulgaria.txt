Slavata na Bulgaria

 After many years being away, The Sheriff - the main character - return to his birthplace, a small mountainous village where only a dozen of old people live. The Sheriff owns an old 'retro' car left by his grandfather. By it he delivers groceries from the nearby city and uses it as hearse to drive the deceased to the cemetery. The main character has a dream - to take part in an old cars race. In order to make the dream come true he sets off for the Big City.
