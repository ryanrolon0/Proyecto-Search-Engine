Tribute to Nick Mancuso

 This short film explores the prolific career of Canadian-Italian and Genie award winning actor Nick Mancuso. With over 120 films to his name, this biographical film creatively reveals the emerging career of Nick Mancuso and takes us through to his present time and success. He he has starred with many actors from Charleton Heston, Kim Basinger and Tommy Lee Jones to name a few. He is also a recipient of the Lifetime Achievement Award from the 2005 Toronto Italian Film Festival.
