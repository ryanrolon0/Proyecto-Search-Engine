Tre på en motorcykel

 The farmerboy, Niels Anders, is stopped on his motorbike by a desperate criminal couple, who just escaped prison. They want to steal his bike, buy they don't know how to drive it. Instead they force Niels Anders to bring them along. As Niels Anders gets a crush on the woman, he makes up a plan to get rid of her violent man, cause he knows that you shouldn't let other people get the kicks for you.
