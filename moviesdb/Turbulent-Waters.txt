Turbulent Waters

 Despite abundant natural water resources, the hydro power production in India seems to be jeopardized by Narmada Bachao Aandolan (NBA), the Save Narmada Movement lead by Ms Medha Patkar, who is well known to National and International Media for her numerous hunger strikes, self drowning attempts in holy Narmada river,Police arrests & releases! This film tries to find the answers to various questions related to this Anti Dam Movement by talking to various people right from NBA Leaders,Politicians,Administrators, Dam Promoters and NGO's to a Common Man and affected Villagers with Michelle Garforth as a Narrator and comes out with many shocks & surprises.
