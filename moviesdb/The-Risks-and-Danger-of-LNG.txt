The Risks and Danger of LNG

 The energy industry is targeting coastal communities throughout the world for building large, vulnerable and dangerous Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) facilities. This documentary film informs the public about the imminent perils of LNG by demonstrating its vulnerability to accidental disaster and terrorism. The film serves as a dramatic counterpoint to the energy industry's promotional safety-spin by vividly exposing their safety myths, and revealing the actual risks and danger of LNG.
