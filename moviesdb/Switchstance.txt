Switchstance

 SWITCHSTANCE is a short comedy about an over-the-hill cop Frank Turnbull who goes undercover as a teen skateboarder to bust a gang of international smugglers. As Franks slowly makes friends with the skater kids he once thought were dangerous punks, he begins to appreciate what it's like being on the other side of police prejudice, closing the divide between the badge and the board. Produced as part of the Fuel TV Film Experiment.
