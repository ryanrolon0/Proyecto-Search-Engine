Untitled Knott's Ghost Town Project

 A family adventure based on Knott's Berry Farm's Calico Ghost Town and all of the rides and original characters created for the theme park. The story is set in the late 1800's and centers on the gold mining town of Calico which has been overrun by ghost and evil supernatural creatures. Knott's Berry Farm is the second largest theme park in Southern California, visited during the last ten years by an estimated 30 million people from around the world. The park is owned and operated by Cedar Fair Entertainment Company. In addition to Knott's Berry Farm, Cedar Fair owns and operates ten other amusement parks, six water parks, one indoor water park resort, and five hotels.
