To the Wire

 Reggie, an average office goon, gets frustrated at his blue screen computer and blames the dirty power surging through his office building. With vengeance on his mind, Reggie electrocutes himself in an attempt to mess with the power in the boiler room. Now, months after Reggie is buried, Thomas the innovated genius builds the ultimate time machine. Thomas' companion's Tom and Jon help him go on a wild adventure through time in an attempt to go back to stop Reggie before dying. The only problem is, the time machine has a mind of it's own and decides to run amok!
