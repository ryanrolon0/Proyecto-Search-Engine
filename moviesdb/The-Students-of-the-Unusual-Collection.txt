The Students of the Unusual Collection

 The Students of the Unusual Collection features many short films based on the stories from the award-winning comic book horror adventure series. This includes Under the Bridge directed by Terry Cronin, Utter Chaos directed by Kendall Bruns and Josh Flowers, Recalcitrant Jones and the Deadbeats directed by John Taddeo, Milkmaids directed by Pat Martin, The Strangler directed by Katharine Leis, Tattoo Love Magic directed by Ricardo Porven, & Phase directed by Anthony Cothron.
