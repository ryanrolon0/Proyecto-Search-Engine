U Street

 Known as the "Black Broadway" of musical greats like Duke Ellington, U Street at its historical heyday is revealed through the eyes of James, a floundering college freshman who travels back in time to complete a history assignment. On an unforgettable journey, James witnesses the epitome of U Street's high society...his ears are tickled by the sound of "sweet jazz" and his eyes are captivated by the sight of elegant sophistication. A new James awakens.
