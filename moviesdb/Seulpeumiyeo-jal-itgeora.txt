Seulpeumiyeo jal itgeora

 A man and his family struggle with poverty. His wife, unable to bear such a life, deserts her husband and their three children. The man next loses his house as he cannot afford the rent. While living homeless in the streets, his youngest daughter dies. Vowing to end his children's suffering, the man kills his oldest daughter and intends to kill himself and his son. His son, however, refuses to cooperate.
