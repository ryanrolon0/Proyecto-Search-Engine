VirtuaGirl

 'VirtuaGirl' is a high tech romantic comedy about a geeky computer salesman named Adam Debutts who finds himself hopelessly unlucky in love until the day he encounters VirtuaGirl, a girlfriend simulation program that runs on a fully interactive, amazingly mobile PC! VirtuaGirl teaches Adam the ways of love, building his confidence and drive. However, the relationship quickly takes an ill-fated turn when Adam falls for a real girl. Cast away in a world that is not PC compatible, VirtuaGirl is left to contemplate life, love, and revenge!
