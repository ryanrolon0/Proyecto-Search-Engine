Runner

 A mysterious cowboy passing through a quiet Texas town, runs into a local sheriff who's none too happy to see him. When the sheriff confronts the stranger, things take an unexpected turn for the worse. This modern western thriller will keep you guessing until the exciting finish and nothing is what it seems. This modern western thriller, set in a small town just east of El Paso, and just North of the border, will keep you guessing until the exciting finish. A mysterious cowboy stirs things up when he rides into town in his 1977 Road Runner and duel hand cannons. The local Sheriff is quick to take an interest. Why is he here and what does he want? The Sheriff is determined to find out, but he soon discovers that things are not what they seem.
