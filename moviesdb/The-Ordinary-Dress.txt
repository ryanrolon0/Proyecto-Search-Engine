The Ordinary Dress

 A woman finds a wedding dress in thrift store and realizes that it's no Ordinary Dress. She brings home the promise of romance and love everlasting. That evening, when HE comes over to watch hockey and enjoy a little of her home cooking, the woman realizes that she may have lost sight of other important details in finding her 'happily ever after'. The Ordinary Dress is a short comedy about a resourceful woman who salvages what she can of her dreams and creates her own happy ending.
