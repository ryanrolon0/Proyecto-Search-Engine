The Cincinnati Kid Plays According to Hoyle

 For the movie _The Cincinnati Kid (1965)_ (qv) which centers around the high stakes game of stud poker, magician 'Jay Ose' (qv) is hired to act as technical advisor, specifically teaching the actors how to handle a deck of cards for close up scenes. Actress 'Joan Blondell' (qv), who plays Lady Fingers in the movie, that character one of the dealers in the climactic high stakes poker game, is given instruction on how to deal the cards properly for her scenes. Blondell practices what Ose has taught her between takes. Just for fun, Ose also shows her some slight of hand card tricks. This promotional short for _The Cincinnati Kid (1965)_ (qv) showcases the card handling skills of gambler and magician 'Jay Ose' (qv). He instructs 'Joan Blondell' (qv) on the techniques used by professional poker dealers. Joan will use what she learns in the film's climactic poker game.
