The Rookie Fireman

 Another of the "Variety Views" continuing adventures with Shorty the Chimp geeting loose and running amok. Tiredr of waiting at the fire house for a blaze, Shorty sets one in the building himself. At another fire, Shorty makes a jungle-style rescue by climbing hand-over-hand on wires leadinging into a building. All narrated by Tom Shirley, and this one was Universal production number 1353.
