The Dog

 Owen, a telemarketer, steals some money from his company's account when he's informed his services are no longer required and flees the city. He gets into a car accident on the outskirts of a small town called Bewdley and is rescued by some of the locals. Owen is then forced into the middle of a twisted town feud that he barely gets away from with his life. He returns to his wrecked car to retrieve the stolen money but...
