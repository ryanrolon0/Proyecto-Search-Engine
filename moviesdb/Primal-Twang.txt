Primal Twang

 PRIMAL TWANG through the guitar's colorful and surprisingly controversial 3500-year history. Starring flat-picking pioneer, Dan Crary, Primal Twang features an all-star cast of guitar legends and dramatizes the wildly eclectic forms of guitar music throughout the centuries, revealing its sociological impact in every era. Some of the best guitarists in the world deliver electrifying performances as the 'instrument of the gods' emerges from antiquity, gathers steam over centuries, and explodes in our time to become the universal musical instrument.
