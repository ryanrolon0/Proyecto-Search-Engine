Ships Wrecked Cove

 Paul and Max two long time friends living on their own for the first time are planning an apartment show for their band SHIPS WRECKED COVE! Paul has things under control until the morning of when Max wakes up with mixed feelings about music and their landlord Mr. Burke confronts them with a late rent notice. Lost with how to come up with the money the guys decide to sell everything in their apartment, which sends them on a journey through town pulling Paul and Max further apart.
