The Visitor

 Old comic Sonny Sherman is hospitalized, feeling forgotten and neglected. He was hoping to listen to Milton Burl on 'Vaudeville's Greatest' but his radio is malfunctioning. Showbiz is in his blood he's still got what it takes but he fears the parades gone by. He is hoping for a miracle cure .He's hoping for another chance in the spotlight. He's hoping for a visitor.
