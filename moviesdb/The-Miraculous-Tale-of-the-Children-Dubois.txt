The Miraculous Tale of the Children Dubois

 When a great devastation befell the Dubois house, young Juliette was forced into a life with out magic, it is not until twin brother Nikolai returns from the great beyond to shepherd her back to the innocence of her childhood that Juliette must decide between the reality of her present or the magic of her youth. Alana Morshead, Morgan Krantz, Rebecca ZOE Leigh and Jeff Fahey. Director Cassandra Lee Hamilton's journey of two magical twins separated by one twin's death, but re-united by their desire to escape the normal world of adults and return to their enchanted childhood.
