The Love of Lazarus Lazare

 The Love of Lazarus Lazare reinvents one of the oldest stories known to man. In the waning moments of immortality, Lazarus recounts the events of his strange life to his last love, Anne Wheeler, as he awaits death in a solitary hospital room. Before a solar eclipse ends, he must make a final decision-one that will dramatically alter the lives of Anne and a mysterious visitor.
