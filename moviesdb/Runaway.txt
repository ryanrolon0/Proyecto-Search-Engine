Runaway

 Akbar decides to use his ill-gotten money and status within the community to buy a seat of power in the local government. During the process of his campaign, negative attention is drawn in to his activities and a smear campaign is started against him. Using his apparent legitimacy as a gold trader and his gangster capabilities he quietly silences all opposition while promoting his positive image. This brings him to the attention of Babu, a strange character that has made it his life's mission to take corrupted and evil men back to the faces of their past. Babu takes Akbar using all of his skills and begins a long confrontation to force Akbar to confront the misdeeds of his past and to face his family and the future. Akbar cleverly leaves a trail behind and his most deadly henchman sets out to save his boss from Babu and seek out vengeance.
