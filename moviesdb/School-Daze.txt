School Daze

 In the South of the United States are taking place confrontations between two groups of students who have different ideas and are not able to accept the one of the oponent. A contemporary comedic look at black college life. Dap (Fishburne) and Big Brother Almighty (Esposito) conflict over the greek frat system while the Wanna-be's and Jigaboo's conflict over self-identity and self-esteem.
