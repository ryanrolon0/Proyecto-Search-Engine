Sherrill Milnes at Juilliard

 Sherrill Milnes at Juilliard view inside a classroom at Juilliard, one of the world's most revered music schools. Instructor Sherrill Milnes allows documentary cameras to visit his opera master class for two full days. The result is more than 12 talented students who perform songs and rehearse as Milnes offers impressive critique, feedback, and demonstrations of phrasing and technique. ~ Betsy Boyd, All Movie Guide
