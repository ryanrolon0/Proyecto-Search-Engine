Take Me Somewhere Nice

 After establishing himself as a photographer, Jared decides he wants to see a son he has never known. An 8 year old named Jamie. When they first meet, the years of seperation made their relationship very distant. But when Jamie takes a liking to his dad's camera, a new bonding begins. Take me somewhere nice is a story about a father, a son and how love is a simple word in a complex world.
