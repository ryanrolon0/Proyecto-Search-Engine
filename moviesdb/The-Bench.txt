The Bench

 Amongst the hustle & bustle of a capitalistic world, Charlie finds solace only during his morning waits for the bus. That is, until one day when his peace of mind is taken from him and replaced by numerous nuisances. Charlie turns inward to realize that without his bench he is nothing. Will he ever see it again? Who could be behind it? Is it still a bus stop? This dark comedy is sure to answer these and take you to a place when insanity comes in the form of a bench.
