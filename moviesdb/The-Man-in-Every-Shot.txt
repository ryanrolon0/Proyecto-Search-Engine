The Man in Every Shot

 Ex-Formula 1 rookie of the year and Motor Racing Champion Tiago Monteiro is an Official SEAT Sport driver in the World Touring Car Championship (WTCC), an Official ORECA driver at the 24 hours of LeMans and 1000km of Algarve, the owner and team principal of GP2 Ocean Racing team, the owner of two brand new hip restaurants in his native Porto, the owner of a clothing and events company, not to mention a husband and father of two. This is the amazing story of the world's busiest athlete.
