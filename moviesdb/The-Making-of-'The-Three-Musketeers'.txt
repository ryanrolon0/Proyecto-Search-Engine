The Making of 'The Three Musketeers'

 This made-for-tv documentary gives classic movie fans a behind-the-scenes look at the making of The Three Musketeers, the 1973 film adaptation of Alexandre Dumas' classic novel of adventure in 18th century France. Also included are interviews with the cast and crew, who share some of their experiences from working on the film and discuss the special efforts that went into bringing the project to completion.
