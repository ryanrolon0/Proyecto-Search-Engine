Shredderman Rules

 Based on the kids' book series written by 'Wendelin Van Draanen' (qv) comes "Shredderman Rules." Nolan Byrd ('Devon Werkheiser' (qv)) is a scrawny and shy 9th grader who, like all the other kids, is bullied by Bubba Bixby ('Andrew Caldwell (II)' (qv)). When their teacher, Mr. Green ('Tim Meadows' (qv)), allows the students to do a computer project, Nolan, who has a particular knack with technology, decides to catch Bubba in action. Several other books have followed.
