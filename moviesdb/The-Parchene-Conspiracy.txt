The Parchene Conspiracy

 Aliens known as the Parchene, who originally came from earth, want to regain control of the planet for their leader known as the Fertile. They must discredit the Krellus who have the Core Charter of ownership for the planet. Also they must prevent another species known as the Tzimm in their conquest attempt. A human news reporter uncovers a human suspect associated with the Parchene after the murder of 11 human negotiators.
