Scene and Heard

 "Scene & Heard" chronicles the dynamic musical history of the college community of Isla Vista, CA as recounted through rare archival footage and interviews with past residents from throughout its five tumultuous and vibrant decades. Tracing Isla Vista's unusual origins, controversial development, and always contentious relationship with the neighboring University of California, Santa Barbara, this documentary sheds light on the well-known but often misunderstood community as filtered through the evolution of its unique musical culture. "Scene & Heard"'s amalgamation of lively music, rare imagery, and intimate interviews provides both a time capsule of Isla Vista's past and insight into its future.
