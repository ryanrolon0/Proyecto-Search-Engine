Up the Dirt Road

 The world and the global economy is currently in the grip of an international recession. Nowhere has it hada more terrible out come than in the very beating heart of the capitalist engine, the United States. As millions of people in America are losing their jobs and their homes ordinary people are having to turn to extreme measures to make money. Writer and Reporter Lincoln Hudson finds the economic down turn to be especially hurting the good citizens of 'Sin-City' Las Vegas. While investigating the inevitable growth of the adult entertainment industry (Prostitution) he comes across a normal US citizen who after losing his job decides to sell his 'ANAL-VIRGINITY' to the highest bidder. Not one to pass up a good story Lincoln Hudson manages to interview the gentlemen concerned, his girlfriend and of course his parents. The film looks at the business of 'sex for money' and asks the question 'In times of financial hardship how far would you go?'
