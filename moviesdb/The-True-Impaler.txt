The True Impaler

 Some say his quest to become President is a million to one shot. But, Jonathon The Impaler Sharkey is about the show America and the world, he is one in a million! A man with the heart of a warrior, and a deep caring for those feeling helpless who are in need of a true leader! Like Rocky Balboa, Jonathon is ready to defy the odds. Not only in his quest to become the next leader of America, but, contrary to what his doctors say, make his return to the ring and become a world champion. Get ready, for the true to life Rocky Balboa!
