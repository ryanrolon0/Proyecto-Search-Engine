The Masterpiece

 Hidden away from the press's prying eyes at a luxurious countryside artist's retreat, a legendary painter (Hollinger, The Witches of Oz) anxiously awaits the arrival of one "approved-of" Journalist (Wool, Law & Order SVU) coming to review his latest work. As he works furiously on his opus, the Artist begins to suspect that his Manager (Golden, In Praise of Shadows), a failed writer, is undoing him out of jealousy. When the Journalist arrives, everything spirals out of control- leaving the Artist's creation and his sanity hanging in the balance...
