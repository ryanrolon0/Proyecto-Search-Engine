Teardrop

 One afternoon Dan is brutally jumped and accosted by a gang in his neighborhood. The leader of his crew demands that he murder his punisher and thus officially earn his 'membership' into the crew and a teardrop tattoo under his right eye. Dan stands at the most important crossroads in his life, will he kill to be a member or risk walking alone? We see the film through Dans eyes and know what he experiences but do we know Dan?
