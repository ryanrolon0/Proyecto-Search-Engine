Searching for the Big One

 An edgy piece about Mike and Julie, two complete strangers who come together with one self-gratifying goal, an affair. While trying to please their own ideas of lust they find themselves thrust into their insecurities and reveal the uncomfortable, hard truths that only strangers could ever admit to. While trying to find self comfort in order to move forward with their plans. Their pasts seems more complicated and convoluted, twisted and jaded that not even these two strangers would be able to forgive and fall into bed.
