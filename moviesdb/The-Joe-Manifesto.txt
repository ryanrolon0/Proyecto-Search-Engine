The Joe Manifesto

 Approaching his mid-thirties and reluctantly engaged, bored office worker Joe meets the outlandish job and partner swapping Veronica, who inspires Joe to be 100 % honest in his life. Joe breaks up with his fiancE, demands a job demotion, and turns his orderly life into chaos. When he realizes he has taken it too far, Joe has to figure out a way to put his life back into order again.
