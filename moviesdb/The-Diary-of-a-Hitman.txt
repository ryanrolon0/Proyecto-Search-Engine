The Diary of a Hitman

 It is a comedy/crime/thriller film based around a garrulous Cockney stoner, "Dimes", who deals with foreigners and their issues with the living. "Dimes" is not your average hitman but instead is a marijuana smoking, benevolent, tenacious individual, who creates a whole new persona for the stereotypical hired gun.
