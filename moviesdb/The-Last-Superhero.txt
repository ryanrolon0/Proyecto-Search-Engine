The Last Superhero

 Julia lives in solitude in a small and muddy village. She is nine years old and she is just discovering she doesn't like playing with her dolls anymore. These are not always easy to control. Her world is disrupted when she meets an old man on the playground. When Julia discovers he is "Tornado Man", her favorite Superhero, she decides she is going to befriend him no matter what. She wants to make him strong again so that he can continue saving the city. However,Tornado Man is now old and weak and he wants a quite life. Julia will have to decide how much she is willing to give up in order to have a friend and companion in her life.
