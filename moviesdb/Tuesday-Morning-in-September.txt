Tuesday Morning in September

 A filming of September 11th, 2001 shot from across the Hudson River in Jersey City. With over two hours of continuous real-time footage, 'Tuesday Morning in September' may very well be the most inclusive account of 9/11. It is all taken from one camera, from one vantage point. 'Tuesday Morning in September' has the effect of grounding individuals in something real. There is commentary, but the account itself is objective. The viewer can have their own experience to form their own opinions. This account of 9/11 is authentic and has never been released to the public or any production company. It has been stowed away for nearly a decade.
