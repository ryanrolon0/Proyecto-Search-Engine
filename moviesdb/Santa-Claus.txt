Santa Claus

 This made-for-tv documentary treats comedy fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Santa Claus elf who leaves Santa's workshop to start up his own toy factory. Features interviews with star Dudley Moore (in character as the elf Patch) along with other members of the cast and crew of the film, who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it all together.
