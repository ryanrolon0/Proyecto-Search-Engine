Stingy Jack

 Dr. David Wells thought he made the perfect move from bustling New York City, to peaceful Emerson Falls. Gradually, as the family settles into their new home, the town's dark secrets are revealed that point to a horrible tragedy 28 years ago, that left a young boy dead, and the townspeople fearing for their lives. The family soon discovers there's more to the town than meets the eye. The age-old legend of Jack Hawthorne is real. And Dr. Wells must find a way to protect his family from what is known as Stingy Jack.
