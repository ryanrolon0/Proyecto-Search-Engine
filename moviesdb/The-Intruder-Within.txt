The Intruder Within

 Personnel on an oil rig near Antarctica discover a bizarre fossil that exerts a mesmeric influence over some of them and gradually regenerates into the horrible embodiment of an evil that has laid dormant since the beginning of time. An egg is accidently release from time when oil drillers bring it up getting oil from Antartica aboard a drilling station. After drillers are attacked after the egg has hatched, the egg now a full creature is bent on murder and destruction. Three drillers risk their lives to stop it.
