Commissioned by the BBC and produced as a series of webisodes, Us 5 has been developed for integration into an interactive web space for Mencap. The episodes are based around the lives and dilemmas of five characters. Each addresses issues of independence, self-determination and (self-directed) support. Stories were developed during creative workshops with the actors who play the central characters.
