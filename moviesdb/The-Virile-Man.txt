The Virile Man

 Gary, a man struggling with his sexuality, decides to call a psychic hot-line. Gary tells he is happily married to this wife for seven years already and has two kids. He is also happy with his wonderful house and car. But something has been going on in his life, a slip-up, multiple slip-ups. He had sex with the guy he plays racquetball with, but nothing else happened. He wants the psychic too know that he is absolutely not gay and is seeking confirmation. Gary is mostly concerned about his relationship with his son. When playing ball with him, his son said he threw like a girl. Can the psychic really see through Gary and convince him he is not gay?
