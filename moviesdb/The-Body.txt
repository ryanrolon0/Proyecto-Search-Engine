The Body

 In the near future, the body of retired Garda detective John O'Doherty is mysteriously found dead. His old partner Alan Cross arrives on the scene and takes the scene and takes on the case. In a strange turn of events, Alan is shocked to discover that John is still alive and well, all the while his carbon copy lies dead in the morgue. How can this be possible? Is it a clone or his twin? The pair find themselves working side by side once again to solve the mystery. As the delve deeper into the case they discover the answers that surround the body.
