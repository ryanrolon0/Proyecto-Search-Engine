Rude Awakening

 Four strangers are forced into a coalition for survival when they awaken in the wilderness. The four wake up scared the four realize the extent of their predicament when one of them drops unconscious without warning. It quickly becomes apparent that they are being watched, and soon they are given directives that their lives depend on. The three left standing are frantic and increasingly apprehensive as a clock counts down to the deadline for each obligation. In a fit of anxiety, one of them realizes the horrible truth of their plight.
