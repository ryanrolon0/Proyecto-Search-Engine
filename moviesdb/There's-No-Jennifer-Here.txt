There's No Jennifer Here

 A short film in the vein of the cult classic "When a Stranger Calls", "There's No Jennifer Here" poses the question you are not living it to the fullest?" Made as part of the 48 Hour Film Project, "Jennifer" was written, directed and edited in less than 48 hours. It is a Mystery/Thriller that will keep you on the edge of your seat, and keep you thinking about its themes long after it's over.
