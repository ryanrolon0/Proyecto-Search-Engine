Unparalleled

 A simple hard working man named John becomes troubled and suicidal due to his wife's adulteress affair with his best friend Andre. Through his emotional turmoil he is pushed through parallel universes in search of the perfect version of Sarah. At first he is confused by what is happening but soon he tries to take control of his life. Unfortunately he cannot change destiny and every time he finds Sarah she is with his friend Andre. This drives John over the edge and hungry for revenge.
