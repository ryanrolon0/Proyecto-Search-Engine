Roam

 Roam is the story of Luis (16) and Tito (9), two Hispanic homeless brothers trying to stay together as they struggle to survive in the streets of Los Angeles. Luis and Tito have been on the streets since their mother - a heroine addict - abandoned them two years ago. Luis has taken care of his younger brother since then, sacrificing his own childhood to protect him. However, he has started to feel fed up with this and dreams on a new life without ties. The day our story takes place, Luis will be given the opportunity to make his dream come true. But will he be able to leave Tito and go away?
