The Life Frequency Project

 The protagonist - Petrol is an intruder to the world based on the video-games' reality. Being stuck there he meets Zef - one of the guardians of that place, who is ready to help him. In the following three chapters Zef and Petrol hit the road, crossing green plains and frozen lands in a search of the mysterious artifact which will help Petrol to come back to his home world.
