Short Sight

 The film is about Creo, an openly gay man in his early 20s with a strange ability. Ever since losing the sight in his left eye due to a homophobic attack in his teens, Creo has discovered he is able to see manifestations of a person's true, repressed self through his blinded eye. Following a day in his life, "Short Sight" delves into the secrets people hold and they ways they lie to themselves.
