The Unknown

 We follow the life of Boston actor, LOU MONTE, as he struggles to make it through the crazy, amusing world of the entertainment industry. LOU MONTE, played by Christopher Tranchina, a 30 something year old, Boston actor drives to NYC on a weekly basis trying to "make it." We follow his humorous journey into the gritty, true to life, reality of the continuing fight to believe in yourself against all odds.
