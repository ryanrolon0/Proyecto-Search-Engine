The Prisoner

 A documentary containing highlights from the 35th anniversary celebration of Patrick McGoohan's cult TV classic 'The Prisoner'. Recorded live at a London cinema in 2002, the event featured guest appearances by leading British character actor Kenneth Griffith, stuntman Frank Maher and film librarian and director Tony Sloman who all reminisced in depth about their time working on the show. The documentary also features additional interviews with the guests which took place backstage at the event.
