Sooni Unsooni

 An Indian couple in Las Vegas entangled in web of love and lies. The story portrays the life of an unhappily married couple of how they seek love outside their marriage. Unlike American culture where if the relationship does not work out, divorce is an easy solution, in Indian culture 'divorce' is a social stigma and you are looked down upon which forces the couple stay with each other even if they are unhappy.
