Snitch

 Jessica is a young lady who wants to become a world renowned journalist. Will, a young man, also has dreams of making it big, dreams he'll conquer by any means necessary. When Jessica is assigned to cover a break-in at a local church, she gets a bigger story than she bargained for when she finds outs her on and off again boyfriend Will may be involved. 'SNITCH' is a story about the choices we make, whether it be out of greed, anger or love.
