Saga of Sonora

 TV special which starred Vince Edwards as a hero who must stop gold-greedy villians, Jill St. John and Zero Mostel, and prove his love to his girl Leslie Ann Warren. This was a TV special filled with music, comedy, and a western story about love, greed, and gold. Many famous faces at the time appeared in this show including Roy Rogers, Dale Evans, Frankie Avalon, Cass Elliot, Carl Ballantine, George Gobel, Sam Jaffe and George Kirby.
