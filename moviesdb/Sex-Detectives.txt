Sex Detectives

 P.I Charlie Martin is hired by wealthy smut peddler Jack Bowen, who is preparing to divorce his wife, Maranda. Jack recently got caught with his pants down with a woman in Hawaii sporting a tattoo of a scorpion on her ass. And now there is a picture floating around of Jack, and the tattooed behind. Martin's job, find the source of the picture, and get the negative, before the incident becomes a problem in the divorce trial. It's a routine assignment for Martin until he meets the enemy - Miranda, and falls for her. Now he must find the tattooed woman and get to the bottom of the blackmail, hoping all along that Miranda isn't involved.
