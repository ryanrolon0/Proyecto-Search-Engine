Talk It Out with Jodi Leib

 Talk It Out with Jodi Leib is a documentary television series now available on DVD. In this episode, Jodi takes audiences on tour with the Black Eyed Peas as they travel America singing their eclectic style of Hip-Hop. Experience live concert footage, artist interviews, and the music video for Where Is The Love. Enjoy inspiration and enlightenment through music and conversation. Let's Talk It Out!
