The Birthday Gift

 "The Birthday Gift" is a psychological drama, told in a non-linear structure, about Hilary Parker, a former runaway obsessed with her childhood doll, who returns home for her father's birthday with a mysterious gift that will help her remember the past she's been repressing. 'The Birthday Gift' is a psychological drama that infuses the past and present to tell the story of Hilary Parker, a former runaway with an unhealthy obsession toward her childhood doll. Hilary is repressing a tragic childhood memory, which Dr. Schwartz, her psychiatrist, is trying to help her recover. Although Hilary hasn't seen her family in years, Dr. Schwartz suggests she return home for her father's birthday, in order to discover why she can't remember her past. Unknown to Hilary, the birthday gift she's brought for her father is the key to unlocking the secret she's determined not to remember.
