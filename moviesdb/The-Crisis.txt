The Crisis

 "The Crisis" is about a marketing agency in crisis as they hash out their plan of attack against the small island nation of Cuba in the last thirteen hours before the Cuban trade embargo set to be lifted. A crisis of soul, sex, wit, and and emotional frustration ensues. Welcome to a world where the curtain finally fell on Cuba. A woman must wrestle with her personal demons, even as she is confronted with a larger problem. What to do when the U.S. trade embargo against Cuba is suddenly lifted and her firm is tasked with the job of being the first wave in a cultural assault on the small island nation. First they have to plan the attack, then they have to execute, all the while a freelance reporter from the Times, or is it The Herald is out to get the scoop, whether he is sleeping with one of the instigators or not. A crisis of soul, sex, and emotional turmoil ensues. Welcome to our crisis.
