Second Guest

 On a cold winters night, a park lies dark and still, whilst in the furthest, darkest corner a mass of scrub and undergrowth lies black, silent and twisted. Elsewhere, the quiet of a spacious flat is disturbed as Pete enters, beginning preparations for the night ahead. Lights dimmed and table set for three, a knock at the door marks the arrival of Suzy, the pair making conversation as they await Pete's fiancEe, Kim. But as the delays mount, the origins of the absent guest become ever more unclear...
