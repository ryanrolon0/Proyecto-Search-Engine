Sari Kizla kopuk Ahmet

 Ahmet and Filiz have been saving money for their marriage. Filiz's family objects to this, since Ahmet is poor and they want her to get married to the rich Haci Bey. As Haci molests her, Ahmet and his friends save her. A so-called Ahmet inherits a fortune and his heirs are after him. Getting the smell of money, Filiz's family wants now Ahmet as their groom. They inform Suzan about this. Ahmet and Filiz move to the mansion. Suzan and her husband want the money, she starts flirting with Ahmet, thus Filiz leaves the house and has a car accident where she loses her eyesight. The real Ahmet appears. Ahmet robs the mansion to get money for Filiz's surgery, he Ahmet submits the money and disappears. Filiz regains her eyesight, the police is after Ahmet, Filiz knows that has stolen the money for her. She talks to the real Ahmet and asks him to pardon the other Ahmet. The man gives a testimony in favor of Ahmet. Ahmet is released, happy days await the two lovers.
