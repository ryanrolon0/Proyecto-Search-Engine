Pre-Phoria

 G4tv will give out the best-of-the-best Glow Awards on a show they will call G-Phoria. Pre-Phoria is about the nominees. Host Victor Lucas (IV) discusses the nominated games in each catagory with gaming industry insiders in a roundtable style. The insiders are the Game Deleloper Conference Seamus Blackley Capital Entertainment Group Cliff Bleszinski level designer with Epic Games Geoff Keighley
