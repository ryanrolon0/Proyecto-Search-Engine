The One Last Time

 Downtown Los Angeles - the last customers are finishing their early evening business at the bank. At 6 timer test on the vault. The bank heist is planned to perfection, everything is ready and every eventuality if accounted for, what could go wrong? Wearing Wizard of Oz masks for disguise the heist is on, the one condition for success, they must be out by 18 to plan, until at 18 enter; they have been given the same inside tip.
