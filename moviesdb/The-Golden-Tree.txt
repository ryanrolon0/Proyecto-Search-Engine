The Golden Tree

 Eric and his family are struggling financially and may be evicted from their house if they do not come up with $300 fast to pay the rent. Eric finds a tree in the forest that bears $100 coins. He only takes what he needs to help his family and friends. He is accused of stealing by his teacher, but his grandfather knows the truth. They all live happily ever after. A timely story written and directed by 9 year old Emma Kenney.
