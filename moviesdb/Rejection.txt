Rejection

 Pablo plays guitar at a bar in downtown Santa Fe and skateboards there and back every day. He is bound by the ankle bracelet that won't let him go anywhere else. He meets a waitress at the bar, Isabel, who is a sweet girl and likes him. She sets it up so she can take him to Taos and away from Santa Fe for a day. Everything in his life seems simple and pleasant when Jenny calls him on the phone and Isabel convinces him to give her a ride. When they drop her off Pablo tells Isabel that Jenny is crazy. Steve, a crazy person that he used to know, shows up at his front door and insists that they go together to look for the person who has stolen his girl, Jenny. Jenny is also looking for Pablo, while at the same time trying to avoid Steve. Isabel sees Jenny with Pablo and leaves to be alone, but runs into Steve.
