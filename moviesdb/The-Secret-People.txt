The Secret People

 It's fast cars in an even faster world as- An innocent young boy, bright-eyed and impressionable, experiences life in a whole new way as the baby sitter from Hell takes him down a path of violence and tragedy that the young boy will never forget. A desperate young man, on a mission for revenge, intends to seek closure on something unsolved, only to get more than he bargained for. These are just some of the varied occurrences in - The Secret People.
