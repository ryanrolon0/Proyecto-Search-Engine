The Gritter's

 Two best friends, surviving an almost fatal car crash, stumble upon the only house along a desolate dirt road. In search of medical attention, they only find THE GRITTERS, a family with their own rules, and their own terrifying games! But before the couple can get the medical attention they need, they must first stick around and help the odd family entertain a few guests...themselves! In this riveting horror, The Gritters welcome you to join them in their demented world of mayhem and frightening family tradition, and encourage you to try and play along.
