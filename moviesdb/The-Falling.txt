The Falling

 Amidst his broken life, Police Officer Grayson Reed is about to be thrown into the middle of a feud between the five angels of Heaven and the leader of Hell. As faith ends in the world around him, Grayson will be faced with an ultimate choice that could decide the fate of our world. For police officer Grayson Reed, life has never been so hollow. His closest friend has fallen victim to addiction. His only sister no longer calls him brother. All he has left is the memory of a world lost by the ravages of chaos. But as faith ends in the world, the gates of Hell beneath him become unlocked, And the dark forces rise to claim their victory upon the earth. Now, amidst his sorrow, Grayson will be thrown into an ongoing feud between the armies of Heaven and the armies of Hell. While faced with a choice that could ultimately decide the future of our world, Grayson will try to pick up the pieces to his broken life.
