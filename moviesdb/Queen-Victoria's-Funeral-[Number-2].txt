Queen Victoria's Funeral [Number 2]

 This scene, taken from Earl Gray's Gardens, London, with the Marble Arch in background, shows the funeral processing passing Lord Brassey's house, in the following order Household Officers, Gun carriage bearing coffin, the Royal Standard, His Majesty, King Edward VII, His Royal Highness, the Duke of Connaught, His Imperial Majesty, Emperor William of Germany, Monarchs and Foreign Princes, carriages containing Ladies of the Royal Family.
