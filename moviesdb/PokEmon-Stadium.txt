PokEmon Stadium

 You've battled and trained PokEmon on the Game Boy. Now it's time to clash against your opponents in 3D on the N64. The game has several different modes, including the Stadium, the Gym Leader Castle, a park with 9 different minigames akin to the game "Mario Party", Prof. Oak's Laboratory, the Game Boy Tower, and more. There is also a commentator option for battles.
