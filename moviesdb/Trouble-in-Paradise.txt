Trouble in Paradise

 A suburban couple struggles to rekindle the flame they once had in the bedroom. Isaac Brewster wants to try something bold and daring. Whereas Susan Brewster wants nothing more then nice afternoon lunch. Upon returning from a nice afternoon lunch, the couple's world is turned upside down when a charismatic hit man  tells them they must choose who dies. The two are forced to make a life or death decision in a reality they are unsure of.
