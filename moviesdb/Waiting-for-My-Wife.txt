Waiting for My Wife

 This film follows a man named Liem. Liem was born and raised in South Vietnam, however, for the past 25 years he has been living in Hawai'i. Liem has been struggling with an unspecified illness for over 20 years, yet he still cares for people and his family. Liem's life long goal is to have a family of his own. In his attempt to find happiness, his patience is tested as he experienced loneliness and longing. This film of hope will make viewers thankful for everyday life and inspire them to think selflessly.
