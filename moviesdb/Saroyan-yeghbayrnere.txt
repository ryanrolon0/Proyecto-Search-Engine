Saroyan yeghbayrnere

 1920. Last days of the first Armenian Republic. Armenian Army fights on two fronts Bolshevik mutiny in the northeast. Hayk Saroyan returns to his native provincial town from Russia to assume a minor post at Dashnak Army command center. His brother Gevorg, captain at the same command center, is a real patriot prefering death to "eternal exile", army power to relegious mercifullness. Very soon a suspicion creeps inside him is Bolshevik spy.
