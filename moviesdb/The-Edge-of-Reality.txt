The Edge of Reality

 Life as an Elvis Tribute Artist isn't as glamorous as Derek King thought it would be. Middle aged and estranged from his son and ex-wife, Derek seems past a prime he doesn't ever remember reaching. After narrowly escaping the wrath of an elderly Chinese Prostitute demanding her fees, Derek arranges to meet with his son only to have his plans thrown into chaos when he is offered a dream gig by his manager, Barry Duvall, a has been celebrity with a hardened demeanor. Confused and uncertain, Derek seeks guidance in the lyrics of his Elvis song book; will he turn his back on the son or the show? And will the resulting decision be the end of his days as the king or will he rise, bigger, better and more hip shaking than ever before!
