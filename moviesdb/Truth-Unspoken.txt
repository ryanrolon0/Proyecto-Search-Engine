Truth Unspoken

 Truth Unspoken is a suspense drama about a couples journey through life, lies, love and ultimately a life changing decision. Jay and Tracy seem like the perfect couple and although they love each other, love does not always tell the truth.When Jay finds out his Tracy has been less then truthful with him, he confronts her just to find out more then he could ever expect but Tracy is not the only one with a secret Jay has one of his own.
