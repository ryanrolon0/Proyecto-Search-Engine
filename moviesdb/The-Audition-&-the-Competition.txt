The Audition & the Competition

 Andrea is concerned that Eve might get a movie role she covets, so, at the audition, she grabs the buxom red-head and leaves her bound and gagged in the dressing room. Eve manages to wriggle free of her rope bindings and when Andrea returns she places the brunette beauty in rigorous bondage. When the producer discovers what his potential stars have been doing away from the set, he introduces his own unique method of auditioning. In the second story, the two feisty ladies challenge each other to a contest of who-can-take-it-the-longest.
