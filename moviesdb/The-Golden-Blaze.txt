The Golden Blaze

 This incredible feature-length animated movie follows the troubles of two school kids. Fierce rivals, the two regularly butt heads over all manner of issues, ranging from the trivial to the important. The film enters the fantasy realm as an accident occurs and the two boy's fathers suddenly possess some lethal superpowers, allowing them to enter into battle at the behest of their offspring. A bizarre, funny, and exciting movie, GOLDEN BLAZE is an innovative treat for all fans of fast-paced animated action films.
