The Day My Parents Became Cool

 The Day My Parents Became Cool is a modern costume-comedy - and every teenager's worst nightmare. When the one-thousand-year comet crosses the night sky, a brave new world is created where adults instantly adopt every teen trend in attire and body image. Emo, Goth, Skater, Jock, Metal - tattoos, piercings, thongs, and sagging jeans. On every adult, everywhere, planet-wide. The Day My Parents Became Cool tells the comic story of how one small group of teenagers fight back when every outward manifestation of their individuality is co-opted - by the enemy.
