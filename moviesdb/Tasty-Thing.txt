Tasty Thing

 Guava sets out to raise the money she owes to an abusive boyfriend, by selling cookies out of a wagon around a dead-end town. When one potential buyer gets too handsy, Guava is rescued by a mute Clown. Guava and the Clown build a friendship. When Guava's boyfriend shows up, the Clown takes it upon himself to get Guava out of trouble.
