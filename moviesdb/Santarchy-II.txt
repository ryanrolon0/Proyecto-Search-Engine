Santarchy II

 In the festive and pickled spirit of Santarchy 2004, the Muckmouth crew is back in 2005 to once again fill the hearts of Auckland's civilians with a merry dose of Christmas chaos. The result makes major headlines all around the world. Oh how Santa would be proud. Merry Christmas!
