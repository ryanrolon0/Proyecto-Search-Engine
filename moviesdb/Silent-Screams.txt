Silent Screams

 Silent Screams depicts the tale of a disturbed man, William Fulton, who is plagued by nightmares and guilt about the recent rape and murder of his ten year old daughter, Sarah. Frustrated with the authorities' lack of progress, William is obsessed with catching the killer himself and exacting his revenge for his daughter's death. Eluding the authorities, he embarks on a dark journey where he eventually pulls Dr. Ryan, a young psychiatrist treating William for his nightmares, into a web of deception and lies...but Dr. Ryan is determined to find out the truth and help William end his self torturing journey, and we soon discover that all is not what it seems.
