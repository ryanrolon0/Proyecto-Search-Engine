Sons of Cuba

 The Havana Boxing Academy is a Cuban boarding school that takes 9-year-old boys, and turns them into the best boxers in the world. SONS OF CUBA follows the stories of three young hopefuls through 8 dramatic months of training and schooling as they prepare for the biggest event of their lives so far season, crisis strikes Olympic boxing champions defect to the USA, leaving Cuba at a crossroads, and the boys contemplating a changing world
