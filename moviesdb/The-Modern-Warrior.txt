The Modern Warrior

 We see them mostly in brutal competitions - fighting for championships, for honor, for glory. But who are the people who we have come to know as the world's top fighters? Who are these modern gladiators and what is life like outside of the ring? This is the story, philosophy, and secrets of today's Modern Warriors. In this never-before-seen exclusive footage, contemporary masters of Martial Arts reveal their strategies for life, struggle, and triumph.
