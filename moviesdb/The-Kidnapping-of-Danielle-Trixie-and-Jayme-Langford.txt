The Kidnapping of Danielle Trixie and Jayme Langford

 A naive woman allows a burglar into her house and soon she's a helplessly bound and gagged while the crook rummages around looking for goodies. The victim's buxom roommate then enters, but finds herself in a similar predicament. The burglar is taken by the ladies' beauty, so he strips them naked, then allows first one, then the other to torment their roommate. Finally, it's back to business; the women are hogtied and the thief makes his escape. In the second story line, an aspiring operative flunks her field exam when the man she is supposed to be following gets the drop on her, then leaves her bound, gagged and naked.
