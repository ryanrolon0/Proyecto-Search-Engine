Seeking the 36

 The quixotic quest of teenager Nico Lanson to find the Thirty-Six Just Men (Lamed Vov) of Jewish folklore, here in the world today. After talking with subject matter experts and interviewing potential candidates to be Lamed Vov-niks, Nico meets with Rabbi Zalman Schacter-Shalomi, founder of the Jewish renewal movement. It's a powerful and compelling story of one girl's search to find answers to questions about the troubling state of the world.
