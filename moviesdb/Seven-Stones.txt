Seven Stones

 Eight year old Gemma is searching for a wizard to replace her absent father. Reuben is living a lonely existence between psychiatric care and the streets. He is desperate to escape the bad voice in his head by listening to the good one. A chance meeting causes their fantasies to collide. Seven Stones is a film about the power of imagination. It deals with the fine line between what we perceive as creativity and what we perceive as madness.
