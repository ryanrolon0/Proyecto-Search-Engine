The Broken Quiet

 Edgar, in a drunken rage, drove away his wife, Angela, and five year old daughter, Ashlee. Now, twelve years later, Angela passes away and 17 year old Ashlee must live with her estranged father for six months. Edgar sees it as a chance to reconcile. Ashlee wants nothing to do with him. Complications arise when Ashlee realizes she's pregnant, and decides to abort the child.
