Thompson's Last Run

 A hardened con ('Robert Mitchum' (qv)) being transferred from a federal penitentiary to a Texas institution to finish a life sentence as a habitual criminal is freed at gunpoint by his niece ('Kathleen York' (qv)). The cop ('Wilford Brimley' (qv)), who was transferring him and has been the con's adversary for over 30 years, vows to catch the twosome.
