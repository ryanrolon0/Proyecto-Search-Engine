Toutes ces belles promesses

 After the death of her mother Marianne, a young cellist discovers that her father had a mistress. In order to find her, she casts aside her boyfriend Étienne. The mistress is a pianist named BEatrice. BEatrice and Marianne strike up a friendship while reminiscing about the past. They remember 'Édith Piaf' (qv)'s song her nanny Ghislaine who was a funny companion when she was young.
