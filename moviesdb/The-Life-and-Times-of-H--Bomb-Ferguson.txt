The Life and Times of H- Bomb Ferguson

 A one of a kind look at one of the original founders of rock and blues H-Bomb Ferguson. The film features rare insights, into H-Bomb's early years, his struggles growing up in the racially segregated south, his gold record "Good Loving" with Savoy Records, His friendships with other world famous artist in the 1950's onward, never before seen live performances, and H-Bombs return to the spotlight in the 1990's. H-Bomb Ferguson is currently hailed as the last performing of the original U.S. blues shouters.
