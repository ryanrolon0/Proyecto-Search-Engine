Ulises y la puerta de los titanes

 A couple of thieves, Ulises and Dafne, visits her hometown, where people believe that Ulises is in fact an heroic man. In the meantime, Dafne's former lover, Erick, has become a powerful and dangerous man, who kidnap Dafne. Now, Ulises must face his most dangerous adventure ever, if he want to save Dafne...
