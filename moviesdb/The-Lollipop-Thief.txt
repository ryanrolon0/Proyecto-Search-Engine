The Lollipop Thief

 Celeste and Victoire are 4 year-old little detectives, who love to solve mysteries and chase villains on their spare time. Today, their mission will be to stop and catch...The Lollipop Thief! that odd, funny-looking guy, who dresses like a tree, and dares to terrify the toddlers' neighborhood. His trademark? sneaking into birthday parties to steal all the goodies! THIS HAS TO STOP! and Victoire and Celeste have a plan...
