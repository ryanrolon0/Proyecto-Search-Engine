The Ego of Earl Wattstone

 A mockumentary comedy about an egotistical filmmaker and his motley film crew and actors, working to put together a radical film idea. Earl, is an indie filmmaker without any apprehensions in producing his idea that is deemed outlandish by most. His hilarious journey to produce his film is met with comedic results at every turn. From persistent 1st A.D., to Adult Film Star turned Actress, to a Self Proclaimed Actor who can't act, Earl must complete and realize his film vision before he loses his mind!
