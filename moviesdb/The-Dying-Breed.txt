The Dying Breed

 After Outlaw Brothers Frank & Bertrum Devlin Rob The Cumberland Stagecoach, A Bounty Hunter Soon Tracks Them Down. During A Shoot Out In Sweet Water Canyon, The Bounty Hunter Kills Bertrum Devlin & Apprehends His Brother Frank. As The Bounty Hunter Makes His Way Back To Town To Claim The Reward, Various Obstacles Get In His Way, Including Frank Devlin & A Fur Trapper With A Stubborn Mule In Tow.
