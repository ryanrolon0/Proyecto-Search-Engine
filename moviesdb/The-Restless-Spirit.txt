The Restless Spirit

 The Dreamer is unhappy with his marriage and runs away.He collapses and is found by The Desert Flower,who vince him to return to his family.In various illusions he sees himself in three stories. In first is Napoleon.In the second he is a Knight.And in the third a Sultan.But in all the illusions he die.Meanwhile his wife is about to be send into the desert while refusing to remarry a Stranger.Before this could happen The Dreamer arrives and send the Stranger into the desert.He becomes again a loved and respected member of the Town.
