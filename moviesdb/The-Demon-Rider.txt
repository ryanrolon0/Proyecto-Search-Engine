The Demon Rider

 A ranch foreman captures a notorious gang of gold thieves. He ties them up and leaves them for a pursuing posse while he goes out to find the gold they stole. When the posse arrives, the gang's leader convinces them that the foreman is actually the gold thief, and the posse sets out in pursuit of him. Jimmy has recovered the money stolen by Black Hawk and his gang. But when the Sheriff captures the gang, the real Black Hawk identifies Jimmy as Black Hawk. This sends the Sheriff after Jimmy and to make matters worse, Jimmy has accidentally lost the money.
