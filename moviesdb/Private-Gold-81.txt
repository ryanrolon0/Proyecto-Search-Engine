 In a galaxy far, far away, war has struck and darkness is pervading. The good guys, the Jodi warriors, have been destroyed. The Darkness is spreading. The remaining Jodi leaders assemble the next generation of Jodi warriors, a team of lean and muscular female-fighters and train them to master the sexual powers of the Force. So begins the Porn War. The Jodis learn to become invisible to their enemy through physical movement and mind-play. When confronted, their full-bodied attack is a power to be reckoned with; rendering any force of Darkness and his sword desperately useless.
