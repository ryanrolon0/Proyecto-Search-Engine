Ruby & Lilly Kick the Habit

 In this, the fourth installment of Ruby & Lilly's Big Apple Adventures, Ruby dreams up a creative way to help Lilly kick the habit. Ruby attempts to help Lilly get healthy. The always tough Lilly refuses leaving Ruby to dream up a creative way to kick Lilly's habit. Shot in New York City with music provided by New York City's singer/songwriter bobby blue as well as by the artist Moby, follow the girls as they kick the habit.
