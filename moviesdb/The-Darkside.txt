The Darkside

 In a small mountain village, six innocent women were accused of witchcraft by the Holy Inquisition and burned alive. It runs the year 1589. While the women were being burned at the stake, the demon Pazuzu, a faithful servant of Satan, offered them the possibility of reincarnation to revenge that injustice.
