The Devil's Revenge

 The Devil has a reputation known throughout the world. But when a man's lofty reputation makes its way down to hell, Satan's fragile confidence is shaken. Satan heads up to earth to find out just what is so special about this man, Dale Davish. Outsmarted by Davish at every turn, Satan eventually finds himself back in hell, locked in a heated hot sauce cook-off with the the multi-talented Davish. Will Satan be outsmarted one final time, or will the ever-clever Dale Davish falter, allowing Satan to finally take his revenge? The Devil has a reputation known throughout the world.
