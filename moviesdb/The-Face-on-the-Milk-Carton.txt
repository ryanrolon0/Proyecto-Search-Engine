The Face on the Milk Carton

 A girl happens to look down at a milk carton one day and she sees herself on the back! Her life gets more stressful as she tries to hide the secret from her parents who she knows couldn't have kidnapped her. When a teenage girls named Janie Johnson sees herself on a milk carton under the heading 'Missing Child,' her life changes drastically. She tries to keep her suspicions from her parents/kidnappers partially for her safety and partially for theirs. When she falls in love with her neighbor, Reeve, she tells him everything. Together they unravel all of the secrets surrounding Janie Johnson's life.
