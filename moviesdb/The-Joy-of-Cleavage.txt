The Joy of Cleavage

 The Joy of Cleavage takes viewers on an uplifting and informative journey to the most eye-catching destination on earth breasts. Cleavage makes movie & pop stars, enriches surgeons, tempts politicians, and dominates fashion. This a celebratory look at cleavage in western culture including contributions from fashion historians, advertising creatives, fashion designers and celebrities. The journey will take us from the court of Charles II to today's celebrity glamour.
