The Good Soldier

 Sean Roberts wants to fight the injustices in his country. When civil war breaks out he is transferred to an intelligence unit. As his just war becomes a brutal struggle, the pressure to break prisoners becomes intense Sean must decide how far he will go to defend everything he loves in a war without rules. Sean Roberts is a farm hand working fields he doesn't own, in a country divided by the hatred of past conflicts. He joins a terrorist organisation to fight the injustices he sees in his country. When civil war breaks out he is soon transferred to an intelligence unit, where rendition prisoners are questioned in secret. As Sean's just war slides into a brutal, drawn out battle, the pressure to break suspects becomes more intense. Sean has to decide how far he will go to defend everything he loves in a war without rules.
