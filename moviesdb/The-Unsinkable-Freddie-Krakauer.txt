The Unsinkable Freddie Krakauer

 Freddie, a car salesman in his 30s, is watching life go by and doesn't even know it. He's still in the same town, still living in the fictional glory of his high school years. Molly, the girl Freddie has always regretted letting go, moves back into town. She's newly single and with a child. Can Freddie convince her he's changed?
