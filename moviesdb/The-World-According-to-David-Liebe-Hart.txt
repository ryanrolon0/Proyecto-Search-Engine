The World According to David Liebe Hart

 David Liebe Hart is a self-described actor, artist, puppeteer, singer, and songwriter who has been trying to make it big in Hollywood for over thirty years. While on his quest for success in Los Angeles, David provides his insights on race, religion, the economy, extraterrestrials, the entertainment industry, and other topics. David is joined by his two friends James Quall. 'The World According to David Liebe Hart' is a unique documentary about an extremely fascinating and unique individual.
