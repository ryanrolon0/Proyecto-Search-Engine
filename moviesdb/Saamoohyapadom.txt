Saamoohyapadom

 Dileep and Premkumar are traveling on the same bus and start up a friendly conversation. Premkumar says that he is going back to his childhood village for the first time since he ran away as a child. Also that his family hasn't seen him since , that he only communicates to them through letters ,and that he hasn't sent them no photos so that it will be a suspense when he comes. Premkumar tells Dileep that he has a good job abroad now and is rich and is bringing a suitcase full of money back to his wealthy family. On a bus rest stop Dileep pushes Premkumar off a cliff , take all of his belongings and tries going in his place. But Dileep soon finds out things are not as he expected.
