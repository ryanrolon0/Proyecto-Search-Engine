The Mr. Men Movie

 An sinister villain by the name of Mr. Doom is planning on his most greediest plot that would put our lives at stake, but he could be having one final laugh. Only the Mr. Men and Little Misses who live in the small town of Dilydale can overthrow Mr. Doom's chances and save their planet from disaster. The Mr. Men and Little Misses are enjoying a quiet time in the small but comforting city of Dilydale. But their joy was startled when a mad scientist called Mr. Doom hit the news. He is working on his most evilest plan in his lifetime, taking over the world! Now everyone in Dilydale is about to embark on their most thrilling, suspenseful, and exciting adventure in their lives, preventing worldwide chaos! Will Earth be saved, or is it game over for our friends?
