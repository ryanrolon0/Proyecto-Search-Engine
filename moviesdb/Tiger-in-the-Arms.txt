Tiger in the Arms

 Tiger In The Arms is a comedic look at one man's attempt to win his Mother's approval. His brother is graduating from the seminary. Adam Cuthbert reluctantly agrees to drive his parents to the ceremony. But, on a journey with more strippers than saints, he learns that even the road to heaven can be a road trip from hell. Adam must survive a road trip. His overbearing penny pinching mother and father who hides behind sunglasses and smokes are along for the ride. They must make it to Robbie's, Adam's brother, seminary graduation, but Along the way are supposed gang members, sleazy hotels, dirty hotel desk clerks, but none of the situations are as bad as Robbie's, the bad son who mom thinks is as innocent as apple pie. But his last night before his life in the priesthood is filled with debauchery.
