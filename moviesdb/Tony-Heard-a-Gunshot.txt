Tony Heard a Gunshot

 A night club bouncer, Tony, gets off work and makes his way home where his wife, Jen, awaits his return. It was going to be just like any other Saturday night for the newlywed couple, until a gunshot rings out, breaking their routine. Down on the ground with blood pouring out and consciousness about to fade away, Tony foresees what he is truly going to miss. Inspired by a true story, this is the story of what Tony has left behind.
