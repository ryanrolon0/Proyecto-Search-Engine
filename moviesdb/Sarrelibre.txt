Sarrelibre

 Sarrelibre tells the story of the separatist movement in Germany's Saarland. The leader of a small underground organization has big plans. The time is ripe for revolution and he wants to go to the top. FunEs, Grandpa and the Seperatists are all behind him, however he can't seem to find the right moment. Then the sudden appearance of a bound man with a bag on his head. Is he really a minister? In any case FunEs has kidnapped him ...
