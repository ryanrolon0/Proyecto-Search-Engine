The Making of a Mobster

 This made-for-tv documentary treats comedy fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Mickey Blue Eyes, about a meek young man who must meet his fiancee's father, who just happens to be in the mob. Includes interviews with stars James Caan and Hugh Grant, along with the rest of the cast and crew of the film, who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it to completion.
