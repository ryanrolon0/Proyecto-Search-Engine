Skizo

 David is watching every step of his girlfriend through video surveillance and discovers that she is unfaithful to him. He is furious and his dark side demands revenge. Enraged and insane, he plans to stab her to death in the shower. However, her charm and beauty makes him soft, and he finds himself unable to carry out his plans. Will his dark or soft side win? Will he be able to kill Sarah, and how is his true self?
