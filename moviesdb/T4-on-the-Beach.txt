T4 on the Beach

 More highlights from T4 on the Beach , the ultimate beach party, with some of the biggest names in music taking to the stage live by the sea at Weston-Super-Mare in front of an audience of 30,000 fans. Vernon Kay, June Sarpong, Steve Jones and Miquita Oliver are your willing hosts guiding you through the music and the backstage madness. Celebrity interviews, competitions, party games and of course stunning performances make this the star-soaked must see event of the summer.
