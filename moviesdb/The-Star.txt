The Star

 Mick Rainer (Drew Kochera)is one of the most beloved movie actors in the world. But after his latest film is canceled, three people that work for him decide to take him for ransom and try and change the horrible way he treats people. While escaping from his Kidnappers, Mick is hit by a truck. Mick quickly bribes the driver into becoming his bodyguard and protecting him from the three kidnappers as well as the rioting crowds that are in love with him.
