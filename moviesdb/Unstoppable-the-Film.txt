Unstoppable the Film

 The film focuses on a 22 year old girl named Jessica Daniels who just lost her receptionist job of 4 years and also lost a very important person in her life- her Grandpa whom was a very talented artist. Jessica gets all of her Grandpa's art supplies and decides for once in her life to try something new, to live free and go with her gut. So she lives in the moment and starts painting. Something she has always wanted to do, but didn't because of her full time office job. She falls in love with painting. Unfortunately, Jessica's Mom is not supportive nor pleased to hear about this new craft as she remembers how much time painting took away from her and her Father. Will Jessica decide to quit for her mother or to follow her new found love?
