Take Five and Other Tales

 Three bondage vignettes in one Harmoney Concepts video. In the first, buxom blonde voice actress Amber is hired to do an impersonation - of a kidnapped princess. In the second Sadie and Dorothy are vying to be hosts of the network series, "Tied Up All Night". Between film clips of movie being aired on the network, the two rivals battle to place the other candidate at a competitive disadvantage. In the third, a new roommate discovers that one of the women with whom she shares her apartment is out to get her.
