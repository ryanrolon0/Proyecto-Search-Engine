The Body Male

 From the makers of PROVOCATEUR, The Body Male series is a celebration of male sensuality. Eight male models create eight different fantasies while stripping themselves of all inhibition for all to experience. As artistically visual, dramatic and striking as the images of PROVOCATEUR, this video version is unprecedented in the romanticism of the male body. Inspired and engaging, sexy and erotic, this is the first video of a series that embodies all that is masculine.
