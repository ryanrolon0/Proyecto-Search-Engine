Tere Ghar Ke Samne

 After years of rivalry, two wealthy men, Lala Karamchand and Lalal Jagannath, decide to build their individual bunglows - the trouble is the Architect for both the bunglows is Lala Jaggannath's son Rakesh Kumar, and the bunglows are opposite to each other. To complicate matters, Rakesh and Sulekha, Karamchand's daughter, have fallen in love; are aware of the existing animosity between their families, and comically juggle their lives to meet their dads' expectation.
