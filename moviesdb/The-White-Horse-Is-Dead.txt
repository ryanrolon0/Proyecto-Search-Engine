The White Horse Is Dead

 In a bizarre form of self help, a 17-year-old girl, puts leeches on her body, letting them suck her blood to ease the pain of her life. While her sexually charged Russian mother insights her to use her beauty to achieve the American dream. With the arrival of a handsome gardener, a potent male energy is released into the already tense female household, creating a strange triangle of sex, jealously and horrific manipulation.
