The Love House

 The Love House is a domestic violence shelter for battered women located in Atlanta, Georgia. Melissa Moore is a heroin addicted prostitute who is admitted into The Love House for safety after being severely beaten by her pimp. Monique Jackson, The Love House counselor tries her best to help Melissa transform her life from victim to survivor. Unfortunately, Monique learns that no matter how hard she tries, it's impossible to save everyone.
