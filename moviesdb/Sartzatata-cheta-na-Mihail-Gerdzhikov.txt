Sartzatata cheta na Mihail Gerdzhikov

 Direct descendants of the inspirer of the Uprising on the Transfiguration Day in 1903, Mikhail Gerdzhikov are our contemporaries - four generations, holding different world-views. To them, his ' brave squad', he is the Example, History, The Fairy Hero, their pivot, moral corrective and incarnation of the notion of human freedom. In the emotional narration of his 93-year-old daughter, Magda Gerdzhikova and granddaughter Nadezhda Nedkova unknown and curious facts of the life and deeds of Mikhail Gerdzhikov are brought to life.
