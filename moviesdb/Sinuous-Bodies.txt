Sinuous Bodies

 Jimmy uses his girlfriend Sasha to fund his drug habit. The drug dealer, Raul, supplies Jimmy in exchange for Sasha and gives Sasha a knife at her request. She hands the knife over to Jimmy so he can protect himself. Christopher is a middle class office worker with money who is Sasha's not so gentle "lover." A womanizer, Christopher gives necklaces to the women he tries to control. Sasha reaches a breaking point from the abuse she has taken all her life and she sets up a deadly confrontation between Jimmy and Christopher. She decides to leave both men behind for good, but does not have the strength to escape her lifestyle. The names and places will change in her future, but her situation will repeat.
