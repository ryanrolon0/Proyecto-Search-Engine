The Making of 'Escape Through Time'

 This made-for-video documentary treats science fiction fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Escape Through Time, about two women who inadvertently travel back and forth through time as a result of a mysterious ring from the future. Includes interviews with the cast and crew of this independent film, who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it to completion.
