Step Together

 The film follows the citizens of the town of Pella, Iowa and their attempt to break the world record for 'Most People Dancing in Wooden Shoes'. The film follows several colorful characters in the weeks leading up to the 50th Annual Tulip Time Festival and the biggest display of Dutch pride the world has ever seen. Enjoy the spectacle of a community coming together to clog the streets in the crowd pleasing 'Step Together'!
