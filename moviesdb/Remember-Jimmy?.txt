Remember Jimmy?

 In a small hot country, Leela - beautiful, enigmatic and cynical - 'manages' some high-class trafficking for her boss, the ... . Her piece-de-resistance? An ingenious and wicked courier system. Enter Jimmy, young, small-time, no-good, the perfect courier prospect. But this time the well-oiled system doesn't go according to plan. As Leela and her men rush to finish things off, Jimmy gradually remembers what has happened to him over the last three days. To create the strange, imaginary & unplaceable country, and the high-class world of the anti-heroine, the film was shot in a variety of locations Mediterranean islands of Saint Marguerite, Malta & Gozo; and interiors in Brussels, Luxembourg, and Yorkshire.
