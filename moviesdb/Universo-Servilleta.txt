Universo Servilleta

 Universo Servilleta(The Napkin Universe)is a story told through the eyes of Felix,a 23 year old from Asuncion/Paraguay. Felix loses both his job and his girlfriend,who has decided to flee the country in search of a better life.He takes refuge both in Sancho,his best friend who lives with his sister Julia.Both of them completely alone due to their parent's neglect. Then there's Eme,another childhood friend who's only aim in life is to capture every moment fleeting moment with his Hi-8 camera.
