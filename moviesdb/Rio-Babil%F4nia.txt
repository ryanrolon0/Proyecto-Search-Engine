Rio Babilônia

 Public relations man is invited to guide American millionaire during his stay in Rio de Janeiro. In this course, he gets involved in the most bizarre situations, from orgiastic mega-parties and sexy mEnages-a-trois to confrontations with the police, meetings with drug dealers and movie stars, facing corruption and even murder. An allegoric view of social and sexual relations in Rio de Janeiro n the early 80s, evoking biblical Babylonic pagan orgies in a hedonistic almost gomorrhic way, taking place near Babilônia slums (favela) which is located just behind Leme/Copacabana beaches.
