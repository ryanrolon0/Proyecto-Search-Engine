Violet

 Rumor has it local legend serial killer "Razorblade" Scott has returned to the local Texas community he terrorized before. A group of college kids on a post-grad road trip break down in the town and take refuge at the Dauntless Motel. One by one they start to disappear. Seeking the help of the local sheriff and deputy, they soon uncover the town's dark history. When the terrifying truth comes to light they find themselves in a desperate struggle for survival.
