The Life

 Frank and Andrew grew up together at the same school. Both friends got in trouble while growing up (Thieving). As they grew older Frank kept robbing for thrills while Andrew decides to lead a normal life. This reason for Andrew's change in lifestyle is because of the death of his mother. Andrew begins to listen to his father Shaun. Shaun is a spiritually grounded man who attributes faith as to what helped him cope with death of his wife. Andrew and Frank grew apart for many years until one event brings them back together. Andrew's father, Shaun has become ill. Andrew has just been recently laid off and is looking for work. After many dead leads Andrew becomes desperate for quick easy cash so that his father can receive the proper hospice care. Andrew calls on his childhood friend to make some money doing small time robberies thieving, etc. While Andrew visits his father in between robbery jobs, Shaun discovers how his son is making his easy cash. Because his father is ashamed, Andrew falls into a deeper state of depression. He goes on this last job with Frank with a sense of ambivalence...
