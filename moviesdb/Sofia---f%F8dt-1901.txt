Sofia - født 1901

 A hundred year old woman tells her life story to a radio reporter. Since being born in 1901 and having grown up as an orphan she has lived through the challenges that faced the world in the twentieth century. She has been a woman in a man's world, she has been poor, she has been a nun and a choir girl and an orphanage manager. She has seen great wars, great revolutions and great love.
