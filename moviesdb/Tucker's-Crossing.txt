Tucker's Crossing

 A young boy is dropped off at his Uncle's house by his deadbeat Dad. The boy is quickly put to work and physically and mentally tortured by Uncle Roo. Meanwhile, campers and hikers keep disappearing in the White Mountains of New Hampshire. Someone or something hides and waits for its next victim. What is the connection between the young boy and the horrible atrocities occuring in the darkness of the woods? Step into Tucker's Crossing and find out...
