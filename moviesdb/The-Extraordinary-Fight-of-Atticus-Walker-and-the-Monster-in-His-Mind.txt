The Extraordinary Fight of Atticus Walker and the Monster in His Mind

 Atticus Walker, a 23 year old struggling writer, has the opportunity to land his dream job and the only obstacle in his way is his own insecurity. Unfortunately for Atticus, his insecurities are in the visual form of a 6 foot tall, green-furred, obese, pessimistic Monster. In order for Atticus to live a normal, confident lifestyle, he must finally face the Monster in his mind.
