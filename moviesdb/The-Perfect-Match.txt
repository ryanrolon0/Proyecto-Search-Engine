The Perfect Match

 The Perfect Match is a modern opera, this current production which is being performed in a grandiose theater. It is the love story of Tam and Pal. They start they day waking up in their own separate beds, lamenting about not being loved. That changes when they meet each other in the park that day and instantly fall in love. The poignancy of the love story is juxtaposed against the fact of who or what Tam and Pal are.
