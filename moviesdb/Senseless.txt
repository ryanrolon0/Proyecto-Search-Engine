SenseLess

 Based on true events, SenseLess takes place in Iraq 2006. Following the story of one family we see their unfortunate reality, and their constant battle to bring their war torn home together again. In one of many dark days our story begins as we follow Zaynab a little seen year old girl, on a mission to find her missing doll.. a doll that her father had given her before he disappeared..Living in a time of war, this is the only way Zaynab can feed her dreams and imagination, and most importantly, recreate her memories of a happy home.. or so she hopes..
