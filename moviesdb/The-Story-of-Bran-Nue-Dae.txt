The Story of Bran Nue Dae

 THE STORY OF BRAN NUE DAE gives the background to the making of the feature musical 'Bran Nue Dae'. It tells how this crazy madcap story was based on a stage-play by Broome writer and musician Jimmy Chi and his band Kuckles. And how the stage play was based on the experiences of these artists, growing up in the 60's in the multi-cultural pearling town of Broome in the far north of Western Australia.
