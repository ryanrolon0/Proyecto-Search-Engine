Popcorn

 Joe, a sweet and clumsy movie theater usher, desperately wants to be a filmmaker. Before Joe can make it big, he has to contend with an uptight boss, a bully customer and popcorn flying through the air. Nothing can stand in the way of his big dream, except of course... reality. POPCORN is a fable illustrating that you must do more than daydream in order to realize your dreams. Joe, a sweet and clumsy movie theater usher, desperately wants to be a filmmaker.
