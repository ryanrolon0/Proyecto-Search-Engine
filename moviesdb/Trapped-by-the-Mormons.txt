Trapped by the Mormons

 Isoldi Keane is the the top recruiter in all of Mormondom -- using his mesmeric powers to ensnare young girls into his evil Mormon web of passion, polygamy and pamphlets! Will young Nora be strong enuff to resist his evil Mormon wiles? Will she pray for weakness? Find out in this remake of the 1922 silent horror cult classic Trapped by the Mormons, aka, The Mormon Peril!
