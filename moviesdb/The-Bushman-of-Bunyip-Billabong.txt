The Bushman of Bunyip Billabong

 The Bushman of Bunyip Billabong tells the tale of a careless swagman, who learns his lesson for disrespecting the environment. Based on the ballad of 'Waltzing Matilda' and the legendary myth of the bunyip, the film portrays Australia's distinct outback environment and characters using a unique style of stop-motion animation.
