Rose

 Rose (Helen Clifford) is a drug addicted prostitute who recently lost her child to social services, she is constantly abused by her pimp Blondie (Rami Hilmi) who keeps her drugged up and in debt in order to keep her on the game. However Rose's secret love affair with Blondie's right hand man Tony (Jack Marsden) strengthens and so does their desire to escape Blondie's clutches, but can they really escape their destiny?
