The Princess of Salsa

 "The Princess of Salsa" takes the viewer on a journey into the world of Latin dance sensation, Josie Neglia. It profiles the salsa star who set the Los Angeles Latin dance scene on fire with her passionate, energetic style, thus earning her the title "The Princess of Salsa". Interviews with Josie, family, friends and members of the Latin dance community, reveal the hardships she faced as she tried to break into the L.A. salsa scene. It's a world tour as the international star journeys to perform in the exotic venues of Rome, Paris, and the Caribbean.
