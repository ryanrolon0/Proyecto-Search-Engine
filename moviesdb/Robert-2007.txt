Robert 2007

 For the first time on TV, the Danish Film Academy presented its annual awards to Danish and international filmmakers, with _Drømmen (2006)_ (qv) and _Soap, En (2006)_ (qv) as major winners. Hosted by comedians 'Martin Buch' (qv) and 'Rasmus Botoft' (qv) in character as Dusk and Bomholt, respectively, the broadcast from the television studios at DR Byen also featured a performance by singer 'Dario Campeotto' (qv). Broadcast a few hours after the event, the special was edited to fit a 90-minute time slot.
