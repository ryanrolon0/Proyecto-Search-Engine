Ridden

 Gina is lost. She lives her life in control of nothing more than the moment that is passing. Things become worse when she starts to notice weird things happening in her life. Someone has been watching her...stalking her. Longing for stability she consults her uncle Frank, a Private Investigator. Time ticks away as Gina speeds into uncertainty and tries to find hope and a life for herself. It will all culminate in an event of violence that Gina cannot avoid.
