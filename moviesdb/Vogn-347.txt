 1950s - A man on a train journey, which doesn't seem to stop. All passengers in the train car are neither alive nor dead. Every time the train stops the passengers repeats their tragic deaths. With a woman in red the man decides to figure out why the journey never ends. All passengers are put on the car by their killers, and in order to get off, they'll have to break their patterns and accept their fate. The car is now 60 years later in a museum where two girls break in. One of the girls tricks her friend into the car and locks her inside. She leaves the museum knowing about the cars past and that she has delivered another dead passenger.
