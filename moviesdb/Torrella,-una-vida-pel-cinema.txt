Torrella, una vida pel cinema

 This film is a Doc-Biography of Catalan writer Josep Torrella i Pineda, and also it's the first chapter of a 'Sergi Rubió' (qv)'s project, entitled "Sabadell, bressol d'artistes" artists from Sabadell, born before 1940. Nowadays, 10 years after the shooting of this documentary, the next Rubió project for the next artist comes to life, this time focusing on the Catalan painter Joaquim Montserrat i Camps.
