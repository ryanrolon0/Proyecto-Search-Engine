The Michigan Assassin

 The Michigan Assassin follows the explosive life and shocking end to one of the best pound for pound fighters in the world of boxing. Stanley Ketchel was born in 1886 and in the next 24 years he would live hard and fight hard. From his close relationship with his mother to becoming Middleweight Champion to taking on World Heavyweight Champion Jack Johnson, Ketchel would leave a legacy of triumph and tragedy.
