The Art of the Tease

 The Art of the Tease follows the story of Sadie Powell a sultry burlesque dancer. On the night she has to deliver the performance of her lifetime she finds herself drawn to a handsome stranger. Faced with the reality of agreeing to be the prize in a financially beneficial deal between a rich patron, the club owner and herself, Sadie is forced to examine her life choices and battle her true desires.
