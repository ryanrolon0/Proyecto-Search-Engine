The Pursuit of Pleasure

 Seven women, ranging from a GenX midwife, to a former stripper to a 94-year-old psychologist and sex therapist, offer varied perspectives on the evolution of Pleasure since the US Women's Movement began over 40 years ago. These are not high profile activists, but instead simply women whose lives have been affected by changes in society. A punchy, provocative, witty exploration of the ever evolving question What brings us Pleasure? Seven women including a GenX midwife, a 95 year old psychologist former sex therapist, a former stripper now Ph.D sociologist, a nurse who was once a religious sister, a humanitarian MD, a recovered sex addict/poet and Joani Blank of "Good Vibrations" fame leave no stone unturned, offering their very intimate but not shocking experiences with love, relationships, sisterhood, beauty, food, sex, sensuality and satisfaction.
