Talk Dirty to Me

 Jason has had a rough few weeks. His father died, he lost his job, he lost the love of his life. With a dead-end career in Insurance he turns to his buddy Roy for a new beginning. Roy introduces Jason to the lucrative world of PhonesX Co., an online venture aimed at the lonely and depressed. Jason's luck changes for the better, until a mysterious stranger threatens everything he's worked for. Enjoy the first movie in the Talk Dirty To Me trilogy.
