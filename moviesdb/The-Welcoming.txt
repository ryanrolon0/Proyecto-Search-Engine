The Welcoming

 Arthur Englewood has just moved into a new apartment in a new neighborhood. Arthur longs for a quiet life outside the city where he can live his life and concentrate on his art. The neighborhood is pleasant, serene and very friendly, just perfect for someone like himself. But Arthur's solitude is about to get interrupted when a local group known as the Sunshine Girls comes ringing at his door. The Welcoming is a comedic thriller that proves that sometimes a town's most dangerous citizens are it's smallest.
