Silhouettes in Shadow and Light

 Academy Award Winning cinematographer Vilmos Zsigmond a.s.c. and Producer Jack Robinette document their experiences in creating the independent early American New Wave film "Summer Children" in 1965. Vilmos recounts his techniques and artistic approaches as a Director of Photography and camera operator applying his European aesthetic influences. Intimate stories of their experiences working together on the production of this film on Catalina Island are shared spontaneously between DP and producer. The feature "Summer Children" plays in the background of this recorded narrative of their experiences.
