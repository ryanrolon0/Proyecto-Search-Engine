Three Old Ducks

 Three Old Ducks is a black comedy that explores the side effects of old age. After arriving late for a tea party, Edith, an submissive elderly lady who carries her husbands ashes around, finds herself being bullied by the host, Elizabeth. Despite the fact that her friend Meryl encourages her to stand up for herself, Edith chooses not to retaliate and bides her time. But when she sees her opening for sweet revenge she takes it, only to find out that her immoral decision has back fired dramatically.
