The Professional Housekeeper

 The ability to create an environment of cleanliness, order, charm, and beauty is but one of the many talents of a wonderful homemaker and housekeeper. Becoming a "homemaker" or "professional housekeeper" can be rewarding. It makes you an important part of a family's every day life. Often, households require someone else to do the "homemaking" for them. To be a good household employer, you must know what is needed to get the job done right. For those wanting to make this your career, you will gain the necessary skills and view your life differently as you recognize how critical you are to a successful household. "The Professional Housekeeper for a Perfect Home"Household Training Guide & DVD is designed to train you in all aspects of the home Childcare, Marketing, Cooking, Flowers, Table Setting, Serving, Child and Home Safety and Emergency Procedures. The skills and information imparted in my program will increase your knowledge, initiative and desire to be the best at creating the perfect home! You will have a better understanding of how to have an efficient, stress-free, clean, and organized environment. You will enjoy the tremendous benefits of being a more experienced and qualified homemaker. Anyone completing this training will be empowered and proud to illustrate all skills and professionalism with confidence
