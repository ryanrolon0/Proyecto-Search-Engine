Reincarnation

 Ino lives alone with pots of five different flowers. He falls in love with Hana who works at the garden. One day he eats nothing but only water and meditate to fast for seven days to cleanse his body and mind. After the cleansing, he kidnaps Hana and eats her flesh. Then he makes a pure feces made by Hana's body. And he plants a seed into a pot with the feces. The feces and the seed grow a flower. He believes that by creating new life through his empty body in the form of a flower.
