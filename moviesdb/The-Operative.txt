The Operative

 The first installment of an urban action thriller,that charts the rise and fall of ex SAS operative John Beech and his family. After killing a terrorist informant and foiling a bomb blast in the city, Beech loses his beloved wife and teenage daughter in a horrific execution-style death. Fuelled by deepest pain,rage and precious memories of the family that once made his world complete, Beech must stop the terrorists at all costs and avenge the deaths of his loved ones.
