Santa Fe Saddlemates

 Governor Price sends Sunset Carson to investigate a smuggling ring which is baffling the Border Patrol. Newspaper woman Ann Morton is working incognito in the saloon waiting for a break on the smuggling story. By posing as an expected outlaw named Brazos Kane, Sunset becomes a member of the gang. Ann discovers his real identity and purpose but conceals her knowledge, even from him. Sunset is a special agent hired by L. Bradford Prince to break up a gem smuggling ring. Sunset goes to the border town , Tres Pinos, to find out the who and how. He has help from "Dead Eye" and the saloon singer. Sunset gets a job at the ranch with a fake name and soon learns the how, but needs to find out who is the big boss.
