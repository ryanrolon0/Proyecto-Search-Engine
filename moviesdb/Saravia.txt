Saravia

 Life journey of Uruguayan born poet, composer, and painter Mauricio Saravia, who was diagnosed at the age of five with McCune-Albright syndrome. The successful artist describes a lifetime of struggle against the incurable, rare, and painful disease which atrophies the growth of bones and affects the pigmentation of the skin. An empowering and uplifting documentary of international painter and poet Mauricio Saravia, titled SARAVIA. Saravia's life journey is a story about overcoming limitations and shattering stereotypes that can dramatically change the way you see life. The participants include Dennis Haysbert, Efren Ramirez, Sarah Thompson, Jennifer Sciole, Joseph Cortese, Christopher Coppola, Tangi Miller, Demore Barnes, Kevin Scott, Thomas Mesereau Jr., Robert Axelrod, David Bianchi, Varda Hardy, Patrick Ecclesine and Marjorie Gooson-Cagle, just to name a few.
