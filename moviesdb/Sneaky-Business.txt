Sneaky Business

 'Sneaky Business' Style. On board the S.S. Hookacanuck, a green sock-puppet snake named 'Sneaky' smuggles singing New York City stowaways into Canada for basic Human Rights. In this musical-comedy, Sneaky Snake (a green sock-puppet snake) enlists the aid of Sister Ping (the notorious people-smuggler) to help him sneak twelve singers from NYC into Canada for free medical care, gay marriage, medical marijuana, and a sensible government.
