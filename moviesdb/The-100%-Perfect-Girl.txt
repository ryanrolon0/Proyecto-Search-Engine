The 100% Perfect Girl

 An essence. A purity of soul. An intangible connection. Such are the experiences of a man when he notices an enchanting woman while out for a walk in Portland, OR. As he passes her on the street, the man discovers that she is The 100% Perfect Girl. Lacking any definition for his feelings, he only knows that it's not her face, her clothes or personality that mesmerize him. It goes much deeper as he connects with the core of her spirit. The 100% Perfect Girl explores the realm of the heart and soul in a man's imagination as he struggles to understand his emotions and attempts to speak to her. When it comes to love, the universal sentiments of longing, hope and regret take center stage. The 100% Perfect Girl asks us to look below the surface and inside ourselves as we each search for the 100% perfect partner.
