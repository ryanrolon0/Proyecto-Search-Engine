Rogue Eagle Creek

 When a dysfunctional family downsized to the over developed suburban community of Eagle Creek they discover a dark secret. Based upon Calvary raids building up to the Sand Creek Massacre in Colorado in 1864, shortly after the state was founded. This short historical thriller is about the past watching and judging our actions. A little girl ignored by her parents and step-sister befriends a Native American spirit as her imaginary friend. Appalled by her family hate and disrespect the spirit grows angry remembering the disrespectful lies he was told by the pale ones when alive.
