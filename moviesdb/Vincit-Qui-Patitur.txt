Vincit Qui Patitur

 Memories of Countess Anna de Grenaud-Stancioff range over some of the most important and dramatic periods of Bulgarian modern history -from 1887 to 195. Even nowadays, her penetrating look makes fit for interpreting countless aspects of the palace, diplomatic and political life of the country. The film tries to find in the memories of the past the answers to the questions of today, commented by the grandson of the Countess -eminent diplomat and public figure Ivan Stanchoff.
