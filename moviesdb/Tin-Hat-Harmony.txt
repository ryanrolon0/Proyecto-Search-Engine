Tin Hat Harmony

 Abe Lyman costumes his band as WW1 dough-boys. They entertain with a repertoire of martial songs which include Hinky Dinky Parley Voo, It's A Long Way To Tipperary, My Buddy, Yankee Doodle, Pack Up Your Troubles, You're In The Army Now, My Country Tis Of Thee, Over There, Goodbye Broadway Hello France, and Mademoiselle From Armentiers. War shots with narrative description are used.
