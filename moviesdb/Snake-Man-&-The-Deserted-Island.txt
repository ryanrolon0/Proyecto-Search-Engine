Snake Man & The Deserted Island

 Back in 1915, the ancients had a struggle against power, the beast Hokamina, Took control over the island in which the people lived on, The ancients had to fight, So, days later, Hokamina was defeated, And his spirit was sealed into a rock, and ever since then, The Island has been safe, but in 1920, The island erupted, and the entire island was engulfed in flames, 20 years later, Dr. Albert Wily just discovered the island, There is a mystery within the island, Seven gems of light, in which all can bring peace, and liberty to the world forever. Wily, sends out his best bot, Snake Man to get the job done, get past the island's defenses and bring peace and liberty to the world, forever.
