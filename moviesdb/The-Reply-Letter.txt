The Reply Letter

 There is a mysterious young woman who takes a picture herself with Polaroid camera when she has sex, and sends the photos to somewhere...almost everyday. Her name is Nadya. Even if her partner wants pay for having sex, she never got it. During her life is going, her make-up and behaviors are changed as Femme Fatale. Moreover she feels hopelessness in her life. One day, one strange man visits her. Suddenly, he beats her and spreads photos over her. After then, the flashback of Nadya begins. It's about her childhood in Turkey, her home. Her family are having traditional diner. Meanwhile, we can discover the strange man was her father, and Nadya has never gotten attention from her father because of her younger brother. At the end of the flashback, she realizes how to get attention of father. It is to make trouble. Finally, she shows to achieve her purpose at her room, NY.
