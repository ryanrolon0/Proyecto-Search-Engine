The Summer of 69

 One hot summer morning, George [Ian Karmel] discovers his girlfriend in a very awkward position. Newly liberated, he sets out on a quest to bed Sam [Greta Pauley], the girl of his dreams. Along the way, George runs across hookers, drugs, boobs, bike gangs, bunny rabbits and a really nice basket. At the end of the day, at a raging party, George finally gets Sam alone and finds himself in his own awkward position. In bed. Naked.
