Tinman

 Hoping to rekindle the close relationship they once shared, Jim finds the courage to confront his father more than a decade after being separated, but finds only a tortured, broken man. Renowned Australian actor Rhys Muldoon (The Saviour, Valentine's Day, The Secret Life of Us) and rising star Oliver Ackland (The Proposition, Wasted on the Young) star in this dramatic tale of separation and redemption.
