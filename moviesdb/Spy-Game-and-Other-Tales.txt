Spy Game and Other Tales

 Tyler auditions for a game show where she would spend most of her time trying to escape from bondage, but the show's producer has other ideas about how to use the footage of Tyler's squirming while bound hand and foot. In the second story, Amazing Lass shows Miss Justice the ropes, but heroine and sidekick are captured by a super-villain and Miss Justice's first adventure could turn out to be her last.
