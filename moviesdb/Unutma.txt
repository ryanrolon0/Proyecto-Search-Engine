Unutma

 Forget Me Not" is a short story about a puppet-like girl, Leyla Ciftcikmaz who is obsessed with taking photographs of herself and an old-wise antique shop owner, Koper Kirikkanat who is collecting old dispossessed photographs. Every time Leyla takes a Polaroid photo of herself, she encounters portraits of strangers! Fortunately antique shop owner knows one of the strangers she sees in her self portraits.
