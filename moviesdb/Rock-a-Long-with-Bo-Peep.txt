Rock-a-Long with Bo Peep

 Kids from 2-102 will enjoy this video that features "sounds of the Fifties and Sixties" music. You'll love the antics of those characters from the Puppet Television Network. The musical television production of the famous Bo Peep rhyme has set a new standard for quality and fun in family entertainment. Mac the Beaver, producer if the show has to find a sheep for the upcoming television show. Travis the Fox is a talent agent who represents the newest American star, Shermie th sheep. And then there's Fleeter, who sets his own set of "mousetraps" for the guest appearance of Shirley Glamourous, who shows up on the set to play Bo Peep. You'll rock with the tunes and roll with the punchlines as Shirley Glamorous and Fleeter compete to star with Shermi. And with family love and energy, they all pull together to sing and perform their show.
