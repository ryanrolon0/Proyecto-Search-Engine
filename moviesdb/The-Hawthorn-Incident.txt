The Hawthorn Incident

 Jack Taylor comes to find two things in his possession that are not his, but he does not know who they belong to. In most cases there would be an obvious way to find the original owner or to handle the situation. But due to the delicate nature of the two items he enlists two friends to help figure out the best way to handle the situation. Amongst the different perspectives on how to take care of this problem, the original owner comes calling. Jack then finds himself in quite the conundrum.
