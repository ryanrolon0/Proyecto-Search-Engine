Teree Sang

 Maahi is a 15 year old bubbly girl who is the only child of the high class 'Puri' family. Kabir is a 17 year old teenager from a lower middle class family. Maahi and Kabir become friends and gets attracted to each others life style. Their friendship and intimacy grows day by day. On a camping trip on New Year's Eve, the young couple gets into a physical relationship and Maahi becomes pregnant.
