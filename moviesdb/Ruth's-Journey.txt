Ruth's Journey

 Ruth's Journey is the story of Ruth Flower. In her long lifetime she amassed the largest single collection of antiques in the Midwest. When she died, she left behind many unanswered questions including "Why did she do this?" Director Melinda Roenisch explores the life of a woman who could have done anything she wanted, but instead, singlehandedly turned her mansion into a cluttered warehouse for her treasures. Before she died, Ruth Flower collected over a million antiques. Thousands of people came to the auction of these things. Questions hung in the air... "Who was Ruth Flower?" "Why did she do this?" By unraveling the twists of Ruth's strange, troubling life, the roots and repercussions of her need to amass are revealed.
