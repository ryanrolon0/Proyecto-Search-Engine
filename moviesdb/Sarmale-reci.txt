Sarmale reci

 A mechanic's wife decides to improve her fate by leaving her home for an "Arabian prince". The reasons for the separation are very legibly explained in some notes, but what is happening in the end when we find out that the housewife inside the woman thinks that the taste of the force-meat rolls in cabbage is more tasteful than the taste of adventure? Thanks to the passion that she mixed with the meat, the force meat rolls in cabbage are tasteful even cold.
