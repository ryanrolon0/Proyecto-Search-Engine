The Rhythm of Chaos

 When her sister dies in a car crash, Cam, a social worker with a penchant for tequila and old war paraphernalia, decides to kill the woman she holds responsible, uptight suburbanite Martha. But revenge takes an unusual turn, when Cam unexpectedly develops a relationship with Martha's burned out husband, Hal, in this darkly comic drama.
