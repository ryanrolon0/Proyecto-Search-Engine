Struggle Within

 Sidnie Lensington is Agoraphobic, she is afraid to leave her house. Strange events begin to happen, leading Sidnie to believe that somebody is inside the house with her. But is there somebody inside, or is Sidnie simply going crazy? The suspects are many, and the answers are few in this Psycological Thriller. Is Sidnie insane? Is somebody trying to kill her? And if they are, can she ever escape the house?
