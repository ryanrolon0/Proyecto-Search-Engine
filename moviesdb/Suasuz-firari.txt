Suasuz firari

 Bulent is a medical student who makes his living by singing at a night club. He is in love with Selma. Selma is molested by her boss Turgut, yet Celal Bey protects the girl from him. Turgut's sister Turkan falls in love with Bulent, she proposes him assuring that he will be rich. Bulent becomes the manager of the factory, so Selma leaves him and her job. Celal provides her a job at old rich Rifat's, who is blind too. Bulent gets back singing. After Rifat's death, Selma inherits his money. She buys Turgut's factory which closed since it went bankrupt. Turgut kidnaps Selma, Turkan tells it Bulent. Selma has donated all the property to charities. Turgut and his men get arrested, while the two lovers awaits a happy future.
