The Masseuse Returns

 After a world-class mountain biker endures a devastating loss, he begins to question his loss, he begins to question his ability as an athlete. His manager, Coach sees what is getting Joe down and comes up with the perfect solution - hiring Lee, a stunning female athlete to come in and get Joe back on track. Slowly, Lee massages Joe back into shape with a series of on course and off course lesions that leave Joe begging for more and determined to win.
