Save Jesus

 Little Hanna Montgomery would do anything to keep her parents from getting a divorce, including selling her soul to the Devil. When she does just that, Jesus comes to her rescue only to find that he's too late. Yet never one to shy away from an advantage, Satan bets Jesus that he can't het Hanna's parents back together without using his holy powers. With the fate of Hanna's soul on the line, Jesus accepts the challenge in this comic tale of good vs. evil
