Small World

 A drug deal goes bad and her husband is murdered. Jasmine and her son move from Detroit to Tampa in search of a better life. Her day job doesn't pay the bills, so she takes a night gig as a strip dancer. Feeling torn by religious convictions, Jasmine turns to the church. Soon, the perfect man enters her life. Can she tell him of her past or her job as a dancer? Would he understand? She knows nothing of his past...is he quite so perfect...or will more secrets unfold? Life reaches full circle... Murder, Redemption, Salvation.
