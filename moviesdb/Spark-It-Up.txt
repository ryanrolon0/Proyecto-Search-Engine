Spark It Up

 Like a sketching on a pad, life is as real an art form as it is raw. This is a depiction of young adults and what they are facing nowadays in society. The story is told through a lighter's travel. In the this world there are different kinds of people, all who have unlike points of view. If you were an object in a room that could listen in on how diverse and intense young adults are you'd be amazed. In this story, it happens.
