The Latent Image

 Mary, an intelligent and hard working high school student, finds her moral ethics at odds with her teacher, Mr. White, who has chosen her to be one of his students that he grades on the basis of sexual encounters. Can the truth be exposed by a fellow student in her high school? Will Mr. White be brought to justice? Jacob, a mild-mannered but passionate yearbook photographer must overcome his own fears and lack of confidence in order to reveal the truth and save the girl he loves.
