The Sultan's Birthday

 On the Sultan's birthday, the entire city is celebrating. People bring the Sultan a birthday cake and some gifts. One of the gifts consists of a sexy female mouse in a harem fantasy costume. She begins to dance for the Sultan. The Sultan and everyone else watching the performance display pleasure, and even the buildings start to dance. But alas, this joyful time is interrupted when an army of evil cats come riding in on flying carpets. Their attack disrupts the entire celebration. But of course, Mighty Mouse rides in to save the day...
