The Picture

 Lou, a young and handsome American artist, is seduced by a beautiful house on the Spanish Mediterranean coast. He rents the home from Sophia, an attractive and mysterious young lady who doesn't tell Lou that the house keeps a secret. Lou starts to paint a chilling picture pushed by a strange force and a series of horrible things begin to happen. It's time for Lou to fulfills his part of the bargain he made with Sophia a century ago...
