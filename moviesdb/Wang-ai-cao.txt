Wang ai cao

 The son of a wealthy business man (Alan Tam) sees Yun-Bai (Lin Feng-Jiao) at an art exhibition for the very first time and falls in love with her. Soon he finds out that Yun-Bai doesn't like men who are lazy and who don't have to do anything for their money. Xiao-Zhi (Alan Tam) wants to do whatever he has to do to get this wonderful girl and is lying to her that he's a poor artist. Yun-Bai falls in love with him and now he want to reveal his real identity. But how can he do it without losing her again?
