Retour au pays

 Olivier, a young West Indian from Guadeloupe has brilliantly finished his studies in Paris. With several years of work experience, he decided to go back to Guadeloupe in order to find his family, his childhood friends and build up his life. When he left, he was a teenager. Today, his glance changed and it's a new life he has to discover. Being torn between his work, his family, friend's pressure and his wish to find his childhood marks, he gets in a fierce struggle for his life's stability...
