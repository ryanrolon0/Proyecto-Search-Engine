The Kidnapping of Samantha Ryan

 After years as a struggling actress, Samantha Ryan finally lands some roles as a television guest star. But, no sooner does she finally get some money to pay her bills, her sleazy stepfather shows up on her doorstep demanding a handout. When the starlet refuses, her stepdad and his kinky girlfriend take measures to force the aspiring thespian to hand over the cash. The devious duo kidnap the ingEnue and bind her in series of ever-more restrictive bondage positions. Will the innocent girl crack under their tickling fingers, cruel ropes and smothering gags?
