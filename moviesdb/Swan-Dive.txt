Swan Dive

 Getting drunk with his cat after work, a lonely and depressed Bryan takes his friend Renata's suggestion and turns to the help of a psychic named Willow, who delivers an ominous and dark tarot reading that pushes Bryan to the brink. Not knowing where to turn, Bryan reaches out to Renata, who cannot save him from himself. Bryan takes to the streets in search of Willow only to discover his greatest fears and finds he himself staring directly down into the face of death.
