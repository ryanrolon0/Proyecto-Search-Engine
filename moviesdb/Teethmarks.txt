Teethmarks

 After breaking up with Brenda (his secretary), Richard tries to score some cocaine from the drug dealing intern Andrew. The two meet while the office holiday party is in full swing. Andrew refuses to deal any drugs until Richard pays his tab, suffering withdrawal Richard attacks Andrew leading to a scuffle in the bathroom. With Richard over powered and tied, Andrew obtains Richard's petty cash box key and goes to retrieve the cash. A empowered Brenda goes back to confront Richard and finds Andrew. Discreetly she follows him back to Richard where she gathers her courage. Both men soon come across the vengeful force of a woman scorned and the aftermath will change their lives forever.
