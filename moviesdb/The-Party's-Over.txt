The Party's Over

 In this tense emotional drama, Liz tells her best friend Chris they need to talk through what happened at The Party. Chris knows it's going to be a difficult meeting. He has loved her since the day they met but asking her on a real date has always been out of the question. Her love would be dream come true but friendship was all that was on offer. Chris waits nervously and when Liz finally arrives they are suddenly shy. They are both clear about what happened that night. Neither is ready to hear what the other has to say.
