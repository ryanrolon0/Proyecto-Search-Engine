Tha Sistahood Part 2

 Comedic, Dramatic, Tragic and Timeless. Tha Sistahood Part 2 is a continuation of Part 1 which is based on the Book that is inspired by events that actually happened! After reading her mother's diary, Alexis Thompson decides to pledge the glorified sorority anyways. Against her grandmother's wishes, she willingly goes through the frightening process, but only to seek revenge! All while encompassing a humorous aspect at times.
