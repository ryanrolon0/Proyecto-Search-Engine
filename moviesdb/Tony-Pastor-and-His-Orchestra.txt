Tony Pastor and His Orchestra

 This edition of Universal's "Name Band" shorts (production number 2312) features Tony Pastor, his trombone, his orchestra, the Nilsson Twins, the Clooney Sisters (Rosemary and Betty in possibly their first film appearance)and Mildred Law. The bands opens with "Hawaiian War Chant", and other music includes "Spell of the Moon", "My Sister's a Little Bit Crazy" (sung by the Clooney Sisters), "Movie Tonight", "Girl Singer" and "Paradiddle Joe."
