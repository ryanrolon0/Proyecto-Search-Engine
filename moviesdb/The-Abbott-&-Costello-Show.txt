The Abbott & Costello Show

 Five-disc set of Season One (1952-1953), in which Bud and Lou, perpetually in search of ways to make the rent, live in Mr. Fields' boardinghouse along with a group of quirky characters. Contains Bonus Features that include interviews with Lou's daughters Chris and Paddy, sound-and-color home movies of the Costello family, and the restored 1948 short "10,000 Kids and a Cop." All 26 episodes of Season One (1952-53) with Bud Abbott and Lou Costello living in Sid Fields' boarding house, trying to drum up ways to make the rent. Supporting cast includes Joe "Stinky" Besser, Hillary Brooke, Gordon "Mike the Cop" Jones, Joe "Mr. Bacciagaloupe" Kirk and others. Bonus Features include all-new interviews with Lou's daughters, Paddy and Chris, plus never-before-seen color and sound Costello home movies and the DVD release of the 1948 promotional short "10,000 Kids & a Cop," starring William Bendix, Bud & Lou, and James Stewart.
