The Palace

 Just fifteen minutes from downtown San Diego, Tijuana's Red Light District is filled with prostitution, drugs, kidnappings and government corruption. Amidst the chaos, the most innocent and undeserving victims are the kids. The Palace has become a safe haven and home to 100 of the most victimized children in the area that would otherwise be on the streets, struggling to find their way. Witness their miraculous change brought on by simple beliefs.
