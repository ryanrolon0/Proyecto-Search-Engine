The Quest for Happy Hour

 It is a time of great turmoil in the ancient world. Ragnarok, the day of judgement, is fast approaching. The gods have one last chance to avert certain doom. Using their unique powers, they must make one man happy for one hour. Unfortunately, that man is Derf the Viking! The world's very existence may well depend on Derf's unpredictable temperament. Will the gods succeed in making him happy for just an hour? Or are we doomed?
