Shadowman

 Sophia has been singled out for assassination but the man sent to kill her has fallen in love with her. Alexander a larger than life hit man is the highest paid killer for hire in the country. Victim after victim has fallen to his feet, their last memory and the business end of his custom guns. A true stranger to human feelings, Alexander commits atrocities beyond imagination. but now he must fight for the one he loves. Assassin after assassin will come after her and meet the cold steel of Alexander's gun. But if he is to protect her he must first defeat the Shadowman a twisted shadowy figure representative of Alexander's darker side and most intimate desires.
