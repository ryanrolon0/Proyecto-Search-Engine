Runways & BunkBeds

 International Model Luke Sebastian(Axel Harney) lives a charmed life. He gets paid to walk runways, have his picture taken, and travel the world meeting different beautiful women at every stop. But when he ends up with an atypical roommate Fronk (Luke Richmond), and falls for a beautiful Model named Molly (Monica Percich), Luke is forced to look within and make decisions that might bring his charmed life to a halt. Written and Directed by former International Model Axel Harney. Based on a true story.(Romantic Dramedy)
