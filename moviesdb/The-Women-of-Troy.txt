The Women of Troy

 After ten years, The Trojan War is over. All its men are dead, all its women's fate lie in the hands of their conquerors dread, punishment and humiliation. In a prison, as the women await their fate, they struggle to keep alive their dignity, their humanity and their memories of a civilisation soon to be lost for ever. Two thoughts keep them alive see the punishment by death of the woman they blame for all their suffering Helen.
