The Greatest Pharaohs

 A history of Egypt as demarked by the men and women who lead her. Narmer, the first pharaoh, Snefru, the first pharaoh to commission a pyramid, Khufu (Cheops) the builder of the Great Pyramid, Hatshepsut, the first woman pharaoh to Amenhotep IV / Akhenaten, the first monotheist, Tutankhamun, Ramesses, Seti, Even Cleopatra, all are covered.
