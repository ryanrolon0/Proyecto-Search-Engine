Suljari-e-seo chingu-ga dwae-neun bup

 Young-Hoon, Jeong-Hoon, and Sang-Hoon, all from the same graduate school, have a meeting for drinks. After a while, Sang-Cheol, Sang-Hoon's brother, joins them. As the drinking meeting gets longer, kindness and consideration among them disappear gradually, and finally it leads to a fighting.
