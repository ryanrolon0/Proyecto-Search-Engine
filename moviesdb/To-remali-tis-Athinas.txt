To remali tis Athinas

 Stefanos is an ex-wrestler who has become a heroin addict. He meets Xenia, a beautiful blonde who has just ran away from her home because her father (Christos Natsios) tried to assault her sexually. During the first days everything seems to be normal until Stefanos takes Xenia to a dirty night club and makes her work as a prostitute. When his cousin dies he decides to quit drugs and take revenge so he returns at the night club and burns a massive quantity of heroin. The big boss (Giorgos Loukakis calls Aris (Nikos Tsachiridis) who arrives at Athens to take control of the situation...
