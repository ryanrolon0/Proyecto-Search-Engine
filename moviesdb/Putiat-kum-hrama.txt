Putiat kum hrama

 A documentary about child and teenage crime. How and why is teenage crime exploited as a social issue. The film renders a psychological portrait of young people held in the prison in Boitchinovtsi. It follows the lives of a several young persons -- from petty thieves and habituatual criminals to murderers -- their confessions and the forgiveness of their parents. Is society ready to accept these people and how are they going to start their lives all over...
