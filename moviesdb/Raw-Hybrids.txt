Raw Hybrids

 Enter a natural place where unnatural rules. Lady Bless, wanted? She's the most pure, knowledgeable being of our time. She knows what most do not. She must secretly spread the word. Find out why the truth is suppressed in a world of hybrids. A young man named Passover is being watched by a mysterious woman that apparently is one of the most wanted individuals within all of the mystery organizations of the world. Finally revealing herself to Passover, she imparts to him devastating yet undeniable information regarding everything from the food that the every day individual consumes to the "clouds" one sees in the sky. Having always been a firm believer in conspiracy theories, Passover prepares himself to make the moves, this mysterious woman, that is known only as Lady bless, tells him to make. After revealing his meeting with Lady Bless to his closest friends Sacred and Eyes, the three are entrusted into an organization which is involved in revealing truths that were once dismissed and regarded as extremist paranoia.
