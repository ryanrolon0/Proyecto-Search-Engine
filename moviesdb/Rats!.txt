Rats!

 A simple remodeling project turns into a delusional maze of extermination. With its clear "Men are from Mars, Vermin are from hell" message, attacking on numerous fronts, invades the mind with new awareness of rataphobia's rampant stigma in today's suburbia. As Murray tries to remodel his daughter's basement, he's swept up in a one-on-many battle of wit against whisker. He confides in a quirky exterminator who gives him important advice that is destined for the toilet.
