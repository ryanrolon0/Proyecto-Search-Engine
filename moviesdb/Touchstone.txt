Touchstone

 'Touchstone death and rebirth of a socio-political, healing arts & spiritual center. Members of this Western Massachusetts community are known for their uplifting music & world-renowned Sacred Circle Dancing; and Touchstone Farm, itself, is also recognized for wondrous herb, flower & meditation gardens. Two and a half years in the making, this is an open-hearted exploration of the curious & adventurous spirits who lost, and eventually regained, their 'center.' George 'Shaker' McNeil, who has for decades been a locally-respected farmer, guide, yoga & dance teacher and peace activist, unexpectedly and dramatically re-emerged as Deborah Hall-McNeil. Amidst the center receiving an outpouring of local and international support to overcome building closures by local inspectors.
