The Push

 Kate Roberts, a painter living in Santa Fe, New Mexico, has reached an impasse in her life. Unable to produce new paintings, Kate's life takes a new course when she engages in a passionate love affair with a friend, Thomas Oliver Randall III. Is it true happiness or is it just a push in the right direction? Kate Roberts, a successful painter in Santa Fe, New Mexico, has reached an impasse in her life. She is having problems getting motivated, and is unable to produce meaningful paintings. At the same time, the gallery, where her work is displayed, closes due to the poor economy. She knows something is missing from her creative life, but does not know what it is. On a snowy winter afternoon, she receives a call from a divorced friend, Thomas Oliver Randall III. Her life takes a new course when their friendship is taken to a new level. Fearful of commitment, Thomas does not want their sexual relationship to ruin their friendship. Kate understands and accepts the relationship for what it is. However, they both struggle with their feelings for one another. Once they give into their hearts desire, their relationship blossoms. Ten months later, a new woman enters Thomas' life because she is a lawyer. Kate is devastated. After a few months, Thomas realizes his mistake, but is it too late? Is it true happiness for Kate or just a push in the right direction?
