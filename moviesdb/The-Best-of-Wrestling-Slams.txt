The Best of Wrestling Slams

 This compilation video showcases pro wrestling action from the early 90's, focusing primarily on such now popular figures as the Godfather, X-Pac, Jeff Jarrett and Mick Foley, showing what they looked & wrestled like before they were stars. In addition, the video shows where several former major wrestling stars, such as Koko B. Ware & Cowboy Bob Orton, ended up.
