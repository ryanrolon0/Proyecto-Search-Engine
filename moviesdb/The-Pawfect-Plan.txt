The Pawfect Plan

 This suspense comedy short film follows the on-going epic tale of cat vs. dog. Unknown to the owners, the resident feline is secretly plotting against the lovable family dog. As soon as the coast is clear the cat, in its determined and inventive ways, contrives an evil plot against the dog. Unbeknownst to the family, the feisty feline lays trap after trap to get the hapless dog in trouble and literally kicked out of the house. This leaves the poor canine no other choice but to defend itself against the onslaught of feline induced blackmail and secret schemes. The tables are quickly turned when the canine takes action against these attacks and devises a plan that could only be the PAWFECT PLAN
