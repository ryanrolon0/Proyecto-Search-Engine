Tandem Surfing the Love Story

 Tandem Surfing art of couples surfing. With in-depth interviews and stunning surf action footage it highlights World Champion Tandem Surfers past and present. Filmed in High Definition, on location at some of the top tandem surf spots in the world; Makaha & Waikiki, San Onofre & Malibu, O'ahu North Shore & Cocoa Beach. The film contains vintage footage of tandem surf legends Steve & Barrie Boehne, as well as brilliant HD footage of the newer generation who are elevating the art into the realm of extreme sport, such as World Champions Rico Leroy & Sarah Burel. In the right moment a girl can feel like a feather... Tandem Surfing has been compared to dance, an improbable tango performed out on the waves. However in this dance, the dance floor is moving. It can rise up to swallow you whole as it rolls you around on razor-sharp coral teeth. Meet Chuck & Tiffany, Mark & Debbie, Charlie & Myra, Brian & Cindy, Clay & Kristen, Steve & Barrie, and a whole host of other professional tandem surfers, as they demonstrate liquid gymnastics, watery aerobics, and sultry dance. A romance among four elements - a man and a woman, a board and a wave. In this film we explore the timeless love story of mutual strength, shared focus and balance, sublime trust, communication and respect, ...Welcome to the heart and soul of 'Tandem Surfing The Love Story'
