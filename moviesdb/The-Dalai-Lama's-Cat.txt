The Dalai Lama's Cat

 Peter and Mike go to Tibet. Peter's imminent wedding offers a novel alternative to the traditional stag night. Mount Kailash is sacred to Buddhists and Hindus. A pilgrimage launders a lifetime of sins of anyone... Peter and Mike realize they can only benefit from their journey to the roof of the world. Peter arrives obsessed with a tale of the Dalai Lama's cats, scattered to monasteries around Tibet when he escaped in 1959. Searching for evidence of the cats, or the story, Peter and Mike discover Tibet. This gently absurd travel tale, highlights the daily lives of the people and glimpses some of the planets most beautiful landscapes.
