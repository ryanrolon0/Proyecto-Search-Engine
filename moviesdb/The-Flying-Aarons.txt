The Flying Aarons

 The documentary details their beginnings in high school to becoming one of the most entertaining and creative bands in the Chicagoland area, filling their venues to capacity with screaming fans. The Flying Aarons documentary also deals with the band's low points such as; jealously between members, constant lineup changes, and trouble with the law. The band members also talk about some of their memorable shows and popular songs, as well as give a glimpse of what is in store for their future. You may describe the documentary as This Is Spinal Tap, E True Hollywood Story, and VH1 Behind The Music all thrown into a blender.
