Visions of the Darkside

 The clone war has begun, and the dark Jedis known as the Sith have arisen from extinction. Led by the Emperor himself, the Sith have set out to destroy all the Jedi. After the death of his master, the young Jedi Daren Rendu prepares to join the rest of the Jedi in the fight against the growing threat of the Sith. But before Master Rendu can depart, his old friend, now corrupted by the dark side, has come to confront him. This Sith warrior possesses a dark secret that is about to be revealed...
