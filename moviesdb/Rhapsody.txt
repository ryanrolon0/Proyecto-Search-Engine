Rhapsody

 Jake and Alyssa are a young couple, deeply in love. In a twist of fate, Alyssa is killed on her way home from work one night. Jake is the top suspect, but he takes the law into his own hands and goes after her killer, shooting him in revenge. Jake says goodbye to his family and after a chase by the cops, finds himself in Hell. Redeeming himself, he is sent to Heaven to be with Alyssa.
