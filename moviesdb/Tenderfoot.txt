Tenderfoot

 An independent film about a group of teenagers at school and how a war affects them. This film was made on a micro budget solely by teenagers and was made over a period of one and a half year's. The story centers around several different friendships, Declan (Sam Pimm) decides to spend more time with Shayne (Jake Melhuish) and Ewan (Scott Osbon), two rebellious friends while Clive (Lewis Cross) struggles to keep his old friends close when he becomes distracted by his older brother Gordon (James Armstrong) leaving to fight in the war.
