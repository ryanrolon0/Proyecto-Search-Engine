The Making of 'Terminator 2

 This documentary treats movie fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Terminator 2 about the further attempts of the SkyNet computer system to assassinate John Conner before he can become a danger, as well as the resistance's efforts to protect him. Included are interviews with Arnold Schwazenegger, Linda Hamilton and the rest of the cast and crew who share their experiences from making the film, as well as disscuss the efforts that went into it.
