Sparkling Spiral

 Voluntary action to society is the key in realizing our positive future. In a world where technology blends with daily lives, People new that voluntary actions can bring even a small change to the world. Eiko, a girl who works at a cafe, knew that and practiced 'Good seed makes a good crop'. One day she helps out a couple, with a little help from Emotional Viewer, a devise that can read plant's emotion.
