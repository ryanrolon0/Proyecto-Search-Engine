The Washing Machine

 Ric and Shelley, a young couple with 2 jobs, 1 child and lots of bills. Ric plumbs in a second-hand washing machine, but the machine has the strange ability to switch itself on and interrupt their love making, and is it watching them, especially her? Find out what happens when lust takes on a whole new 'spin.' It's 'Christine' with white goods.
