The Anasazi Mummy

 Archeology students trek into the desert in search of the lost Anasazi tribe...instead they uncover the mummified remains of a long dead witch doctor...when they awaken him from his 100 year dirt nap, the terror begins. This movie features live scorpions and giant tarantulas plus an enormous snake...all alive and out for blood...under the spell of their master the Anasazi witch doctor-mummy. These deadly pawns attack the unfortunate students! Will any escape and live to tell this blood curdling tale?
