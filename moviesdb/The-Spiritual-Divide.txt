The Spiritual Divide

 Jenny Harris loses her husband due to a sudden heart attack and becomes financially disadvantaged. Consequently, she is forced to relocate to Los Angeles with her teenage daughter, Ranisha, and 11 year old son, Bobby. The family moves in with Jenny's upper class sister, who is married to a successful doctor and who also has two children teenager, and Cliff, a 12 year old mischievous son. Jenny raised her children in a Christian environment and wasn't prepared for the head on collision when the secular and Christian worlds collide.
