Tarantulas and Other Conveniences

 Talented boxer, Mark Perez takes one last punch when he finds out that he has a lot more fighting to do outside the ring. He is diagnosed with a malignant tumor in his brain and things get even more complicated when his girlfriend, Cully confesses that she is pregnant with his child. Time is running out and this leaves Mark in a world of chaos and desperation. When he and his friends make a final trip to Las Vegas, everything explodes into an unveiling of dark secrets, new loves, and a surprising twist of fate with the only thing keeping Mark going... his unborn child.
