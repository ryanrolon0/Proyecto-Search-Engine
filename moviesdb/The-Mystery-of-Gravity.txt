The Mystery of Gravity

 Combine the elements of a good natured, inquisitive traveler named Tol Stilts, an energetic scientist, a sleepy radio flyer, and the whimsical imaginations of children. Together they wander their universe in search of explanations regarding life's wonderful mysteries. Whether it is the mystery of shooting stars, or how rainbows bend colors through the sky, with this eclectic group of friends all will be explored and discovered. This educational series will engage the young viewer with song and delight, embracing their imagination while teaching them the design of complex origins.
