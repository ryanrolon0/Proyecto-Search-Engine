Portrait of an Unknown Cabbie

 A Muslim taxi driver roams the streets of Canberra by night, his fares include students, politicians, whores, drunks, junkies, and other nightlife of the nation's capital. His travels have taught him about the dark side of this nation and its people. He quickly learns that he has no place in this society. An outcast, hounded by thugs and ignored by the police, he wanders the mean streets in fear...
