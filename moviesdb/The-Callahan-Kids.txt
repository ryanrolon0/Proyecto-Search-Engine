The Callahan Kids

 If you think Frisbee isn't a sport, well, go f*** yourself!! Follow the Miami Ultimate Frisbee team through the largest tournament in Frisbee's history, Cu1timate's 'Trouble in Vegas.' They came to play their hardest and party even harder, but when mother nature throws them an unexpected twist, there's only one thing left to do! A gripping tale about following dreams and fighting the man.
