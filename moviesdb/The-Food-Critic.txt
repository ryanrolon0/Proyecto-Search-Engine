The Food Critic

 Restaurant owner Gerald, excitedly tells his renowned chef, Brady, that the local food critic will be reviewing the restaurant that night. Wide eyed with worry, Brady reveals to Gerald that he was caught sleeping with the Food Critic's wife and the intentions behind his review are most likely vindictive. Desperate for options to stop the review, both Gerald and Brady come up with a plan to kill the food critic. However, they don't know what he looks like. During a hectic night of dining, Gerald spots who he thinks is the food critic. He quickly tells Brady and they wait. When the food critic decides to use the facilities, Brady makes his move. Finally the two can breath a sigh of relief as their problems seem to be behind them. Little do they know their mistake and a review still gets written.
