Turntable Trauma

 Nonstop music, hypnotic visuals, and an economic recession all make up this stylish hip-hop documentary. Leap into a mystical world where a suburban rap artist feels he must carry a gun for the sake of being an artist. A DJ eloquently describes his colorful mixing styles and dislike for new digital technologies. And the appearance of a delirious New York nightclub culture created from the effects of economic downsizing cause a chaotic nightlife. From color-drenched images to dark surreal humor, Turntable Trauma breaks the norm of what most see in the world of hip-hop music and filmmaking
