The Fallen Faithful

 The Fallen Faithful follows the journey of Moran Douglas, a religious man, searching for a profound and meaningful connection with God.He is also a man of violence, a hired killer. Riddled with inner turmoil, Moran tries to live his life serving two masters, Asa, a power mongering psychopath,and the Lord. He struggles to gain the courage to leave his life of sin behind without becoming a victim himself.
