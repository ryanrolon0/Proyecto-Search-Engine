The Things Unsaid

 Rob and Julie are two polar opposites enjoying an unlikely relationship until deceit and paranoia set in. Speculations about Rob's previous conquests and possible infidelity lead Julie to believe that their relationship is coming to an end. A frustrated Rob starts keeping secrets from Julie, leading to a misunderstanding that hammers the first nail into the coffin of their relationship. As they stop communicating with one another, Rob and Julie speak directly to the viewer to say the things that they couldn't possibly say to each other. There are two sides to the story, but who is telling the truth? And do Rob and Julie still love each other enough to save their relationship?
