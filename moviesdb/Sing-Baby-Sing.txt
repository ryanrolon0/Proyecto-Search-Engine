Sing Baby Sing

 Sing Baby Sing is a documentary about the legendary Houston singer Sammie Relford. Sammie has spent his life performing electrified covers of R&B favorites for local audiences while continuing to unsuccessfully score a record contract for his original work. The film details his incredible stage performances, drawing people of all color, creed, and class to dance and sing together, charmed by "Houston's hardest working entertainer." Combining interviews and live footage on stage, Sing Baby Sing is an inspirational and thought provoking story of one man's reach for the stars. From the murder of singing partner and long-time love Kim Yvette, to the struggle for a record contract and industry support of his music, Sammie overcomes all obstacles with humor, candor, and energy.
