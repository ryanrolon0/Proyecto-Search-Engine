The Scavengers

 Jack and Orson are brothers in crime. They steal cars for money and live day-by-day. After a successful heist Jack hits the town and brings home a girl, Lauren. Jack and Lauren are close, but we sense a longing from Orson, the more sensitive brother. Lauren has a mysterious past, and even more mysterious motives. Is she using Jack? One night she steals the money Jack and Orson earnt for their heist and leaves. She returns however, but this time to Orson. Tensions rise as the love triangle shatters and each person must choose what is most important to them.
