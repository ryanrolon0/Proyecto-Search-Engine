Reporter Raju

 Reporter Raju is an honest, daring and quality reporter, works for his editor, Sharma. Raju is called by a nurse to Royal Theatre for a program  - in fact a secret mission. Here she promises to meet him in his own house after the program has ended. When Raju reaches his home, she tells him that a royal patient had died in the City hospital. The patient disclosed to her that a man with 4 and a half fingers has plotted to kill the heir to his vast estates. When Raju goes to make tea for her she is murdered, but before dying hands over the royal ring given to her by the patient. Raju.Raju is now the suspect to this murder, but escapes.
