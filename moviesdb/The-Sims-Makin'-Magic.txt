The Sims Makin' Magic

 The final expansion pack to The Sims, Maxis picked a mysterious theme!! In this expansion pack, you never really know what spells your sims may cast next, especially if they jump into the hole in the ground, to land at magic town. There are all new themed magic fairs with various rides, and an all new magic town. Cast spells and maybe even move in to the mysterious magic town homes. It's the final expansion pack-beware-your SIms have magic!!
