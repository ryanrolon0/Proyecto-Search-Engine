The Battle of Hong Kong Part 1

 2010, a meteor landed on the Hong Kong Victoria harbor, dividing Kowloon (North) and the Islands City (South). As the dust settled, the Islanders took control of the meteor upon discovering its ability to emit an energy shield to block out the dust storms. The Island City declares its independence in 2020. Countless Kowloon natives rushed to the border looking for refuge. The Council granted 10,000 citizenship to those willing to work as slaves to rebuild their infrastructure...
