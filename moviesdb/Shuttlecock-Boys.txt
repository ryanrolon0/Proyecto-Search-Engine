Shuttlecock Boys

 Shuttlecock Boys - Story of Entrepreneurship Dream, is a heart warming tale of 4 boys from Delhi who play badminton in the night in their neighborhood. These boys who come from humble backgrounds decide to do SOMETHING ON THEIR OWN while playing badminton one such night.This attempt becomes their lifeline putting them on a litmus test of determination, courage, luck and spirit of friendship. SHUTTLECOCK BOYS is the story of Indian Entrepreneurship Dream. It revolves around the lives, successes and failures of four friends who hail from lower middle class backgrounds. Gaurav, Manav, Pankaj and Loveleen become our companions, as they are the protagonists that drive us through the length of this film. Should we accept what life offers to us on a platter or should we try to chase our dreams, taking the path less taken? That's the question our protagonists seek answer to, as they decide to embark upon an eventful journey that will change the course of their lives and careers forever.
