The 2010 Frozen Four Official Championship Film

 The 2010 NCAA Men's Frozen Four was held at Ford Field in Detroit, Mich. The event hosted the largest-ever indoor crowd for ice hockey in North America. Four teams - Miami University, Boston College, Wisconsin and RIT squared off for a chance to win the national championship. Go inside the locker rooms, behind the scenes and onto the ice. Learn the extraordinary stories of four student athletes. And see how the Eagles soared to their third national title this decade.
