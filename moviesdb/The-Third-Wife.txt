The Third Wife

 An 1880's story of murder and revenge set in London's Chinatown East on the River Thames. Fei-Fei, the young bride of a rich and powerful Chinese merchant, fears for her life when she discovers that his former wives died under mysterious circumstances. Only her friendship with a young apothecary offers a safe haven from the alienation and paranoia that surrounds her. As events dramatically evolve in this ruthless community, Fei-Fei strikes out against those who have betrayed her.
