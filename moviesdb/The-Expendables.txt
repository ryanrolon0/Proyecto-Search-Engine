The Expendables

 Fresh out of the surf and sun-soaked world of the West Coast, 'The Expendables through seedy bars, insane backyard parties & major venues with Santa Cruz, California's rock, reggae and surf sensation,The Expendables. This feature-length documentary includes hilarious on-the-road footage of lunatic pranks with fans, friends and roadies running amok. Take a rare look at the group recording their album 'Getting Filthy' and discover what it is to dedicate one's life completely to a love of music and making it big.
