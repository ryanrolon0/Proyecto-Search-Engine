Sweetest Compassion

 The story of Gunther Palmer, the child of Ciao! Manhattan co-producer John Palmer and former Vogue and Elle covergirl Ivy Nicholson, who played a teen heartthrob on the French soap opera Apres tout ce qu'on a fait pour toi and played in a rockband called Mandrake, until his career and his life were disrupted when he began to exhibit signs of schizophrenia in his late teens.
