Radio pacs

 Sebastian lives together with his sister Francesca and her boyfriend Claudio. One day his ex boyfriend Ernesto knocks at the door. They had met each other during the war in Iraq. They had both miraculously survived, but then they had lost contacts. When Sebastian sees again the ex soldier, many memories come back to his mind bearing their burden of sorrow and death. Sebastian has never forgotten the horrors of the war and perhaps neither that love so passionate and so overwhelming...
