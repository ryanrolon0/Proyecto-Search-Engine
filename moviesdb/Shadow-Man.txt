Shadow Man

 'Shadow Man' is an ominous dark drama, about Lam, a South Vietnamese man, who leaves his wife and unborn child in the care of his brother, to join the Americans and fight for liberty and freedom. Confronted with horrific images of war and the moral betrayal of those he sought to support, he returns to his family five years later, only to discover that nothing feels like home anymore. Projecting his demons and shadows, his paranoia turns into jealousy and his loneliness into aggression, leading to tragic results...
