 Special Agent Lisa Forbes is on an unauthorized undercover mission to catch one of the world's most notorious criminals, REBEL. Lisa had a run in with Rebel in the past and was believed to be dead. Ten years later she has reconstructive surgery and comes back with a new face. With the aid of her best friend FBI intelligence person Hawk, Lisa is given a new name and Identity. Now as Jetta, she learns of Rebel's Plan to sell weapons of mass destruction. She is then faced with the task of brining him down alive. All the while trying to keep her cover from Rebel's two bodyguards Kida and Trish.
