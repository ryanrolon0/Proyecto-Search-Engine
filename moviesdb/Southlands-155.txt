Southlands 155

 Southlands 155 follows the story of Julian, along with his two mates, 'Robo' and Lochlan as they live their lives, chilling at the skate park, smoking weed and partying with friends. This carefree lifestyle however, will lead them towards a new circle and into the world of hard drugs. Southlands 155 is inspired by true events, and acts as a message to those who take recreation drugs.
