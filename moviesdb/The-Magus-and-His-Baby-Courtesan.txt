The Magus and His Baby Courtesan

 V. Alexander STEFAN talks about two characters in his fiction-fantasy novel Doctor Faustef Evangela was working in the Gentleman Club "Helen of Troy." Faustef, a physicist in search for human immortality, was in his mid-40s and Evila, as he named Evangela, in her early 20s. They have a long-term romantic relationship that continues after Faustef has achieved immortality. Evila is Faustef's most favorite mortal woman.
