The Beast

 Fishing and jobs are becoming scarce in a small seaport community when tragedy strikes a young couple disappear into the sea and nothing is left but a large claw stuck in their raft. A marine expert identifies it as belonging to an extremely rare giant squid and the hunt is on. Mysterious things happen at the coast of Graves Point at the shore, divers vanish. The sea biologist Dr. Talley thinks he knows the solution of the mystery
