Sinner Come Home

 In a forgotten, Middle American town, Eddie Farnim tolerates a mundane existence while his adoring wife, Jan, prays for a child. After working long hours, Eddie falls into frequent nights spent drinking with high school buddy, Jim, whose own marriage is in the gutter. As tensions rise at home, Eddie catches the eye of his boss's loose wife, Kathy. Cuing in on a conquest, Kathy feeds Eddie with bad advice and leads him deeper into questioning his future and marriage. Following a horrifying catharsis, Eddie faces the most difficult choice of his life.
