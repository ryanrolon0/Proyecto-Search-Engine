Valse Nostalgique

 The young cellist Alvaro has joined a mafia gang which has a strange custom play the victim's favorite songs before they kill them. Now, Alvaro finds himself in a catch 22, when his boss unwittingly brings in the cellist's girlfriend as the next victim. While her favorite song, 'Valse Nostalgique', triggers shared memories, Alvaro has to make a decision his girlfriend or step up against his brutally cold boss...
