Quemando suerte

 Marc is a sad man with a congenital disease that may finish his life at any time. During one of his multiple suicide attempts, a suitcase full of cocaine enters in his apartment through the window. Marc thinks that it is an unique opportunity to change his miserable life, so he will try to sell the drug entering in a dark world that he do not know, discovering that luck, like a coin, has two sides ... and one edge.
