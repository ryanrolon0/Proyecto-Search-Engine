The Gold Rush Boogie

 What happens to man who has been on top of the world with sex drugs and rock n roll then his life changes for the worse within a matter of a few days. One of the hottest disco / go-go clubs on Sunset Blvd. in 1979 The Gold Rush is owned by ex porn star Chester Long. Chester loves his women and everyone loves Chester. He has been at the top of his game able to talk his way out of anything and everything until now. He has gotten himself caught up in a gambling debt with an Italian Mafia and he can not repay in the debt in time. He is taken of his world and put into a new one. He is sent out on a mission to kill or be killed. He must now enter the world of the China-Man.
