The Finkel Files

 Josh Finkel wants to be a rock star, but his parents shipped him off to rabbi training school instead. Little Josh has fallen in with a bad crowd at Zitter-Millstein School for Boys as he faces constant temptation to infiltrate the Catholic girls' school across the lake. 15 year-old Josh Finkel wants to be a rock star, but his parents have sent him away to Rabbi training school, and now little Josh has fallen in with a BAD CROWD of Rabbis-to-be. Together, these seminary bandits raise hell around campus and try to escape to the forbidden Catholic Girls School across the lake.
