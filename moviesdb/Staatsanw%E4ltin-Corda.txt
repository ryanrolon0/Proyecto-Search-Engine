Staatsanwältin Corda

 The talented law student Corda has a severe traffic accident fled after an argument with her older boyfriend, who told her he's married. Now, 8 years later without having seen him, she's the first woman in town to become district attorney. However in her first case she's to sue against landlord Neidard, who looks exactly like her old flame. Old memories are awakened, and make her neglect her professional duties.
