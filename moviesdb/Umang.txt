Umang

 Umang an extremely brilliant student, but due his parents high ambitions was self lost. He wins the 'Child Scientist Award' . On his way back, he falls into deep valley where the tribal people had put up a net for the hunting purpose. Umang enjoys among the tribal people. He gives them lessons based on health, educations etc. With the help of scientific attitude he teaches a lesson to the Mantrik. Young Umang works for the health, cleanliness, education, eradication of superstitions, providing source of occupation, protection of environment and is honored by the government. He receives a 'National Child Bravery Award'
