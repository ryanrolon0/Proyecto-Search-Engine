Seulpeumeun ije geuman

 To her third grade classmates, Mi-na seems cheerful and studious. In reality, her homelife often has her in tears. She alone is responsible for the care of her infant brother and invalid father. Lacking money for his medicine, she occassionaly must skip school to search the mountains for herbs to ease his pain. When the new teacher finds out, she devises a plan to help Mi-na's life improve
