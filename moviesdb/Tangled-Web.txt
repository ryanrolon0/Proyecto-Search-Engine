Tangled Web

 Part One Web is a filmed play about cyberspace and safety. The Goddess Athena and the spider, Arachne, try to prevent humanity from weaving a tangled Internet web. It was written with students over a four year period; conceived of and directed by York Region Drama teacher, Miriam Guttman; performed by students from Westmount Collegiate Institute, York Region District School Board, Ontario, Canada. Part Two Web; 2004-2008. The original cast and writing contributors chronicle their journey.
