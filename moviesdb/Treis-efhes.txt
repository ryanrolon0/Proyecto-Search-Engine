Treis efhes

 Three friends are out getting drunk. At the bar, one of them meets a beautiful girl. While the three friends are driving on their way back to their village, they hit a man who was walking in the middle of a dark street. Panicked, they wrap the body in a plastic bag and dump it into a nearby lake, hoping that it would erase the event from their lives. Their relationship suffers a major blow as the guilt and fear for their wrongdoing dooms their feelings for each other. Their friendship dies and they stop seeing each other, until the day that that very girl that one of them had met at that bar that night, returns to their lives...
