The Legend of the Arrow

 Following the making of the mini-series The Arrow, this documentary provides a captivating glimpse into the magical world of movie making. Join a team of top filmmakers as they endeavor to retell the infamous story of a spectacular but doomed project. Viewers meet the film's stars and explore the painstaking and dramatic process of recreating the world of aeronautics in the 50s, including the process of reproducing the spectacular fighter-interceptor.
