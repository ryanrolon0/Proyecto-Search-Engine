Scavenger Hunt

 As graduation approaches, the seniors of Black Ridge High prepare to kick off their farewell weekend by reviving an old town tradition. What starts as a harmless game quickly turns to a last chance at bloody revenge. These seniors are about to be taught a lesson about reviving the past as a long forgotten mass murderer returns to finish what he started nearly thirty years before. Witness the FUN...the MAYHEM...and the BLOODSHED when you encounter the SCAVENGER HUNT.
