The Nights of Blue

 'Nights of Blue' explores the dark and gritty world of two outcasts of society, both living in Prague. One, an American hustler called BLU; the other a Czech stripper named LENKA. Together they search for the impossible; love in the midst of the sex business. Both BLU and LENKA yearn for an escape from their bleak existence. In each other they search for their dreams and question whether or not these dreams could ever become a reality...
