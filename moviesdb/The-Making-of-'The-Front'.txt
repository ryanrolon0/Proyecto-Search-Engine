The Making of 'The Front'

 This documentary treats movie fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of The Front, a satire about a cashier who hires out his name as a way for blacklisted writers to get their work published. Included are interviews with stars Woody Allen and Zero Mostel, along with the rest of the cast and crew who share their experiences from making the film, as well as disscuss the efforts that went into it.
