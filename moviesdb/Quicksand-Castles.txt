Quicksand Castles

 Pete, a third-grade teacher, gets a call from his best friend who asks him to help bury a body. Pete decides to help, but when the police get involved things start to spiral out of control - his friend is accused of murder, the victim's baby goes missing and the morgue starts filling up with dead guys. Unsure whether it's all related, Pete struggles between saving his friendship and helping the police catch a killer.
