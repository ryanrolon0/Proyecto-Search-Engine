Siberian Hunks

 A journey to Siberia dealt with before he and his buddies can party. Sergey gives Sasha a hands-on lesson. Once the studies are over, Slava and Dimka arrive frat-boy high jinks rule here, with junk food, male competition and macho posturing, until somehow, all inhibitions and clothing are tossed aside. As their nude antics become more physical, youthful testosterone inevitably kicks in. Suddenly, the simple party becomes a pressure cooker of emotions. Boyish horseplay now leads to pairings - fueled by tender kisses and sensual caresses.
