Vendetta

 Released from prison after a 30-year stretch for a brutal murder, Dennis isn't your typical middle-aged ex-con. He may be a free man, but he's being watched by someone even more dangerous than himself. London is a different city from the place Dennis left behind. The people, the places - everything has changed. Even his home is not his own any more. But some things remain the same Starved of female company for three decades, can Dennis be trusted - or will the violent killer who lurks within him reappear during his next encounter? What Dennis doesn't know is that a menacing stranger with a score to settle is following him. Exhausted and twisted with grief, Davey is convinced there is only one way to finally find the peace he so desperately craves death of his sweetheart. While Davey plots his revenge, Dennis plans a meeting with Vicki cold and sleazy sex industry. Will Vicki become Dennis' latest victim? Or will Dennis become Davey's first?
