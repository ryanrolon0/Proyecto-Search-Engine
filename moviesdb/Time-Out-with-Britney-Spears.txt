Time Out with Britney Spears

 Britney Spears, pop singer and actress, recounts her life, from her early years in Kentwood, Louisiana, her work off-Broadway, as a cast member on the popular Mickey Mouse Club, and finally as a multi-platinum selling vocalist. The program is highlighted with her music video clips "Baby...One More Time," "Sometimes," and "(You Drive Me) Crazy," as well as live performances.
