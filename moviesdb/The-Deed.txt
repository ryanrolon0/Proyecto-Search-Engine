The Deed

 Chelsea has always marched to the beat of her own drummer. She lives in a world where it seems as if everyone else is on the fast track to landing the cover of Maxim Magazine. Who cares if she is still a virgin...right? Everything she has ever known changes when the new guy walks into study hall one day. Join Chelsea in this hilarious comedy as she tries to land the man of her dreams. Good thing she has the help of her ' best friend ' Veronica to guide her.
