The Incredible Happenings of Avery Goodman

 Dig Dug Industries specializes in the removal of earth from the ground in order to replenish that earth to the location of its original location. Avery Goodman is Senior Manager of the Mathmatical Tracking of Dirt Extraction. That is until the day Avery has a minor mishap in the counting of shovelfuls. Thus begins the story of Avery Goodman and an incredible happening that starts an incredible journey that makes Avery Goodman a legend in his own time.
