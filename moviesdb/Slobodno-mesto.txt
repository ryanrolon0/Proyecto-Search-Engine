Slobodno mesto

 A young man loses his great love. He locks himself up into the world where they lived together till her death separated them. He suffers surrounded with the things that remind him of her. She, who appears as a ghost in his "self-solitary cell", feels all his suffer, but he does not see or feel her presence. The time passes by...A mutual friend of theirs tries all the time to reach and help him. He refuses her help wanting to be faithful to his memory. The ghost-girl feels his pain increase with the time and decides to do something. The young man decides at one moment that he must go on and invites their mutual friend to his place. The ghost girl separates the two. They both realise that she cannot replace her, that their love with tragic end is endless and strong enough to survive. In a strong emotional finale the ghost-girl leaves the spot and a free place to her friend...
