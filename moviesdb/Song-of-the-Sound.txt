Song of the Sound

 For Libby Bass, an English expert in whale and dolphin communication, the job in New Zealand seems perfect. On arrival she meets American John-Cody Gibbs, an inspirational and intriguing man. Unbeknown to her or anyone around them he is hiding a devastating secret; one which threatens to destroy many lives.
