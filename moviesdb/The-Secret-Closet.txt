The Secret Closet

 This story takes place in 1984 where you meet recent graduates, Kerry Johnson, a superstar athlete and his long time girlfriend, Krystal Owens. During a summer vacation to his small hometown, Kerry discovers he's contracted a mysterious disease. Upset and confused, Kerry can't wrap his mind around the news of his health. Soon Kerry and Krystal both discover a secret that will shake them both to the core.
