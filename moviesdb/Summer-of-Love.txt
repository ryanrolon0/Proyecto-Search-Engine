Summer of Love

 A young couple share a summer romance / trio with a visiting American artist. A story about love, relationships, nature, art, death and the meaning of life set in a beautiful scenery. After the death of her father a young woman retreats to the coast with her boyfriend, only to find herself longing for sexual exploration with another man that soon leads to more... It tells of a couple who return to the Fleurieu Peninsula from Sydney after the death of the young women's father. They become involved with an artist who is inspired by the coastal sands and colourful rock faces of the South. The confronting and challenging story of Amanda and her emotional journey of discovery. The risquE film focuses on Amanda, who arrives at Maslin Beach from Sydney to join her boyfriend Tom for the summer holidays. However, their relationship takes an unusual twist when they become intimate friends with the hunky American artist Michael. Immersed in love for her boyfriend but passionate for the new man, Amanda explores boundaries of sensuality with her lovers. But the arrival of male hungry blonde Briana threatens to upset the cozy holiday threesome. A young couple experience problems after the death of her father; she decides she wants to try something a little bit different, and ends up falling in love with two men, and the three end up living together in a bizarre but comfortable threesome that can never end happily ever after.
