Spies in Crinoline

 Civil War docudrama on Confederate female spies in Union-occupied Northern Virginia, focusing on Antonia Ford, jailed for her role in partisan ranger John Singleton Mosby's celebrated raid on Fairfax Courthouse and capture of Brigadier General Edwin Stoughton. Northern Virginia Confederate spy Antonia Ford is imprisoned for her supposed role in enabling Rebel raider John Singleton Mosby's famed Fairfax Courthouse raid, in which he captured Brigadier General Edwin Stoughton. She falls in love with Union Major Joseph Willard, who escorts her to prison. Will their love stand the strain of their being on different sides of the War? The situation is further complicated by the fact that Willard, separated from his wife, is not yet divorced. He secures his divorce and proposes but asked Antonia that she give up the Confederate cause. She agrees to give up spying provided that he resigns his Army commission and return to civilian life.
