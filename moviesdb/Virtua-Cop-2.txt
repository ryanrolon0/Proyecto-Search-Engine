Virtua Cop 2

 Virtua City is in trouble, and Detective Janet Marshall, has joined up with the VCPD Special Investigations Unit to stop all criminals, robbers and hijackers in sight. So, it's up to the Virtua City Police's best officers, Michael "Rage", James "Smarty", and Janet as well to avenge a murder, as they find Joe Fang, and bring justice back to Virtua City. A criminal organization has taken over Virtua City, and there are multiple robbers and hijackers where ever you go. So, the Virtua City Police's best officers, Rage, Smarty, and Janet, are now out on a mission to stop all criminals in sight.
