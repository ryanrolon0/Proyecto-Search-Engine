Recoil

 "RECOIL" follows the life of Trey Briggs, a cop and die-hard ladies man who is having way too much fun. That is, until Angelo Thomas, a dangerous cold-blooded killer from his past shows up and goes after Trey's girlfriend for revenge. Suddenly Trey, his brother, Ken and best friend, Ritchie are daunted with the impossible task of doing what no one else has done before. Finding Angelo, who's considered the devil himself and stopping him before it's too late.
