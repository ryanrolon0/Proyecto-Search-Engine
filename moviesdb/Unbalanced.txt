Unbalanced

 Lorraine's little girl Amy is kidnapped in plain sight from a public park. She desperately tries to find her but she is unable to do so. The police also come up empty. Months later, Lorraine is starting to lose her mind. She begins hearing and seeing her daughter everywhere and seems to be going crazy - or is she? Eventually, she teams up with a reporter that wants to help her as we (but not they) finally learn the shocking whereabouts of her missing child...
