Prime of Life

 Prime of Life is the story of Jacob and Grace Warner, an elderly couple counting down the minutes until the Lord to takes them away. Until one day, Jacob decides to live his last moments his way. Jacob disconnects himself from his breathing mask and asks his wife she's going to wait. He says he's going to live. He writes a letter and as he passes, the letter disappears in a plume of light. At Jacob's wake, Grace has the letter. It reads to stand up and leave this world to be with her husband.
