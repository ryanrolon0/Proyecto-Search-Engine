The Adventures of Umbweki

 A young African tribesman, named Umbweki leaves his small village and travels eight years to Hollywood to be a star. He has many a hilarious adventure and meets interesting characters along the way. A slap stick comedy about a young African tribesman named Umbweki, who leaves his tribe to pursue his life long dream of becoming a Hollywood star. An eight year adventure that includes many Hollywood spoofs, stereotypes and hilarious encounters along the way.
