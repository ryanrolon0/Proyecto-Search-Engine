The 9th Entry

 Tyler Rikens is giving his last Video Journal Entry. He sets up his camcorder for the 9th time. Tyler tries explaining how his old home made him sick. How he left. How the sickness followed him. How it soon infected his family. How he has exhausted all his options... How... he has given up hope... How all he left... Is 'The 9th Entry' and a gun.
