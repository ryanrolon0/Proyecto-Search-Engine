The Ascension Agency

 Richard is a loner on the edge. Traumatic events from his childhood haunt his dreams and everyday life, his job as a night security guard and worst of all, his sanity. As his violent flashbacks intensify, who knows what he is capable of? Out of desperation he approaches the mysterious Ascension Agency, his last hope of salvation. But is this agency for real or is it part of Richard's imagination? Either way, can they save him before his life spirals dangerously out of control.
