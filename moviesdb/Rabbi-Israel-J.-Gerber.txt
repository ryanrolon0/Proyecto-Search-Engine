Rabbi Israel J. Gerber

 The life of Rabbi Israel J. Gerber is truly a remarkable journey. From an early age, he had a vision of becoming a Rabbi and influencing people's lives. As a teenager, he had a career in boxing before committing full time to the rabbinate. Rabbi Gerber was a chaplain in World World War II, wrote 5 published books, had 4 congregations, and has a family he is truly proud of.
