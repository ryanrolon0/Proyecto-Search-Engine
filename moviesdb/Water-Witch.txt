Water Witch

 "Water Witch" tells the story of Mary Delver, a 90 year old woman who has been divining water, finding lost objects, and healing others in rural Saskatchewan for over 60 years. Mary has honed a God-given gift of well-witching into a mysterious art that she practices in a variety of ways, using everything from eggs to car aerials in her ongoing search for the substance vital to farmers of her community
