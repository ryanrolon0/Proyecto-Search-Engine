She Was an Acrobat's Daughter

 An evening at the local movie theater, including a sing-along led by Maestro Stickoutski at he Mighty "Fertilizer" organ, a "Goofy-Tone" newsreel, and the feature, "Petrified Florist," a spoof of 'The Petrified Forest (1936)' (qv) featuring caricatures of 'Bette Davis' (qv) and 'Leslie Howard' (qv).
