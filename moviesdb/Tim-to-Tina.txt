Tim to Tina

 Tim to Tina tells the heartwarming story of Tim's journey to becoming Tina. She describes her life from the very beginning and the struggle she had to be 'normal' when she was younger. With interviews from her daughter-in-law, son and friends, her life changing decision to become Tina is described in this short eight and a half minute documentary. Tina charismatically tells anecdotes from her past in humorous and emotional ways, tying together all the sections of her life that she has left behind. The documentary looks at the physical and emotional process she went through, and asks Tina a very important question
