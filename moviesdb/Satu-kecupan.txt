 Mocked by her friends for having virgin lips, cornered in the radio talk show that she hosts herself, Desi then encourages herself to get a real kiss from Ardi, her very conservative boyfriend. A weekend at the beach is her ultimate chance. Her naughty and mischievous friends suggest some nasty ways to get what Desi wants. All just lead to chaos that break the two lovebirds apart. Why a simple smooch can cause that much of a catastrophe?
