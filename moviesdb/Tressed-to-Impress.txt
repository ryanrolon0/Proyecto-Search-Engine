Tressed to Impress

 Glamour Magazine and Herbal Essence are coming to together to give three lucky girls a week of a lifetime in Los Angeles, CA. While in LA, these girls will be living the high life...staying in luxurious accommodations, eating at the hippest restaurants, chauffeured around in a luxury vehicle, pampered at the top spas, and experiencing LA's one of a kind nightlife. It won't be all fun and games though. Along the way, the girls will meet with experts from different fields to learn what it takes to be an Herbal Essence / Glamour girl. We will see them sweat as they compete in challenges that test their mental and physical strength...While all girls will try their best, only one can be crowned the winner and given a grand prize of a designer shopping spree and a six page spread in Glamour magazine.
