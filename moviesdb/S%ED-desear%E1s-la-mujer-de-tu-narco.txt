Sí desearás la mujer de tu narco

 An ordinary couple living in a country house work in the business of exotic plant growing. But that is a cover, the real business of this husband and wife is experimentation of homemade cocaine plants. Of course the couple work for the mafia. Time to time, a supervisor of the mob spends a few weeks with the couple checking their work. The las supervisor falls in love to the lady and a deadly love triangle begins.
