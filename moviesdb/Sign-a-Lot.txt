Sign-a-Lot

 Join our playful See me Sign Kids on their signing adventures as they travel to the magical land of Sign-A-Lot, where everything is hands on! Your child will have fun playing and signing along with their new Sign-A-Lot friend Lee, the See Me Sign Kids, and their mischievous animated pals, Lulu Lefty and Ronnie Righty. In "The Big Surprise," your child will learn over 80 signs while visiting Picnic Park, Exercise Alley, Field of Feelings, the Opposite Playground, and Music Meadow. Get your imaginations and hands ready to learn to Sign A Lot!
