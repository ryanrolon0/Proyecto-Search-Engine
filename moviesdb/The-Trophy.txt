The Trophy

 FOUR MEN- ONE WOMAN. Only One Can Claim The Ultimate Prize. PITCH Food Chain of a Man's Life's Accomplishments, Power, Money and Fame all take a back seat to the Beautiful Woman. Men die for them, go broke for them and kill for them. They represent Man's Greatest Treasure and Greatest Weakness. In a society where money, fame and plastic surgery take center stage, Angela LeBlanc shows that there are women out there who still seek love first.
