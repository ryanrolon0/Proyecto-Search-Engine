Stop at the Greenlight

 Joyce pays 25 grand to Bruce for a marriage of convenience so she could pursuit her cellist career in New York and Bruce will move to Hollywood to be the next Bruce Lee. But the sudden visit of an immigration officer puts them in chaos with no "rehearsals". Then Bruce creates an imaginary scene of "Seven Daffodils" he learned from acting class and it has everyone falls for it. Is it method acting, or is it for real? And what about that magical 25 grand?
