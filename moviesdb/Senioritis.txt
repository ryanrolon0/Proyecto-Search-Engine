Senioritis

 Made over the summer of 2010 with no budget and borrowed equipment, the film follows the journey of one student through his high school senior year where he chases after girls, avoids homework and struggles through college applications. Told without dialogue and using trick editing, the film shows each day in the school year with seamless transitions from day to day. Featuring all original music by student bands ands composers and great performances from student actors as well.
