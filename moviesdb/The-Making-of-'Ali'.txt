The Making of 'Ali'

 This made-for-TV documentary details the work that went into the production of Ali, Michael Mann's 2001 biography about the greatest boxer of all time, Muhammad Ali. Film fans are treated to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of this glimpse into the life and troubles of the legendary sports figure, as well as to interviews with the director and cast members, who give their insights into the efforts that were needed to bring this project to completion.
