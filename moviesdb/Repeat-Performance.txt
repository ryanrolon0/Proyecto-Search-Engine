Repeat Performance

 Sheila Page, a Broadway star, shoots Barney, her murderous husband on New Year's Eve. She flees her apartment and goes to her Producer, John Friday. When she arrives, it is New Year's day, a year earlier. She has been given the chance to live life over and correct the errors of the past only to find that the end will be the same although the path will be different. Actress Sheila Page murders her two-timing boyfriend and goes to her friend John Friday for help. He gives it to her in the form of granting her a chance to relive the events prior to the shooting. Will the same thing happen again?
