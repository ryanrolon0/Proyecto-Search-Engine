Soñando silencio

 Lay is a young woman who belongs to a terrible terrorist group. After the death of Winter, his unique and great love, Lay decides to isolate of the world in a small village of sea. In your new destination she will be accompanied at all time by The Contact, your unique link on the real world. One night, in the middle of the rain, Lay will know Dante, a writer wounded by love since it was losing his pair. Lay and Dante will live through a strange and ambiguous relation ... but Lay knows that a past like her never disappears.
