Seouliyeo annyeong

 Sung-il is a man living in Japan. There he falls in love with Yu Mi, but her parents object to their relationship based on Sung-il's Korean nationality. On a business trip to Seoul, Sung-il meets and falls passionately in love with Mun Hie. By the time Yu Mi comes to Korea to find her lover, he is already married and has fathered a child.
