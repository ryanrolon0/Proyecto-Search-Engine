The Fire Chief's Bride

 Alfred adores the fire chief's daughter, but the chief objects. Alfred hides in the closet and when the chief discovers him, he comes out disguised as a woman and tells the chief 'she' has long loved him. The chief falls for the 'beautiful woman' and phones for the minister. A fire breaks out and the chief leaves for the fire. The minister arrives and the young folks induce him to marry them. After many ludicrous happenings, the chief gets the surprise of his life.
