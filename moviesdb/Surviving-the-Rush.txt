Surviving the Rush

 It's the hottest day of the year for the manager of the tiny one-screen movie theatre the Nostromo but that's only the beginning of his problems. The year's most hotly anticipated film is opening exclusively without warning plunging the Manager and his employees into a frantically comedic world of foul-mouthed nuns, angry rooftop wildlife, and a capacity crowd of eager moviegoers that quickly turns into a violent mob.
