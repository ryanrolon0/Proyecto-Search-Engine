Riffed

 Spencer, Mitsuko, Sidney, Justine and Ben will do it all, even turn on each other, to save their mind-numbing jobs at Global Corp., as Mr. Hackman, A Re-Engineering Facilitator for the corporate giant, makes them jump through the hoops of his twisted logic, in the pursuit of efficiency. That is, until the five bottom-rung employees are pushed too way, way too far. Even for them.
