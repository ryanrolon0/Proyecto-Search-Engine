Pram Factory

 This documentary is about the Pram Factory, an radical alternative theatre group based in Melbourne in the 1970's. Through the use of archival material and interviews with the participants, it traces it's development and demise. This group gave rise to many of Australias most successful actors, writers and directors, including Max Gillies, Bruce Spence, Graeme Blundell and John Duigan, as well as the legendary Circus Oz.
