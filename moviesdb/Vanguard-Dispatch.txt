Vanguard Dispatch

 Behind enemy lines, in a ship past its prime, the crew of the USNS JAMES GREGORY has to scout planetary systems in search of the dreaded Chu'a - sworn enemies of humanity. Their only ties to the outside are the letters they send home. From seeing the hottest celebrity, to stories about daring last-minute survival, these letters give a slice of life in the United Galactic Space Navy in a war where the stakes are the highest in the history of mankind.
