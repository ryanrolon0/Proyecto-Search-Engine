The Minority

 The nicest guy in the world, gets pushed to the brink of insanity. Working for a credit card company, honest, law-abiding Jake Jackson is accused of stealing a co-worker purse and fired. Confronted with many prejudices against minority citizens, Jake struggles regain his normal life but continues to fall prey. Not until he single-handedly captures David Fletcher, a wanted serial killer, does Jake find his well deserved respect.
