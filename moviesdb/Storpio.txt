Storpio

 A 55 year old artist bound to a wheelchair struggles with the difficulty of daily life in an Italian seaside village. Reminded daily by the sea that has taken love from him he pulls himself and his wheelchair up flights of stairs as a daily ritual to procure his artistic supplies. Returning back down to the sea where he spends his lonely days painting. He is witness to the miracle of life as he saves a young boy from the fate of his lost love. This is the story of Storpio.
