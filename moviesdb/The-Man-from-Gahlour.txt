The Man from Gahlour

 The film - The Man from Gahour - is about Dashrath Manhji who single handedly carved 360 feet long and 30 feet wide road from a hill by using hammer, chisel and shovel. This incident took place in Gahlour village in Gaya District in the state of Bihar in the year 1962. Dashrath took 22 long years to finish this arduous task.
