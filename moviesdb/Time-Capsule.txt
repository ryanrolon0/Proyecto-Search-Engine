Time Capsule

 In the year 2006, five top students of the prestigious Benton Academy created a senior final project for their schools centennial time capsule ceremony. Using blackmail they coerce the ceremonies director to bury the video taped project without being viewed. They plan a night of uninhibited fun and rebellion. But when a horrible event is caught on camera, the young men have to make a decision that could destroy their futures and uncover a haunting past...
