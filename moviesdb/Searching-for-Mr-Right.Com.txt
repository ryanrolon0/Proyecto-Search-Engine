Searching for Mr Right.Com

 Love is a disaster for Marie and her best friend Liz. Simon is desperately trying to save his Internet personals business. After chance brings them together they create, 'Searching for Mr Right.com," a web site to locate husbands for Marie and Liz. Simon sets the on-line countdown for a Valentine's Day wedding in six months. They discover a little bit about love, dating and each other as the search through the thousands of hopefuls. The double wedding on Valentine's Day arrives in a flurry of yelling, confessions and proposals. Now all they have to do is pick the right guy.
