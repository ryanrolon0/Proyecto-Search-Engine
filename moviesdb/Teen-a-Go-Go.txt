Teen a Go Go

 The 1960's have long been represented as a decade of chaotic change and tumultuous growth hippies, yippies, sex, drugs and of course, rock and roll. Teen-A-Go-Go captures and examines this important time in the development, history and evolution of Rock music. Teen-A-Go-Go will explore the musical influence of the British invasion in Fort Worth and Dallas with these two vibrant music scenes serving as a microcosm for this evolutionary stage of rock music across America.
