The Land Pirates

 The Land Pirates are small group of guys who do all their pillaging, raping, and general pirating on Land only (They hate water!). In this episode, Land Pirate Whitey Brownbeard must go through the Land Pirate ritual of 'The Drinking of the Cups' before they can okay his upcoming marriage to Maureen. Meanwhile, Maureen's friends are scheming to prevent the wedding from happening.
