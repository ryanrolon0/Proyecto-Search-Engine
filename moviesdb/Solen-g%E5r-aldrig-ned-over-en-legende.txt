Solen går aldrig ned over en legende

 Elvis is dead, and yet.. The King lives in the heart of thousands of fans. They travel to his grave in his hometown of Memphis. This documentary celebrates the 15th anniversary of Elvis' departure from our dimension, bringing interviews with fans from all over the world, and with Vester Presley, the man who discovered Elvis in 1954, with his Elvis' bodyguards, and many others.
