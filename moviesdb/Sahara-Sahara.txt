Sahara Sahara

 In the face of a global shift, Sahara Sahara is is a pre-apocalyptic myth surrounding an organized group of vigilantes that want to put an end to Big Oil and change modern society. Urbanites calling themselves Sahara Sahara become host to one of the most publicized and controversial cases of violent activism to hit modern day North America. At the epicenter of the scandal stands none other than Sahara herself. Revered as a revolutionary hero and symbol of strength, Sahara is accused on multiple charges of vandalism, disruption and theft. With a growing following, the gang take over unsustainable industries and shut them down with their innovative mesmerizing tricks. But their efforts are soon thwarted by private officials, who have no choice but to order bounty hunters to put an end to the gang of misfits.
