Slice

 Pete, a Pizza Delivery boy for Paolo's Pizza thinks his job has no meaning. What he doesn't realize is that across town the local Yakuza Crime Boss has kidnapped the beautiful Asuka in order to punish her boyfriend Oshoba! He gives Oshoba only 20 minutes or less to satisfy him or Asuka dies! Oshoba's luck changes however when his ninja assassin ex-girlfriend Neegu shows up and informs him that The Boss has a penchant for Paolo's Pizza! But that's nothing compared to the torch she still carries for him! Can Pete get there on time? And more importantly will he get a tip?!
