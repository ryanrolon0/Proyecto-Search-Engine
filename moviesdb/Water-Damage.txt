Water Damage

 Kate Johnson is the practically the definition of perfection. She has the best boyfriend, the hottest friends and she is the most popular girl in high school. But her world is sent for a spin when all that she knows is lost. During a morning swim on one of the most important days of Kate's life, tragedy strikes! Kate gets wet and is unable to dry off. Her newfound flaw has a drastic effect on her life and all that she loves begins to unravel.
