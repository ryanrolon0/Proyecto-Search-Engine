Stretti al vento - Storie di navigazioni in solitario

 Little-huge human odysseys told by the most important Italian solitary sailors, stars of the national and international maritime scene, with different ages, sex, ideas and navigation aims. Eclectic personalities, out of the ordinary, that throughout their stories bring back sensations, sorrows, adventures and misadventures at sea on board of their sailboats, continuously fighting against themselves and against everything that surrounds them, the infinitive Ocean, counting only on their strengths and on nature mercy.
