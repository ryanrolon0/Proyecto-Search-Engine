Psychosis

 A depressed teenage boy named Cody finds strange things beginning to happen around him, and to his delight, realizes that reality is collapsing. Meanwhile, faraway, a young girl obsessed with drawing in her sketchbook stops taking her medication. Through the ensuing chaos, Cody meets a girl who finds similar joy in the anarchy and the two go through the changes together. An innocent romance blooms amidst the end of civilization.
