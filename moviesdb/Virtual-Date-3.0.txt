Virtual Date 3.0

 Two losers - a video-game enthusiast and a guy building a tower of playing cards - are interrupted by the arrival of a third friend who has a new virtual reality game, Video Date 3.0. The video-game enthusiast puts on the gloves and helmet first, and he chooses to take his date on a picnic. A few minutes into his fantasy, the card tower builder grabs the equipment and takes his fantasy dancing. Then their friend's interrupts, and he takes his fantasy date to the love hotel. Can anything go wrong with his virtual trip?
