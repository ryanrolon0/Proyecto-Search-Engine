Star of the Orient

 Year work as a photographer. After a frustrating attempt to make a living as a legitimate photographer, a friend suggests she contact a certain individual who handles erotic photographs and movies. In the process, she meets another female photographer with similar needs, and the two pool their talents as well as their emotions. Together, work and sexual desires intermingle as these two shutterbugs embark on an erotic path with the most beautiful and willing young people who enjoy performing for the camera.
