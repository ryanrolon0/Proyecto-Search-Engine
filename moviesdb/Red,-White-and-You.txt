Red, White and You

 Keith Kenworthy is a man on the move. He plays golf with your father, sits next to your mother at PTA meetings, and knows how many points your son scored in last week's high school basketball game. He's everything to everyone - he's your local Marine Corps Recruiter. However, Kenworthy is different. He's the best in U.S. history. He's recruited so many young men and women the military couldn't help but create a new award just for him, the Spirit Badge. With newfound fame and wartime publicity an ex lover traces Kenworthy down through an article in the local paper and throws him a curve ball
