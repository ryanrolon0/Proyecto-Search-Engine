Süpermenler

 The German professor tries out his time machine in Turkey. Everybody is after this new invention - Superman Detective Murat, his cheating colleagues Atak and Matrak as well as the mob leader. Agatha, the daughter of the mob leader, steals heroin with her father's man. The mob leader wants to detect their place by the time machine. Murat, Matrak and Atak struggle for the machine, but the machine blows up, the Professor loses his memory. Atak and Matrak kidnap him. The professor repairs the machine and writes the formula down, the machine is forwarded to the gangster's ship but the three supermen catch them. Everything is back to order and the future of the world is safe now.
