Train to Win

 Geared toward kids 12 and up, Gretzky and Howie Wenger, physiologist for Team Canada and the New York Rangers, show how to implement various exercises for an off-season training program. Topics include weight training for driving opponents off the puck, in-line skating for better puck handling, increasing foot speed, improving coordination, aerobic conditioning and shortening recovery time and eating before the game and throughout the season. Contains Gretzky career highlights.
