The Wreck Hunters

 This is the fascinating tale of a group of wreck diving friends who set sail for a wreck diving trip on board the dive boat "Mon Cherie" that will will take them for an unforgettable voyage to a location south of Block Island, Rhode Island. Come and join these divers for a unique look at the always dangerous and sometimes deadly world of the deep ocean wreck diver. It is here in one hundred sixty feet of cold, dark, and grimly inhospitable water that they will descend to the wreck of the submarine USS Bass. Share the humor and the danger as this group of extraordinary individuals make the exiting dive to this historic shipwreck. The USS Bass was launched in 1924 and enjoyed a long career in the Navy before it was finally sunk as a target in a mine test in 1945. At three hundred and forty two feet in length, this submarine was the largest non-nuclear boat ever constructed by the US Navy. Join the Wreck Hunters for real life high adventure that is as good as it gets!
