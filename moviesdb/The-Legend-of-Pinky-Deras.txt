The Legend of Pinky Deras

 A look at the amazing career of Art "Pinky" Deras, the greatest Little League Baseball player of all time. He led his Hamtramck (Mich.) team to the 1959 Little League World Championship and the 1961 Pony League World Championship. He spent five years in the St. Louis Cardinals organization before working as a police officer for 30 years in Warren, Mich.
