The Confession of Lee Harvey Oswald

 A startling, suspenseful take on the most famous killer in American History, The Confession of Lee Harvey Oswald looks deeply into the mind and soul of the man who changed the course of history singlehandedly. Or did he? Taking no conspiracy theory - or accepted fact - for granted, this film looks at those three fateful days in Dallas and reveals something startlingly new. And eerily possible.
