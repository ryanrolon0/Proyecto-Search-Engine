Warplanes

 This documentary serves as an overview of combat aircraft from World War I to the present. In-flight and airshow footage of these warbirds are included B-17 Flying Fortress, B-25 Mitchell, B-24 Liberator, B-29 Superfortress, F-4-U Corsair, AD-4 Skyraider, T-11 Vampire, MiG-15, F-86 Sabre, F-4 Phantom, F-15 Eagle, A-10 Thunderbolt II, F-16 Fighting Falcon, AV-8 Harrier, B1-B Lancer, and the F/A-18 Hornet.
