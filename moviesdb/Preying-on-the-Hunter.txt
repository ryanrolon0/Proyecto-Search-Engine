Preying on the Hunter

 Preying on the Hunter is a dark tale of the revenge, deceit and greed found in one woman's struggle to achieve her lifelong dream. Nila Barnes, legal secretary and aspiring author, is tormented daily by her incompetent, arrogant young boss Hunter Turnbill who has something to hide. When Nila discovers his secret, she enlists the help of her friends to carry out a nefarious plan to finally bring Hunter down. But things heat up when Clay Dresden, an attractive young private investigator digs too deep and gets too close, Nila realizes she might lose it all.
