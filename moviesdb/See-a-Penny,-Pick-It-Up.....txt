See a Penny, Pick It Up....

 Times have changed, gone are the days when a penny saved was a penny earned. It seems that penny have became worthless. Some people pass them by without even a second glance. If you look closely at a penny, on the backside you will read, 'United States of America, One cent" and on the front you will see, "Liberty" and "In God we trust.' A penny is dropped, as it rolls along the bustling streets of down town Los Angeles, it find a homeless women. From the moment she picks it up, good luck seems to be on her side. Penny for your thoughts, would you stop to pick up a penny, is it worth your time? Could this be your lucky day?
