Sweet Alice

 A lovable cowboy arrives in Hollywood in search of his runaway wife. He's aided by a beautiful but no-nonsense detective who finds out that the wife has become an X-rated film star. Their quest leads them to some of the biggest box office names in adult films and into the midst of a climatic x-rated film shoot where Alice is center stage in a revealing orgy. Sweet Alice is no longer sweet. Sweet Alice combines the magic formula of a bittersweet love story, topnotch acting, high production values and a lush musical score.
