The Making of 'Showgirls'

 This made-for-TV documentary provides movies fans with an in-depth, behind-the-scenes look at the making of Showgirls, about a young woman hoping to get her big break working as a nude dancer in Las Vegas. The documentary includes interviews with the film's cast and crew, who who share their experiences of working on the set and discuss the efforts that went into bringing the project to completion.
