Ravelo

 'RAVELO' is the true story of professional boxer Jerson Ravelo. They story follows Jerson through a series of successes and struggles as he punches his way towards becoming a champion. From the harsh streets of Newark New Jersey to the plush paradise of Grand Caymen Island for a final epic battle. The people are real, the drama is real, and more importantly the story is real. This is boxing. This is 'RAVELO.'
