Power Poison

 An orphan, Ike, is saved and trained by sophisticated money launderers after losing his family. Ike soon realizes he may be entrusted to helm the organization just when his boss and mentor, Victor, starts a very lucrative transaction with alleged government officials. Meanwhile, terrorists hire one of Victor's middlemen to talk him into moving a biological weapon across the Mexican border. It's in this Gordian knot of shocking bullet-fests that Ike uncovers things aren't what they seem and comes to face his fatal choice.
