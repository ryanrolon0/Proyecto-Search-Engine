The Best of

 A documentary about the legendary Blacks In Action TV, which was the first TV show to interview Notorious B.I.G., Jay-Z, 50 Cent, Diddy & Craig Mack before they were famous. The documentary features stories from members of the cast, plus, classic interviews from celebrities such as Notorious B.I.G., Jay-Z, 50 Cent, Diddy & Craig Mack, Wyclef Jean, actress Charnele Brown(from 'A Different World'), O.D.B., Wu-Tang Clan, Chuck D, Afrika BamBaataa & Zulu Nation, Kool Herc and many more.
