The Hobo Killer!

 In The Second Of The J.P. Ritter Series, He Takes On The Story Of David Nesmith (Donald Morgan, Dead In Love), A Man Who, After Being Vilified By The Press, Has Been Convicted Of Killing Thousands Of Homeless People, Earning Him The Less-Than-Flattering Nickname, "The Hobo Killer." In Addition To Speaking With David Himself (In Prison), J.P. Brings Us The Story Of David's Life Through Reenactments (With Puppets), Archive Video Of David, And Interviews With His Parents, Teachers, And Friends, Not To Mention The String Of Former Friends And The District Attorney Prosecuting The Case.
