To Live and to Heal

 "To Live and to Heal" 2 X 1-hour documentary series for RTE, concerning energy use for healing and life. One pro-gramme deals with Transcendental Meditation, the second with Reiki and other healing arts. The series was shot in Ireland and the US. "To Live and to Heal" takes the viewer to Heavenly Mountains, North Carolina - The Spiritual Centre of America and to Maharishi's University of Management in Fairfield, Iowa. We meet leading academics, scientists and artists who show how consciousness based education leads to happiness.
