The Driven

 Ariel Reinard a world-class cellist has a lot going for her until she finds out that her roommate Khia, and the group of people with whom she associates in the diamond exchange industry, is plotting to kill her fiancEe Matt Casey. She takes matters into her own hands and devises an elaborate plot to escape with him to Japan. Matt, who is having an affair with his assistant has just squandered fifty million dollars of company money on a trusted deal gone sour and time is running out.
