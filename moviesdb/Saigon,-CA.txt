Saigon, CA

 Westminster, CA. 1994. Based on a true story, SAIGON, CA follows two Vietnamese brothers who battle each other as they embark on a violent crime spree through California's Orange County area, stealing computer chips for a Hong Kong gang (the infamous Triads), as they both fall in love with the same Amerasian woman. SAIGON, CA is a potent mix of tortured relationships, violence and religion among California's Vietnamese lost generation.
