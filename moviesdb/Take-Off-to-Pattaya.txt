Take Off to Pattaya

 Take Off To Pattaya is an erotic travelogue with storyline featuring the romantic adventures of three sexy flight attendants vacationing at Thailand's famous Pattaya Beach resort. How much fun can three attractive single girls have on a week's holiday in Asia? Arriving in Pattaya, fate rolls the dice when they encounter a fashion photographer who ask the girls to join him on an outer island photo shoot. The next morning, two of the girls discover their friend has run off for some extra fun with the photographer. Stranded without transport, the remaining girls chance upon two young western guys on motorcycles who offer to help them search for their missing friend. For several days both groups play cat and mouse while experiencing the exotic sights and sound of Pattaya. When circumstances and timing finally reunites all six adventurers the girls discover they have been manipulated by the guys right from the beginning. However as the guys slap each other on the back for successfully tricking the girls and getting away with it, one of the girls decides to take her revenge...
