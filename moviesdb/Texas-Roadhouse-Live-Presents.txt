Texas Roadhouse Live Presents

 This is an edited experience through the world's most famous bike rally, Sturgis Bike Week. Filled with rides, events concert footage and a special Live two way broadcast to our military troops in the Iron Horse Brigade while stationed in Faluja, Iraq at Camp Tajid. Featured performances by WAR, Eddie Money, Tanya Tucker and Slow Ride hosted by Jay Allen owner of the Broken Spoke Saloon in Sturgis.
