The Ride of Tom & Valkyrie

 This is the story of the heroic psychological journey of Tom, a depressed insomniac locked in a small Chinatown studio that he's afraid to leave. Grieving the loss of his wife and daughter, and tormented by memories of a dysfunctional family, he hallucinates about his childhood heroes Franklin, a samurai, a '70's sitcom star, and a comic book hero--all of whom help Tom in his struggle to maintain his sanity. Meanwhile, Valkyrie, Tom's beautiful neighbor--whom he has never met--zips across Manhattan on series of disastrous blind dates in pursuit of her goal "to find the last real man in New York City, by midnight, tonight. The psychological journey of Tom, a depressed insomniac locked in a small Chinatown studio that he's afraid to leave. Grieving the loss of his wife and daughter, he hallucinates about his childhood heroes samurai, a '70's sitcom star, and a comic-book character. Meanwhile, Valkyrie, a beautiful trauma surgeon--endures a series of disastrous Internet dates while trying 'to find the last real man in New York City, by midnight.' Belonging together but unaware of each others existence, Tom and Valkyrie struggle to discover what it means to be a man--only to find the heroic common denominator of both sexes. He wants to be alone, but his heroes won't leave him. She wants to be in love, but her standards won't let her. Do parallel lines ever meet?
