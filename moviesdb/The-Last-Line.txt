The Last Line

 Camping out for ten days becomes more than just a bid for a good seat as Star Wars fans connect for the last time, and for the last film. Join the funny, quirky, and sometimes bittersweet experiences of Star Wars fans' last camp-out to screen the final installment of the series. Thus comes to an end a tradition that has spanned 30 years from the movie premiere in 1977. Fans reveal and discover their shared passion for Star Wars goes beyond the films, impacting their families, friends, and communities.
