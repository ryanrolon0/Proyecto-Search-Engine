Un singe sur le dos

 Francis is a successful car sales assistant, however recently outdone by other employees. His excessive alcohol consumption lets him lose control over his behavior once in a while in the presence of his family and his friends. His friends are confused, and his wife is afraid and in despair. When he is dismissed, he loses his security. One morning he wakes up in a bar in Bangkok and doesn't know how he got there. Francis has lost everything he obtains money by fraud to get back home again.
