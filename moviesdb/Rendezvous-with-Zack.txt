Rendezvous with Zack

 Delilah, Billy and Lance hold a bartender hostage while they wait for Zack and their money after a botched bank robbery. Delilah wants Zack, Billy wants to run, and Lance wants Delilah. With the cops closing in, Billy itching to put a bullet in her back, and Zack way past due, Delilah sticks to her guns. She'll make her rendezvous with Zack no matter what happens.
