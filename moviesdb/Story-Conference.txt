Story Conference

 A movie producer announces that 'Lillian Roth' (qv) has been signed to do a movie and he calls a story conference with a director and writers to come up with an idea for the film. As they work through various ideas, performers act out the song and dance numbers. A girls getting a divorce, a laundry worker who gets dumped by her boyfriend and singing scarecrows are all pitched and the producer suggests that they all be melded into one story. Studio executives are holding a story meeting. As each tells his idea it is illustrated by song and dance routines. Spoofs 'Honeymoon Hotel' number from _Footlight Parade (1933)_ (qv)
