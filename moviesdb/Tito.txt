Tito

 Tito is a young man who suffered brain damage when he was 6 months old after his crack addict mother dropped him on the ground. He was adopted and at 22 he has become a productive human of society. Everything seems to be going well until one of his daily walks back from work take him to unknown territories of the mind. Confusion, anger and sorrow take over after he meets a stranger on the street. Shocking & unexpected consequences will follow to the end.
