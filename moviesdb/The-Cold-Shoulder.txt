The Cold Shoulder

 Kenneth and Daisy have been in a strong relationship with each other since it is built upon special emotional circumstances. Circumstances due to Kenneth's uniqueness as an individual. However, their relationship is deteriorating because Daisy is now striving to be in a relationship built upon normal circumstances. A relationship which Kenneth cannot provide no matter how hard he tries. This film sheds light on the foundation of Kenneth's behavior. A topic that has long been misunderstood by many.
