Product of the Philippines

 What would you purchase from the grocery store with less than $3.00 left in your bank account and a family to feed? Jennilyn 'Ehje' Antonio was left with this desperate dilemma and purchased a kilo of peanuts. With no prior knowledge or training, she came up with her own delicious brand of homemade peanut butter. Her homemade product "Ehje's Peanut Butter" is not only unique in taste and quality, but unique in its story. Watch how Jennilyn, a simple and honest woman, goes rises above hardship to rescue her family from a life of poverty.
