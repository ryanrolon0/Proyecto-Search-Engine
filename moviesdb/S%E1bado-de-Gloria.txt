Sábado de Gloria

 Carlitos and Maria are friends and live in a small island in the Colombian Caribbean Sea. They spend their time fishing, selling sweets and jewelery, and playing with friends. Maria's rooster, called Bruce Lee, shows his physical superiority when he escapes, and kill one of the champion's rooster of El Carrasca. Carlitos will have to fight his rooster, against Maria's will to pay back El Carrasca.. Only their friendship will save them from the amazing world of rooster fights and bets..
