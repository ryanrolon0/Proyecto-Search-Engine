Roll Out, Cowboy

 Chris "Sandman" Sand is a Rappin' Cowboy from Dunn Center, North Dakota (population raps. He looks like Hank Williams but sings like Dr. Dre. Roll Out, Cowboy follows the 39-year-old country/hip-hop musician as he tours the American West during the 2008 Presidential election. Small town America isn't as conservative as we think.
