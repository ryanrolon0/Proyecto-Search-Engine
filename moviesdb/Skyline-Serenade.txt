Skyline Serenade

 A Universal "Musical" short (production number 6223) featuring Ted Fio Rito and his band, with Candy Candido, June Haver and Allen Cole as the vocalists, and also featuring The King's Men, tap dancer Ruth Daye, and dancers Igor & Poggi and Inestia. Songs include "The Reluctant Dragon", "Don't Mind If I Do" and "Tonight We Love (Tschaikowsky's "Piano Concerto in B Flat Minor").
