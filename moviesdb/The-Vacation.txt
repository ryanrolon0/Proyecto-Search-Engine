The Vacation

 A young couple in the first day of their two weeks vacation is driving along the highway, in danger of running out of gas. The girl (22 years old) demonstrates a joking annoyance to her companion of his inability to keep the car's gas tank full. The argument between them continuous up to the point that their reach a petrol station. The girl leaves the car to take a short walk by the time he is filling gas tank. When he picks her up again in the highway they become spontaneously engaged in a role-playing game as if a stranger driver is giving a lift to a girl in the highway. Although, the game begins innocently, this behavior leads to identity crisis for the participants.
