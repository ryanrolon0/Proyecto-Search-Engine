The Inspire DVD

 The Inspire DVD - Tales from the Roadside is Australia's first documentary film on modified Harley Davidsons & Custom built bikes. It features Some of America's top bike builders to Australia's upcoming builders and Australia's number one builder Dylan Robb.It goes beyond the so called "Reality" shows and shows just what it really takes to build a custom bike. From Ideas, building, the law & the future of the industry.
