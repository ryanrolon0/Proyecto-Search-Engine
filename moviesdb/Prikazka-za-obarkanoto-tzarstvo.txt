Prikazka za obarkanoto tzarstvo

 In the Kingdom of the great King Slug live the happiest animals of the world. Everything is mixed up there. No one has the right to grow up end everybody loves when someone dies and cries when someone is born. But everybody shakes with fear not to make angry the great king who actually no one has ever seen. In this kingdom arrives a little mule, that succeeds to unite the animals and together they drive the unpopular king away. But a new problem arrives everything mixes up.
