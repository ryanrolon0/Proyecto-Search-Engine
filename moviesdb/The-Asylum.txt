The Asylum

 Esperance is trying to leave Rwanda with her 6 year old daughter wanting to live a wholesome life in North America, Arlene has made it passed the border...alone years go by and suddenly she is receiving phones from a woman saying she's her mother but is it? Arlene has grown curious and is willing to go back in her past to get some answer. Her over protective best friend Diane doesn't like the sound of any of it knowing the nightmare she went threw to get over it, but is by her side to make sure things go smoothly.
