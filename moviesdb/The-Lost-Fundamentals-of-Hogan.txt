The Lost Fundamentals of Hogan

 Published in 1957 by Ben Hogan, The Modern Fundamentals of Golf records the teachings of one of the greatest ball strikers of all time, which have influenced players, coaches and millions of golfers ever since. In this program, BBC TV Golf Commentator Alex Hay introduces PGA Professional Luther Blacklock, who revisits the relevance of these fundamentals and reveals, for the first time, five principles that will, he believes, become industry standard.
