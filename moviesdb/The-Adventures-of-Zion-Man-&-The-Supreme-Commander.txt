The Adventures of Zion Man & The Supreme Commander

 Separated from there mother by attacking slobs, brothers Z and SP are forced to grow up and survive in the wilderness outside of civilization. Things get weird when aliens from Planet Zeron land on earth to capture females. Confronted by the aliens, Z escapes capture and becomes Zion Man. Taken prisoner to be reprogrammed, SP outsmarts the aliens and becomes The Supreme Commander A deal is made with the aliens as he reunites with Zion Man to create a powerful army to defeat and conquer the beautiful and brutal female fraulein army of Queen Lucealot. Their mission; to free all men from bondage, to win women as their brides, and take over all of Zion!
