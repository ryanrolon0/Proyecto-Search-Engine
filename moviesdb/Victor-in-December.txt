Victor in December

 Victor in December is a comedy about a group of childhood friends who live together in their hometown.Set between Thanksgiving and New Years the story is narrated by JAY,a young man obsessed with seeing life through the eyes of his favorite book, The Great Gatsby, which he belives Fitzgerald based on his town. Like Gatsby, JAY is trying to preserve life the way he remembers it, while his friends and the wooman he is in love with try to get him to embrace the future...
