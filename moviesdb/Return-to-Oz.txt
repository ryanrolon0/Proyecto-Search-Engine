Return to Oz

 A documentary of the underrated 'Return to Oz'. Behind the scenes footage. How the movie came to be and its production and marketable struggles. Famous filmmakers and more, that were attached with it. There is footage of interviews, with Fairuza balk and Nicol Williamson. There is footage of John Fricke (Oz Historian) interviewing the surviving munchkins, at Ozfest in 2006. There's footage from 'The Mickey Mouse Club Oz(1957) and more. It's also packed with clips, stills, book references and more from 'Wizard of Oz' and 'Return to Oz'. There are clips of movies that were somehow related to that genre and time.
