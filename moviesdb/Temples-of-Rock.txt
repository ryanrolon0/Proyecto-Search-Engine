Temples of Rock

 Door's organist Ray Manzarek hosts a journey to some of the most influential clubs, studios and theaters in Rock and Roll history. Their story is told through exclusive interviews with Ringo Starr, Graham Nash, Smokey Robinson and many other classic rock icons. Viewers will visit Sun Studio, The Cavern, Motown's Hitsville studio, Whisky a Go Go, Fillmore Auditorium, CBGB and the Fillmore East.
