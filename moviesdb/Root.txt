Root

 A woman is living in a post war totalitarian society that has fallen under a military rule. To keep the workers at bay the government has destroyed and prohibited all plants and flowers. One day as she is going home from work she witnesses a man from her apartment building being captured for raising a secret plant. She awakens from the robotic sleep and tries to grow a plant of her own. Her attempts are discovered by the military and they try to chase her down. In the events of her escape she loses her beloved plant. She manages to get outside the city walls where the nature is untouched.
