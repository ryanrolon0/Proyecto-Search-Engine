Punching Hitler

 When Willis house-sits his boss' place, he decides to show his two drinking buddies, Bernie & Don, a strange machine in the garage. After fooling around with the device, the trio discover that it is a Time Machine. After a quick jaunt to 1908 Austria to punch an 8 year old Adolf Hitler, the trio go on a madcap journey across Hitler's timeline to punch him every single day of his life.
