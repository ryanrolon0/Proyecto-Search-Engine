Strangers I've Known, April 4 1975

 Strangers I've Known explores the construction and deconstruction of a conversation between two people and the dialogue a man has with himself during that conversation through the use of interweaving sound that represents the man, his emotional instincts, and his conscious, rehearsed, process.
