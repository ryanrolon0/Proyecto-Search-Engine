Shades of Sunset

 It is 7 in hand, filmmaker Dom Ricco and Cinematographer Andy Strahorn start a day long journey down the length of Sunset Blvd. documenting life on the historic spans of pavement through a new lens. Traveling through Eco Park, Sliverlake, Hollywood, and Beverly Hills, all the way to the pacific coast, they capture a gradient of life, activity, and color; capturing the true shades of Sunset Boulevard.
