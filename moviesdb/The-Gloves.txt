The Gloves

 The struggle of four young men trying to make a name for themselves in the sport of boxing while simultaneously working to overcome their own adversities. Set against the backdrop of the New York City Golden Gloves, the world's largest boxing tournament, these four men battle each other and their own obstacles for a chance at a better future both for themselves and for those whom they love. An action-packed boxing movie and an intimate portrait of the difficult and often troubled lives of these aspiring athletes, "The Gloves" walks the fine line of explosive entertainment and provocative inspiration.
