Ultra

 Two battle-hardened soldiers and one military biological weapons technician are dropped via helicopter into the middle of nowhere. Their mission-to locate and investigate the whereabouts of a group of soldiers sent on an extremely classified military operation, and what happened to the communication with the soldiers. Soon into their mission, the three military personnel begin to encounter something more than the whereabouts of the missing soldiers, and the investigation soon becomes a struggle for survival against a benevolent, monstrous force that they don't fully understand...
