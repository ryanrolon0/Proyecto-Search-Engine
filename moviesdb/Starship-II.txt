Starship II

 They did a bad thing, and now they have to do it again. In the sequel to 1997's "Rock N Roll Starship," Bob, Doug, and Jorge accidentally go back in time-and stop themselves from destroying the Ramses Colony. But, in saving the colony, they also screw up the entire space-time continuum! Now, Bob, Doug, and Jorge have to go back in time to stop themselves from stopping themselves!
