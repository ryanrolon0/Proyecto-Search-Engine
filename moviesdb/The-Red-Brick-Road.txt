The Red Brick Road

 Parker was a confident child. Quinn was not. This coming of age story follows two lifelong friends as they take a cross-country road trip, and figure out what it really takes to grow up. Seeking adventure and escape, what they find on the road of life will test their friendship and change them forever. Sometimes the path behind is forgotten. Sometimes the road ahead is uncertain. Sometimes the greatest jounrey of all... is finding youself.
