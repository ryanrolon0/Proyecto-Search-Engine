Shoplifting

 1992. Australian suburbia. Pete is teaching his youngest son the family business off the Internet and with the introduction of new security devices in department stores, Pete's times seem to be changing. As the eldest son begins to take control of the direction the family is heading, Pete must work out a way around the new security devices or lose his place in the family entirely. The lengths he goes to shock even him as they begin to threaten what is left of his imploding family.
