Transcend

 Documentary following filmmaker Armando Gonzales, as he battles to complete his latest short film TRANSCEND. He struggles through the pit falls of production all while fighting terminal cancer. This shocking document gives the viewer intimate access to one artist, to his process, and to his last days. " In order to transcend the physical, you must take a leap of faith. " Directed by Moses. The short film plot summary; A woman finds herself trapped within a waking dream. Searching for a way out of this repeating nightmare, she finds a paradise vision that just always seems to be beyond reach.
