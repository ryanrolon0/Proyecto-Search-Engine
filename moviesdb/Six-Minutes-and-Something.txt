Six Minutes and Something

 EARTH!!! What a name for a planet. God decides that he can no longer stand it, and that he wants to restart it with a new name. But before he does, he decides to give an unsuspecting girl (Debbie) his powers for the remaining minutes of Earth. Debbie decides that she is going to spend her last minutes with her true love. The only problem is the he doesn't know that she loves him or that the world is ending.
