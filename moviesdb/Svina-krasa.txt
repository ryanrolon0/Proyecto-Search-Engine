 This little film recounts various episodes from Lativinan painter George Eliass. Eliass is thought to be the famous, or infamous, Peter The Painter, who was involved in the 1910 siege of Sidney Streetin Great Britain. The event almost derailed the career of the then Home Secretary at the time, a man named Winston Churchill. The film deals with confirmed events from Eliass's youth.
