Suzy's Party

 Suzy's Party is the story of two actresses whose lives collide. Both of their dreams and nightmares crash and they become submerged into each others' lives. In this movie a film is being made, which is bases on a film that has already been made. Charles travels all the way from England to find the woman in his dreams, Suzy. A fortune-teller has a giant cheetah locked up in a cage and warns all to stay away or pay the consequences. Suzy the actress falls into a deep depression while preparing to film a scene for her upcoming movie. The other actress is warned that the role in her new movie, should she accept it, could ruin her. What will happen? Will these two actresses ever find out the meaning of their fateful meeting? What will the end hold for Suzy and will she be able to handle the truth of her existence?
