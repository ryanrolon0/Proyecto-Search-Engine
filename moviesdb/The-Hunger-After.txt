The Hunger After

 What happens when life hits you when you least expect it. In an increasingly self-centered world, do you stay in your bubble or choose to step outside of it. Three complete strangers, going through very similar turmoil, find themselves serendipitously linked. Daniel is coping through utter disappointment, Sarah is dealing with co-dependency issues and Jessica is heading down a path of no return. These three strangers are tackling their own relationship demons and, in a world increasingly self-centered, they find themselves trapped in an emotional bubble. Life has a serendipitous way of bringing people together- if only they allow themselves to see it. There is a bigger picture innately connected
