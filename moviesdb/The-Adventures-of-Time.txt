The Adventures of Time

 In Silvertown, Sheriff Seine and Bucktooth Billy are acquainted with Señorita Elisea Ramos; who pleads a ride back to her hometown of Los Ramos, Chihuahua. However, before the ride, Elisea is transported to a different era via time machine. Later this occurrence is discovered to be made by Crazy Christopher Crawford who is in obsessed with Elisea and wanted to stop her from leaving her job as a saloon girl in Silvertown.
