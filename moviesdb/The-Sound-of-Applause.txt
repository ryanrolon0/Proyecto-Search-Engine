The Sound of Applause

 Tate returns home from a taxing day at work to a more taxing evening with his dysfunctional family; socially inept sister, Tara, and controlling mother, Bette. It's Bette's 65th birthday. Before the candles are blown out, an earthquake leads to Bette's heart attack and an opportunity for Tate to escape the emotionally suffocating house. Does he?
