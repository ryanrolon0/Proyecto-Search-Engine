Sexy Space Sirens from Siberon

 Two sexy space sirens come down to earth from the distant planet Siberon. We watch their journey as they make their way up the ladder of power and prestige till they reach the top! The Siren's goal - to destroy the Human race. Their Weapon - the Hollywood hype machine. Sexy Space Sirens from Siberon is a dark twisted comedy that takes a jab at Hollywood and all it has to sell.
