The Little Guide to Barber Techniques and Follicular Manipulation

 One morning, ten year-old Floria Frinkmeyer awakens to discover that her hair has come to life. It works its way into the family dinner, gets infested with huge bugs and even tries to swallow her mother's hand! With her parents Mr. and Mrs. Frinkmeyer horrified, they quickly hire a barber to fix the problem. Klaus Von Sauerbraten is a mysterious barber that arrives in a small box. When he is unleashed, he soon discovers that Floria will not allow him to cut her hair, as she has a great fear of scissors. Klaus must resort to The Little Guide To Barber Techniques And Follicular Manipulation to tame Floria's dreaded locks before it is too late!
