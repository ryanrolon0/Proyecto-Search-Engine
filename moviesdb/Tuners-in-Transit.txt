Tuners in Transit

 No need to pack your bags, if you're a tuner enthusiast, this road trip will take you on the TOUR, with hundreds of your closest tuner friends. Every year Superstreet & eurotuner Magazines drive to Atlanta for a huge car show. Cars and drivers from everywhere join forces, driving from Texas, Baltimore and Florida. Import Access and our host STR's Scott Miller, followed this crazy and wild trip through Hurricane Isabella, state lines and hooter girls galore. If you've never been, then put your seat belt on... and get inside the adventure any car lover can enjoy.
