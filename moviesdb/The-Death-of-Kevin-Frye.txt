The Death of Kevin Frye

 THE DEATH OF KEVIN FRYE is about a man with a dark, secret past, who falls in love with an unsuspecting newcomer to town. He desperately wants to leave his past behind and start a new life with his new love but two shady former employers have one last job they want him to do, an offer he wouldn't dare refuse. With the police hot on his trail, and all his dreams for happiness threatened, he has last chance for hope.
