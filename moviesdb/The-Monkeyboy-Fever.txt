The Monkeyboy Fever

 On the eve of his senior prom, a boy is bitten by a wild, lovestruck monkey and transforms into a primate himself overnight. Now, he must find a way to cover up his new appearance and get rid of his monkey admirer before meeting his prom date. With stunning special makeup effects and edgy thrills, The Monkeyboy Fever delivers an outrageous twist on a classic genre.
