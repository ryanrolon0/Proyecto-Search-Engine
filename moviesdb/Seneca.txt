Seneca

 It's the last day of the summer at Camp Seneca and all the kids have gone home. As the rest of the counselors eagerly prepare for the staff banquet, Jasmine can't pack her bags fast enough. She has spent the summer pining for Nelson, who barely knows she exists. Despite her friends' efforts to cheer her up, Jasmine ends up alone and feeling sorry for herself while everyone else is having the time of their lives. But the night is full of surprises, and an unexpected encounter with an old friend helps Jasmine forget her disappointments and rediscover her love of camp.
