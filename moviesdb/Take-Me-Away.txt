Take Me Away

 Every summer Rabiah and her daughters leave Belgium to visit their hometown of Taza, Morocco. When the eldest is engaged to her handsome crush, they travel to his village for the wedding. On their journey through the countryside the women are forced to reassess their ambitions when a slight change occurs in the relationship that binds the three women together. Eager to reach their destination the mother is faced with her greatest duty while the bride's fantasy is tested.
