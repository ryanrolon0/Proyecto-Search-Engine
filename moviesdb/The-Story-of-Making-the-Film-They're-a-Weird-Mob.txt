The Story of Making the Film They're a Weird Mob

 This documentary treats comedy fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of They're a Weird Mob, an Australian film about an Italian man who arrives in Sydney to work at his cousin's magazine, only to discover that the job no longer exists and he cannot understand what any of the locals are saying. Features interviews with the cast and crew of the film who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it all together.
