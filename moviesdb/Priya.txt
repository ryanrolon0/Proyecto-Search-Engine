Priya

 See the world through Bollywood eyes in 'Priya', a short Bollywood inspired romantic comedy about Sam (Matt Crook), an Australian, nineteen years old, who falls in love with Priya (Mandahla Rose), an Indian girl who has just arrived at this high school. The film follows Sam's comedy of errors as he tries to impress Priya's father who only allows her to go out with other Indians. Not willing to take no for an answer, Sam is prepared to do whatever it takes to win the father's favor, and Priya's heart...
