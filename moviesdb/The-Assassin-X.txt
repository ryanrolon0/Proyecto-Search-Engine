The Assassin X

 Clean, Professional, and Brutal are just a few words that describe X, a Collector and Assassin for the sadistic crime boss Mr. Harold. After defying the orders of his previous mission, and sparing the life of an innocent bystander, X finds he must now go toe to toe with the very organization that spawned him. This explosive thrill ride takes us through the life of the Assassin X, who he is, how he came to be, and all of what stands to be lost in the nearing battle of epic proportions. The stakes are high as X prepares to square up against a league of ruthless killers.
