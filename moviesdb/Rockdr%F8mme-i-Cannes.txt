Rockdrømme i Cannes

 At the MIDEM market in Cannes, France, in February 2004, Danish music artists delivered the opening show at Hotel Martinez. This TV documentary shows excerpts of the performances and interviews key artists and some of the press and business people involved. The artists include Kashmir, Swan Lee, The Raveonettes, Kira and the Kindred Spirits, and Mark Rosener who the programme follows on his journey to establish contacts while building his aspiring solo career.
