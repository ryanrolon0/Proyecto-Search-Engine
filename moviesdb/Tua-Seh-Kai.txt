Tua Seh Kai

 "Great World" is today one of Singapore's most popular shopping malls. But in decades past it was the site of a famous cabaret, an amusement park--even a prisoner-of-war camp. Acclaimed director Kelvin Tong brings Great World's legendary past to life in a series of interconnected stories spanning five decades. Five eras. Five Stories. One Great, Great World. Welcome to 'Great World', Singapore's legendary amusement park. Where lovers cavorted in the 60s. Where Hollywood stars and cabaret girls partied in the 50s. Where bombs fell in the 40s. Acclaimed director Kelvin Tong brings Great World's colourful past to life in five interconnected stories.
