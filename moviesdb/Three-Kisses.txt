Three Kisses

 Story about the Leone's from Queens, NY. Frankie Leone is on a quest to find happiness. A quest to stay married to the girl of his dreams Angie, but things aren't going exactly as planned, especially when he and his family find out Angie is cheating on him with Frankie's long lost cousin Veto, whom is already dating Frankie's sister Stephanie. Things get even more complicating when Veto also starts a relationship with Frankie's brother Tony's girlfriend. The Leone's seek revenge on these cheaters by planning death by of all things......pizza, spearheaded by the mother Sofia.
