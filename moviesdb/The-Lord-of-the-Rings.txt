The Lord of the Rings

 Based on the movie The Two Towers, the video game follows up the adventures of Aragorn the Ranger, Legolas the Elf and Gimli the dwarf. When the three companions are pledged to aid King Theoden of Rohan against the forces of Saruman's army of Uruk-Hai, you play through levels such as the Westfold, The Plains of Rohan and more to save the future of Rohan.
