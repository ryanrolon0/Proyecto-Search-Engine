The 36th Stranger

 In every generation 36 people are born to spread enlightenment to the world. Viewed as saviors to most but an evil organization called 'The Method' view the 36 as a threat. The first 35 are killed off with ease by 'The Method'. However, one remains, without knowing his relevance to the world, he is the 36th Stranger... In every generation, 36 people are born to spread positivity and enlightenment. However, since the beginning of time "The Method" have vowed to terminate each set of 36 as generations have gone by. However in present day, the first 35 were killed with ease, yet one remains alive. Without knowing his relevance to the world, he is out numbered and on the run and has no idea why. He is the last hope. He is The 36th Stranger...
