The Legendary Script

 A tale of mortal combat 1000 years ago in China. The story was inspired by historical conflicts between the numerous ethnic groups in China, particularly the Hmong and Han Chinese. At a time when fighting superiority dictated survival, the appearance of a script detailing flawless fighting techniques left behind by a legendary martial artist triggered and endless search and bloodshed. Unknown to everyone is a curse that comes with the script and the secret to break it.
