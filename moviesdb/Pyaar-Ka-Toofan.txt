Pyaar Ka Toofan

 Shyamu and Pinky are inseparable childhood sweethearts, however, Pinky's dad Thakur Pratap Singh does not want her to be friendly with Shyamu as he is poor, and asks her not to see Shyamu again. When she insists on seeing Shyamu, he arranges her travel abroad. Years later, Pinky has returned home, and has not forgotten her feelings for Shyamu. When she goes to meet Shyamu, she finds out that he is to marry a village belle by the name of Roopa. When her dad finds out, he forbids her to see Shyamu, and restricts her movements within the house, and assisting him in this task is Pinky's brother, Rakesh, who will even kill anyone who dares to go against him.
