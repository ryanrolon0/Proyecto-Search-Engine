Qualified to Satisfy

 Are you qualified to give everything to your partner in terms of sensual experiences? That is certainly the question all the members of this cast are trying to decipher as they go through multiple attempts to give their all in this production from Robert Prion. And even though many of the men here are newcomers, they certainly make up for it with their ardor and deep desire to truly satisfy every partner they encounter on their quest for a better understanding of what really makes a good lover.
