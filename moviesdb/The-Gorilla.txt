The Gorilla

 Told through the personal stories of affected individuals and the experience of experts in the field, "The Gorilla" is an examination of the intersection between substance abuse, mental illness, and homelessness. The film explores the cycle of substance abuse and homelessness and the role of the mental health care system in North Carolina. The film also reveals many of the triggers for substance abuse and suggests alternative approaches to substance abuse treatment and homelessness prevention.
