The Perfect Gentleman

 The Perfect Gentleman is a cat-and-mouse thriller, set on an elegant estate during the 1950's. The story follows Jonathan, a charming, handsome suitor, poised to marry the daughter of a wealthy tycoon. The couple appears picture perfect, until the eve of their engagement party, when an unexpected guest threatens to destroy Jonathan's plans for the future. Winner of The Deluxe Award and The Hollywood Foreign Press Award, The Perfect Gentleman is a cat-and-mouse thriller, set on an elegant estate during the late-1950's. Hart Turner plays Jonathan Crenshaw, a charming, handsome suitor, poised to marry the daughter of a wealthy tycoon. On the eve of their engagement party the couple appears picture perfect, but things take a turn for the worse when an unexpected guest (Luis Selgas) threatens to destroy Jonathan's plans for the future. Shot in historic locations throughout Pasadena, California, director Michael Rohrbaugh captures the lifestyle of America's upper crust, and one man's yearning to join its ranks. Produced through UCLA's prestigious Graduate School of Theater, Film, and Television, The Perfect Gentleman will keep you guessing until the very end.
