Tu pirata soy yo

 A casual first date to the beach turns into a life threatening escape for a high school couple. When the father of the innocent and sweet Mari Machado realizes that his daughter is about to date a boy she met through the internet, he takes every measure to prevent it. Wilbert, the one-eyed, hairy, mustached lover knocks on the front door of the Machado household. In turn, Mr. Machado defends his daughter's honor with medieval tactics. This unpredictable, romantic comedy quickly becomes an adventure movie, where an impossible love faces the most bizarre obstacles eye-patch and a psychotic father.
