Private Gold 27

 In 1834, Tatiana and her family lived peacefully in an old windmill located on the property of the Marquis of Icecrown, until one day her father, the miller, had to ask the Marquis to lend him 1,200 escudos due to a series of poor harvests. Since the Marquis is wicked and cruel, he decides to get his money back and, while the father is away, he forces the younger sister to screw all the members of his escort and himself. Deranged by anger, Tatiana decides to avenge her sister. But to do that, she needs money and finds herself forced to become a prostitute.
