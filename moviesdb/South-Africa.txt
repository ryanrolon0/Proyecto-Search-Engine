South Africa

 Oscar winning documentary director and Shakespearean actor Sir Antony Sher, both ex South Africans, investigate the murder in Cape Town of up-and-coming actor, Brett Goldin, and discover that 13 years after the arrival of democracy in South Africa, the country of their birth is suffering one of the highest murder rates in the world with 50 people a day being killed. What is going on in South Africa and is the rainbow nation which brought so much hope to the world just a few years ago, under threat?
