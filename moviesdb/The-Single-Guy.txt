The Single Guy

 Donny thought he knew what true love was, but that was until he met Amy. As his life becomes happier, he begins to learn about love and life, but this comes with a consequence. His best friend for life is put second. Who will he have to choose? Featuring the music of Betsy Spivak, Faiground, Goat Factory, and Vince Perez, The Single Guy is a real love story that touches the mind, body, and heart.
