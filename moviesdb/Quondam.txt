Quondam

 Jack has destroyed his life and lost all that is dear to him. Now dying he is haunted by the ghosts of his past and the mistakes he has made, as he journeys toward redemption. Quondam studies the nature of addiction and the destructive effects it could have on the people around you. It is the exploration into one man's psyche as he struggles to find peace, by facing everything he has lost, in his dying moments.
