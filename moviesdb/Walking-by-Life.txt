Walking by Life

 Jason Randleman and his wife, Amy, are expecting their first child. They are happily married despite the financial debts Jason has been hiding from his wife. Jason is vain and has too big of a thirst to impress those he works with. Jason and Amy's lives come to an abrupt halt when they discover their unborn child is having fatal problems. Jason is now commissioned to do whatever it takes to find the funds necessary to pay for the surgery that will save their child.
