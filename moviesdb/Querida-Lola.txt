Querida Lola

 Just when ten years have gone by since Lola Flores, one of the biggest Spanish stars in music, cinema and TV, passed by, this documentary hosted by Carmen Sevilla with testimonies of famous people that knew him and with tons of archive footage of her appearances in the media commemorates the career of an artist that still nowadays very few people has been able to forget, and will need much more time to do so, if they ever want to.
