The Perfect Daughter

 She can run from her dangerous past.... But she can't hide!!! A thriller about one woman's escape from a life of danger and violence - and her struggle against the deadly forces that seeks to pull her back. Alexandra Michaelson has spent two years as a runaway on the city streets, but after an accident, she awakens with no memory of her recent past. Her family welcomes her back, but soon she's visited by a man who claims he's her boyfriend. While trying to rekindle her memory, he tells Alexandra that somewhere she has hidden $100,000 stolen from a dangerous drug dealer!
