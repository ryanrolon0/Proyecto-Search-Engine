Temple of Fear

 On a desolate desert road... Christine, was shaken and violently disrupted by a deadly and horrifying premonition. As the terror began on the haunting journey of courage and the fight for survival, she struggles to prevent the inconceivable disaster from happening. Christine's mind infuriated with the unexpected nightmare...she continues her excursion to alter the future, but without the help of Elaine, who has no idea that a sinister maniac has chosen her to be his next victim. Now as fate hunts them, would Christine be able to convince her cousin of what's about to take place, save her, cheat death and revise their destiny? In the bleak desert, isolation becomes your ultimate fear.
