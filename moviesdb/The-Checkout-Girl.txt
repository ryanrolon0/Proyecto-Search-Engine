The Checkout Girl

 A young man and woman are caught off-guard by an accidental grocery store confession two find themselves engaged in a full-scale simulation of their dreams. Whatever has happened in their sleep- whether a meaningful connection, a coincidence, or a mistake- is about to happen again.
