Small Time Crime

 Small Time Crime is an intense crime drama that focuses on a dying, hallucinating bank robber who's trying to flee the mob and spend the time he has left with his psychopathic actress girlfriend. When they stop in his hometown, he's drawn back into troubles with one of his oldest friends. But with the trouble comes the chance to save the one true love of his life from the clutches of a notorious drug dealer. And as the story unravels,so does he.
