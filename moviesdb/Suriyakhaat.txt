Suriyakhaat

 While driving to see his girlfriend, Totsapark gets into a car crash and dies instantly. At the same time, a solar eclipse is taking place. Dr. Pun, a scientist with intimate knowledge of reincarnation, awakens Totsapark's spirit from the dead in order to use the spirit to be a killer weapon. Totsapark is resurrected as a terrible zombie, and he is obsessed with pursuing Jiratikan (Ornjira Lamwilai), his girlfriend when he was alive. While Jiratikan is trying to escape from her zombie lover, she seeks the assistance of Wut to solve the mystery of the living dead.
