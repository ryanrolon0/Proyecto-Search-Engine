The Making of 'Volle Maan'

 This made-for-tv documentary treats film fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Volle Maan, about a group of student friends celebrating their graduation and looking forward to their futures. Includes interviews with stars Schuurmans and Jansen, along with the rest of the cast and crew of the film, who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it to completion.
