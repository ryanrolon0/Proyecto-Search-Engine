Tex Williams & Orchestra in Western Whoopee

 In this Universal "Name Band Musical" (correct title "Tex Williams & Orchestra in Western Whoopee"), Tex Williams introduces his newest hit song, "Don't Telephone - Don't Telegraph- Tell a Woman", and he and the band play six other songs, with assistance on some by a trio known as The Corral Cuties (Della Norell, Judy Clark and Pat Alphin.)
