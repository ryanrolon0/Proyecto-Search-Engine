The Lonely Mr. Scrim

 The Lonely Mr. Scrim is an old man who lives in a house built on a tree. A tree that grew from a barge, which floats on a sea. He lives here with his dead wife. His life is turned upside down when a giant cooperation threatens to tear down his home and put in a mall. Making matters worse, a strange creatures shows up. Can he save his home? Will he finally let go of his dead wife? Will the creature eat all of his underwear? Maybe...
