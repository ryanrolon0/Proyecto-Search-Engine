Space Cadet

 Seven-year old Sarah's vivd imagination takes her on a surreal adventure from her bedroom closet into outer space. When her older brother John takes her pet guinea pig Arsenio captive, she must find a way to save him. With a little help from Command Post 12, she might just find a way. To escape her mundane life, seven-year old Sarah often imagine herself in a 50's sci-fi T.V. serial called 'Space Cadet'. In this episode, an evil alien (her brother John) takes her co-pilot Arsenio (her pet guinea pig) captive up in the Tower of Doom (tree house). Sarah attempts to climb it, but quickly realizes is she is terrified of heights. Feeling hopeless, she pays a visit to the Commander (Grandmom), where she learns about her Grandfather, a decorated Korean-American WW2 Flying Ace. When Grandmom pins his Flying Cross medal on her, Sarah gets up the courage to climb the tree house. With a few tricks up her sleeve, she manages to save Arsenio and also John from almost falling off the tree house. Realizing his wrong ways, John decides to become a 'Space Cadet', and the two siblings go on another adventure.
