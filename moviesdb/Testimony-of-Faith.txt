Testimony of Faith

 The personal story of Dr. Gorden Foster, a surgeon during WWII. His story is told in his own words with pictures he photographed during the war. From a country doctor in a small town to crossing paths with the great hero of WWII Audie Murphy. Dr. Foster was there at the opening of the gates of Dachau with a doctor's point of view and ventured into Hitler's Eagles Nest. His personal faith stayed his path and courage during this journey.
