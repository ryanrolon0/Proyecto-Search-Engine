Raisin' Junior's NASCAR Komedy Follies

 Don Swainer and Lester Klunk are Dale Earnhardt Jr.'s two biggest fans. They're on a mission to 'raise' Junior's profile to make him the most gigantic star ever. Not just NASCAR famous, but world wide famous. In this, the final in the series of Raisin' Junior comedy short films, Don & Lester spoof Dale Jr.'s racing rivals by presenting their version of a NASCAR themed Laugh-In.
