Sea Melody

 Ted Fio Rito and his band are on tour and band singer Muzzy Marcellino finds the girl of his dreams, but the girl's mother objects to having a musician in the family. The Ted Fio Rito band (led by Ted Fio Rito) is invited aboard a ship for a farewell party but are trapped on the boat and unwillingly set sail for the Orient. Muzzy is happy because the girl is on board and the Ted Fio Rito band (led by the always-billed as Ted Fio Rito or Ted Fio-Rito but never as Ted Fiorito) charms the girl's mother with music played by Ted Fio Rito/Ted Fio-Rito and his band.
