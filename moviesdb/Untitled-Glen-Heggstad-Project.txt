Untitled Glen Heggstad Project

 A docu-drama about an ambitious South American motorcycle journey gone awry, the fascinating Glen Heggstad life-story. Chronicling chapters of his extraordinary life experiences beginning in his tumultuous youth, through hard-times as an outlaw motorcyclist, to international martial arts champion and concluding with his capture and eventual incredible method of escape from a ruthless Marxist rebel army in the remote mountains of Colombia.
