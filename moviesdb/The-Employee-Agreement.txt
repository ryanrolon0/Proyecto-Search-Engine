The Employee Agreement

 Tommy is proud of his work on the Simpson Report, but his boss Tolleson thinks otherwise. A firm slap to Tommy's face will help Tolleson reinforce his point that sloppy mistakes cannot be tolerated. Tommy, absolutely confused about the encounter, declares that this type of behavior is not tolerated in the workplace, but he is quickly reminded that due to the new terms of employment that he recently signed, that it is in fact, OK. In an office environment where corporal punishment is allowed, what is the result?
