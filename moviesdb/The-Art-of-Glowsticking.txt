The Art of Glowsticking

 This documentary explores the art and culture of glowsticking. It shows glowsticking in the club and rave settings. It also shows glowsticking at small and large get-togethers. Much of the video is dedicated to finding out what the culture means to these glowstickers. This documentary interviews people who are new to the art and some of the more well known glowstickers in the world.
