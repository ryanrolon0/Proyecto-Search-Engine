The Lunch Box

 Two mafia gangsters Luis and Carlos stole a lunch box from a little girl to hide the money that will be use in a drug deal, they stop at a bar to burn some time but after they leave, they realized that the money was left at the bar. Josh a young man buried in debts and with his mother sick of cancer finds the money and run away with it as this may be the solution for all his economic problems. Luis and Carlos must find the money or their family will be in danger, after finding out where Josh lives they try to break in the building as Josh finds a interesting way to escape without been notice. But that's not the end.
