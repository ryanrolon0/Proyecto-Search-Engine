Tracking Evan

 The film is the true story of Evan Moss, who from birth had epilepsy from Tuberous Sclerosis (TSC), and how his family turned their hardship into a useful resource for everyone afflicted with epilepsy. The film tracks Evan from birth through surgery to remove 3 tumors in his brain at the age of four, and also highlights his parents who took their frustration with providing him the right medical care into SeizureTracker.com, an on line tool for logging seizures and medications.
