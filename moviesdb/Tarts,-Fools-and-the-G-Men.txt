Tarts, Fools and the G-Men

 One object. One location. One hour. Three crews. Prostitutes by day and assassins by night. Clowns by day and con men by night. Cops by day and...dirty cops by night. Battling time, traps and each other only the toughest crew will walk away with the prize and their lives. A highly stylized, action packed adventure where all three stories intertwine. Each crew must assault the secret location and find the package. When they arrive simultaneously an epic showdown occurs, guns are drawn in a Texas-style showdown. When the individual crew members realize they know the others in personal ways negotiations ensue as crews try to flip other crews to their side.
