The Headhuntress

 Spotlights LA's premiere corporate recruiter, Wendy Doulton, and her work in the fast paced and cutthroat world of headhunting. With one of the sharpest tongues in the business, this project follows Wendy and her staff as they try to keep up with the constant demands of her corporate clients and the private individuals she makes over for the job market. Survive a meeting with Wendy and you can survive an interview with just about anyone.
