Solitude

 Solitude is a short physiological thriller set in the woods. It's centered around a man who tries to find civilization in a post-apocalyptic world. He has followed a set of train tracks in the hope they will lead him to a better place. Suddenly a girl appears at the edge of the forest. She is bruised and bloodied. Who is she? Where has she come from? She tells James they have to find shelter as soon as possible for they are not alone. A story of friendship, suspense and mystery unfolds, as James and Adriana try to escape a terrible fate.
