Restrained Couples

 Bondage film with six vignettes of male/female peril. In the first vignette, a couple is kidnapped, stripped to their underwear, then bound and gagged by a pair of female kidnappers. In the second tale, a couple learns about the perils of social networking. The third story a couple is tied and gagged in their own backyard. The fourth tells of a pilot and stewardess who are abducted and left to struggle far from their airplane. The next story tells of an art photographer whose next session goes horribly wrong. The final tale tells of a double-crossed cat burglar who struggles to free herself from her bonds while sitting beside her erstwhile victim.
