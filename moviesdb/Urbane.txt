Urbane

 When naive medical student Lili's room mate Mia commits suicide Lili feels she's to blame. But Mia had a dark secret as a 'cage dancer' at the exotic Gemini Club. At Mia's funeral Lili meets Mia's friends and colleagues from the Gemini Club, amongst them owner Richard Germaine. Invited to Mia's wake Lili takes her transexual friend Edie, and discovers a world she's never known. Spiralling into drink, drugs and sex, Lili is warned by mysterious stranger Fredericksen that her life is in danger. Can Lili trust Fredericksen or should she listen to Richard Germaine? As things spiral out of control Lili discovers she's got no one to turn to!
