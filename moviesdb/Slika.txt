Slika

 Film was made by children with a little help from professionals. Simple youth story about love, jealousy and backstabbing "friends" was written by 12 years old Nadja Sicarov. Schoolfriends Vesna, Marta, Mitja and Rok are real friends until Marta starts to plot things. Will the friendship survive? The movie was made for CIAK Junior, Children's International Film Festival 2004 in Treviso, Italy
