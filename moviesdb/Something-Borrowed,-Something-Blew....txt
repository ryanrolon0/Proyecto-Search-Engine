Something Borrowed, Something Blew...

 The wedding is about to start in 15 minutes... the only problem? The wedding is about to start in 15 minutes. Get an inside look at tumultuous final minutes of the single life and the inner thoughts of both bride and groom - with help from a jilted maid of honor and a party boy best man moments before their "magical day", in a 'he said' 'she said' format.
