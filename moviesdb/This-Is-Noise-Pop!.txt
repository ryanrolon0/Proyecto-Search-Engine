This Is Noise Pop!

 More than a decade in the making and featuring never-before-seen footage, 'This is Noise Pop!' explores the genesis of indie rock through interviews and personal moments with bands captured during and after their Noise Pop Festival performances. Featuring The Flaming Lips, Sebadoh, Stephen Malkmus, Death Cab for Cutie, The National, The Donnas, Guided by Voices, Creeper Lagoon, Beulah, Bob Mould, The Decemberists, Superchunk, The Shins, Britt Daniel, Modest Mouse, Granddaddy and more.
