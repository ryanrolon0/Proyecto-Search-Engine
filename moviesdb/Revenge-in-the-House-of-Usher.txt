Revenge in the House of Usher

 A traveller arrives at the Usher mansion to find that the sibling inhabitants are living under a mysterious family curse senses have become painfully acute, while his sister has become nearly catatonic. As the visitor's stay at the mansion continues, the effects of the curse reach their terrifying climax, and he must choose between his concern for his hosts' safety, and his own. Eric Usher, last descendant of the Usher clan, lives alone in a big old house on a rocky cliff with his memories, madness and Helen, a vampire-ghost. Eric invites Alan Harker to stay with him so he can get some of his built up anguish off his chest and Alan soon finds himself tangled up in the Usher world of madness.
