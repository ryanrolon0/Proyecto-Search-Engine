Riders of the Painted Desert

 The story of Texan John Tunsten, a sharpshooter for the American army, who is left wounded and alone in the Chihuahuan Desert during the last days of the Mexican-American war. He encounters another survivor Tomas Escamilla. Neither can speak the other's language and both hold hatred for the other side. In spite of their differences, the stranded soldiers form a bond and make an oath their families.
