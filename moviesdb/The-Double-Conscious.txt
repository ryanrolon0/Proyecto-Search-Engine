The Double Conscious

 Racial anxieties and inequalities have long been a fixture in American life. 'Race & Rhetoric' brings together a collection of speeches from well known historical figures that articulate the social ramifications of this problem. It is an educational tool intended to provoke critical thinking by displaying the thoughts and opinions of a wide range of prominent figures over the span of a century.
