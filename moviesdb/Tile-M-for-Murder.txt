Tile M for Murder

 Tile M for Murder is a short dark comedy about a couple playing scrabble, based on the award winning short story 'Death by Scrabble' written by Charlie Fish. Austin and Alice are having another game night, the only problem is that Austin can't stand his wife. As the game unfolds, Austin realizes that something unusual is happening in their small cabin. Perhaps for his next turn, he'll opt for M-U-R-D-E-R...
