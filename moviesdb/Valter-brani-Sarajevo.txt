Valter brani Sarajevo

 Retreating German armies are in desperate need of fuel. Valter, mysterious and charismatic resistance leader can jeopardize their supply. Germans carry out cunning plan in order to eliminate that obstacle. The end of WW2 in former Yugoslavia is getting near as German forces continue their retreat from Balkan Peninsula. Huge German armies are desperately low on fuel and German Command plans complex operation to secure the supplies. But Valter, the Sarajevo resistance leader, will get in their way.
