Racing Around the Clock

 The Mont24 was a 24-hour mountain bike race held in the scenic pine forests of Canberra, Australia, with over 1300 competitors. "Racing around the Clock" follows two teams riding the race and an amateur team of six female riders. Live the journey of these engaging, passionate and down to earth people. Experience their motivations, group dynamics, physical and psychological barriers, and thrill... don't be surprised if this film makes you want to take up mountain bike riding! The film was created by a crew of professional volunteers with a soundtrack featuring music from upcoming Australian artists.
