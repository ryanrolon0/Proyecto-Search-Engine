The Making of 'Toy Story'

 This made-for-tv documentary treats animation fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Toy Story, Pixar's groundbreaking family movie about the secret lives of a child's toys. Includes interviews with the cast and crew of the film who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing this first-ever full-length computer animated movie to completion.
