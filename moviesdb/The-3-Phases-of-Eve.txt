The 3 Phases of Eve

 Eve has a problem of which is prudish, the second of which is sexy and seductive, and the third is an all-out, wild-eyed nymphomaniac. She relates to her doctor all of the sexual adventures each of her personalities engages in. One day she finds herself in the middle of a swingers orgy and calls her doctor for help, which he is more than happy to give her.
