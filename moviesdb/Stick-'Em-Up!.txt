Stick 'Em Up!

 Stick 'Em Up! is an alluring and captivating film that delves into the little known world of wheat pasting, an inner city art form that's as provocative as it is misunderstood. Documentary filmmaker Alex 'PR!MO' Luster delves into the minds and motivations behind several guerilla street artists capturing the lifespan of their art... conception, creation, placement and ultimately the removal by the city abatement enforcement. With commentary by legendary street artists and from top local law enforcement officials, "Stick 'Em Up!" is a gritty, street smart documentary that reveals the secret truth behind some of the prolific images you see everyday.
