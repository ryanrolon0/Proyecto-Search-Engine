Sex Slaughter a.k.a. Cum na vossa boca dans votre bouche

 An untouched Unicorn comes to a bar seeking for the high life of glitz and glamor. At this bar she meets up with a mysterious identity known only as Tuxedo Mask who promises her everything she ever wanted. They engage and dangerous relationship is formed. The Unicorn soon realises that the life she has always dreamed about isn't all it's cracked up to be. Laced with with sex, murder, crime, and corruption, Sex Slaughter takes the concept of Experimental Kinestasis to near untouched limits.
