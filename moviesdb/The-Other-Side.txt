The Other Side

 Welcome to The Other Side, a short animated film which attempts to answer the question that has plague mankind for ages the road? Meet our determined but not so bright Chicken and join him for what is certain to be the most epic adventure poultry has ever encountered. This Looney Tunes-inspired comedy will warm your heart and make you laugh and cry as you witness our hero hurdle every fowl obstacle that blocks his way. You're sure to fall in love with the bravest little Chicken in history, in a world where the sky is certainly not the limit.
