Powers of Three

 Set against the backdrop of Ireland's primal landscape, 'Powers of Three' presents the extraordinary tale of Tom Lowe, Fergal Smith and Mickey Smith risking all to extend the meaning of courage, commitment and artistry in one of the most brutal locations on earth. Both in the water and out, Powers of Three journeys beyond the traditional clichEs of the surf film genre to penetrate the unflinching minds of three Artists united across the long dark winter by their shared big wave obsession.
