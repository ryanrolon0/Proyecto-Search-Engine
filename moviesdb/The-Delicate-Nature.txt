The Delicate Nature

 A naive, young, small-town dealer becomes in over his head and struggles to escape a drug ring operating along the Canadian border in Montana. Ten years later, an FBI agent investigates the operation along with the dealer's unexplained disappearance, much to the disapproval of the local townsfolk. Murder, rumor, conspiracy, and a flight for survival. The crimes portrayed are based on actual allegations, and the film was shot on location.
