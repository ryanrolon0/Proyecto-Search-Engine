The Ataris Live at Capitol Milling

 Features a Rockumentary for the "In This Diary" music video and includes an exclusive "for fans only" concert. Filmed at Capitol Milling, an abandoned warehouse space in downtown Los Angeles. The "In This Diary" video clip and the concert show some awesome live performances by The Ataris and give an insight into the writing of Kris Roe. Features "In this Diary", "In This Diary" Making of, "Take offs and landings (Live)", "Teenage Riot (Live), "My Reply (Live)", "Unopened Letter To The World (Live)" & "Song #13 (Live)".
