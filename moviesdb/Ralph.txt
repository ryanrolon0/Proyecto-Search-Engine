Ralph

 A documentary short film with a running time of approx. 36 mins. The piece is narrated by Stephanie De Palma, daughter of Ralph John De Palma. The film depicts the life of her father, Ralph, exploring the trials and demons that he faced, eventually leading to his ultimate demise. By speaking with those whose lives he touched, Stephanie learns about who her father was, his alcoholism and the impact that he had on her life and others. This heartfelt exploration of alcoholism and those it affects, offers a firsthand glimpse into the emotional rollercoaster of the disease that is. and has been, extremely prevalent in many American families.
