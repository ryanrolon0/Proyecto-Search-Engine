The Emancipation of Jonny

 Jonathan Tyler (Aiken), a typical 16 year-old teenager, unravels on an inner-journey as he struggles to balance his dreams with his reality. Meeting tourist Piki Little (Duff) on the way, the two accompany each other on an emotional roller-coaster, as they both aim to fulfill their dreams. A heart-felt, emotional drama unravels on the journey of a 16 year-old teenager, as he faces the ultimate decision, balancing his dreams with his reality. Based on the true-life of 16 year-old director, Jonathan Nguyen, Jonathan Tyler struggles to live an ideal teenage life as he is constantly bombarded with strict parental expectations.
