Unlocking Rose Red

 This half-hour documentary program offers an in-depth examination of Ellen Rimbauer's life at Rose Red through the pages of her newly discovered diary. A narrated blend of interviews, stills, illustrations, film footage and sound design will bring the diary to life and provide viewers with historical and social context. In addition to interviews with experts both in favor of and against her efforts, Joyce Reardon (Ph.D., Parapsychology) details her discovery of Ellen's diary and the history of paranormal activity at Rose Red. Her expedition inside this historic Seattle landmark is the first of its kind in thirty years
