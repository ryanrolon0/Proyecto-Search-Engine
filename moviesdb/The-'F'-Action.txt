The 'F' Action

 Everyone who watches movies has a dream about being in the movies. The "F" Action is for you! "Fun and Exciting!" "Informaive & Inspiring!" The "F" Action will take you through a journey of filmmakers, directors, and movie-goers at a film festival. If you are interested in the film or movie industry, here's your chance to get yourself there! Filmed in glamorous Coconut Grove, Miami and at AMC Movie Theaters. Special features information on how to market, present, and promote your movie. And! How to prepare set-up, and display your movie at a film festival
