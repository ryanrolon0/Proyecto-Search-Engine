The Big Nothing

 "Mr. Squeegee" demonstrates his window-washing bravado while spouting off half-baked theories of the universe. When he spots a bizarre calamity on the other side of the glass, he tells his Grandma he'll have to call back later. What's this strange ticking box, and how did it do what it did? More importantly, what will happen if he fiddles with a few of these buttons?
