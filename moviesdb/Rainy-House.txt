Rainy House

 This short film was executed in a surrealistic style to challenge the extensive and diverse possibilities of animation. It employs a variety of media types and techniques, among them collage, 3D animation, and drawings. The plot reflects the empty feeling that can accumulates in modern life and lead to inner conflict and self-doubt. The ending intends to stimulate introspection within the audience, and it implies that life has boundless meaning if we remind open to new possibility.
