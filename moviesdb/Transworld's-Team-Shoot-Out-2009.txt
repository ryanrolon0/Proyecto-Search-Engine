Transworld's Team Shoot Out 2009

 Transworld Snowboarding had four of the worlds best snowboard teams in Burton, Rome, DC, and Forum compete simultaneously at separate, elite resorts around the country; Sierra-Tahoe, Aspen-Snowmass, Bear Mountain-CA, and Northstar-Tahoe to claim the ultimate prize as winner of the 2009 TWS Team Shoot Out.
