Usha

 A fiction film deploying aspects of the mythological. The demonic King Banasur (Dudhale), a devout disciple of Shiva, wants to eliminate Vishnu and his followers in the guise of Krishna (Tembe), king of Dwarka and an incarnation of Vishnu. Krishna overcomes Banasur's designs by getting his daughter Usha (Mantri) to fall in love in with Aniruddha (Vasudeo).
