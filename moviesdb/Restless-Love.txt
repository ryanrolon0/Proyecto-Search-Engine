Restless Love

 Charli and John have spent five unforgettable years together, but the time has come for Charli to move on. In order to do so, she is contemplating to leave John. As a final verification she chooses to survey their intimate relationship to see if there still is a glimpse of hope or purpose for her to stay. The couple perform an almost dance like love exploration, when Charli suddenly brings it to a halt.
