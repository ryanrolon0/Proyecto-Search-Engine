Scarycrows, Scaryhoes

 Metrie is a Military brat, only child following her fathers foot steps, she then breaks the code of silence after being abused changing the course of her life, she is then is offer a job on the police force as a Detective working the streets of hard knocks. When Detective Metrie, makes a major bust large sums of cash comes up missing Compromising her position on the police force, caught up by greed and power She then believes that she is above the law taking matter into her own hands only to Struggle with her violent pass choosing her own path.
