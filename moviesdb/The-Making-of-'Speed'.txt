The Making of 'Speed'

 Hosted by Dennis Hopper, this documentary treats movie fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Speed, about a hot-shot S.W.A.T. officer who must stop a mad bomber from blowing up an out-of-control bus filled with passengers. Included are interviews with the cast and crew who share their experiences from making the film, as well as out-takes, goofs, and other things didn't make it into the final print.
