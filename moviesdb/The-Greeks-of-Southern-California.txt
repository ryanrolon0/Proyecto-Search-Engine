The Greeks of Southern California

 The Greeks! The Hellenes! 'Oi Ellines!' These proud, passionate , and industrious people migrated to America in the early 1900's. Many landed in New York and Boston, while the more adventuresome headed due west to California. Despite their hardships and struggles, these Greeks displayed courage, perseverance, and unyielding faith to prosper as 'Americans.' Listen to these humble souls retell their remarkable stories in 'The Pioneers.'
