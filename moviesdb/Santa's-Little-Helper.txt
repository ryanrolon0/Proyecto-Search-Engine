Santa's Little Helper

 Cindy knows it's going to be a bad day when her blind date is late, a gorgeous looking man comes towards her but then turns away to another man and Peter, an old friend, annoys her with his talking. Just when Cindy thinks she has had it all, the blind date finally appears. Jack, completely dressed in elf suit, claims to have Santa as his boss. Cindy doesn't believe the lunatic and wants to leave. But Jack knows something from Cindy's childhood and that draws her interest. Could Jack give her the key to real love?
