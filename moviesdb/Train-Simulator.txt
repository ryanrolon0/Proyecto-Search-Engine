Train Simulator

 Now you can say "On the board!", right in your own home. Now you can operate steam locomotives, diesel engines and electric trains from your mouse and keyboard. Now you can get the experience of a train operator. Train Simulator makes it possible! Microsoft introduced the first Train Simulator to Windows, something many have been waiting for. Now fans get to experience how it's like being a train operator and conductor. Select some of the most popular, amazing routes from the United States and Japan and explore through the ambitious, 3-D world on the tracks. Each route were carefully designed to look like a clone of the real route. Discover the route's traffic signs, tunnels, bridges, rail crossing, track switches, deers, moving vehicles and many more.
