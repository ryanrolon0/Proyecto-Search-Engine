Taipei

 Lifestyle changes abound as Aaron, a Black American journalist, gets more than he bargained for when he is captivated by Xiao-mei, a beautiful Chinese choreographer. Despite a physical confrontation with Da-wen, Xiao-mei's hotheaded brother, Aaron and Xiao-mei continue their courtship. Overwhelmed by the charm and spirit of bustling Taipei City and their passion for one another, they consummate their relationship with a mesmerizing pas de deux. In accepting different customs and cultures, they prove the old adage 'men are men and women are women' is universally genuine.
