Rage Against the Darkness

 A documentary about life in nursing homes presented in 3 episodes. "Bunny and Leona" Two sisters split apart into seperate nursing homes after 30 years of living together. "Gert's Secret" A 102-year-old woman struggle to maintain her happiness. "Living Dangerously" Phillip Rowley and Helen Beck try to cope with life without being sent to a nursing home.
