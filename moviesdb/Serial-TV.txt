Serial TV

 Lance Red, the creator of Serial TV, is a fugitive wanted for aiding and abetting murders during the show. Serial TV is in essence, "The Real World" with serial killers. Initially, the serial killers follow their contractual obligations to not harm their house mates, but frustrations soon get the better of them and they begin to murder the show's crew. In order to cover up the murder and keep the most highly rated show on TV alive, Lance decides to hide the bodies.
