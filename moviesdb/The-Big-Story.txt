The Big Story

 Two small-time thugs kidnap a senator's daughter. The ransom isotope the U.S. government developed as a black project. Soon the first thugs run afoul of a gang of international terrorists who capture the isotope, the first pair of thugs, the senator's daughter, a bevy of beautful government agents and a brace of superheroines. Can a lone operative rescue the kidnapped ladies before they all come to grief?
