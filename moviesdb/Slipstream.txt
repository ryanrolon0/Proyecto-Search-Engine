Slipstream

 Slipstream - A slipstream is a region behind a moving object in which a wake of fluid (typically air or water) is moving at velocities comparable to the moving object. Inversely it is also a genre of fantastic or non-realistic fiction that crosses conventional genre boundaries between science fiction/fantasy and mainstream literary fiction. An animation celebrating the life of artist, comedian, actor, and rapper Rudy Ray Moore. This film was screened at Festival of (In)Appropriation at UCLA, New York Film Anthology Summer Series 2010, and Calarts Experimental Animation Student Showcase 2010.
