The Micro-Climates of Clint

 Clint, a Native American Gay Man with HIV, invites us to accompany him on a Spiritual Road Trip through the many Micro-Climates of his lived life. His story begins alongside his Family Cemetery on the San Isidro Indian Pueblo where Clint was born. It ends in San Francisco's, AIDS Memorial Grove. Between, Clint experiences Mormon Baptisms, working in the Mail Room of Twentieth Century Fox, homelessness on the streets of San Francisco, rescue by a Transsexual named Roxanne and the death of his companion dog Zonda.. The wisdom and heart gained from his wanderings bring Clint to Epiphany. Painting his journey, using art to reach back to help youth are what matter's to him, Clint creates a mask making program for Children with HIV.,
