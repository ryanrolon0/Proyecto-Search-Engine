The Tub

 Joel is an antique collector and his pride and joy is an old antique bathtub, where an old film legend had supposedly died. Joel and his Wife Geneva have a strained marriage. They each seek outlets from their private lives and when Joel heads away for the weekend to visit an old film director, Otto who was connected to the tub's history, Gen brings her young lover over. But a passionate moment inside the mysterious bath will prove fatal, leaving Gen in a compromising position to await her husbands' return. Gen has a plan. Joel has a plan. But Otto has plans of his own.
