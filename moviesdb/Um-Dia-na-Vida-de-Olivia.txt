Um Dia na Vida de Olivia

 Olivia is a twenty year old girl who's about to turn twenty one but doesn't know what to do with her life yet. She is not in college and doesn't have a job, lives with her boyfriend because she can't stand her dysfunctional mother and has no point in life. The movie shows a day in her life, the day where she learns about life, love, herself and people around her.
