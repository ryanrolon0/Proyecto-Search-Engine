Shin Angel

 After high-school perverts Keisuke and Atami have a run-in with the "woman with the disappearing face," it seems that they have had an encounter with a real ghost. The problem worsens when Keisuke is being haunted by this spirit. With only his girlfriend, Shizuka, on his side, Keisuke begins a couragious quest to confront this ghost and solve this haunting problem once and for all. His quest turns out to have a couple of unexpected sexual situations, however......
