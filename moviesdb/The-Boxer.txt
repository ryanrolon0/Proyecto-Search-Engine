The Boxer

 Sugar Ray's personal life has been a captivating, colorful roller coaster, awash in both strife and success. Now, the story of one of the most vibrant and exciting athletes in history comes to the big screen in this long anticipated, first-hand account of the man, the boxer, and one of the most legendary figures of our time.
