The Door

 A young man named Chris tries hard to impress his girlfriend Allison, but quickly finds out it is harder than it seems. Trying to redeem himself, Chris gets overtaken by an unknown entity that sets its sights on Allison. Now aware of the situation, Allison tries in vain to protect herself but all that comes to end when she is met behind The Door. A young man, Chris, (Howell) tries hard to impress his girlfriend, Allison, (Ochmanek) but quickly finds out it is harder than it seems. While trying to redeem himself, Chris gets overtaken by an unknown entity that has been release by one of Allison's friends. The creature immediately sets its sights on Allison. Unaware of the situation, Allison tries in vain to protect herself, but soon comes face to face with the creature behind The Door.
