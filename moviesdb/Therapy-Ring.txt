Therapy Ring

 When a recently divorced teacher Patrick Cullen joins a dysfunctional divorce support group, he gets a crazy idea to seek revenge on his ex by stealing her engagement ring. The kicker? It just so happens that his exes fiance is planning on giving her the former first lady, Jackie O's ring! Patrick, undeterred by this huge undertaking, decides to involve this wacky group of divorcees and split the proceeds, all in the name of revenge! This comedy heist is filled with spoof air supply songs, and our star's original track, 'Single Fellas'!
