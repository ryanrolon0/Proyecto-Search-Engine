The Answer

 When John Middlebrook loses his job, he literally doesn't know which way to go. He encounters a mysterious boy who tells him to "follow his heart," and indicates that John should wait inside a nearby phone booth. John is faced with the biggest decision of his life continue on? Middlebrook's choice brings him face to face with skeptics, thugs, an angry landlord, a bitter ex co-worker, and even the NYPD in a quest to find The Answer.
