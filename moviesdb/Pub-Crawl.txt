Pub Crawl

 It's a movie about a Pub Crawl in Camden, London. It takes place in a one day period on the day of the Grand National. Old friends re-live the days and memories of their youth. Ray, an Internet Entrepreneur and Bill, an out of work press photographer, have their first pint of the day in the Halfway House. They meet up with painter brothers Tony and Paul. Mick a publican tips them an Irish Raider to win the big race. Enter, Bob a film producer who they meet at the Fiddlers Elbow. The guys watch the race and Ray wins £7,000 pounds from Mick's tip of Numbersixvalverde. To celebrate the winner they start a pub crawl around Camden.
