Trois de la marine

 Antonin, HonorE and Papillote are sailors on the ship "L'Invincible". The three friends are in love with three pretty girls in Toulon love with Mireille who, as for her, loves Antonin, and Papillote only has eyes for Angele who, after a fifteen-month-long cruise,presents him a newborn baby that she claims is their son! A new woman appears as sexy as she is mysterious. Antonin, although disconcerted, falls under her spell. What he does not know is that Patricia is not a journalist as she says she is but a secret service agent who has been assigned the mission to investigate Eric Bergen, a shady fellow the trio meets everyday at Arsene's CafE...
