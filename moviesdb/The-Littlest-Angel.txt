The Littlest Angel

 Recently arrived in heaven, 5-year-old "LITTLEST ANGEL" wants to return to Earth with his friend HALO, a cute little talking dog with wings, in order to retrieve his treasure box which he misses. While on Earth he is abducted by the creepy owners of a Traveling Freak Show. LITTLEST is finally rescued by his angel friends and returns to heaven just in time for their Christmas Party at which LITTLEST offers up his treasure box as a Christmas present. Based on the 15th best-selling hard-copy children's book of all time by Charles Tazewell, with over 15 million copies sold worldwide in all major languages.
