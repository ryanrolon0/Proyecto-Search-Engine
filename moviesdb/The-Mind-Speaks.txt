The Mind Speaks

 One the mind begins to speak, there could be a no way out of the inner dark world. 'Stop talking' we say, but the mind won't listen, it got his own special way. Searching for God and trying to get all the answers to our questions, is a common thing for today's society. We never find the answers, we pretend that all is well on our outside world, but the inside has it's own world of hate, confusion, misunderstanding and blame. But how to get rid of all?
