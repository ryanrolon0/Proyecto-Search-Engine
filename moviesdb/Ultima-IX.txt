Ultima IX

 The final adventure of the Avatar, he has been called upon to save a dying Britannia from the Guardian's machinations, an adventure from which he has been warned that there will be no return from to either Britannia or Earth. The Guardian has assaulted Britannia, erecting stone columns that have corrupted the 8 Virtues, and the Avatar must destroy the source of the Columns and purify the Virtues before Britannian falls upon Britannian.
