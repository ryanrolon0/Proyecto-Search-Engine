Star Trek Voyager

 Lt. Commander Tuvok, chief officer of the USS Voyager, has started a force against the dangerous races of the Delta Quadrant. You play as a member of this Elite Force of Starfleet Security officers as you battle the Borg, Hirogen, Species 8472, and other races of the Delta Quadrant. Your arsenal includes weapons seen on Voyager, as well as new alien and prototypical Starfleet weapons.
