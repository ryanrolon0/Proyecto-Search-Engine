Schnee in der Neujahrsnacht

 Episodes from the 1999 Sylvester night in Berlin, accompanied by the blind radio-DJ Zippo as a bus driver on this day. In the bus is a corpse with a suitcase full of coke, which belongs to Totos' prison-pal Frank. Then there is the desperate woman who is pregnant and above their heads is ex pilot Rory, who has a devious plan to kill his unfaithful wife... Moreover, a bear ran away from the zoo!
