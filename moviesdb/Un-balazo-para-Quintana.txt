Un balazo para Quintana

 A beautiful woman called Angel's Eyes (alias Quintana) has come back to defeat Woman God, the leader of crime. However, Woman God has hired the best killers in town to keep her empire intact and also to kill Quintana over an account that is past due. The hunting begins for a reward of one million dollars. This is the story of a fight between two fantastic women, the power within their mafia and the world of hired assassins.
