Vampire Winter

 They are the most dangerous supernatural creatures on earth. They are immortal, insatiable, ingenious, and irresistible. For centuries, a single force has kept mankind safe from their domination Until now...A team of scientists has created the ultimate weapon, one that could trigger a nuclear winter of eternal night. To gain control of this weapon, a master vampire, Gallow, will send his vampire mistresses to seduce the scientists and make them his undead slaves. The vampires' only obstacle weakness he commands her to destroy Don and Paula and help bring about the vampire vision of eternal night...
