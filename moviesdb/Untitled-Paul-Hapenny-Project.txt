Untitled Paul Hapenny Project

 DOWNCITY is a gritty thriller surrounding corrupt cops in Providence, RI. Corrupt Detective Tommy McCarthy and his long-time partner and friend, Alex Hawkins, are being squeezed by Internal Affairs Captain Kelly. Kelly has flipped Hawkins, holding a prison sentence over his head to set McCarthy up. Hawkins, a black cop, is married to a white woman. That causes tension between Hawkins and the overtly racist Captain. McCarthy has to use every bit of street knowledge to save himself and Hawkins.
