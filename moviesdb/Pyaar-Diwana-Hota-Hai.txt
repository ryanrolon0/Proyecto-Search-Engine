Pyaar Diwana Hota Hai

 Naive, easily influenced and illiterate Sunder re-locates from a village to the city. His attempts to woo girls are mocked, and he is ignored and made fun of by his room-mates. One day he befriends an NRI, Payal Khurana, who thinks he is disabled and dumb. She feels sorry for him and decides to assist him get his voice back - albeit in vain. Sunder is thrilled at the attention he is getting, and decides to continue to pull wool over her eyes to be near her. The question remains how long will he continue to fool her?
