Specjalny Osrodek Szkolno-Wychowawczy Ballet

 An experimental film that revolves around a performance by the children of the Special School of Podgorki (SOSW) in Poland. Choreographed by the children of SOSW and directed by Anna Gaskell, using music from ballets such as La Sylphide, Cinderella, La Bayedere, and others, to create a completely new ballet. SOSW is a 40-year old academy and boarding school for general education students and children with mild to severe learning disorders from ages eight to 18.
