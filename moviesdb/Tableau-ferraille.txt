Tableau ferraille

 Tells the story of an idealistic young politician's rise and fall. Daam, a well-intentioned but vacillating European-trained politician must choose between two social paradigms exemplified by his two wives. The first, Gagnesiri, is the village beauty, who waits patiently for Daam. Unfortunately, they are unable to conceive a child, so Daam takes European-educated KinE, who is eager to get ahead by marrying a politician. Daam becomes involved in a shady business deal with PrEsident, a local businessman; when the details are made public, he is forced out in disgrace.
