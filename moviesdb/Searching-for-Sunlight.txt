Searching for Sunlight

 Carrie and Dean live the American Dream. Married for 6 years, they co own a successful car dealership in a Philadelphia suburb. With their spacious house and adventurous vacations, they seem to have it all and yet their marriage is on the brink of divorce. Carrie has suffered from a lifelong depression. For which Dean lacks sensitivity, according to Carrie. But is it really that simple? As Carrie continues to search for sunlight, questions are raised that make you wonder exactly what is going on behind these troubled eyes?
