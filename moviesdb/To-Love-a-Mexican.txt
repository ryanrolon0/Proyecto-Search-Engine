To Love a Mexican

 Enter the world of Rae Bristol who thinks she has the ideal life with her boyfriend Richard until she meets Megan while attending seminars in Las Vegas, who enlightens her about how much her life is controlled by him. Later, by chance, she meets a Mexican while playing blackjack, who bestows a passion-filled, magical kiss upon her giving her only one choice... To love a Mexican. From there, Rae only finds herself in more trouble than before. Topping off with her two friends Ruby and Emily with their own relationship issues. Rae's struggle with her own self-confidence and choice making pulls her through to a very sweet ending.
