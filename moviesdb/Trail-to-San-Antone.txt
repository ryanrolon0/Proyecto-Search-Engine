Trail to San Antone

 Gene is out to help a crippled jockey when a wild stallion runs away with the speedy mare he plans for the jockey to ride, so Gene takes off in an airplane to bring them back. Gene's hands have bought a stolen horse. When it escapes Gene has to pay for it. He catches the thief, a horse trainer. Learning he did it for injured brother, a jockey, he puts them to work on his ranch. Now he has to find the horse, have it trained hoping the brother will be able to ride it in the big race.
