Simple Wishes

 Los Angeles has the fifth largest economy in the world; and it is the 2nd most populated metropolis in the nation. Los Angeles is also the nation's homeless capital, containing no less than 17, 000 homeless children. With the compelling stories of four families, you will be taken through their journey and how they overcame homelessness through transitional housing. The issue needs to be brought out to the public or the great tragedy will continue to grow.
