Rollerboy

 From a suburban backyard to the world stage, Jayson Sutcliffe 'Rollerboy' skated his way to the top; his dream fueled by an insatiable quest for fame and a deep personal secret that drives his alter ego to achieve his dream at all costs. Across 20 years Jayson embarks on a quest for gold that sees him competing against his hero, devising sensational performances that challenge stereotypes and break rules. During his career Jayson won 13 international medals including 4 golds.
