The Apology

 Self exiled for his sexuality and political beliefs, a legendary showman escapes from Iran after the Islamic revolution. Thirteen years later, while living in Germany, he's offered a safe passage home to see his dying mother if he publicly renounces and repents for his actions and beliefs. Beliefs that have come to define him as an anti-fundamentalist activist and cultural hero. He struggles to reconcile the ideological with the personal as his very life hangs in the balance.
