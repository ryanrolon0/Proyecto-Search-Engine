Surveiller les tortues

 After being laid off from their fish deep-freezing factory, two workers, Esther and AndrE accept a temporary job offer. They are assigned to look after the turtles of a well off couple while they are on vacation. After a couple of days spent in the luxurious villa, Esther and AndrE neglect the house and spend all their time in the garden. They finally take French leave and join a groups of fishermen, by the side of a lazy river.
