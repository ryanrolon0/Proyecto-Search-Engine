Puls

 Marta is a vampire who is trying to adjust to a life with humans working as a doctor in an emergency room. But at the same time Marta is a single mother who is trying to deal with her rebellious son who is going through puberty. While Marta is concealing his crimes asking herself how many victims it takes for a vampire to finally get through puberty, two detectives, the rational Andja and imaginative Danilo are trying to catch the serial killer who seems to fall in love easily.
