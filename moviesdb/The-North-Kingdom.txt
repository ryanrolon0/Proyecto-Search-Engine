The North Kingdom

 This is a love story about a girl, Siobhan, who is torn between her lover, Prince Padrig and the guy that she was forced to marry, Lord Killian. Siobhan sneaks out of her residence to find her lover, Padrig, but while looking for Padrig she runs into Lord O'Braonain, the man responsible for slaying her father. In the mean time Killian finds the dead body of O'Brainain, and then finds her with Padrig. Killian, in a rage of the past events, takes on Padrig for the right to claim Siobhan.
