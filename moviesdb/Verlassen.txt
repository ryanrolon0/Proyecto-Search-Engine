 'Christoph Stark' (qv)'s family drama deals with a divorced couple, searching for their daughter, who had run away to Italy... Claudia wants to move to Berlin with her new partner, but her daughter Julia destroys her plans, when she steals her car and runs away to Italy with her boyfriend. Thus, Claudia and her ex-husband Dieter have to go there together, which opens up old sores...
