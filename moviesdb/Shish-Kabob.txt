Shish Kabob

 Sako Markarian, an Armenian American ex-gangster has returned from service in Iraq. War has changed him. However, he is drawn back into his old lifestyle in order to protect his cousin Vahe against Gago, his former boss in the Armenian underworld. Sako takes revenge against Gago after Vahe is killed. Sako's Mexican friends come to his aid and all hell breaks loose. At the same, Sako's cultural values are put to the test as he tries to develop a relationship with Anoush, a beautiful Armenian woman. Anoush's love for Sako goes against her family's wishes, who disapproves of Sako's background. Sako is tormented over the loss of life in his community, family, and friends. Revenge runs it's full course as the Mexicans line up behind Sako against Gago's forces. Planned execution style killings are the order of the day. Can Sako draw from his experiences at war to bring peace to both factions and end the cycle of violence?
