Real with Me

 REAL WITH ME is a post 9/11 satirical coming of age story about a man's journey for stardom only to lose love. One year after 9/11 out of work actor Finn Reilly decides this is the year that he will become a star. Working as bartender at a downtown NYC nightclub Finn meets Leticia Castillo. An actor herself Leticia sees through Finn's superficial ambitions and falls hard. Through the course of this rocky relationship Finn and Leticia both realize that in order to have what they want they must accept who they are. Executive Produced by acclaimed producer Susan Batson ('Raisin in the Sun'),
