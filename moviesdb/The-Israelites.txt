The Israelites

 Just when you thought celebrity was useless think again. Monica, a famous actress, moves back to her hometown when comments she made about the government create a negative media frenzy. While living back home she reconnects with her childhood sweetheart, Leo, and the secrets he confides in her during his time working in "the army" confirm the alternate reality she's trying to expose. Can they get the word out to the masses before its too late?
