Torment

 After returning home from a business trip, Alice White discovers that her fiancEe has gone missing. While on a frantic hunt to find her boyfriend, she enlists the help of police detective Elyse Cote and a helpful lawyer friend Samantha Lewis. Together they push past all the obstacles in order to discover the truth about the disappearance. The plot thickens when they accidentally discover that the disappearance might be connected to a rash of kidnappings in the area. They discover soon enough that nothing is as it seems.
