Stealing God's Money

 Two criminals, Luke and Jordan, trying desperately to get out of their lifestyle in crime, take on their last job - a kidnapping. There is just one problem. They kidnap the worst possible kid. Stephen doesn't want to go home, and his parent's don't want him back. But the rest of the crime world around them doesn't know that. With a Stephen's life now hanging over their heads, Luke and Jordan have to figure out what to do with him before it's too late.
