Tonatiuh, Father Sun

 Follow as a young man unravels the mystery and ancient history behind the name his father gave him. Our protagonist learns that he is not named after a European-style God as he has believed his entire life, He was named, in fact, after Tonatiuh, the Mayan Father Sun. This exploration is interwoven with other stories of human migration and the difference a person can make in the life of another. Would Jesus the Christ, if he were alive today, honor national lines in the sand?
