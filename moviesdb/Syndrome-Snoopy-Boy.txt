Syndrome Snoopy Boy

 A custodian, tormented by an obsession over security, starts spying his young neighbor, a Muslim of Arab origin and suspects him to be a terrorist. The young Arab shaves off his beard, and in the eyes of the custodian, this simple detail awakes a series of serious suspicions. The results of his interpretations of the innocent acts appear all arbitrary and irrational, but in same time, sarcastic and hilarious.
