The Booby Trap

 Doug and Luke have just found the biggest discovery in their adolescent lives be the coolest thing since water balloons and dinosaurs! When the tape turns out to be a harsh lesson in human anatomy, the boys are horrified. To make matters worse, Mom has just come home and the tape is stuck in the VCR! Now Doug must scramble, or risk losing everything he loves innocence, boyhood normalcy, and TV privileges.
