The Product of 3c

 In a world saturated by media it's hard to know one's owns truth... or so it is for Michael Halley, who suffers a traumatic event in childhood and carries that experience into adulthood and his wildly successful career as a top New York advertising executive. Burying his trauma by turning it into a product. This is a story, or haiku if you will, with little dialogue, told through images, photographs and sound, evoking a memory or dream.
