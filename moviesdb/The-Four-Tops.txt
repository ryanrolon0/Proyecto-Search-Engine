The Four Tops

 'Reach Out - Definitive Performances 1965-1973' showcases 22 legendary performances by the hit-making Motown group. From rare local television clips to celebrated appearances on the national stage, unreleased a cappella mixes of such songs as 'Reach Out I'll Be There,' 'Baby I Need Your Loving' and more, taken directly from their original Motown session reels, as well as many vintage and recently recorded interviews.
