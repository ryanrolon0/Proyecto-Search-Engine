Tæt på troldmanden George Lucas

 Television special about the restoration and 1997 re-release of the Star Wars trilogy. 'Helle Retbøll Carl' (qv) interviews director 'George Lucas' (qv), producer 'Rick McCallum (I)' (qv) and staff at Industrial Light & Magic on the restoration process and the whole idea of re-releasing the classic films to a new generation. Featuring clips from the trilogy and 1976 rehearsing test footage of 'Harrison Ford (I)' (qv) and 'Hamill, Mark (I)' (qv).
