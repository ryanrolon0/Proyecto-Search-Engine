The Old Corral

 As the sheriff of a small western town, Autry sings his way into a relationship with Eleanor, a singer from a Chicago nightclub who earlier witnessed a murder. After seeing gangster Scarlotti murder a man, Eleanor Spenser heads west. Recognizing her by her picture in the paper, the local saloon owner hires her and also informs Scarlotti. Scarlotti arrives to get rid of Eleanor but Sheriff Autry has also seen the picture and he sets out to not only protect her but to also get Scarlotti.
