UFO Invasion at Rendlesham

Now SCI FI brings you this shocking exposE, complete with documentation, new physical evidence and firsthand accounts by both military and civilian witnesses - including Penniston and Halt, who have jeopardized their reputations and military pensions in order to seek out the truth. Join host Bryant Gumbel as SCI FI presents its exclusive investigation of one of the most important UFO incidents of the 20th century.
