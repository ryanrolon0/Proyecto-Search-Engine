Thoroughly Modern Mili

 International hero or immoral thug? You be the judge! Join BBS journo Dean Iderathernot as he uncovers the life of a foreign mercenary posted to the U.S. to help with the war on terror. Milicent-Therese (Mili-Teri, to her friends) is the first female French Foreign Legion captain to be invited to the U.S. to carry out a top-secret mission.
