University Heights

 A story of life. A story of love. A story of hate. A story of guilt. "University Heights" tells the story of four lives on a college campus which have been torn apart - lives which could not be more different than the other. Throughout the course of a few days, each past is revealed - and each future will be uncovered. A tale of drugs, relationships, sexuality, racism, and deceit, "University Heights" grasps the everyday struggles of humanity - finding yourself.
