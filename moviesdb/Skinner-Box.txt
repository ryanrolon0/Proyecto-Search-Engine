Skinner Box

 When sorority hopeful Samantha turns up at the local ER bound and gagged, with a bag over her head, the race is on to find her captor. As her state of mind is severely fragile, questionable psychological tactics may be the only way to deconstruct Samantha's steps in their search for answers.
