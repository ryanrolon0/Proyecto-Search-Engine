The Classroom

 Ian is an average man who has struggled to attain the American Dream and be a success in the eyes of the world. But no matter how much energy he puts into the things he thinks are important to be happy, the more estranged his relationship with his wife, Tai becomes. The story begins with Ian revisiting the classroom where he met Tai twelve years ago. He is in a life and death situation that will gravely affect the future of his relationship and he needs answers. Miraculously, Tai shows up and gives him the information that will save his life... but has it come at the cost of hers?
