The Adventure of Kokochin

 Princess Kokochin, the daughter of the Chinese emperor, is escorted to Persia by Marco Polo for her marriage to the King. But after Marco leaves, Kokochin finds herself alone in a foreign court and brutalized by her husband. She seeks solace in a sensual relationship with a fortune-teller but when the couple are betrayed, they flee along the Silk Road. Attacked by bandits, the lovers are separated and Kokochin sold to a slave house. Surrounded by cruelty, Kokochin must dig deep inside herself to find reserves of courage to survive and continue her journey...
