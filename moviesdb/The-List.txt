The List

 Mimona Njoye' is a beautiful, aspiring actress and model who catches the attention of an obsessed talent agent whose 'list' is made up of entertainers with bad professional habits. If his efforts to take control of their careers and guide them to success meet with resistance and they continue to display characteristics similar to those of his abusive mother, who was a failure, he takes them off his list, killing them. Starring Timberly Wilson as 'Mimona Njoye' and Kewin Muhammad as 'Kewin Rowe'. Failure is not an option!
