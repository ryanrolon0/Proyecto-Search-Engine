Thami's Gift

 Thami decides to make herself pregnant so that she can give her sister, Dumisa, a baby because Dumisa is unable to have children of her own. Thami is unaware of how pregnancy works or how babies are actually made. She is falsely led to believe that a simple kiss from a boy will make her pregnant, Thami begins her unforgettable journey which will change her life forever. Thami is a well-meaning young girl whose best intentions collide with misinformation to produce hilarious results. Desperate to help her older sister who is unable to conceive, she makes it her mission to help, and unwittingly embarks on what becomes a voyage of self-discovery in the unlikeliest places.
