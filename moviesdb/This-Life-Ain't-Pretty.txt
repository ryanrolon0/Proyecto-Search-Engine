This Life Ain't Pretty

 A socially conscious film that challenges the stereotypical beliefs associated with H.I.V./A.ID.S. in the African-American community. Backed by sobering statistics, extending beyond the Black community and spiraling into a national effort to raise awareness, its message is one of triumph over adversity, and hope, in the face of tragedy. In a gripping, one of a kind story, Brittney Nicole Lawrence's life is changed forever, in a single breath, with the utterance of four letters...A.I.D.S. Inspired by a true story, the film takes an intriguing look at the disease, and chronicles the journey of a young, attractive, heterosexual African-American woman that learns that she is HIV positive in the prime of her life.
