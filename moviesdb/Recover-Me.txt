Recover Me

 Recover Me is a reality based drama that illustrates how a single mother battling a drug addiction in an impoverished community in Richmond Virginia, faces all odds as she strives toward positive change, love and financial security. After attaining the things she thought impossible, she must again struggle to maintain her sense of self when a crude twist of fate challenges her once again. Recover Me entertwines multiple struggles of the characters and delivers a fresh urban mix of music, culture and breathtaking Martial Arts. Will prove to be entertaining and inspiring.
