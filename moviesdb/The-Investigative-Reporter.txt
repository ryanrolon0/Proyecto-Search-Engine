The Investigative Reporter

 A buxom reporter attempts to get the goods on a criminal mastermind. She breaks into his house and discovers incriminating photographs in his bedroom. Unfortunately, the house isn't as empty as the reporter believed; the bad guy manages to sneak up behind her and knock her out. When the reporter regains consciousness, she discovers she's being held as a naked prisoner by a pair of bondage fiends who - between interrogation sessions - bind, gag and torment the buxom beauty with clothespins, vibrators and tickling sessions.
