Van Gogh

 A film short on the making of _Lust for Life (1956)_ (qv) which starred 'Kirk Douglas (I)' (qv) and 'Anthony Quinn (I)' (qv). This behind the scenes look focuses primarily the various European locations used in the filming including the coal mines, Holland and Arles, in the south of France. It also shows some of the challenges the production faced, including rebuilding various buildings used in some of Van Gogh's paintings. This promotional short takes the viewer to the European locations used in the filming of _Lust for Life (1956)_ (qv), which tells the story of artist Vincent Van Gogh. To make the picture as authentic as possible, the film was shot in many of the locations where Van Gogh spent his life, including the actual buildings in which he lived and worked.
