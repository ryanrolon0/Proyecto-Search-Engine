Scarface

 Based on the movie of the same name, Scarface puts you in the role of Tony Montana, a ruthless gangster, during, and after the climactic final scene of the film, in which Tony is holed up in his office with rival drug lord Sosa's agents amassing outside his door. Tony escape's the gun fight, killing his enemies. Now he's left with nothing, his family gone, his friends gone, all he has left is really his balls and his word. He must travel through various locations (Miami, the Bahamas, etc.) and get revenge on Sosa, along the way, rebuilding the Empire he once had and assuming power in 1980s drug-filled Miami. "I came here on a boat. Not a dime in my pocket. I had friends double crossed by greed. Now Sosa thinks he's gonna take everything i ever had. He thinks i'm going to lay down and die... But Tony Montana don't die that easy. First you get back the money. Then you take back the power. Then, you get the women. But then, you get revenge!" They thought Tony was dead, but they were wrong. Tony is back! Be the original, be Tony Montana in this video game sequel to the legendary movie. Tony has lost everything, but that never stopped him before. Climb your way back to the top through drug deals and brutal intimidation. Buy fronts in Miami and slowly rebuild your empire. Become the Kingpin of a thriving underworld economy and then get revenge on the one who took everything away from you... Sosa!
