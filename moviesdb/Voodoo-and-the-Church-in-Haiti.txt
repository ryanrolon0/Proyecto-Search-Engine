Voodoo and the Church in Haiti

 In the Caribbean nation of Haiti, voodoo is still widely practiced. Haiti is a country of many contrasts and voodoo/Catholicism. Despite the efforts of the Catholic Church, most poor villagers practice some form of voodoo, a religion which has been passed down in the villages since the nineteenth century. Voodoo and the Church in Haiti examines Haitian life in general and some voodoo practices in particular.
