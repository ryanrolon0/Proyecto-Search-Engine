Sisterhood of the Shewolf

 In the sleepy little town of Los Lobos, the locals are used to strange happenings and deadly 'coyote attacks'. When feisty city girl Rhonda (Beverly Lynne) trails her missing girl friend to the town's ominous Lycan institute, she's convinced something dreadful has happened and will stop at nothing till she's found her friend. CVan Rhonda's courage be enough to defeat this savage beast and uncover the Shewolf's dark secret?
