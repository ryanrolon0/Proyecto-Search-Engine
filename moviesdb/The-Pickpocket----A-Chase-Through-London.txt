The Pickpocket -- A Chase Through London

 A pickpocket distracts a well-dressed man on the sidewalk and steals his watch. When the man suddenly notices what the pickpocket is doing, the thief knocks him down and runs off. A crowd quickly gathers, and police officers give chase. The desperate criminal steals a bicycle and tries every other trick he can think of, in an attempt to escape. A pickpocket is caught in the act, and leads the police on a lengthy chase.
