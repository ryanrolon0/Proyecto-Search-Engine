True Story

 John is a nightwatch at a hospital, where his collegue Claus is having an affair with John's ex, the nurse Rikke. On TV the re-construction show 'True Story', hosted by Harry J. Rubbenfish, tells about death and tragedies. Inspired by the show John decides to take vengeance upon Claus. Suddenly mysterious things start happenening at the hospital, and Harry J. Robbenfish seems somehow connected.
