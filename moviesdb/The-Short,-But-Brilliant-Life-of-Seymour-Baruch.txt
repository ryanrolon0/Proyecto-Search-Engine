The Short, But Brilliant Life of Seymour Baruch

 It's Seymour's birthday...or is it? And who's that little girl? Maybe faith, maybe hope, but definitely not justice, as Seymour Baruch can find no justice in life. Day after day, Baruch raises a cocked gun to his head, playing a game of Russian roulette with the devil's advocate. Yet, day after day, Baruch is rejected from hell, heaven, or whatever God has to offer.
