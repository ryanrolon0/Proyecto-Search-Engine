To the Dogs, Part I

 A transformative Irish Mexican crime movie about true power. A young man's losses propel his fall from integrity into the world of crime. The action occurs in mid-Century California, a turbulent place where Mexicans and Irish struggle for land and money. The hero, young Jack, fights for survival and power in this world. And with his rise, he falls to the gutter, to the dogs, where also lies the power of redemption and the knowledge that true power comes from within. TO THE DOGS provides rich characters, plenty of action, and dynamic entertainment for our transforming times.
