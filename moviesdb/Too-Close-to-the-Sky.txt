Too Close to the Sky

 The `new` generation are breaking loose in Japan rebelling against accepted thinking, protected from the reality of the past and threatening the key fabric of established society for perhaps the first time. But are they really the new generation or was it always like this? 'Too Close to the Sky' is an intense 24 hour journey inside the secrets, lies and tragedies of a modern Japanese family as a Father and Son come face to face not only with each other, but with the past.
