These Charming Men

 These Charming Men is a half-hour comedy series that surrounds the lives of three 20-something bachelors, Johnny, Tommy and Andy, as they struggle to achieve their dreams of becoming stars of the silver screen. Follow them in their day-to-day lives as they chase champagne dreams and caviar wishes amidst their menial jobs, constant rejection and the possibilities of meeting the love of their lives - or at least the love of that night.
