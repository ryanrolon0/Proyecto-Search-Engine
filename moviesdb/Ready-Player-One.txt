Ready Player One

 Film centers on a young outcast named Wade Watts. In the near future, Watts escapes from his daily drudgery by logging into an online game called 'The Oasis'. When the game's billionaire founder dies, he offers players his fortune as the prize in a virtual treasure hunt. Watts gets in on the action, but soon finds himself facing off against corporate foes who will go to any lengths to get the money -- in both the real world and in The Oasis.
