Under the Trees

 Frank Dorsey is a young, lonely man in need of his family. Frank lives alone because of an argument he had with his parents years back. On his way home from work one day Frank's truck 'Marty', breaks down on the desolate Old Canyon Highway. In the distance Frank sees a man hiding a large plastic bag in the foliage, the man gets in his vehicle and leaves. Frank decides to check out the scene, a decision which leads to a journey he will never forget.
