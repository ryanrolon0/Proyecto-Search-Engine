The Hairy Ainus

 The harrowing story of one man stared down by fate. Based loosely on a lost text by Balzac and set in a French shanty town The Hairy Ainus follows Jacques through his life. The Hairy Ainus is a story of gritty truths, of those who hang on to life by the hairs eventually to be brushed aside as our time comes. Existentially riveting, this is a true masterpiece of silent film. The Hairy Ainus is a story of those who slip through the cracks.
