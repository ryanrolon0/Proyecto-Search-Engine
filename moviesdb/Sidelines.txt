Sidelines

 Rhys is madly in love with Gregg; his partner from New York. He feels it is now time to tell Josh, his eldest son about their relationship. Follow this thought provoking short film about a relationship between father and son as it uncovers the effects of change within a family unit. It tempts us with the most unlikely of questions father with another man? Betrayed? Merciful? Keep an eye on Josh's journey as he discovers the answers that were up to this moment, unthinkable.
