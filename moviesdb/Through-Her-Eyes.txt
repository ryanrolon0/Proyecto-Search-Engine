Through Her Eyes

 Through Her Eyes is an account of a Muslim Indian army officer who after many years of service returns home to find that the woman he loved has turned to the convent as a dedicated nun. The loss of his love leads to a loss of his faith in God. With a strong opposition from his family, he then embarks on a journey to bring her back in his life. This enlightening tale illustrates the oneness of religion, the power of faith and the significance of spirituality in love.
