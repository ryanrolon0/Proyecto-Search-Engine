The Hurricane Kid

 Ryu is an up and coming MMA fighter who is trying to reach the championship level. But his fights don't end in the ring. The MMA organization that was once pure is now controlled by corporate moguls who control the outcome of the fights. With an array of punches so fast and powerful, Ryu is known as 'The Hurricane Kid".
