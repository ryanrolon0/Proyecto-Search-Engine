True Confessions of a Sushi Addict

 Tina had never eaten Sushi until her date took her to a Japanese restaurant. She never met her date again, but the sushi stayed. And in large quantities! Tina can't stop anymore. She has to eat sushi, nothing else tastes good anymore. Hundreds of dollars she spends on the Japanese fish delicacy. Until one time it goes wrong. Her credit cards are blocked. The ATM won't turn out, while the waitress waits for money. Tina is broke, but still addicted. There must be more ways to get sushi without paying.
