Tape Gagged Girls Can't Speak

 Beautiful babes are bound and tape-gagged in a number of vignettes. In the first story, a beautiful burglar breaks into the wrong house and, rather than call the cops, the would-be victim chooses to strip, bind and gag the perpetrator. In another tale, a beautiful party girl stumbles on another burglary attempt and is promptly bound and gagged, then humiliated when the crook exposes her ample breasts. In the next story a pair of foxy crooks has a falling out, with one burglar leaving her ex-partner trussed to a chair before making off with the goodies, but before the criminal can make her escape, she's apprehended, roped to a chair besides her partner and left to squeal behind the duct tape muzzling her mouth. In another yarn, two naive businesswomen are captured after removing substantial amounts of money from a bank machine, then robbed, bound to chairs and tape-gagged. In the last tale, a pretty Asian woman is bound in a ball-tie, silenced with adhesive tape and left to struggle on the floor.
