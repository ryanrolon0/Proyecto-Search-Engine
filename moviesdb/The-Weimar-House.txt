The Weimar House

 All is well in suburbia, USA, where Joan and Hank can go to church and fume at the newspaper with the peace of mind that their marriage is blessed by God and the state. But all that tranquility comes to an end when Gail and Alex move into the Weimar House across the street. This new couple's presence sets off a chain of events that culminates in a fiery showdown between the neighbors.
