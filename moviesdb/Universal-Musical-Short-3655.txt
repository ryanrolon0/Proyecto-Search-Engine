Universal Musical Short 3655

 This Universal musical featurette headlines 'Maynard Ferguson' (qv) and His Orchestra wailing on "Wailing Boat"; 'Peggy Ryan' (qv) and 'Ray MacDonald' (qv) dancing two numbers (which may have been 'borrowed' from "Shamrock Hill", which was not a Universal production); The Sabres with a vocal and instrument number; and songs by Russ Arno and 'The DeCasto Sisters' (qv).
