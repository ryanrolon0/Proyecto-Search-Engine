Secrets to Savor

 At the reading of her father's Will, newly divorced Julie finds out that she is now the joint owner of a restaurant that her father co-owned in Miami Beach for the last 40 years. Because they were always so close she doesn't understand why he never told her about it. She decides to start a new life in Florida and embarks on a journey of discovery. Julie uncovers the mysterious secrets her father held for over 60 years tracing back to Germany in WWII.
