The Great Coney Island Egg Toss

 A July 4th Coney Island tradition that you never heard of -- The Great Coney Island Egg Toss. Each July 4th, on the sidewalk beside the famous handball courts of Coney Island -- specifically, on West 5th Street and Surf Avenue -- the handball players and beach visitors stage a very colorful contest. This documentary explores the 2010 Egg Toss.
