The Animatrix

 Anthology of nine short anime (Japanese animation) films tied in to the 1999 blockbuster "The Matrix" and its sequels. "Final Flight of the Osiris" imminent attack. "The Second Renaissance, Parts 1 & 2" the war between man and the machines, how mankind scorched the sky, and the creation of the Matrix. "Kid's Story" manages to escape the Matrix. "Program" training simulation when one decides to betray his crewmates and re-enter the Matrix. "World Record" the Matrix by sheer physical effort during a record attempt. "Beyond" young girl searching for her cat discovers a haunted house caused by a glitch in the system. "A Detective Story" a hacker named Trinity through the looking glass. "Matriculated" of humans capture a machine scout and insert it into a 'human matrix'.
