Tod einer Ärztin

 This summer there is no holiday for Kommissar Hunkeler. Instead of spending the vacation with his girlfriend Hedwig in Alsace, he has to deal with a mysterious case in the city of Basel liberal politician, has been murdered in her office. Among the suspects are several drug addicts who she supplied with Methadon. Also her rejected and despised son could have had a motive to kill his mother. But Hunkeler's instinct regarding the depths of human psyche leads to another track.
