Scab Girl Asylum

 Scab Girl Asylum is a documentary feature film that investigates one of Australia's horrific stories of mistreatment over many years. The film examines the story of Sue Treweek who was once a Forgotten Australian (Orphan) who at a young age was transfered into an adult mental Asylum. Her story is so horrific that it will amaze most people how she even survived to tell the tale.
