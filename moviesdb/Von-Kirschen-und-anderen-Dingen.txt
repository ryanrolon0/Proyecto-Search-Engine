Von Kirschen und anderen Dingen

 About four mentally disabled individuals who live and work in Hoehenberg. A small village in northern Bavaria, Germany. The Documentary focuses on Bastian, who is looking desperately for love and companionship in his daily life in the village. At the farm which he is working on there are also Jan and Petra occupied, who are a couple since many years. They have found what Bastian is still looking for. Also in need for a partner is Nadine. She is only 21 years old and just moved to the village.
