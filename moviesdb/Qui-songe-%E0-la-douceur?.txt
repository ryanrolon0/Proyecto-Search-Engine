Qui songe à la douceur?

 It is August. Mr. Naud, the manager of a household appliance store is in his garden, taking cuttings. This is the last day of Mr. Nauds's life, for he suffers from a brain tumor, but just now he is still alive and even if he falls to the ground three times on this beautiful summer day, he tries desperately to busy himself, to ease the pressure in his head, to dream his time away...
