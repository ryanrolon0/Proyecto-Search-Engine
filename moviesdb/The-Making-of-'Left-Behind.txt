The Making of 'Left Behind

 Janaya Stevens, who plays Choloe Steele in the movie, hosts a video in which actors from "Left Behind" and other Cloud Ten Picture films are interviewed, special effects designers, and even musical artists whose songs appeared in the film (while the singers had cameos). Including the interviewed cast are Peter and Paul Lalonde, Vic Sarin, Nick Mancuso, Brad Johnson, Kirk Cameron, Chelsea Noble, Clarence Gilyard, Mark Bradley Morrow, Margaret Caldwell, the band of Jake, Clay Crosse, Bob Carlisle, Rebecca St. James, Gordon Currie... etc.
