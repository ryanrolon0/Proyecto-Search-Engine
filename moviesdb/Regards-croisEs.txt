Regards croisEs

 This documentary takes a sensitive look at the daily lives of users, volunteers, and professionals in an ongoing social and cultural center, the "Paul Bert Network", located in the heart of Bordeaux. A population of men and women, on different paths and with different nationalities work, meet, come together, and hang out. Moments of life and views collected in this place where the actions are intended to rebuild politics, solidarity, and the possibility of living together.
