Untitled Dystopia

 'A Pre-Apocalyptic comedy about an Artist and her friend at the end of the world...' Untitled Dystopia follows Ajax an artist trying to complete her best piece of work before the imminent end of the world. She is joined by Henry a worryingly optimistic disembodied talking head whom came in the post one day. Perhaps the only person that can help is Morgan a lone figure not giving up in the bleakest of circumstances
