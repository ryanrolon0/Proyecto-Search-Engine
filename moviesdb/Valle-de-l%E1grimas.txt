Valle de lágrimas

 Lupe, an expectant mother working as a college fraternity house cook, is a warmhearted woman who is always there for others. Despite a life of poverty, she keeps an optimistic attitude and puts her fate in God's hands. Having just reunited with her husband JosE, who left her behind in their homeland to find work in the United States, she and JosE are excited to start their new life with a child. Things don't work out exactly as planned, and in a moment of desperation, JosE does what anyone would do find a way to survive.
