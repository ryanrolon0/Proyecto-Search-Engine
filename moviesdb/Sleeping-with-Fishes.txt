Sleeping with Fishes

 Keith an addicted gambler in South London, has 32hours to find £20,000. His addiction begins to ruin the lives for those he cares for, his girlfriend, Carla, a prostitute, has her own addiction, heroin. Matt, his brother in law, needs money to own his music business. Henry, fresh out of prison, wishes to sail away. Sean, obsessed and wired wants to organize one last job for them. Detective Sergeant McQueen in six figures of debt, becomes embroiled in blackmailing Keith to save his own neck. Their personal needs bring them together to secure each of their dreams through doing one last job together. As the day and addictions progress the storm worsens and the once silent waves are now crashing dangerously towards their eyes.
