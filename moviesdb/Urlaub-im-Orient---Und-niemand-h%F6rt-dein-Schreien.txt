Urlaub im Orient - Und niemand hört dein Schreien

 Martin and Nina have just won a dream vacation to Morocco. A luxury hotel and the exotic sights of Africa promised a perfect romantic getaway. But it turns into a nightmare when Martin awakes one morning to find his girlfriend missing. He rushes to the room of the couple's friend Hannah, only to find her dead. In panic, Martin desperately tries to find some help in locating Nina but to no avail. He heads out into the city to search for his girlfriend alone.
