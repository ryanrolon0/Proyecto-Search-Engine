Sete Minutos

 Filming of the play having the same name, telling the story of a theatrical company and what happens in a staging of Macbeth. One night, one of the Actors, the one playing Macbeth, interrupts the performance after he hears a cell phone ringing in the audience. From then on, the company undergoes a series of funny, burlesque, romantic, and even dramatic situations involving the actors and the audience.
