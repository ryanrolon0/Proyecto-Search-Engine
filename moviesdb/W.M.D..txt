W.M.D.

 It's 2002; America and the UK are pushing hard to invade Iraq. Intelligence sources indicate that Saddam Hussein has stockpiles of biochemical weapons ready for almost immediate launch; the perfect justification for war. But when Alex Morgan - an ordinary MI6 desk officer - uncovers deliberate flaws in the evidence being used to support the invasion, he is forced to decide whether to risk his career and even his own safety to expose the truth.
