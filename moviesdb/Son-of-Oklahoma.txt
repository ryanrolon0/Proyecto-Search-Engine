Son of Oklahoma

 Verdugo finds a young boy on the desert and raises him as his son. Now a grown man, Dan is framed for a stagecoach robbery by Brent, the same man that shot his father and tried to take him and his mother away twenty years earlier. A young boy, Dan Clayton, stranded on the desert by kidnappers led by Ray Brent, is found by Manuel Verdugo who takes him and raises him as his own son. The grown-Dan begins the search for his parents and the man who wounded his father many years ago and tried to take Dan and his mother away. The trail takes him to Oklahoma.
