Sense of Time

 Katie Maguire, a young and vulnerable mother of two children is taken from Ulster, placed under the protection of MI5, her identity changed she is given refuge from the sinister, deadly reach of Irish Nationalists who consider her a traitor. Hidden in the peaceful county of Oxfordshire in a sleepy market town which is steeped in the history of the English Civil War, she begins to rebuild her shattered life, fiercely protective of her children and the memory of her heroic husband, and holds down a nursing job at the local psychiatric hospital. During her frequent visits to the local museum she encounters a strange link with the past and a lost love affair with Sergeant John Brooke - a phenomenon which will eventually save her life...
