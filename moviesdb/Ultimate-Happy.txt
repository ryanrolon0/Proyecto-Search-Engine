Ultimate Happy

 "Ultimate Happy" is a short film that explores escapism in the sub-culture of the circuit party scene. The circuit is a series of underground gay parties that promote sex, drugs,and dancing. It's a coming of age short that follows the journey for happiness in a society that substitutes fleeting pleasures for the wish to find ultimate happiness. A contest to declare the next Ultimate Circuit Boy brings together three guys who are all in a different stage of the circuit evolution. Each character has his own ambitions, dreams, hopes and fears. They all have one ultimate wish, to be happy.
