The Misappropriation of Kelly

 When Kelsie introduces her friend Kelly to Jessica, a hot new casting director, little does she know that Jessica harbors a grudge against Kelly for unknowingly stealing her boyfriend. Under the guise of realistically testing the lovely actresses, she binds and gags them securely, then tells them they've been kidnapped and held for ransom. Will the buxom beauties live to act in another bondage film?
