Secession Path

 During the American Civil War, a Confederate captain (Hornblower) suffers from post-traumatic stress disorder, forcing him to confront his role in the war. Charged with behavior unbecoming an officer, the captain is given one final chance to redeem himself. A mission takes the captain to the C.S.S. Neuse ironclad gunboat and into the swamps of the North Carolina wilderness. There, Hornblower finds himself and more.
