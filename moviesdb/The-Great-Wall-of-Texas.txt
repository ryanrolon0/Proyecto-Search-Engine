The Great Wall of Texas

 Set against the backdrop of anti-everyone who isn't us sentiment rising across the U.S., this is the story of Charles Medford, a civil engineer with a radical border fence plan who finds his time has come when he presents his 'Texas Fence for Defense' to the Governor of Texas and the 'Border Fence Steering Committee'. Miscommunication, xenophobia, bureaucracy and environmentalism collide resulting in a surprisingly logical conclusion.
