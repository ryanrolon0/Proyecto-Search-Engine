The Bowman Trilogy

 Three scary and suspense filled stories in one movie. First there is The Trainer his new client while training alone in the gym one night. Next is The Other Side of Pain girlfriend cheating. Can his neighbor, Mr. Henry, stop him from jumping off the roof or will he end it all? Then there's The Last Pocket pool gambler from California, goes to New York to look for his father, not knowing his father, and ex-gangster, sent Darius and his mother away because of a contract to kill them was placed on their head more than 20 years prior.
