The Big Announcement

 When Bill finally proposes to Laura, she excitedly invites their friends up to a mountain cabin to share the great news. But Bill is consumed by doubt and fear. Is he really ready for marriage? While the guys go fishing to discuss life from the male point of view, the women have a meeting of their own...which quickly escalates into male bashing. Soon everyone begins to doubt their own relationship, and what should have been a romantic weekend in the woods explodes into an all-out battle of the sexes in this fresh, quirky, unforgettable comedy about modern love.
