The Greatest Show on Earth

 The Greatest Show on Earth is the captivating story of The Magnificent Mr. Beauregard, a man whose life is left for ruins after the closure of the circus in 1956 and the death of his wife shortly after. This unique drama follows Beauregard into his new life at a manufacturing company, where he attempts to leave his ringleader persona and past life behind him. Writer and director Richard Cameron White tells this poignant story about love and loss, trials and true happiness.
