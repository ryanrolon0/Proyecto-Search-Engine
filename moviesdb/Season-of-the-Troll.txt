Season of the Troll

 Season of the Troll is chapter one in the short film "Tran-ilogy of Terror". It begins when Peaches Christ discovers a hideous little person and makes her part of the popular Midnight Mass stage-show. Peaches and sidekick Martiny nickname the creature "Troll Girl" and poke fun at her while onstage. During the taping of a TV cooking segment, Martiny eats an entire casserole named "Cheese Blackout". She falls violently ill. Peaches must hire a live-in nurse to care for Martiny. When the nurse murders Martiny via an intravenous cheese overdose, Peaches discovers Troll Girl in nurse disguise and the terror has just begun!
