The Shushybye Dream Band

 Live concert for preschoolers and parents featuring the 7-pc Shushybye Dream Band and on-stage appearances by the Shushy characters SNOOZLS, DOZIE, ZEEZ, THE SHUSHYBYE KING and CONDUCTOR MCCLOUD. Concert film present original 17 sleep-themed rock songs and lullabies, all designed to encourage children to look forward to sleep time.
