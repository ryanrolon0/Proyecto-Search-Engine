The Truth About Mobile Phone Crime

 Two million mobile phones are reported stolen in the UK each year, with half of all street robberies involving the theft of a mobile phone. The London programme investigates mobile phone crime in the capital, exposing the failures of the mobile phone industry to keep its customers safe. We follow the Metropolitan police as they combat mobile phone crime in London and travel to Ghana to see how stolen UK phones have fuelled a crime wave that's sweeping Accra.
