The Hamptons Bikini Bloodbath

 Want to make a movie with your friends? Think again. Follow a näive film director as he sets out to make "the raddest, awesomest, and hottest horror movie ever made," The Hamptons Bikini Bloodbath.This satirical mockumentary depicts David, the on and off-screen director and his motley crew of friends as they attempt to make an early eighties-inspired slasher film. As the summer progresses, their tight-knit friendships unravel. Fueled by vanity and raging egos, the characters find out the truth behind the relationships that bind them. Will the film get finished? Will their friendships make it?
