The Erving Freeman Show, Volume I

 When it comes to laughter and silliness, there's no arguing over the Erving Freeman show. This slapstick puppet comedy definitely delivers! From haunted kitchens to angry mimes, Erving Freeman continually battle's life's challenges and faces his greatest fears and insecurities. Erving is joined by a variety of colorful puppet characters including his big brother roommate Norville Howard; the guy with the advice Erving never takes. If there is trouble, Erving will find it! If there's a wrong way, Erving will take it! If there is a laugh to provide, The Erving Freeman Show will supply it!
