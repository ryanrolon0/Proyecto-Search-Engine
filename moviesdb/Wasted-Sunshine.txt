Wasted Sunshine

 Wasted Sunshine explores the destructive power of alcohol on lives and love ones. Victor Moore, successful lawyer, enjoying his Florida retirement with his beautiful second wife, Melody are swimming happily in his glass bottom bottle of alcohol. Visited by his estranged son, Danny, a now successful NYC photographer and a six month AA member, the two men need to separate their addictions from their true feelings. When hospitalized for a fatal bleeding ulcer, Victor confronts his worst fear, betrayal by the two people he loves the most. Caught between his sorrow, his past additions and his overwhelming attraction to Melody, Danny searches for resolution with his dying father.
