Sweet Judy Blue Eyes

 Sweet Judy Blue Eyes is a slice of life about six New Yorkers who meet for drinks after work. Michael, who is battling an addiction to cocaine as well as his insecurities with women is on a blind date with Judy; a workaholic who can't find the time for a relationship. Mark is a gambling junkie who has just cashed in big at the office and has yet to tell his wife Natasha, who is pregnant and has yet to tell Mark. Kevin and Penny are expecting a baby in the next month. The six friends postpone their issues for a night on the town, not knowing that by morning, America would be changed forever.
