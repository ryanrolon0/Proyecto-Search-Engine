Saving Sara Cruz

 Follows the story of a brash rap/hip hop artist who transforms himself into a specialist protector for music artists after his life and music career implodes when his wife and young son are murdered in a drive-by shooting meant for him. The specialist is hired by a former bodyguard and close friend who now leads a security team that protects a young Latin pop diva when a stalker's threats become deadly. A relationship develops between protector and diva causing clouded judgment. He is released from her security team by the diva's mother just when she needs him most, leading to a final horrific climax.
