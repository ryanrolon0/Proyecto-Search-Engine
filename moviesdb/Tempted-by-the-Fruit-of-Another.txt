Tempted by the Fruit of Another

 After only two years of marriage, Julia already feels her husband drifting away from her, and up until now she didn't know what to do. But one night, when Julia and Chris' friend Kevin comes by to visit, Julia finds herself looking at Kevin in a different way and has trouble getting him out of her mind. These feelings only increase after Kevin starts leaving phone messages, making it clear that his feelings are more then just friendship. Julia needs to make a decision about whether to pursue the calls from Kevin and jeopardize her marriage, or stay with a man she really does love, but feels is slowly drifting away from her ever since they agreed to be husband and wife.
