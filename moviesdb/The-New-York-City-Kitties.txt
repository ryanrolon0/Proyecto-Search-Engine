The New York City Kitties

 The New York City Kitties, four gorgeous female Super Heroes, battle New York City's most lucrative and infamous criminal mastermind the "Handsome Devil", in order to protect the City's most prized and dangerous possession from falling into the hands of evil. While Miss Kitty, Hell Cat, Priscilla and Alley Cat race against time to save the world, not only is one of their covers blown by an unlikely character, but another Kittie stumbles across the truth about her mysterious past which changes the outcome of the battle between good and evil!
