Thank God for His Glory!

 In this feature presentation, Roderick L. Evans introduces his audience to dimensions of the glory of God. There are many dynamics to the glory of God. Christians, however, must realize that the glory of God will always reveal His power, presence, personality, and the perseverance of Christians. In this message, Christians will be refreshed through understanding four types of God's glory glory, the fading glory, and the joyful glory.
