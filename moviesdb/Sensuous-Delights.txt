Sensuous Delights

 A collection of historic loops. Noteworthy is the appearance of the legendary Tina Russell in two of the segments. "Hot Tub, Hot Rub" and "The Girl Next Door." In addition, a very young John Leslie picks up a hitchhiker in "Wunderlust." The different parts are sewn together by some shot-on-video footage. Part of the Fantasy Peeps video series. See review in Adult Video News, July 1984.
