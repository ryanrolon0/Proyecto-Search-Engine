St George's Day

 Two infamous London gangsters, Mickey Mannock and Ray Collishaw. Both men are top of the food chain when their world is turned upside down as they lose a shipment of the Russian Mafia's cocaine. Set in London, Amsterdam and Berlin, the story races across Europe at breakneck speed as Mickey and Ray attempt to stay one step ahead of the Police and pay back the Russians by pulling off a daring diamond heist.
