The Making of 'Something Wicked This Way Comes'

 This documentary treats movie fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Something Wicked This Way Comes, based on Ray Bradbury's novel about a mysterious fair that comes to town granting peoples' wishes in ways they never imagined. Included are interviews with the cast and crew who share their experiences from making the film, as well as discuss all of the efforts that went into it.
