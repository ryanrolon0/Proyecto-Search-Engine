Unfamiliar Faces

 Sam and Kyle, best friends since grade school, reunite on a college break for a weekend of bonding at their favorite lakeside campground. But the weekend takes a turn for the worse when Sam finally decides that its time to reveal a long held secret situation by distancing himself from Sam, Kyle betrays his own latent homophobia and discomfort, despite having known Sam his whole life. Will Sam's secret be the end of the friendship?
