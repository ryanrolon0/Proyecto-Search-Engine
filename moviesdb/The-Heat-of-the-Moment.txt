The Heat of the Moment

 All of us have done something in our lives that we regret. Many have thought about setting things right with a lost love and many have wished horrible things upon those who have hurt us. With the shock still raw from their breakup Alex makes a deal with The Devil to get his love Mia back. Meanwhile The Devil comforts a distraught Mia. Under the influence, and still overcome with emotion, The Devil persuades clueless Mia to tell her everything she'd like to put Alex through. Now Alex faces a hell of his beloved's making with his soul and her heart in the balance. How far would you go to get your true love back in The Heat of The Moment?
