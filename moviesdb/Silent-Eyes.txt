Silent Eyes

 The legendary and brutal serial killer 'Silent Eyes' has finally been captured and killed. The small Australian town of Gretinsberg can now finally return to normal. The teens of Gretinsberg decide to throw a massive party to celebrate the end of a long curfew. All but one of them has forgotten the horror that once plagued their town. Oscar Novat lost his parents and almost his own life at the hands of Silent Eyes, and since then has been plagued by frightening visions that tell him Silent Eyes is far from finished.
