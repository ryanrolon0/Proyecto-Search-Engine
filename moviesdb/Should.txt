Should

 Tino is a tormented young man who has spent his entire life fighting to survive. When the love of his life, Kristina, is killed in a car accident, Tino holes himself up in his apartment cycling downward in a spiral of pain, guilt and self-pity. Gabe, Tino's best friend, tries to rescue him from this crippling self-destruction, but to no avail. Does Tino choose a path from which he can never return, or does he make the choice to redeem himself in the eyes of his dead girlfriend?
