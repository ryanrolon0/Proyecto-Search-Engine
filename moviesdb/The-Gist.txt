The Gist

 Dave Bravo is the toughest of the tough guy bushmen. Dave is elected by the Hutu Clan, an indigent tribe in the most inhospitable regions of the African Serengetti. To rid the area of the wild animals that have been terrorizing the village locals. Dave Bravo wrangles the pesky,savage, beast and saves the village of an impending doom. But proves not to be so dynamic or tough after all.
