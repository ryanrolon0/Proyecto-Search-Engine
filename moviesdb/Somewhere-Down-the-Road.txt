Somewhere Down the Road

 "Somewhere Down the Road," a romantic dramedy, tells the story of Nick, a young, up and coming hotel manager whose life is suddenly interrupted by a family emergency; finding himself back in the small town life he was so eager to leave behind. While dealing with family issues, he meets Katie, a tender-loving, soon-to-be-married nurse that is caring for Nick's ailing father. Nick and Katie immediately share an attraction that cuts through all surfaces. Their feelings and emotions as well as the question as to whether they should take a chance on each other or not, are put to the test.
