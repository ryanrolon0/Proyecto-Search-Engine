Show

 This is a movie about a talk show which features a wacky host named Saddam Hussen. He interviews, several wacky guests on various types of topics. But when Marthar Stewart gets there, all hell breaks loose and everyone begins to revolt. There are only two characters in Show.Madhav and Ridhima.They meet at a remote place and are forced to wait for someone they had come to meet.To while away the time they enact a play.They decide to play the characters of husband and wife.The play that begins innocently,pulls Madhav into a vortex of emotions as it begins to reflect more of his disturbed personal life.The thin line between reality and unreality disappears,the play becomes real.Will it be too late for them to come back to reality?
