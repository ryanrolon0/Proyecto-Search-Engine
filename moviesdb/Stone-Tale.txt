Stone Tale

 Tomb stone is telling the story about the bravery and eternal fight between the forces of good and evil. Tired of the long flight, one bug alights on the tomb stone and by the strange coincidence her destiny becomes entangled with the characters of that story. It is a story about each of us, about the week who, when give up and think that they are finally defeated, find the strength to fight back against the stronger evil and change the course of things.
