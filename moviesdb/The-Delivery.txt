The Delivery

 Based on true crime occurrences in and around Charlottesville, Virginia, The Delivery is the story of two criminals, Travis and Richard, who create a plan to have others steal for them. After taking hostage a Chinese delivery boy named Eman, the criminals use a young woman they've kidnapped, Ada, as leverage to have Eman steal for them. As Eman ventures off to complete his task, Ada slowly begins to realize just how dangerous her kidnappers really are.
