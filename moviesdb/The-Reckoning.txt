The Reckoning

 In 1875, a devoutly religious Texas woman, Amaris Fanning, discovers her sister and brother in law brutalized and murdered. After her sister dies in her arms the chase is on and her guns are loaded. In a desperate chase across Texas she gallops off to hunt them down one by one and settle the score. Will she find them and, more importantly, what will this church going woman do if she actually catches them?
