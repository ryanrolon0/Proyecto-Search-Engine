Sithariya Muthukkal

 Would a young woman's dream for a baby girl come true in a village where female infanticide is rampant and female foeticide is the normal practice? Broken Beads is a high octane story of the dreams, despair and destruction that confronts Muniamma, a young woman who arrives in her husband's village in Tamil Nadu, India, and is baffled by the wickedness around her.
