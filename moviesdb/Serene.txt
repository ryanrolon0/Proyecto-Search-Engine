Serene

 A young man trying to fly home to see his love is postponed by a storm. When the flight is finally able to board and get in the air, the storm sends the plane careening down. In his fear, Justin closes his eyes. When he opens them, the plane has landed, and he sees that he is the last off of the plane. Spilling into the empty airport, the hero runs to find his love. Finally, he reaches her. As he places his hand on her shoulder, he knows he has finally gotten where he needs to be; yet as she turns to him, his eyes open...
