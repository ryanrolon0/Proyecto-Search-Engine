Sex, Obsession, and the Desert

 One dead body, three likely killers, and an unforgiving desert - knowing who to trust can save a life, or destroy it. SEX, OBSESSION and the DESERT is the newest film from producer/writer/director Chris Roland. This psychological action/thriller explores sexual obsession and its relationship to humanity. The film is set against the striking deserts of South Africa and Namibia, requiring no sets and only three cast members. The taught story rocks with passion, over the top action, and a story that keeps you guessing at every turn.
