Twin Thing

 Twin brothers (Donald and Tom) together with their families meet at a seaside town, where Tom owns an apartment for the weekend. The film follows their arrival and first day together, demonstrating the closeness of this normal modern family. However Don is taking the opportunity of this meeting with Tom to break some devastating news that will shatter the family idyll. Being twins Donald is concerned for Tom as throughout their lives they have shared common illnesses, and Donald now faces his toughest challenge. Ultimately the film reflects how the twins bond whilst strong is also so fragile.
