The Wine Guy Unplugged with Anthony Gilardi

 The Wine Guy Unplugged is a half hour television show hosted by Anthony Gilardi. Anthony takes you on an adventure through wine regions of the world while you discover your new found passion for all things wine! Anthony will help you approach wine in such as way as to not be intimidated, but to be empowered and confident when talking about, serving, pairing, or selecting wine. Each episode will take you to a new region of the world while you explore exciting wineries, vineyards, and local treasures that make each region unique. You will meet, talk to, and learn from industry professionals and get the inside scoop from the people who know best. Anthony will bring with him, on these adventures, celebrity guests that you will follow as they make their own discoveries about wine. You will learn more about your favorite celebrities wine of choice, why they enjoy wine, and how they entertain with wine. Come join Anthony Gilardi as he breaks down the barrier and exposes wine in an exciting whole new way!
