Walk Away

 Steve, early-20's average size male is in a moral dilemma when he witnesses a women's apartment, Sarah, being invaded by a burglar. Before it's too late, As Sarah makes a fearless struggle to get back what's hers, Steve must decide whether its best to call the authority's, not get involved and risk the burglar's escape before there arrival or put himself in harm's way and risk his own safety in this action/psych/thriller with a twist that'll change the way we look at situations beyond our control.
