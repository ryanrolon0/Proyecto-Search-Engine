The Flame

 As the State of Qatar prepares to be the first Middle Eastern state to host the Asian games it sends its message of peace to the world - a tiny flame that will evolve into a beacon of hope for the Arab world. This is the story of the team of Qatari nationals and international specialists who work across cultural and linguistic barriers to bring the flame's light of peace to Asian, and return it safely home again for the most spectacular opening games ceremony of all time.
