Return to the Land of Souls

 A documentary about the contemporary African society and its coexistence with animistic rites in Ivory Coast, directed by the photographer Jodi Esteva. He returns to Ivory Coast to follow a person with a close relation with animistic rites who wants to visit his master's grave, travel ling from the city to the countryside. In his journey, this person will meet old priestesses, and will lead us, privileged audience, into amazing ancestral ceremonies as they have never been seen. All this adventure makes an excuse to talk about the current African culture and how it develops in a changeable world
