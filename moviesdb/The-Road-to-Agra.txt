The Road to Agra

 "The Road To Agra" was a short documentary created in 36 hours for the Imagine Cup World Festival 2006 as part of the competition. The film explores the culture shock that Imagine Cup 2006 competitors felt when they arrived in India and traveled on a 6 hour bus ride from New Delhi to Agra. The film earned a 3rd place win for the team of Scott Lynch, Zach Lynch and Cam Crosson.
