Redline America

 For years motorcycle enthusiasts have wished for a film, which celebrates the joy of riding and captures the true spirit of adventure and freedom of motorcycle touring. Well, having twenty-one days off, a TV cameraman decides to make such a movie and heads west. With the help of his biker friends from all over the nation, he sets off on a journey to document the country's best roads, but discovers that it is the journey that matters in the end...
