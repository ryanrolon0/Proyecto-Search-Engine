Veteran's Day

 Kevin Houser is a USMC Corporal that is very patriotic, has a great wife, and loving family. He is shipped out to Iraq and his squad is ambushed and he is taken POW and assumed KIA. A year goes by and his wife, Ashley, is convinced by her friends to move on. She falls for a man named Trey Paul and Kevin escapes his captivity and returns home. Kevin is dealing with PTSD and his wife conceals her affair to help Kevin recover. But then Kevin finds out about her affair and he declares war on her.
