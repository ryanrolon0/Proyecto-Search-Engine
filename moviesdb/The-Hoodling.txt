The Hoodling

 A boogie woman Delilah Dansberry (Mechelle Epps) is oblivious to her pretentious friends and their selfish ways. After she gets the mark of the beast she is transformed into D Lovely (Mechelle Epps) who is down to earth, and lets her egotistical friends know exactly how she feels about them. D Lovely is reunited with her cousin Renee (Natasha Marc) who has been trying to wake her up for a long time!
