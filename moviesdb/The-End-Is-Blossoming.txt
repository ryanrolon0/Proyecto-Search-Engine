The End Is Blossoming

 The first of a series of films based on the 1940s mafia wars of Ybor City, Florida. A Sicilian mobster comes to Ybor City to take control of the illegal bolita lottery. With him comes a beautiful young mother who is indentured to the Sicilian. A local bartender falls for the woman and vows to free her from the mobster's grasp.
