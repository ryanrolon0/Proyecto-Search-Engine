Secrets du manga - Seoul District

 The documentary follows Korean comic artists Park Chul-ho and Park Hong-jin during the process of creating a Korean manhwa (equivalent to Japanese manga). Rehearsing actors, studying martial art fighting scenes, meetings with real figures of the Korean mafia (Jopok), reviewing trick animation and comic drafts - as both protagonists the movie changes from real to fiction and animation giving a brief introduction into the world of East Asian animation.
