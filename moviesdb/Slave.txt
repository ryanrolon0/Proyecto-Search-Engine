Slave

 When David's (Sam Page) fiancE Georgie (Matassia Malthe) is kidnapped on a vacation in Spain, she id forced into brutal sadomasochist submission in a world of bizarre sex and drugs Dragged and forced into a floating "Pleasure Palace" she quickly becomes the favored concubine in a harem of kidnapped women belonging to the White Arab, a dangerous and depraved drug dealer. Meanwhile, her boyfriend David must wade through a quagmire of corrupt officials, drug dealers and crazed locals to unravel the mystery of Georgie's abduction and stage a brazen rescue... before it's too late.
