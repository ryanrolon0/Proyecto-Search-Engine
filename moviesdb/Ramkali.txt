Ramkali

 Police Inspector Sultan Singh comes from a poor family and lives with his mom, Rukmani, and dad, Ram Singh. He is asked by his superiors to work in the region of his village under Police Inspector Rajiv Chouhan, which he accepts. When he arrives home, his finds his parents destitute, his father, Ram Singh, a bonded laborer under the cruel control of Thakur Shankar Singh and his equally cruel brothers Bhushan, & Zaalim. When he finds out that his father is being whipped, he decides to intervene, a fight ensues, he ends up killing one of the Thakur's employee, his father is killed, he is arrested by Inspector Chouhan, but manages to escape and become a bandit himself, out to avenge his father's death and his mother humiliation. He comes to the aid of a dancer named Ramkali, but finds out subsequently that she has an alias - that of Bandit Raksha, who is also on the path of revenge against Shankar Singh and his brothers. What Sultan does not know is that Raksha and Ramkali are two different people, and that Raksha has a boyfriend named Inspector Dilip Singh, who has been assigned to arrest him at any cost - dead or alive.
