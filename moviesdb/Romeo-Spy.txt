Romeo Spy

 The 1970's and the Cold War is at its most intense. Framed by his bosses, British policeman John Symonds goes on the run and vows revenge on the system that betrayed him. An unexpected encounter leads to the promise of a new life - as well as a chance for payback - by working for the KGB. John's biggest weapon? His charm. It will be his task to befriend and seduce the wives and daughters of diplomats and CIA chiefs. But when John falls in love, he must take desperate action to escape both his old enemies and his new friends.
