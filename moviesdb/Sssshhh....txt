Sssshhh...

 Malini Gujral is brutally murdered on a college campus, and no one knows who did it or why. Then Malini's sister Mehak starts getting threatening phone calls from a man who claims that he murdered Malini and is now out to kill her and anyone else who dares to stand in his way. Mehak is terrified, she does not know who the caller is, but he could be anybody. Most of the people around her have a motive for murder. And then the killings start.... A joker in black cloak threatens and kills a group of students and teachers on a college campus, with the killer leaving no evidence at the crime scene. The main target is a young and beautiful woman by the name of Mehak Gujral who is said to have receive several attacks and threatening telephone calls from the culprit. The police must now be able to arrest the culprit or let the killings continue.
