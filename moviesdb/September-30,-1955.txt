September 30, 1955

 This is a Documenatry looking back on the final day in the life of James Dean. Giving you an account of the morning he left his Sherman Oaks home, to his final breakfast with his Dad and Uncle, to the car crash that would end his life at such a young age. This is done through narration, photographs, and of live shots giving you a detailed look at September 30, 1955 and what it is today.
