Un cuore dentro al mare

 Vince lives In a little fishing village in Sicily, Italy. He is a simple man who works as a fisherman, but Vince is also a talented artist who loves to paint and sketch. His childhood sweetheart, Stephania, is now attending university and she's discovered refinement and culture outside of the fishing village. Vince is a conflicted young man. The loves of his life are all diverging. Stephania, his sweetheart, has pulled away from Vince, she wants more than a simple fisherman in her life. His love of art makes him an outcast among his fisherman colleagues. Vince has to chose between the girl, the art and the Sea. In the end, he choses the sea, the only place where he finds his comfort and true self.
