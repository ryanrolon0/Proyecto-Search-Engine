Salkins Schwert

 Salkins Schwert is a fantasy movie about a 12-year-old boy who wakes up in a strange, medieval world. Not knowing his own identity, he is ordered to conquer the dragon of the Northern Kingdom, in order to return to his real life. He is aided by an armorer and a female ranger. Salkin's sword changes condition several times on, the boy conceives the coherence between the state of the sword and himself. But there is more behind all this.....
