Third Party '08

 The story revolves around Roger Deerborn, who is a third party candidate for the President of the United States. Joining him on the campaign trail are his unpredictable running mate Strobe Jensen and his chief strategist Jesse. He is supported through his trials and tribulations by his daughter Anne Deerborn and his press secretary Heather. Melissa is a young radio reporter who tries to interview Deerborn about his campaign. When they find out that Deerborn's 2008 election night results drop from 0% to 0.0% to 0.00% how will they react to his defeat?
