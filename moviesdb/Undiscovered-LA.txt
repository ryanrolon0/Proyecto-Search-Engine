Undiscovered LA

 Undiscovered L.A. is a feature documentary that goes onstage, backstage, and into the heads of the people inside the L.A. emerging music scene. From the lifestyle, issues, and ambition that face the musicians to the mindset of the industry professionals that help shape their careers. Undiscovered LA is a feature documentary covering the state of the current emerging rock music scene in Los Angeles. This film goes onstage, backstage, and into the heads of over 50 unsigned bands to examine the world of the up-and-coming musicians in Los Angeles and how their experiences and opinions oftentimes sharply contrast those of the industry professionals that shape their careers. From the club bookers to the studio owners, music supervisors, program directors, publicists, agents, managers, and producers, leading industry experts offer a real world perspective as to the inner workings of the music business, sweeping changes that have taken place over the last several years, and where it all may be heading. In a business that has suffered its own massive financial meltdown and a city that teeters on the verge of losing its position as the mecca of the music universe, journey through the pay-to-play club culture, limitations of the do-it-yourself style, remaining revenue streams, impact of digital media, newfound views of "making it", and finally, whether Los Angeles is still the best place to breakthrough.
