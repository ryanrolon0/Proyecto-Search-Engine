Sniper 2

 A former Marine sniper is lured back in on a top-secret mission to take out a Serbian general who is committing atrocities on the Muslim population. His partner in this mission is a death-row convict who has been given the chance to earn his freedom. Retired Marine sniper Thomas Beckett (Tom Berenger) is called back into service by the CIA. Beckett is tasked with assassinating Gen. Valstoria, a Serbian general who is responsible for hit-and-run ethnic cleansing operations. Beckett is teamed up with Cole (Bokeem Woodbine), who was to be executed for killing a federal officer. He is offered to be set free if he participates in the mission. After successfully taking out their target, the two-man team realize that their mission is more than what they expected...
