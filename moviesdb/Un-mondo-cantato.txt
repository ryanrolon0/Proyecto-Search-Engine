Un mondo cantato

 The desire to create a document for future generation and give a world audience the possibility to participate to this unique rite inspired the author Giancarlo Soldi for this film The Maggiaioli is a ancient tradition in Tuscany taking place in the village of Castiglione d'Orcia magic night of the 30 of April the music of their voices " a cappella " spread out in the middle age village until down. The singers go around celebrating the beauty of the nature, the beauty of the architecture and the beauty of people. This is an emotional film, and the author followed the Maggiaioli for 4 years telling the story of this exclusive group of incredible singers, performing in an unique stage landscape of VAL d'Orcia
