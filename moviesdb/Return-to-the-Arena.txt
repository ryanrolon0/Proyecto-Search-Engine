Return to the Arena

 This made-for video documentary treats classic science fiction fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Rollerball, the 1975 film about the lone hero of a blood sport played in a future world ruled by corporations. Includes interviews with director Norman Jewison and writer William Harrison, along with other members of the cast and crew of the film who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it to completion.
