Shoot Out in Swansea

 This made-for-tv documentary treats film fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Twin Town, director Kevin Allen's story about two Welsh car thieves who cook up a scheme to get workman's compensation for their father after he breaks his leg on the job. Features interviews with the cast and crew of the film who share their experiences from working on the project, as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing it all together.
