She Married an Artist

 American artist Lee Thornwood and his model, Sally Dennis, appear to be made for each other, but he weds Parasian designer Toni Bonnet instead...and sings Perlez-Moi d'Amour/Speak to me of Love " in both French and English along the way. Later, when the marriage breaks up, his housekeeper, Martha Moriarity, attempts to bring Lee and Sally back together.
