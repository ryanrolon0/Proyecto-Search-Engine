The Stepmother

 Stepmothers have long been associated with bad, even evil, behavior. Which is why Tera Dice is more than a little suspicious of Dad's hot, new wife (Michelle Lay). When Tera brings her fiancE Alan Stafford home to meet the family, Michelle can't hide her burning attraction to her future son in law. Will husband Jay Huntington learn of Michelle's secret passion, or will vengeful housekeeper Ann Marie have a different plan in store? Stephanie Swift costars in a scorching encounter with Johnny Castle in this tale of seduction, forbidden desires, and passion.
