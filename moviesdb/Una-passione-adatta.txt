Una passione adatta

 Una Passione Adatta (A Fitting Passion) centres around a shoehorn, a shoe salesman and his customers. The shoe salesman is the 'Shoehorn Gigolo' whose fetish for arousing the sensual side of his customers - by the delicate and stylish use of his shoehorn - is only matched by their ready acceptance of and indulgence in the shoehorn game.
