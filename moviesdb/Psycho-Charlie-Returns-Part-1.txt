Psycho Charlie Returns Part 1

 A 2 part miniseries featuring the character of Charlie from Psycho Dance and Psycho Vampire. Charlie now thinks he's a mad scientist and sends out his creature out to do his bidding. Combining the best elements from the first 2 movies, Psycho Charlie Returns is a must have tape for almost everyone! Dave Castiglione returns as the cop hot on his trail and Dawn Murphy plays his girlfriend. As in Psycho Dance, his victims are chloroformed and then danced with. Can Officer Johns save his girlfriend or will she meet her doom just as the others have?
