The Curse of Crystal Geist

 When Crystal Geist witnesses the untimely death of her boyfriend, she is sent to Corbin High, a school known for mentally troubled teens. As she begins to adjust to her new school, strange things begin to occur. Now Crystal finds herself in a battle of sanity as she attempts to discover if the students are playing a sick joke on her or if the sightings and visions she is now experiencing are coming from a supernatural place.
