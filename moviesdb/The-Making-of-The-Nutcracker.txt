The Making of The Nutcracker

 Two attractive, young, dancers overcome various obstacles in their potential ballet careers, as they prepare to dance the leads opposite each other in most popular ballet of all time, THE NUTCRACKER. Go behind the scenes, in the rehearsal studio, and even onstage under the spotlights to experience the honestly exposed triumphs, follies, and heartbreaks of the ballet world.
