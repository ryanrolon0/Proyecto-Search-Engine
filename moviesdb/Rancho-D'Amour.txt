Rancho D'Amour

 Rancho D'Amour centers on Emily, a timid, aspiring romance novelist who gets rejected for a book deal, catches her fiancE in the arms of another woman and loses her job. Looking for an escape, she heads to the falsely advertised, run down "romantic resort" Rancho D'Amour. An unexpected discovery leads to love in the least likely of places and soon Emily finds that romance lives at the Rancho D'Amour.
