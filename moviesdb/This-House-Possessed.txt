People who cause Sheila pain or who try to tell her the real story of what happened in this house, they die in strange ways girlfriend of the singer, and the Library woman who wants to give her some newspaper articles. Can Sheila find out the truth behind all this and can she escape the house that possessed once and wants to possess once more?
