The Interrogation of Clark Grant

 The Interrogation of Clark Grant is a psychological thriller about Clark Grant, an intelligent African-American college student, who is arrested at a murder scene. Clark is interrogated by a mysterious police psychiatrist and partakes in a verbal game of cat and mouse until a stunning revelation is unleashed. "The Interrogation of Clark Grant" is a psychological thriller revolving around Clark Grant, a young, intelligent African American college student, who is arrested at the scene of a murder. Unable to remember what happened, Clark is interrogated at the police station by a mysterious psychiatrist. What ensues is a verbal game of cat and mouse between Clark and the psychiatrist until a stunning revelation is unleashed.
