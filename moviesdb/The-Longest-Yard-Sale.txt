The Longest Yard Sale

 Carmine Daveda, a video game review writer coming to terms with the premature death of his father, is cornered into helping his mother with her yard sale. Even though he must endure the daily complaints about his "job" from his mother he develops into an expert yard salesman. While Carmine is trying to reconcile with his mother an anticipated- yet unexpected- event occurs and the truth about who her son has become is revealed.
