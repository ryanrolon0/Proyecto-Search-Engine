Potter's Field

 Potter's Field is a dark comedy about a trailer trash teenager named Daryl Hexler, whose mentally and physically handicapped brother dies in a freak bathroom accident. With no money to bury or cremate, Daryl must dispose of the body himself. In between attempts at selling the body to science, staging impromptu cremations at the beach, and illegally cruising down carpool lanes with his dead brother by his side, Daryl learns one of the toughest lessons in life
