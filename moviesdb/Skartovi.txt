Skartovi

 The story of Petar and Dusan, thirtysomething porn aficionados and their big night out which begins as a casual drinking spree at a high school party, courtesy of Petar's teenage skinhead brother, and turns into a killing. After picking up a drunk underage schoolgirl at the party and her untimely demise of bootleg whiskey poisoning, they must enlist the help of the ex-Secret Service agent cousin to dispose the corpse.
