The Story of Us

 A suicide love story of sorts. This film uses the idea of suicide to over exaggerate the idea of love blinding us from reality. We do and give up things that we normally would not when in love. When its over the great times over shadow the many bad moments. This story follows the main character, Him, having these great memories of his love. Realizing that he will never find another like her he decides to end it. While in the moment of ending it a reflection of a broken tile reminds him of a fight him and his lover had. This stops him and he starts to remember all these moments that he realizes, was she that great...
