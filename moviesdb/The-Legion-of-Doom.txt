The Legion of Doom

 Malcolm McDowell narrates this entertaining look at DC Comics' most nefarious villains, from Bizarro to Black Manta to Lex Luthor. Featuring interviews, comic book panels and film clips, this documentary delves beneath the surface of these villains' demented acts to expose the deeper pathology that drives them. We'll discover why this rogue's gallery is every bit as compelling as their Justice League counterparts.
