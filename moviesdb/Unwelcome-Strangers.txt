Unwelcome Strangers

 Two brothers reunite after spending years apart because of their fathers death. Johnny (the Younger brother) watched his father die when he was a kid. Martin becomes a DA and Johnny gets into a life of crime. Martin and his wife plan a get away vacation to a cabin in the woods, that soon becomes interrupted by Johnny. Once they make it to the cabin, their vacation becomes a nightmare when the cabin becomes surrounded by hit-men. with so many twist and turns who is the real criminal? they must work together to get out alive.
