The Black Prayer

 Sentenced to life is prison for several sadistic and brutal murders, killer Monica Sterling escapes to seek revenge against Bud and Sheila Kirkland, the young couple who's testimony put her there. Knowing how ruthless Sterling can be, Bud and Sheila turn to supernatural sources in order to protect themselves, as well as their sister Molly, who is suddenly kidnapped by the merciless killer and her string of misfits.
