Take Good Care of Honeybun!

 Dave, a business owner and workaholic finally agrees to take his family on a well deserved and needed vacation. But who will stay home to take care of Honeybun, the family pet? Grandma is upset when the family leaves for vacation and sticks her with the chore of caring for Honeybun. Antics follow as Grandma deals with the dog. Will her resentment of being left out of the family fun, affect the care she gives to the dog? Will Grandma take good care of Honeybun?
