Waiting for the Snow

 This documentary is the voice of the children of the forgotten village in Morocco. Deep in the Atlas Mountains in Morocco, the weather is freezing cold for months on end, making it hard to live for humans and animals alike. Only the warmth of the family helps one to survive. Like many other children in Morocco, five-year-old Ismael is living below the poverty line. While Ismael and his family struggle to make a living, the child discovers the beauty and hardships of life. If only the snow would fall ...
