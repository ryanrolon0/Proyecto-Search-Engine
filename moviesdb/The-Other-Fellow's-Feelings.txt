The Other Fellow's Feelings

 Judy, a pretty young high school student, is being constantly teased and tormented by Jack, a classmate. It seems that Judy accidentally dropped a bottle of perfurme near Jack, and now he won't let up on callling her "Stinky" and making remarks about how she "smells." Judy is being slowly driven nuts by Jack's relentless torturing of her, until one day she finally snaps.
