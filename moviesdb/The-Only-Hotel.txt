The Only Hotel

 Escaping from muggers, Courtney flees into a run down hotel in the back streets of London's murky under-belly. She is offered work in return for shelter and pin money. But Courtney has landed herself in deep trouble as she discovers initially friendly Carl turns out to be a money laundering gangster. Courtney hatches a plan with Jenny to steal Carl's money. The pressure mounts when Georgio comes to collect the money from Carl. Courtney goes through a dangerous journey of discovery and self-worth.
