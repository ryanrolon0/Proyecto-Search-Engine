The Fun Room

 As a child, Eddie Roberts witnessed a horrible crime against a woman. As an adult the childhood experience manifests itself in his own behavior as he abducts and tortures young women. One of his victims is Heather, the girl who rejected him when they were younger. Heather must survive Eddie's tortures until she can be rescued, or escape. Eddie Roberts saw something horrible as a child a woman. The experience unhinged his mind, and now as an adult, the experience has led to his torturing and murdering young women. Heather, the young girl that once turned him, and who's mother was murdered before Eddie's eyes, is now grown up too, and Eddie's captive. She must survive until she can be rescued by Ryan, her homicide detective boyfriend, or escape on her own. It is a race for time for Ryan as Eddie tries to break Heather, mentally and physically.
