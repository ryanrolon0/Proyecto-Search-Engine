The Youngster

 Wren moved out to Los Angeles to pursue his musical dreams, bringing with him his high school sweetheart and a fellow band mate. As his dreams start to become a reality he takes $1500 from his band's account and buys girlfriend a ring, only to find her in the arms of another man the night he was to propose. Now forced to work odd jobs to payback the band, this youngster learns the ropes about life and love from the quirky and wise people of LA.
