Six

 A dying mercenary decides to visit his parents for the last time before undertaking another dangerous mission. Along the way, he picks up a stripper who is on the run from her abusive boyfriend. Unknown to both, each has a "ghost" from the past who are tracking them, with the intent of murder. Complicating their situation is a kidnapping ring that both will become entangled in, and will further put them in jeopardy.
