Tanguero

 Tanguero the female tango dancer arrives late for the last dance rehearsal before the performance. The teacher does not want to lose time and therefore rehearses the tango with a male dance student in place of the female. After several attempts and ever more emotional arguments the two succeed inspite of everything of putting a fiery dance onto the dancefloor. However, now they are disturbed by the female tangodancer, who rushes in in a delicate moment..
