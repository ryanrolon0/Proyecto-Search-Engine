Social Experiments with Simon Snow

 Meet Simon Snow, a brilliant young scientist devoted to studying and understanding the science of human interaction. As Simon presents his findings on the socially perfect human via this black & white, educational film, his philosophies and theories are soon challenged by a factor he has forgotten to take into consideration...chaos. Using the form of a 1960's, black & white educational film, Social Experiments with Simon Snow studies, debates, & finally celebrates the social imperfections that make us human.
