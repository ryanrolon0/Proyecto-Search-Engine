The Heartbreaker

 Have you ever suffered the pain of a broken heart? Ever wanted to make that person feel the same pain? Sebastian Wallace can help. He's a professional heart breaker. When an average guy gets his heart broken, he hires Sebastian to date his ex-girlfriend with the intent of breaking her heart once she falls in love. Sebastian has come up with a proven system for stealing a Woman's heart- never get too involved. His world comes crashing down when he starts to have feelings for his latest victim. Now he must decide if his business is worth the price of falling in love.
