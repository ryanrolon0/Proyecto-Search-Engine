Systyr Act

 $5 carwash and $2 baptisms! Take a queer romp through the life of the world's most beloved nun cover band. These brides of Christ let it all hang out in this no-holds-barred, backstage peek at some of the band's most intimate moments, then share the world premier of their chart-topping music video. Tracing their path to glory and their rise to stardom, 'Systyr Act Under the Habit' answers the question that fans have been asking for years.
