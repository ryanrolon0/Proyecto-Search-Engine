The 10th Kingdom

 A behind-the-scenes look at the making of "The 10th Kingdom", a mini-series about a fairy tale adventure that spills over into our world. Includes footage of the mini-series, as well as interviews with the director actors, and a look at all of the special effects work that went into making this fantasy world a reality. A documentary video that takes viewers behind the scenes of the hit mini-series, "The 10th Kingdom". Includes personal perspectives on making the series from several of the actors, including John Larroquette and Ann Margret, as well as a look at all the hard work the special effects and design teams had to do to make the world of fantasy come to life.
