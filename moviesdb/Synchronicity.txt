Synchronicity

 On a grey day in the city, the final answer for one desperate man`s problems beckon him from above. As he makes the journey to what appears to be the only escape, a humdrum daily chore in an apartment below is synced to his every move. The combination of events explores the often overlooked element of coincidence, an exaggerated magical reaction compounding the unforeseen domino effect which lubricates the wheels of daily life.
