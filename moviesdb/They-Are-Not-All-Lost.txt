They Are Not All Lost

 This series delivers compelling entertainment that will inspire audiences to get involved in the issues that affect all of us. Young people of color speak to the celebrity host. Other young people (and the public) will view their peers making positive choices in the face of adversity. Today's young people are the future of this multi-cultural society. Their perceptions and how they are perceived, can and do make a difference across America and around the world because, "THEY ARE NOT ALL LOST."
