The Price of Acorn

 Delia and Burly marry in the town they grew up in. Nine months later Acorn is born. When he is five Delia proposes to Burly that they open a bed and breakfast. Soon, their first guest arrives. Adderly is in town to give a lecture. He takes a keen interest in Acorn and offers to take him to the city for better schooling. He also buys the family a washing machine. Delia and Burly agree that Acorn can go to Toronto. A few days pass and Adderly hasn't returned her calls. They drive to Toronto and burst in on Adderly. Delia finds Acorn in a closet. Reunited, they return home.
