Under the Sky

 Joanna Logan, a suburban New Hampsphire relocates to New York City after her parents accidental death. She starts her career as a Social worker to work among the homeless. Her friend Harry a stock broker was looking into insurance settlement amount from her parents' death to menage her portfolio. But she decides to donate her money to charity. One night she was attacked by a rapist, but saved by an homeless guy an Indian musician from the streets. The incident leads to her friendship to this person helping to revive his musical talents. While working on the project she submits her finding could help the homeless but could hurt the drug dealers who were using the homeless. She receives an award for the best social worker. Same night she was attacked by two homeless, inspired by the bad guys. She ended up in the hospital in coma. Something miracle happens at the end, a suspense that makes the film a good film.
