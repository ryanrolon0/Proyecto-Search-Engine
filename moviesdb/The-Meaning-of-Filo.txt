The Meaning of Filo

 Bob is a recovering alcoholic who is having nightmares about being murdered by a mysterious assailant. After being refused help by everyone he knows, Bob finds his "murderer" working at a local corner store. Terrified and alone, Bob embarks on a journey to defeat this dark presence that seems to follow him everywhere... But will he find the strength he needs without returning to alcohol? Will he see the connection between his nightmares and his drinking? Will Bob discover the meaning of Filo?
