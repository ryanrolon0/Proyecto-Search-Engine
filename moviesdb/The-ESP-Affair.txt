The ESP Affair

 Connie Morris is an elementary school teacher with an unhappy marriage and a penchant for romance novels. When a nightly series of intense dream fantasies mysteriously implicates her in adultery, Connie's waking life begins to unravel. As mysterious precognitive visions and hallucinations plague her days, Connie is forced to question her own sanity. The connection between her dreams and waking nightmares slowly reveals itself, as Connie stumbles upon a national government conspiracy involving ESP and mind control. Risking everything to regain control of her life, Connie discovers the potential of her own extraordinary psychic gifts, hidden dormant inside her.
