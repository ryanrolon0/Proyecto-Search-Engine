Vaccines

 This is documentary highlighting the importance of vaccination to prevent disease. Many people have concerns about the side effects of vaccines, but have forgotten about the consequences of the diseases themselves. This film features interviews with people concerned with this topic. They include those who have acquired vaccine preventable diseases, the mother of twins, one of whom it autistic, as well as interviews with Pediatric physicians and infectious disease experts. The film-maker is the 15 year old son of Pediatricians. He conceived this project while listening to his parents at the dinner table, and subsequently directed, filmed and edited this project independently.
