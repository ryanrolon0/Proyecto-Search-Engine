Rio Grande Ranger

 Sayres and his outlaw gang operate out of a town just across the border and out of the jurisdiction of the Texas Rangers. Ranger Bob Allen is sent across the border where he poses as an outlaw hoping to lure the gang back into Texas. He gets into Sayres' gang displacing the gang boss but the disgruntled ex-boss is able to expose the hoax and Bob is made a prisoner. Texas Ranger Captain Winkler dispatches Rangers Bob Allen and Hal Garrick to the Texas-Mexico border town of Shonto to stem the activities of a band of desperadoes operating from there.Working undercover Bob plans to enter the territory as a fugitive while Garrick goes to Shonto and tells Jim Sayres, head of the gang, that there's a thousand dollar reward for the delivery of Bob across the border into Texas.Bob hopes the gang will take him across the border, giving the Rangers a chance to capture them.Riding into the Shonto Basin, Bob meets Sanda Cullen, daughter of rancher John Cullen. Later, Bob disarms Sneed, a Sayres gang leader, who has tried to capture him and collect the reward. Sayres is impressed and makes Bob the gang leader over Sneed. Bob, in order to keep up the ruse, is forced to ride with the gang after Cullen's herd but the hacked-off Sneed warns the Cullens and the gang is ambushed. Bob escapes but is seen by Sandra who thinks he is one of the outlaws.Sneed sees Bob and Garrick together, tells Sayres and they make a plane to trap the two Rangers.
