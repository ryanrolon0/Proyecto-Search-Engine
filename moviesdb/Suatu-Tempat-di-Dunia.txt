Suatu Tempat di Dunia

 The film takes place in a small village on the island of Java, Indonesia. Made for the Jatiwangi Art Factory Village Film Festival 2010, it shows day to day life in the village from the point of view of the creator of the film, Yupica Gaiano. Yukkunn. Staying with a local family, he integrated with the villagers, sharing in their day to day activities whilst collaborating on a local arts project. The film retells the vivid impression the villagers made on Yupica Gaiano. Yukkun, reinterpreted through his own personal experience of the local and the global.
