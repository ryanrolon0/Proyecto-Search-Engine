Strive

 An upcoming local Hip-Hop group (Northern League) and humanizes them by introducing you to the people and their histories, struggles, what makes them tick, and their history as a group. It then takes the viewer on a ride to a show the group is performing in and follows them up to the stage. Strive solely features music by the Artists of Northern League/Hustle Now Records and focuses on the differences between the two primary members of the group and and the differences between them, but also show how they come together to perform.
