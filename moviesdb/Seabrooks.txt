Seabrooks

 Seabrooks is based on a true story about young man name Antar Seabrooks, which finds himself getting into the wrong trouble selling crack cocaine. In the process he haunted my his parents overdosing in his nightmares and has a hard time finding himself. Antar decide to go on the streets and sell drugs to make a fast living but seem to follow the same track his parents went down in drug abuse and selling the substence. This story is a touching story about a person that lost and finds redemption the hard way. Seabrooks( Jaquan Frazier) is a young man that finds himself haunted by the death of his parents Overdosing on drugs as he witness at a young age. Seabrooks as an older man find himself staying with his two cousin Barry( Alaster Sampson) and Natalie ( Bre Clark). In the story you find Antar Seabrooks getting into trouble on the streets selling drugs and getting into the wrong crowd, which can hurt him in the long run. This film is based on a true story about a man struggling with life challenges and finding redemption.
