Sista kontraktet

 Young police officer Roger is on the trail of the international master assassin, while the assassin follows his plan to murder Swedish Prime Minister Olof Palme. A thriller about the events in Sweden and around the world, leading to the murder of Swedish prime minister Olof Palme February 28, 1986. The main character is Swedish policeman Roger Nyman. When he suspects that a murder of Palme is being planned, he gets strangely little support. Is he just not being believed or are there forces working against him?
