Voodoo Woman

 Deep in the jungles a mad scientist is using the natives' voodoo for his experiments to create an indestructible being to serve his will. When a party of gold seekers stumbles upon his village, the scientist realizes that Marilyn the expedition's evil leader is the perfect subject for his work. Deep in the African jungle a mad scientist, Dr. Roland Gerard, is experimenting with turning women into powerful beasts. He is certain that the combination of tribal voodoo and modern science will provide him with what he seeks. His wife Susan knows what he is up to and is kept a virtual prisoner in their hut. When an expedition arrives looking for gold, Gerard has new guinea pigs to experiment on
