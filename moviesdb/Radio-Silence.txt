Radio Silence

 A prisoner of his psychological state, Sean, a young man seems to be following the footsteps of his mother who committed suicide while he was a boy. Sean listens to a song that propels him into a surreal landscape, a journey that takes us into his subconscious mind. Here he sees how he's been tangled in the web of his past. He realizes his desperate state and through the love of his girlfriend, chooses a different path.
