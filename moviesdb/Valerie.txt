Valerie

 After surviving a violent sexual assualt, a Long Island teenager suspects that she was attacked by a Vampire. Soon she begins to believe that she is turning into one. She is haunted by violent erotic dreams. Soon people around her are acting strangely and folks in her small town are dying. Fearing that she is responsible Valerie Decides to find the Vampire who attacked her and kill him, ending his spell. Fantasy and dream mix with reality as she comes closer to resolving the truth of what actually happened to her that night.
