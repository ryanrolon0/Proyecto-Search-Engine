The Wildest West Show of the Stars

 Stars from the world of television, film and music compete in a modern day rodeo with all the excitement of a classic Wild West Show. The stars participate in activities ranging from Pony Express relay races and quick draw contests to Mechanical bull riding. Country singing legends Glen Campbell and Tammy Wynette perform some of their hit songs for the enthusiastic audience. The special was taped at the Los Angeles Equestrian Center and at Knott's Berry Farm in Buena Park, CA.
