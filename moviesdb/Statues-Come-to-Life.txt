Statues Come to Life

 Despite being scared, two young siblings, a brother and sister, investigate if the old tale of the statues coming to life at the temple after sunset is indeed true. As the moon rises, the siblings are amazed to see the female figurine awaken from her slumber. The female figurine awakens her male counterpart. They begin a South Asian folk dance to the gods against the backdrop of the moonlight. But what will the statues do if they find out about their two child intruders?
