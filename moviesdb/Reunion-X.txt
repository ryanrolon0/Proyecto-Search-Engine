Reunion X

 As earth approaches 2012, it also approaches the time of The Next Portal, a moment during which the great archangels wish to start evolution over from the beginning. Morgan, a yoga teacher in Los Angeles, will find out her true heritage as an Atlantian being while being torn between Jonathan, the one she thought she loved, and Maitreya, the one who will guide her towards a greater destiny than she ever could have imagined ...
