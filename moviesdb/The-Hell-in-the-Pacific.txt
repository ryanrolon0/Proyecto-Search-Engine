The Hell in the Pacific

 The Modding Theater's Conversion WW2 modification for the Medal Of Honor Allied Assault series that whisk players far into the eerie swamps and beautiful but desolate battles over the hills and islands of Japan and neighboring villages. In this Multiplayer TC/mod installment for MOHAA, players must work hard as a team and offer strategic wit in order to hold their own in the many new game-play modes offered in HitP.
