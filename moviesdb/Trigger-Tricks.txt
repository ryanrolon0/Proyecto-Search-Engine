Trigger Tricks

 Out to avenge his brother's death at the hands of cattleman Kingston, Tim gets hired at Dawley's sheep ranch. The sheepmen are greatly outnumbered but Tim has some tricks planned that will even the odds. Texas Ranger Tim Brennan rides into the sheep camp of Betty Dawley seeking to avenge the killing of his brother, Betty's former foreman. Bett persuades him to intervene in her feud with Tom Kingston, a neighboring cattle rancher. He hires out to Kingston and rounds up Kingston's gang with a ruse involving a Victrola, and forces Kingston into a confession before Sheriff Jack Thompson. Tim and Betty decide to make their alliance permanent.
