Thru

 While trying to cure his self-inflicted agoraphobia, out-of-work musician Dullis Overby meets a struggling conceptual artist named Avel Soleil. The pair is seemingly made for one another afloat in a turbulent world. But when Avel reveals that she has created the ultimate installation art piece out of Dullis' worst fears, he loses the ability to differentiate reality from hallucination and must fight to retain his sanity.
