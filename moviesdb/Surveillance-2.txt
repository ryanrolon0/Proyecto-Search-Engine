Surveillance 2

 At the the very center of Western financial power, the New York Stock Exchange, behind the veil of abstraction provided by a never-ending flow of data and the systematic drive for greater and greater profits, are the floor traders, those who actually execute trade orders. All-too-human, they are examined on their cigarette breaks, pictured in capsules of their own thought, leaving us as viewers to speculate about who they are and what they think.
