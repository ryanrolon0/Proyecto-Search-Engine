Soñadores en el hospicio

 The J.T. Borda Mental Hospital, Buenos Aires, a place abandoned by government and society, causing constant violations of human rights. The main character was born more than two decades ago, that promotes alternatives that improve the life quality of patients with mental illnesses, with the objective of producing art as a tool of social transformation. Throughout the personal statements of the interns, a plot seeking to change the social imagery concerning mental health is built.
