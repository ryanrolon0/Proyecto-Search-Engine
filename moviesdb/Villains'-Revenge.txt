Villains' Revenge

 In the bedroom is a door to the fantasy world, and imagination is the key to unlocking it. In this bedroom is Jiminy Cricket. Within this bedroom there are two kinds of play Jiminy Cricket as guide, must journey into four of Disney's most beloved stories to restore the happy endings before the Villains take over forever. The fate of Peter Pan, Alice in Wonderland, Dumbo and Snow White rests in hands.
