Uuf Kya Jaadoo Mohabbat Hai...

 Daughter of a rich man, Pari (Pooja Kanwal) falls in love with a poor boy Yash (Sameer Dattani), whose dream is to open a dance academy. The relationship is disliked by her childhood friend, who wants to marry her for her wealth and his father who plays tricks to separate the young lovers. Will they succeed with their evil intentions or will true love triumph over all odds?
