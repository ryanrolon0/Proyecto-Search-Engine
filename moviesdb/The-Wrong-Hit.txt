The Wrong Hit

 The Wrong Hit is a dark comedy about a group of paranoid idiot hitmen, with identity issues. They are scared of getting killed by the most feared hitman in the business, after one of them, The Itch, steals a contract. One by one they come to meet at the victim's place to prevent the Itch from running. This odd meeting of contract killers leads to the clash of personalities, egos, and paranoia leading them to believe that they were set up to be killed.
