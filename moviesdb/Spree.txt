Spree

 A man wins a shopping spree, sells his winnings to his parents then takes the cash and buys porno and lottery tickets. He doesn't win the lottery but does win a contest in one of the porn magazines. The prize porn star must feed him porridge. However, when she arrives she falls in love with him. Her obsession eventually pays off and the man returns the love and devotion. After they play Guess Who and make love five times he sells her to an Arabian prince.
