The Gay Nineties

 Songs popular in the "Gay Nineties" era are played and sung in this two-reel musical and some are acted out by the cast; Clyde Cook, Yvonne Davis and Sandra Gould in "The Gay Nineties" number; Chester and Hienie Conklin and Snub Pollard and director Earle C. Fox in a "Keystone Kops" segment. One of the principal numbers, "The Man on the Flying Trapeze" features The Rhythm Rascals.
