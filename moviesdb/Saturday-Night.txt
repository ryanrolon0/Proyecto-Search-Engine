Saturday Night

 Charlie is a trainee teacher. He has a normal life an maintains the reputation of a normal bloke. Charlie hosts a party one Friday night where he meets a mysterious girl called 'Kim'. The two hit it off and after romance blossoms he discovers that Kim isn't all that meets the eye. Charlie soon meets Alex, an intense ex-lover, who makes it clear to keep away from Kim. Charlie ends up fighting for his life and doing things he found unthinkable.
