Right to the City

 In a city run by moneyed interests, a united front is forming to win the real and lasting changes needed to make the city a place that provides decent living conditions for majority of New Yorkers, not just the wealthy few. Documenting months of actions, meetings and on-the-ground organizing this video shows how an alliance of social justice organizations working together to address the root causes of social problems, can have a real impact on the way NYC is organized, guaranteeing basic rights for all individuals living in the city. Interviews feature scholars, activists, and organizers who are fighting to a more just, healthy, affordable and equitable city.
