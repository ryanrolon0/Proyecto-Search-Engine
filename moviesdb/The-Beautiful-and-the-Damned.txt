The Beautiful and the Damned

 "ZELDA" is the true story of Zelda and F. Scott Fitzgerald whose romance is synonymous with the myth of the Jazz Age his eccentric muse, who went notoriously mad. This is the story behind the myth--of their lifelong love affair, with all its turbulence and passion--in which their words were so shared and borrowed, their mania so mutually fed, that in the end the genius of the one and the madness of the other were one and the same
