Varnnakazhchakal

 Kunju is a member of a joint family and he is being treated for insanity. Sridevi is a bank employee having a part time service with her uncle who is a manthravadi. Sridevi and Kunju meet when she comes to his for treating him. He tells Sridevi, his story. The story of how he met Malavika and how he reached at this mental state.
