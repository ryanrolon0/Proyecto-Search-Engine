Shadow Warrior

 You are Lo Wang, a shadow warrior. Shadow warriors are the best of the best and Lo Wang is the best of the Shadow warriors. Shadow warriors are employed by major companies for defense and Lo Wang is employed by the Zilla corporation. Power corrupts and Lo Wang discovers Master Zilla's plan to take over Japan, using creatures summoned from the dark side. A man of honor, Lo Wang quit, only to be Zilla's next target......
