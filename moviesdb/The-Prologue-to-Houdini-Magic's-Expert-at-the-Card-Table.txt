The Prologue to Houdini Magic's Expert at the Card Table

 S.W. Erdnase is the author of the most important book in card magic, The Expert at the Card Table. Nevertheless, the true identity of Erdnase is a mystery. Historians have researched for years and have only been able to come up with theories regarding the true identity of Erdnase. One of the more popular theories states that he is a man name Andrews. This short film covers the life of Andrews as if he were the true author of The Expert at the Card Table.
