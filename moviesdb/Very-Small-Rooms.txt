Very Small Rooms

 Very Small Rooms is the story of Isabelle, a young woman whose tedious daily routine is interrupted by the sudden appearance of a strange key. They key leads to an equally strange house, where Isabelle is shown a fantastical alternative to her life outside. But when Isabelle grasps the reality of the magical world she has stumbled onto, she begins to change her own fate- and the fates of everyone around her.
