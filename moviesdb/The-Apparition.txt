The Apparition

 A story of accidental death that leads to the fatal encounter with a resentful ghost. When a group of artists set out for a few days of peace from the craziness of LA, their few days of peace in a haunted cabin turns out to be crazier than LA! Based on a seemingly true story, young Roxanne, a high school cheerleader suddenly disappears. Her boyfriend John is the suspect. No evidence and no conviction. For years the mystery goes unsolved, until Roxanne seeks revenge!
