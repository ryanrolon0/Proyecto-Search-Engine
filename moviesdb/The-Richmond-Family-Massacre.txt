The Richmond Family Massacre

 Priscilla Richmond and her boyfriend Billy Baxter are spending the weekend with the Richmond family. The family led by the oppressive father Bob don't approve of Billy, neither does Priscilla's best friend Jewel. Unfortunately for them Billy has a little more then an anger problem and when he snaps there's no turning back. Meanwhile Detective Wade McCall and the hotshot reporter Ace Barrow are on the hunt for two renegade females dubbed the Venomous Vixens. With Billy and Jewel both eager to save the not so helpless Priscilla and the Venomous Vixens staying one step ahead of their entourage of would be captors The Richmond Family Massacre is an action packed collision course to insanity. What would you be willing to forgive for love? Don't forget...Sometimes Love is Messy!
