The Ghost That Talked

 A re-creation of events that occurred during the Shawville, Quebec haunting of 1889, and its investigation by paranormal enthusiast and journalist Percy Woodcock. This is followed by a discussion of poltergeist phenomena in Canada by P.S. Lambert, expert in the field and author of the book Exploring the Supernatural, which itself contains an account of the Shawville haunting discussed in the film.
