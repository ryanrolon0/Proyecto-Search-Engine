Tomb Raider III

 Lara Croft's back! This time, while in India, she runs into a man who hires her to find several artifacts made of a rare meteorite rock. After travelling through Nevada, England, the South Pacific, and finally Antarctica, she runs across him again. He uses the artifacts to activate the meteor and mutate himself. She kills him, the facility blows up, and she heads home. Join Lara in her biggest adventure to date in the phenomenal Tomb Raider series. Play through five huge worlds, in any order, on the quest for an ancient meteor rumored to have life-giving powers. From the jungles of India to the icy wastes of Antarctica, Lara is ready for anything. Prepare to be amazed! New locations the South Pacific, Antarctica, Area 51, London and India. New moves and vehicles situations. New weapons and enemies pirahnas and the infamous T-Rex with new weapons like the Grenade Launcher, Desert Eagle and Rocket Launcher! Hi-res graphics and Dual Shock Analog Control for the first time on the PlayStation.
