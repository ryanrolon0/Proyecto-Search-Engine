Shikar

 Manager Ajay Singh find out that a man by the name of Naresh is murdered and inform police inspector Rai, but all evidences are tampered with at the crime scene and as a result the murderer became difficult to trace. From here on Ajay's life takes a new turn in trying to find the culprit who have murdered Naresh, in the process he meets a young woman, Kiran who have some crucial evidences that might lead Ajay to the culprit who have murdered alcoholic and womanizer Naresh. Womanizing and alcoholic Naresh attempts to molest Kiran, who resists, and as a result Naresh is shot and killed by a bullet wound, and Kiran absconds. The police believe Kiran committed this crime, and are on her trail. Kiran meets with Ajay, and both attempt to find the real killer, and get in the bad books of gangster Ranvir. Ajay and Kiran are on the run, not only from the police, but also from Ranvir and his men who have cornered them in a jungle full of elephants, bears, tigers, and other ferocious beasts.
