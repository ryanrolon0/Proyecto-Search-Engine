The Secret World of Superfans

 This feature documentary takes an intensive look inside the phenominon of "Fan-dom." Through interview and observation of both the fans as well as the actors and directors, with a focus on the "Horror, Sci-Fi Fantasy and Super-heroe" film genres. The fans interviewed are intense as are the actors/directors. An amazing insight to something that has an affect on every human being...who has ever been a fan of anything.
