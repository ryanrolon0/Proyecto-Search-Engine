Scrambled Eggs

 Julia has had her eye on Tom for over three years now. Julia knows everything there is to know about Tom, every move he makes, even what he eats for breakfast everyday. The only problem is... Julia's never spoken a single word to Tom. In fact, She's not even sure he knows she exists. With senior year drawing to a rapid close and college close on the horizon, Julia realizes that time is running out and that it's up to her to finally ask Tom out. The result is what she never would have expected...
