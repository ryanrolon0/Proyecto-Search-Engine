Subtle Joe

 A simple look at the life of Joe Simms, a quiet, reserved, and compassionate man, whose sense of peace and purpose is rooted in his subtle, non-judgmental reactions to those around him. Joe's non-reactive nature provides a mirror upon which others can reflect and, ultimately, resolve the nagging of their overly active minds.
