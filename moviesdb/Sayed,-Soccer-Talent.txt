Sayed, Soccer Talent

 Sayed Abdullah Sulaiman is a 9 year old boy from Kuwait. Because of his amazing soccer skills he wants to go to Europe badly. The young player has his video put on the internet and gets invited at Feyenoord and FC Barcelona for a trial. Following Sayed up-close at home and during his trip the result is a documentary about cultural differences, the power of the internet and of course a young Arabic boy with a dream; becoming a famous player in Europe.
