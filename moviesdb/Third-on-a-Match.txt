Third on a Match

 Third On a Match is the story of two young adults (Richard and Rosa) who fall in love and get married. Shortly after she dies in Dec. of 1941 giving birth to their daughter Merri. After being left alone to raise a child he is drafted into WWII after a mix up with the government records. He is forced to give Merri up for adoption and answer to the call of duty. While in Europe he witnesses his best friend being executed by a German officer, and revenge begins to consume him. Will he be able to make it back alive to find his daughter?
