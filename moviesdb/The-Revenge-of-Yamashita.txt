The Revenge of Yamashita

 In the first tale a federal agent searches for the severed hand of a wizard which is reputed to possess supernatural powers. Her efforts are thwarted by two villainesses, each with her own henchman, who are determined to obtain the artifact for their own evil ends. The second story involves a buxom British blonde who is abducted by two beautiful criminals and left bound and gagged. The resourceful heroine manages to escape, nab her former captors, leaving one tied to a chair and the other spreadeagled on the bed before escaping.
