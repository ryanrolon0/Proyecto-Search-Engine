The Villain

 The cowboy Handsome Stranger will escort miss Charming Jones to get a large sum of money from her father, Parody Jones. But the rich Avery Jones wants to lay his hands on the money and hires and old cowboy, Cactus Jack, to rob them when they ride back from her father. However, Cactus Jack is not very good at robbing people. Charming Jones has to make it from the Train Station to her ancestral homestead to collect her inheritance. The Handsome Stranger acts as her escort and bodyguard. Cactus Jack has been hired to stop them both.
