The Relationship Theorem

 The story of a non-descript teenage girl, who decides to create a scientific theorem to formulate how to make love happen. With her two friends standing by her side to help with research, she sets her sights on the school's most popular boy to make him fall in love with her. Using her theorem, she conducts a series of experiments that help prove her theory. What she discovers is that emotions aren't part of the equation.
