The Scam Artist

 In a near future America ruled by corporations, the Devil is in the details and nothing is what it seems. When the daughter of a wealthy CEO and her boyfriend scheme to fake her kidnapping and skip the country with a small fortune, her lover solicits the help of a seasoned freelance mercenary; but everyone has ulterior motives. As their plan spirals out of control, the three partners in crime come face-to-face with their own inner demons. Soon, two men's greed and one woman's desire lead to murder... and a horrifying personal revelation.
