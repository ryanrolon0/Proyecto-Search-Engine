Vatten tjockt som blod

 Emilio's adoptive father Bengt feels abandoned when his son moves from Sweden back to his birth-country Colombia. Bengt travels there to try to reestablish a relationship between them. Together they search for answers about Emilio's roots. The countryside road trip that ensues turns out to be a powerful inner journey for both Emilio and Bengt. A journey that will change their relation forever.
