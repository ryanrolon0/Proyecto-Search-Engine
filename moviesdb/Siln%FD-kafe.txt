Silný kafe

 It's a story about the small things in life and relationships, things that everybody knows and has experienced. When people forget to brush their teeth before they go to work, they leave bread on the butter knife, they use too much milk to their coffee, they pour milk over their lovers head in anger, , they despise affairs, except their own affairs. It's about the small things in life that everyone can relate to, mainly shot in a small village, with small film crew and small budget. The only thing that will be big, is the amount of sold tickets.
