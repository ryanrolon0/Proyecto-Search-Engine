The Titans of Windsurfing

 Big-wave windsurfing history catches up with the present as European Champion Tristan Boxford takes us inside the progression of the sport. On Maui, Hawaii, meet the legends, hear how it all started and where it's going. Hang out with pioneers like Robby Naish, Jason Polakow, the Angulo brothers, as well as windsurfing's present rulers Levi Siver, Kauli Seadi and Daida Moreno. From board-shaping to riding big waves at Jaws and Hookipa, learn all about the birthplace of a sport from the athletes who live and breath it.
