She's Blonde Like Me

 Artist Fia-Stina Sandlund and actor Alexandra Dahlstroem are to give a performance at the Venice Biennale. Dahlstroem, in character as Julie, will answer questions about Sandlunds up-coming feature, a feministic reinterpretation of the famous August Strindbergs play Miss Julie. This is Dahsltroems audition.
