The Great Venice Robbery

 After years of petty theft "missions" Chuck, a tunneled vision bum, comes up with a plan to pull off the heist of his life - The Big One. He picks the best men he can get from a click of losers on Venice Beach boardwalk Winston, a proud ex-socialist, Pookie, a dim-wit street performer, and Manduke, a junkie with a twisted sense of humor. Led by Chuck's tour-de-force personality and driven by their own agendas, the crew sets off to execute his extravagant, high-concept plan to rob a heavily-guarded supermarket...
