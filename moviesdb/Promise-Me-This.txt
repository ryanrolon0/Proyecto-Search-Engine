Promise Me This

 Promise Me This is an original screenplay about James and Camille Sanders, a married couple, anxiously awaiting the arrival of their first child. During the pregnancy it is discovered that Camille has developed an illness that will prevent her from being able to carry the baby full-term. The options they are given are to abort the pregnancy or have the baby taken early, both of which present their own set of complications and consequences. While no decision is easy, a decision has to be made. James and Camille must decide the fate of this child that they've never met, but have already come to love.
