The Omen of Motley Crue

 Down and out Baxter Cratznick would love only to have a romantic relationship with his secret crush Baxter's maliciously evil bad luck is all but impossible to overcome. Yet, on a Vail ski trip with Angie and mutual friend, Jeff; a chance encounter with an angry Forest Gnome will render Baxter's luck the luckiest on earth. The result is a hilarious journey of self-discovery that no one could have predicted...
