Strannitzi v islyama

 This documentary is about unorthodox schools within the Islam, which are being practiced by Bulgarian Muslims to these days. The film delves into some of the mystic teachings of some of the orders, such as the Bektashiyah, etc. The authentic materials have been studied in the regions of Ruse, Razgrad and Silistra towns in Bulgaria, focusing on the ethnographic characteristics of the religion.
