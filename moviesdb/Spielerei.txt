Spielerei

 Huub is a tired businessman who enters an old, luxurious but totally empty hotel. Every time Huub presses the reception bell, his interest is drawn by a new sound, coming from all kind of rooms. In those rooms he finds guests from the hotel who are stuck in their movement. They act like a rundown LP. At first Huub doesn't know how to handle himself in this strange situation, but after a while he starts to experiment with the guests. He discoverers he has the power to control and move them. This new found power can do strange things to people..
