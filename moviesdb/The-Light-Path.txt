The Light Path

 Cal and Leah are a young couple living a happy life, until unexpected events shake their lives... forever. All of this is happening because Cal is hiding a big secret from his wife, he's an undercover Alien with a mission to accomplish before completing the job, Cal steps back and betrays his 'people', choosing the love for his wife to his mission. Driven by anger, Cal's Superiors kidnap Leah. Cal is now forced to start his run against time, trying to save Leah, then telling her the truth. But things never go according to plan.
