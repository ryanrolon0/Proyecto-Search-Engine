Sweet and Low

 Part of the Paramount Musical Parade shorts (production number FF6-1)and the studio pulled in Catherine Craig, a Paramount contractee with about a dozen films in 1946-47, whose contract option wasn't taken up, and Richard Webb, working in a couple of Paramount films at the time, and cast them as a husband and wife who throw a masquerade party at their home, with various people and groups coming by and doing a turn, including a very young Sammy Davis Jr as part of his uncle Will Maston's Trio.
