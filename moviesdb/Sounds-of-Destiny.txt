Sounds of Destiny

 After the death of Marco's father, Susan, his mother, decides that he has to be an economist like his father. Marco is musician and his dream is to become a composer. That dream is interrupted when Susan forces him to enter in the Economics University. He starts the Economics classes to makes his mother happy, but he can't live his life without music. Susan gets worried about Marco and search for help. She finds Shalimar, a Gypsy woman, who tells her that Marco is unhappy and depressed, his destiny is become a great composer. Susan realizes that Shalimar is right. Marco becomes a composer and years after he finds Shalimar again and they fall in love.
