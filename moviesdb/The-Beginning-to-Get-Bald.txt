The Beginning to Get Bald

 A story about a young American actor Mike who has just finished a mini series on Television. He is not a great actor but he is good looking and he does not care much about money or women in his life, as they come to him easily. The problem starts in his life when he starts to loose hair and he starts to become really paranoid about his hair problem. We show his life in progressions of six months . how his problem worsens and how his life becomes traumatic and all the aspects of his life start to change.
