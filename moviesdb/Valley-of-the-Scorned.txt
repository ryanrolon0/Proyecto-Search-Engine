Valley of the Scorned

 Valley of the Scorned, is a sci-fi action drama that takes place in a post-apocalyptic future brought about through humanity's overuse of resources. The planet lay dry and barren now, leaving civilization in ruins. Common resources needed for survival are scarce, and whatever survivors still around after the collapse of the world's societies fight to live. Drought has spread throughout Earth's lands, empowering those in control of water resources to rule over the rest of humanity. In the midst of all the chaos arises a lone warrior, who must face off against the tyranny of those in control in order to lead Earth's remaining inhabitants to freedom
