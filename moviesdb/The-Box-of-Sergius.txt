The Box of Sergius

 Charlie Hyde is your rare imaginative student who misses deadlines and loses scholarship competitions. His under extraordinary life is all about to change when he receives 5 estranged letters and a mysterious package from an unknown brother, Henry Edward Hyde. Within the package contains an ageless puzzle box with the power to inspire or corrupt any willing individual. Unknowing to Charlie, a corrupted man, Kasimir Maya will seek out and murder any person standing in his way to once again possess the box. With the courage and genius of Charlie and a band of unlikely companions, he must find a way to vanquish this demon. A teenager receives an ancient box that not only unlocks the secrets of history, but reveals a supernatural presence with a dark past. Combining hidden powers that bend time, space and our perception of the world he must band together with a group of strangers to vanquish an evolving evil.
