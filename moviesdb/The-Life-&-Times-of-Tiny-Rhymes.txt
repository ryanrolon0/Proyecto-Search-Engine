The Life & Times of Tiny Rhymes

 He came straight out of da 'wood to record a multimillion dollar album that transformed Applewood Colorado's pride Lloyd Perry into celebrity rapper "Tiny Rhymes." But Tiny Rhymes will soon find that life isn't always a plate of chocolate chip cookies in Hollywood ... For an indepth look at the scandalous career of the simple white boy who brought the Midwest coast onto the hip-hop scene, join Mike Green in intimate interviews with music producer Warren Hill and Tiny Rhymes himself as he finally speaks out about the music, the drugs and certain girl named after not just one, but two cars.
