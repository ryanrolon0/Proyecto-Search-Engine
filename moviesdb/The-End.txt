The End

 In the mid 1940's Sam Clarke fell madly in love with a young woman named Clare Hayes. It was war times in Europe, a time of heightened emotion and adventure. Their time together lived in his heart forever and inspired the project of his lifetime. With hope and doubt, he hires actress Alex Keyes to assist in his endeavor.
