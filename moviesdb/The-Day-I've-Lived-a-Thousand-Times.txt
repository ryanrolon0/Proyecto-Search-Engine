The Day I've Lived a Thousand Times

 James a Architect in Los Angeles has been reliving the day he lost his love Christine over and over again for the past 5 years after Nuclear Destruction has hit Earth, leaving him with just his memories of happier times. James (Aaron Trout) is just your average man living in Los Angeles, Great Job, Great House...but nothing was ever better or sweeter than that of his love for Christine (Nicole Bell). It would seem that nothing could stand in their way, until the day that James was ready to finally propose Nuclear War breaks out on the world taking his one true love away. Every day for 5 years James spent his day reliving every moment of his life with Christine until all he could ask for, all he ever wants is to be with her again.
