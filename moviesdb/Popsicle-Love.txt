Popsicle Love

 When dreamer Mike is looking for a little pick me up, he looks no further than his freezer drawer. You see Mike has an infatuation with Popsicles. When he and the Popsicle are together, nothing ever seems to go wrong. Mike and the red Popsicle gallivant through a glorious day as they go on an emotional and physical journey together. By days end, then and only then, do we you see if their love and relationship is truly meant to be.
