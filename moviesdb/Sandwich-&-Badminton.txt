Sandwich & Badminton

 Elderly people whose presence became useless in a modern city , Seoul, South Korea. Lim Kwiok(75) has spent every day traveling the city to obtain small donations of food and money for the past seven years. She explains her complex situation very simply by saying that she just hits the road so as not to think of bad ideas any more. Yoo Jungok(73), who lives alone under an overpass, tries to make ends meet as a junk man . But it becomes harder for him to survive with his outmoded cart in this urbanized city . One day, after telling his hard-luck story, he sells his old tools and leaves the overpass for returning to his home town.
