Rubberwub

 Rubberwub is defined as a noun, meaning 'continuum mechanics of love that deform reversibly under stress.' On the first hot day of summer, Rich would unwittingly coin this word. Broken up with over the phone by the love of his life, Lucy, hours before his planned engagement proposal, Rich sets out on a frantic journey to appeal Lucy's decision. Is he stupid? How far will one Dick go for love? This film is jam-packed with spills. That's it. Just spills.
