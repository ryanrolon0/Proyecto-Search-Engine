Strange Hearts

 Strange Hearts follows the mundane but somewhat suspenseful activities in the daily life of an alienated couple. Frank, the husband investigates this unusual relationship. When Frank comes home and catches his wife, Claire, sobbing to herself in the shower, he is alarmed. But Claire insists that everything is alright, despite some other suspicious details scattered around the house, and the couple proceed with dinner. Things slowly unravel however, as Frank's curiosity gets the better of him and he decides to look into the matter.
