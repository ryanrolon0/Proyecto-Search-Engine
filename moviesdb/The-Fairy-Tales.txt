The Fairy Tales

 Three fairy tale princesses (Snow White, Sleeping Beauty, and Cinderella) find themselves living together under one roof in the Hollywood Hills. Tempers flare and bitchiness ensues as the girls cope with their new roommates, all while trying to find their happily ever after. Cinderella, Snow White, and Sleeping Beauty find themselves living together under one roof in the modern world. Snow White wakes up to a dreamy musical number in the kitchen, much to the dismay of the hungover Cinderella. Sleeping Beauty takes special joy in causing conflict between the other two roommates. And, an unexpected revelation from Prince Charming throws the house in chaos. This is just one day in the life of 'The Fairy Tales' the reality show that follows these three princesses as they struggle to live peacefully together and find their ever-elusive happily ever after.
