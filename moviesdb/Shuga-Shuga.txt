Shuga Shuga

 Robbie thinks little about lying to his pretty young wife about working late as he makes a beeline for a seedy strip club in hot pursuit of a 'dancer'. But after a painful encounter with Soho's underworld he soon realizes that he is not the only one telling untruths. Crestfallen and repentant he goes home to his pretty little wife, the only person in this deceitful and wicked world he can 'trust'...
