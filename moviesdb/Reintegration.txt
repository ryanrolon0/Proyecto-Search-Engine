Reintegration

 Jacob wakes up without memory and identity. He is questioned about his past and sent home to a woman he doesn't remember. Madeleine is married to an ex-con who escaped with his life but without his mind. She struggles helping him integrate into the routines of married life, while perpetuating a cruel secret. Dr. Alan Scharpe is head of neurology on a massive research project into the feasibility of human teleportation. He'll stop at nothing to get it right.
