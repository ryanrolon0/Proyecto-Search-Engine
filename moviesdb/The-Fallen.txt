The Fallen

 A film based on a true story. Daniel is a medical student from England, but for economic reasons he stops his studies in the second year of his program. This forces him to make the important decision of leaving his family and his country in order to immigrate to Canada to work in an Accounting Company. New doors are opened to Daniel where he has to pass through the different challenges of being in a new country where his destiny is based on a piece of paper. This makes him realize that he needs to finish what he started. This film shows that no matter how small or big the goal, if we have faith we can reach it.
