The Rally

 The Rally is the journey of 4 families and the challenges they face in the small, crime riddled town in which they live. As God's love explodes into their circumstances, they each find themselves at a local Rally which will change the town forever. The Rally is a gritty drama which follows several families living in a town riddled with crime and corruption. As the local church works to bring positive change to the town, they are confronted at every turn by those that desire to keep the city in bondage... Until today.
