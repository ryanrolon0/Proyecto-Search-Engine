Tãu

 Tãu - the first part of a musical and visual trilogy centered around the Transilvanian lands. Tãu unveils the natural landscapes, by exploring 9 unique natural places with their mystique, atmosphere, legends and meanings. The second part is exploring the human component, the traditions and practices, the connection with the nature, the way this relation shaped the spiritual horizon of the local inhabitants. The last part focuses on the spiritual elements, the values that defined and made the Transilvanian lands immortal.
