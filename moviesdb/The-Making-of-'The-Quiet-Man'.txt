The Making of 'The Quiet Man'

 Hosted by film critic Leonard Maltin, this fortieth anniversary documentary treats film fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of The Quiet Man, John Wayne's classic film about an Irish-American who returns to his homeland to make a new start for himself. Along with archive footage of the filming process, this film also takes a look at some of the experiences of the cast and crew members, as well as discussing some of the influence The Quiet Man had on later films.
