Tails You Live, Heads You're Dead

 One day at the bar, office clerk Jeffrey Quint gets himself talked into a game of liar's dice by a lone stranger who introduces himself as Neil Jones and gets on Quint's nerves by confessing he kills people for business. Quint doesn't really believe him, but when Jones tries to run him over on his way home it's getting clear that game time is far from over, and that it's Jones's call...
