 Grey Malcolm is a talented young assassin who's ready to get his life back. But before he can leave, he has one final assignment who has the city's underground up in arms. As Malcolm tracks his target he learns that his rival is also on the trail - and will stop at nothing to get Malcolm out of the picture. As the trail goes cold, Malcolm realizes that those close to him are being used as the bait - and that this assignment will prove to be the most challenging job he's ever seen, and it could be the most deadly.
