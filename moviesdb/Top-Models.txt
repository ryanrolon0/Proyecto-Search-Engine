Top Models

 Linda Evangelista, Claudia Shiffer, Carla Bruni, Naomi Campbell... The international superstars of the world of high fashion. The top designers' super-paid, super-cuddled, super-famous pets whose pictures and stories grace the covers of magazines around the globe, and whose privileged lifestyles are envied by young women everywhere. "Top Models" takes you behind the scenes of the world of high fashion. The high fashion superstars tell their stories, describe their lives, and confide their dreams and their secrets. It's a fairy tale come true. Hosted by two exceptional celebrities famous models of the '80s.
