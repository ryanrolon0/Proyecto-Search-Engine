Strandgut

 Content five-year-old. He lives with his father in a lonely gate-keeper's house. The boy, unpredictable and vigorous, is too much for the father, who, himself, has had many disappointments in life. But Thomas has still one hope mother to the seaside. He doesn't know that no train will ever stop at the house and his mother died a long time ago. One day, however, a train stops and a woman gets off...
