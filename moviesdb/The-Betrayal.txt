The Betrayal

 Two covert lovers pay the ultimate price when they try to outsmart the girls live in drug dealer boyfriend. Amber and Kevin have been seeing each other on the side for sometime now. All they need is one more shipment before the can break away from the chains that bind them. However, Amber's drug dealing boyfriend has discovered their plan and has made a few plans of his own. The lovers plan unravels as they try to make that one last score and break for freedom.
