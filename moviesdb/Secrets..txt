Secrets.

 Drawing inspiration from the PostSecret world, "Secrets." follows Raymond, a lonely thrift store employee with a painful past and gloved hands to prove it. While putting away a bin of donations, Raymond stumbles across a Polaroid photograph tucked inside a picture frame. The photo shows an unsettling image of a mastectomy victim and the haunting words about me is a failure. Even my body." Troubled by this discovery, Raymond consults the master donation log to find other items the person may have donated, sending him on a hunt through the rest of the store. As the clues piece together and Raymond finally comes face to face with The Woman, he discovers he's not as alone in the world as he once thought.
