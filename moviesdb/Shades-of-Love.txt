Shades of Love

 A young woman on the eve of her first success as a fashion designer is visited by a man who informs her that she is a long lost princess and heir to the throne in a fair off country. Doubtful of the truth of the situation, she investigates and finds that there may be somthing to his claims. Suddenly she finds herself in the position of having to chose between a new life and love, and the sucessful carreer that she has dreamed about since childhood.
