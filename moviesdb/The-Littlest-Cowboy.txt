The Littlest Cowboy

 Gammons Gulch; a small, quiet, old west town that has recently discovered gold in a nearby mine. The discovery attracts a gang of outlaws headed by the notorious Paco. This gang begins causing trouble throughout the town. Unable to control them, the Sheriff sends for help. Marshall Dookie comes to the town's rescue as he takes charge of Paco's gang and once again brings peace to Gammons Gulch.
