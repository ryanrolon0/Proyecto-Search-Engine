Sun Goddess

 Gerold David, the world's top video game designer, is on the cusp of releasing his next hit game when his icy ex-girlfriend Karina sues him "for time wasted" during their relationship. As he fumbles to save himself from Karina she puts her current boyfriend on trial in this new independent comedy drama. A successful video game designer is being sued by his ex-girlfriend while she herself tests her relationship with her current boyfriend by pretending to want to break up with him in this romantic drama with light comic touches.
