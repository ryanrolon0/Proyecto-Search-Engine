Vampire Bootcamp

 A retrospective Cast & Crew interview documentary on the Making of Leif Jonker's DARKNESS for inclusion on the restored and remastered 13th Anniversary DVD titled DARKNESS Premiere at the Warren Oldtown Theater in Wichita, KS on May 29th 2005. It was released throughout North America (included on the DARKNESS VAMPIRE VERSION Single and 2-Disc DVD editions from Barrel Entertainment and Ryko Distribution) on May 30th, 2005.
