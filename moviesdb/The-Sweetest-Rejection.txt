The Sweetest Rejection

 'The Sweetest Rejection' is a non-fictional tale of two strangers who meet in Prospect Park on sunny day weekday afternoon. Sasha Reynolds and Deon Spencer are both having tumultuous days. They both escape to the park for some afternoon relief. A series of events happens. through-out the day leading up to both strangers meeting each other in the park 'The Sweetest Rejection' is a funny, light-hearted and thought felt short film on relationships, fate and karma.
