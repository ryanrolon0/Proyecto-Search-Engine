Veils of Color

 When a wealth Catherine Vreeland becomes ill, her son Johnathon comes down to find a caretaker for her. Fireworks fly when Johnathon hires a young black college graduate, Jurian, as a favor to a college buddy. Will they be able to overcome their differences? Only time will tell in this Driving Miss Daisy-esque hilarious story about lifting our veils of color to see each other's hearts.
