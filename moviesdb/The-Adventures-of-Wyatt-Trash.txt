The Adventures of Wyatt Trash

 Wyatt Trash is going through some changes in his life, he catches his wife cheating on him, becomes the host of the new Tv show 'Confruntations' and is struggling to find out who he is now. He meets a Pastor and his beautiful sister and decides to change everything to impress her. That makes things take a turn for the worse and he has to decide what he really wants in this world.
