Ramones Are Not Dead

 RAMONES Are Not Dead is a non-stop rock'n'roller-coaster ride into the world of those who love and follow the Ramones...still. The Ramones really are dead but they are immortal in the hearts of so many, who include them fully in their lives. Guitars in one hand, camera in the other, Australian tribute band Ramonettes travel the world to delve into the underground cult of the die-hard fan.
