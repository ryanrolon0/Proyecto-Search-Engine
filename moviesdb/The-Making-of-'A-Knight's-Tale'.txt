The Making of 'A Knight's Tale'

 This made-for-TV documentary details the work that went into the production of A Knight's Tale, Brian Helgeland's story of a squire who impersonates a knight after his master suddenly dies. Adventure fans are treated to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of this mix of medieval drama with modern music, as well as to interviews with the director and cast members Heath Ledger and Alan Tudyk, who give their insights into the efforts that were needed to bring this project to completion.
