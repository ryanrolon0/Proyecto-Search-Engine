Senescence

 Senescence is the story of what happens when technology has finally made obtainable the two most worldly desires of man place in the near future amidst the city of Los Angeles, Landen, a guy in his early twenties struggles to make ends meet. Cecille, a wealthy older woman, is trying to recover from the loss of her deceased husband. Landen and Cecille's lives cross paths when they stumble on what appears to be God's answer to all their prayers
