Silent Nights

 Silent Nights focuses on people caught in a web of deceit, corruption and murder Claudia Jaye, The Prostitute-A call-girl who enjoys her work; Nick Fisher, The Gambler-Schizophrenic on heavy medication; Ian Devlin, The Romantic-A shy loner who's killed before; Carmen Archer, The Model-A voluptuous woman with dark secrets; Shane Widmark, The Replacement-He'll do anything to get ahead. They're all involved with Bryce Morgan who runs the illegal activities and knows everybody's dirty secrets. When Morgan is murdered, the others realize the killer is one of them.
