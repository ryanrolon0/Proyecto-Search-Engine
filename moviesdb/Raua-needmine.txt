Raua needmine

 Metsatöll performs its own songs and several by Estonian composer Veljo Tormis in arrangements for rock band and male choir by Tauno Aints. Works performed are "The Giant-Hero of Osel", "Litany to Thunder", "Serf's Song", "My Sacred Grove", "Wolfrage", "An Aboriginal Song", "God, Protect Us from War", "I Cannot Find My Song", "See You on the Battlefield, Damn!", "Song of the Turkish War", "Sons and Daughters of Northern Winds", "Woodwrath 2", "Curse Upon Iron", "How Can I Recognize My Home" and "Charm Against Snakes".
