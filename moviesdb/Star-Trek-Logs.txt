Star Trek Logs

 Originally broadcast as part of MTV Networks' BIG PICTURE series, THE STAR TREK LOGS finds Counsellor Troi of Star Trek bridge of a deserted Enterprise-D as she recalls the events of the "Battle for Peace" that eventually led to the historic signing of the Khitomer Accords between the Federation and the Klingon Empire. Featuring William Shatner, Leonard Nimoy, DeForrest Kelley and the rest of the STAR TREK crew recalling the events of STAR TREK VI Federation history.
