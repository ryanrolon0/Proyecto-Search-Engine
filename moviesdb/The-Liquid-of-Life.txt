The Liquid of Life

 A cinematic voyage through anxieties and blood. Mapping various concepts connected with blood, through recreation of events and personal experiences from the filmmaker's life and meetings with people whose lives or professions are linked to blood. Investigating the medical, cultural, psychological, linguistic, artistic and cinematic meanings of the liquid that vampires tend to like so much and also reaches insights of us - as humans, Israelis and Jews in Israel today.
