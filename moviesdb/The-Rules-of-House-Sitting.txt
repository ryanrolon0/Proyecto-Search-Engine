The Rules of House-Sitting

 This is a comedy about a student who is asked to house-sit for his professor. He writes to a local advice columnist, MISS BAD MANNERS, and asks the questions - 'What's the deal with the food thing?' Miss Bad Manners proceeds to give a list of the most common 'rules of house-sitting' which the student promptly proceeds to break, paying the consequences along the way. The film ends in disaster when the student breaks the Golden Rule of House-Sitting
