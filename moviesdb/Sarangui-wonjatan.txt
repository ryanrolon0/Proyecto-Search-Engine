Sarangui wonjatan

 Based on the life of Son Ryang-won who spent six years in prison during the period of Japanese occupation. He was released at the end of the war. He, his wife and two children devoted their lives to helping lepers. His sons are killed in an uprising in Yeosu in 1946. When the Korean War breaks out, he helps many people escape to the south, but he is captured and tortured by North Korean soldiers
