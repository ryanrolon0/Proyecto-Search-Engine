Think Again

 This moving documentary chronicles the plight of children lured into prostitution. Heartrending interviews with three former sex workers are at the emotional center of the film, as it sheds light on the causes and consequences of sexual exploitation of children aged 10-14. Special emphasis is placed on the role of the Internet, which has become a major tool for luring disaffected youth into the sex industry. Viewers report that the film made a deep impression, educating and motivating them to defend themselves against the dark side of their on-line social networking sites.
