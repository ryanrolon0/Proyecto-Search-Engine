Top 12 Moments of the BET Awards

 On June 28th, the best and the brightest will gather for the 9th Annual BET Awards Show; an evening that has consistently served up the largest names from every corner of entertainment, and jaw-dropping moments of unbelievable surprise. Top 12 Moments of the BET Awards will gather a unique blend of familiar Black American entertainment voices, to share their reflections on the moments that have made the past 8 years of watching and participating in the BET Awards Show so incredibly memorable.
