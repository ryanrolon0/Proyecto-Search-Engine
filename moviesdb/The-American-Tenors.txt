The American Tenors

 This is the DVD version of The American Tenors' PBS Special shown nationwide in March of 2003. Nathan Granner, Daniel Montenegro and Mauricio O'Reilly showcase some of the world's most beloved songs, including "With a Song in My Heart", "If I Loved You", "Be My Love", "Shenandoah", "Amazing Grace" and many more. Also contains bonus concert footage and interviews with the tenors and their creator, Frank McNamara.
