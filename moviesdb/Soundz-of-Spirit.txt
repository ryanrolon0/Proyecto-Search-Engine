Soundz of Spirit

 Award-Winning Film Soundz of Spirit the creative process is recognizing the importance of the journey" Directed by Joslyn Rose Lyons Unlike any other hip-hop documentary to date, Soundz of Spirit director's cut is a riveting feature length documentary film that explores the relationship between the creative process and spirituality in hip-hop culture. After premiering at the HBO Urban World Film Festival, the documentary traveled extensively through the indie-art-house festival circuit from the Black International Cinema Berlin and London Raindance, to Blacksoil in Rotterdam, Europe, and with the International Hip Hop Film Festival in Canada and Australia. Soundz was named Best Music Documentary by the New York International Independent Film & Video Festival and was released on DVD for a limited time with an original soundtrack. "Soundz intellectual discourse and righteous musical all rolled up in one". The Source magazine "Soundz of Spirit goes all out to explain the creative process and spirituality behind hip-hop music using artists' own words." Black Beat Soundz of Spirit draws the connections between the creative freedom and the spiritual outlet that are provided by hip-hop culture for the current generation. The documentary captures artists in their moments of inspiration and takes in-depth looks into their creative process, from conception to completion.Featured in Soundz of Spirit are KRS-1, Andre 3000 (Outkast),Common, Cee-Lo, Nappy Roots, Talib Kweli, Blackalicious, Jurassic 5, Dilated Peoples, The Last Poets, Aceyalone, DJ Q-Bert, Living Legends, Saul Williams, Shock G, Mystic, Zion I, Medusa, Michael Franti of Spearhead, Radioactive, Del, Davey D, Planet Asia, Goapele, Martin Luther, and many others.
