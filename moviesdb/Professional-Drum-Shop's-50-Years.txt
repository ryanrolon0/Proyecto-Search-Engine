Professional Drum Shop's 50 Years

 Professional Drum Shop story of 50 years in Hollywood on Vine st. 1959 to present Music/Drumming/recording industry in Hollywood in the 50's to now. Invention of the plastic drum head. 15 to 20 interviews of famous drummers from Woody Herman band to Steely Dan to motion picture and TV recording percussionist. The effect of the Beatles on the music industry and the drum set changes that occurred.
