Something Else

 Urban African American Couple faces the struggles of life and the challenges it takes to make something out of themselves. Just when life was going perfect they lose everything worked for in one night. This story is about a man who realizes just want is important to him. When face with the impossible we learn just how much a man can take when he is forced to build him life from scratch....Something Else by Ernest Pierce A short film brought to you by Pierce Productions. A young man who has everything is challenged one night to make a decision that could affect his future. He must make a choice that could affect everything. A compelling story about life and the challenges people face. What do you do when you you must make a decision about your past and compare it to your future. This film encompasses all the emotions that humans go through growing up around urban areas.
