The Making of 'Summer Lovers'

 This documentary treats movie fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Summer Lovers, a romantic comedy about a young couple who goes on vacation in Greece and discovers the sexual openness of the locals has a rejuvenating effect on the pair's love life. Included are interviews with the cast and crew who share their experiences from making the film, as well as disscuss the efforts that went into it.
