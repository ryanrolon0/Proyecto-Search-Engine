Urban Exploration

 Urban Exploration is short Documentary Film about young people who gain access to forbidden environments for the rush of the taboo and the prize of photographic proof. This documentary about documenting disused containers of people, environments and derelict, collapsing landscapes. This story shows how boredom can lead to creativity, adventure, mystery and danger. No re-enactments, no script, no stock footage or talking heads. Just raw experience and real life situations, themes and more...
