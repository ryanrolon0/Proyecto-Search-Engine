Space Bites

 Our hero Sherbet Von Bushystem or Berty, is from the planet Mesh, where all food has become strictly forbidden, especially ice cream. The new evil ruler Queen Judiwa, now forces all the inhabitants to eat.....Vitamin...Oriented...Manually...Induced...Tablets. (V.O.M.I.T.) as their only source of food. Our story begins with Berty fleeing Mesh, and the tyranny of Evil Judiwa. Berty crash lands on Earth in his rocket powered Ice Cream truck where he meets a gang of earth kids. Berty gives the kids ice cream, and when they eat it they somehow acquire super powers. The kids befriend Berty and travel back to Mesh in order to save the planet from Judiwa's rule. Together, they fight the V.O.M.I.T. Squad, save the day, restoring food, ice cream, and freedom to the galaxy!
