Time Out for Murder

 A bank runner is accused of killing a woman for whom he deposited a large amount. Reporter and bank official investigate. The first entry of "The Roving Reporters" finds bank clerk Johnny Martin arrested for the murder of Peggy Norton. With the aid of a pretty young bill collector, Margie Ross, Barney Callahan and Snapper Doolan, reporter and photographer for "The Clarion" newspaper, set out to show that gangster Dutch Moran may have been involved.
