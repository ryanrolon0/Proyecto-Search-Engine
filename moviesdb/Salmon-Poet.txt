Salmon Poet

 Salmon Poet is a poetic journey, an ode to Nature through the voice of the poet Walt Curtis and the vision of filmmaker Sabrina Guitart. This artistic-ecological film, evokes the spiritual journey of the salmon through the Oregon landscape. Nature, shamanism and the return of the salmon to reproduce and regenerate the eco-system are the main themes of the film. Salmon Poet, honors Mother Earth, but also the Voice of the Poet, the prophet who speaks a message of ecological alarm and offers his poetical meditations on the earth itself.
