U-513 Lobo Solitario

 In 1943 a WWII German U-boat is sunk by an American airplane in the waters of Southern Brazil. In June 2009 the legendary Schurmann family began an expedition in search of the U-513, her survivors and the real history behind the Lonely Wolf. The journey will take them to the hot deserts of USA, cold forests of Germany, and deep in to the sea. Will they find the elusive and mysterious ship and revel its real secrets that are frozen in time on ocean floor?
