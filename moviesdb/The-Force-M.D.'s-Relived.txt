The Force M.D.'s Relived

 The Force M.D.'s Relived chronicles "The Rise, The Fall, The Resurrection" of the Force M.D.'s, one of the influential groups from the early days of Hip Hop, known for its pioneering innovative synthesis of R & B with break beats, heavy bass lines and other elements from the emerging Rap movement. The film fuses a classic vintage footage with live interviews with group members and influential figures in the music industry.
