Time of the Wave

 Kitamura Ryu- a typical, overworked Japanese man suddenly finds out that is dying from cancer. Forced to face the limitations of the time he has left and haunted by quilt Ruy decides to reconnect with his daughter, which he haven't seen for many years. When he fails to find her Ruy take a trip, going back to the Nature and there experience the pleasure of living in the NOW. Among the bamboo trees and the sun's rays of light he finds the center of Self within.After tapping to the wisdom of the Cosmic Time Ruy make a journey back to the city(Tokyo), symbolical starting a new beginning-free of fears and illusions.
