The Only Thing

 Sid is a young actor on the rise in New York City. His career is definitely taking off, but he's terrified of making a mistake. One night after a performance, Sid is heading home when he sees one of New York's most lauded actors leaving the show. Sid races after The Man and asks if they can have a drink. Peppering The Man with questions, Sid hopes to learn the secret to lasting fame and success, but The Man only offers more questions. Sid's fear of failure cripples him and he alienates the one person who loves and believes in him
