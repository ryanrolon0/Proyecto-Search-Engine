Silip

 "Revenge brings peace to no one, living or dead!" Four recently unemployed scriptwriters and a TV director decide to produce an independent digital horror film. As part of their research, they try to contact a spirit by playing Spirit of the Glass. After a successful but frightening encounter, they proceed to do an ocular of an old house they can shoot their horror movie. One by one, the writers go to the house and each have their own terrifying story to tell. A series of deaths befall the writers and director with only a digital camera as the lone witness to the killings. SILIP gives us a peek of what really happened...
