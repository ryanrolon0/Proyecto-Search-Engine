Something You Should Know

 There are few other bands in pop music history whose followers are as legendary as Duran Duran fans. Something You Should Know Fan Documentary is an intimate history of Duran Duran fans that explores their tireless devotion from 1978 to their explosive reaction during the 25th Anniversary, the 2003 Reunion tour, the 2004 UK Tour, and the release of Durans' first studio album, Astronaut, in 21 years. Something You Should Know culture phenomenon known as "Duranies". The film is also a glimpse of the band's influence, the fans' behavior, and explains the band's relevance in the history of pop music.
