Table and Chairs

 Lui (18) and Dan (14) are brothers. After the death of their mother, Lui moved out the day he turned 18, leaving his younger brother to deal with their abusive father by himself. It has been several months since the brothers have seen each other, during which time Lui has been living with his girlfriend Sarah and her family. Sarah and her mom are moving away and she wants Lui to come with, meaning Lui will be abandoning his younger brother permanently. Lui agrees but must first pay one last visit to his younger brother before he goes. What he finds when he gets there will change the course of their lives forever, and one thing's clear, he cannot leave his younger brother behind...
