Tin God

 Ethan and his half brother Jesse are constantly locking horns in their love/hate relationship with each other. Jesse the businessman has little time for his younger brothers artistic longings. Ethan wallows in his self pity at times, supported by whatever work his older brother throws his way. Ethan is searching for something deeper, he has a fantasy of finding true love so when the mysterious Sasha comes to town he is immediately enamored. Ethan's ex girlfriend Cassie watches, still in love with Ethan she resents the competition and tries her best to win him back. Despite her best efforts it become obvious to her that she is nothing more than second best. Sasha in the meantime has met up with Ethan's older brother Jesse, and is happily playing both brothers. Jesse is even aware of what he is doing but grossly underestimates his younger brothers feelings for the attractive stranger. Eventually Cassie, fed up with the situation confronts Ethan. Their fight turns physical as he chases after her only to witness her run down and killed on the street. Ethan torn by grief and anger then sets off on a murderous rampage, seeking revenge for himself and the one person that truly loved him...Cassie
