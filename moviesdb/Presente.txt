Presente

 (Tucson, Arizona)- A documentary by J.M. Aragón that uses artistic video imagery accompanied by a compelling musical score from renowned Puerto Rican folk artist, JosE "Pepo" Saavedra, to convey a reflective look into the Migrant Trail Walk of 2005. The 7 day/75 mile walk from Sásabe, Sonora, Mexico to Tucson, Arizona, USA that raised awareness about the deadly journey to the U.S., the tragic cost of border militarization, and also an opportunity to walk in solidarity with migrants.
