The Templar Code

 Two archaeologists finally find the clue that will lead them to what they've been trying to find for the last 3 years millions of dollars hidden by members of the Templar Knights. But when the clue leads in the direction of North Korea, they'll have hire a soldier to lead get them across the border. From France, to North Korea and England, their adventure is filled with betrayal, action, and suspense. They'll have to overcome dead end clues, North Korean soldiers, and mercenaries, if they want to find the coin.
