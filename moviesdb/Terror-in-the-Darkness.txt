Terror in the Darkness

 Stacey Smith's greatest desire was to be a working, Christian actress. After Stacey is turned down for a major role in a Christmas play, she discovers the real reason that she was not cast in the project. Discouraged, she retreats to Hollywood to seek fame and fortune. In the mist of her journey, she realizes that you do not have to be in the dead of the night to experience Terror in the Darkness.
