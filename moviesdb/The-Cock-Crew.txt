The Cock Crew

 The scene is a small paper-making factory presided over by the shiftless Ari and his long-suffering wife Taryn. Employees include Sybill and Windy, pale young women who toil endlessly at the pulp vats, and the more mature Helen, who is past breeding age. As Ari sits astride his beloved chimny, a Care-Giving Woman appears and offers and offers a rooster to Taryn. The crows of this beast cause ecstatic, erotic disquietude in anyone within earshot. The cottage becomes a tropical whirlwind of desire until Helen manages to drown the rooster in the bubbling pulp...
