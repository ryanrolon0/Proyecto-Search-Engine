The Making of 'The Corsican Brothers'

 This documentary treats movie fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Cheech & Chong's The Corsican Brothers, a comedy about the adventures of a pair of twin brothers who can feel whatever the other is experiencing. Included are interviews with stars Cheech Marin and Tommy Chong, as well as the rest of the cast and crew who share their experiences from making the film, as well as disscuss the efforts that went into it.
