Retreat

 Jennifer and Ray are a couple whose marriage has hit a low point. Having spent months arguing, Ray decides it is time the pair take a holiday to try to help solve their problems and rekindle the flame of passion in their lives. What starts off as a pleasant trip to the english country-side soon turns it arguments and disaster, when the car crashes leaving them stranded in the middle of nowhere.
