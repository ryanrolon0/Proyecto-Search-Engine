Reumert prisen 1999

 TV-special featuring the 1999, and second annual Reumert Awards (given to Danish theater productions and stage shows), broadcast live from Folketeatret in Copenhagen, this year. The TV-special also features extracts from the year's most prominent productions, performed live. Whole entire show hosted by _Hans Otto Bisgaard_ (qv) and _Camilla Miehe-Renard_ (qv).
