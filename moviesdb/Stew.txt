Stew

 Taking place over a single day, Stew tells the story of a young Vicar whose hectic lifestyle is finally catching up with him. Waking up after a wild party, Stew remembers that today is the day he has planned to cook a meal and propose to his girlfriend - the only problem is, he's got his duties at the Church to attend to as well. What follows is Stew's hilarious attempts to juggle all of his responsibilities, as disaster after disaster finally begins to push him over the edge, culminating in a shocking and unexpected finale.
