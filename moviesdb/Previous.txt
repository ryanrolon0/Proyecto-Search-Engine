Previous

 Time a fundamental structure of the universe, a dimension in which events occur in sequence. However; one man, born in a clock, possesses the amazing power to see into his immediate past. His name is Miles Previous. 'Previous' is an action packed comedy movie made in the style of an 80's cop show. From an explosive intro, to a gripping story, to five original commercials, 'Previous' is the best movie you will ever remember. Join Detective Miles Previous and his friends as he uses his incredible power of remembering what he just did, to solve the case of the missing time.
