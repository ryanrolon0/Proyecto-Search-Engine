The Squared Circle

 Michael Maine is a college wrestler with one objective in mind collegiate wrestling heavyweight championship. When that's snatched from his grasp by a cheating opponent, he leaves to find a new direction.....after a dark altercation. He hooks up with an old friend who's making it in the world of Poressional Wrestling. Mike finds a new life in that world and falls in love with it. Together, they rise through the ranks toward greater and greater success, but though their friendship runs deep all is not well. Past jealousy is inflamed by a love triangle and once the spark is lit, it isn't long before the fuse leads to a showdown.
