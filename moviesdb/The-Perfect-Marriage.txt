The Perfect Marriage

 Annie Grayson murdered her Californian husband Martin to collect his $250,000 insurance, shared with her lover Brent Richter, who provided poison and idea. But it's squandered, they split up and she moves to Philadelphia as Marrianne Danforth. Three years later she's remarried to real estate tycoon Donald Danforth's son Richard and their charity executive. The next year Brent tracks her down and sees trough her claim to be happy. They plan to murder dad in law first, which succeeds, then Richard. Devoted secretary Carrie Hollings gets wind of Marianne's lies, so she's killed by Brent, but the suspicion doesn't die with her. When Annie begins a steamy affair with seductive con man Brent, he uses his charm to convince her to murder her husband and split the inheritance. But when Brent squanders the money and starts sleeping around with other women, Annie packs up and leaves him. By the time Brent finds her again, she's happily married to Richard, a wealthy real estate heir. Will Brent succeed in convincing Annie to repeat the deadly scam? Watch and find out!
