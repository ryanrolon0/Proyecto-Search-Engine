Shakespeare ABC's It's Easy as 123

 We meet the actors of this innovative web series. We learn their alias and what they love about the work of William Shakespeare. This AFTRA series features a team of talented artist who unpack the work of The Bard in collaboration on theater row in Hollywood CA. The technique in this series is based on Lee Strasberg and Stella Adler. Anyone can learn Shakespeare. This new hot series demystifies the idea that iambic pentameter is hard to figure. It is all based in musical timing which unfolds in this delightful weekly series where the artist discover that William Shakespeare was one of our very first rappers.
