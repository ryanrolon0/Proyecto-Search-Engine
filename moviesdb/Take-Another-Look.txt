Take Another Look

 Sarah is one of those high school girls who feels like she's worthless and unattractive because of what she reads in fashion magazines, what she sees at school and who she interacts with. one day she is challenged by a mysterious character to meet someone called "Seefulls" then before she knows it she's in another world full of people just like her, forcing themselves to be fit, trying to be popular and struggling to look beautiful. later she questions herself and challenges herself to believe in who and what she really is. . .
