Skeleton Squad

 Meet Holly Hatchet ... born into a treacherous world of yuck, she has defied all odds and survived with her brother Chris fumbling by her side. Their father John Harper, a dream chaser, pissed off the dream world and was brutally slain for his actions. Moving to North Shore Holly met Vinny, Chris met Benny, and together they made an oath to close the door to the dream world forever.
