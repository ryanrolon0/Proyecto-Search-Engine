Unbeknown

 Los Angeles is a city full of immigrant stories. A young immigrant dreaming to become an American Citizen faces the hard reality of living in an apartment full of illegal immigrants owned by an abusive landlord. Without warning one day the landlord increases the rent and he is forced to go find a job as a day worker instead of taking his English exam. On the street he realize it's not going to be an easy day until a well dressed man in a nice car pulls up and chooses him. When he arrives at this guy's house, he is impressed with the large luxurious home. Soon he finds the man lied and he is now faced with unpredictable dilemma.
