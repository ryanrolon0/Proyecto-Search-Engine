Titty Slickers 3

 Morty and Harold take their unloving wives, Sheila and Tiffany, to a dude ranch for the weekend in hopes of rekindling their failing romances. The plan works, but not between the two married couples. The wives have eyes for a dude named Clint. And the husbands take notice of a couple country girls named Tammy and Patty - but things may not work out as all hope or expect.
