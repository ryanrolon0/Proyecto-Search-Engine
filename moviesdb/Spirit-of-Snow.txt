Spirit of Snow

 A meditation on skiing, snow, and people; Spirit of Snow Uniquely captures the ambiance and pace of a day of back-country skiing. Part historical documentary part ski film, and part celebration of the unique personalities that helped define back-country skiing in America; spirit of snow will take you on a journey into the back-country through the eyes of the earliest pioneering powder skiers, a 10th mountain division veteran, and an expert avalanche forecaster.
