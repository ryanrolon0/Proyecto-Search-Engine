Smash Grab Therapy

 Jack is a Police Officer with a stressful job and a very odd way coping robs liquor stores. He never takes the money, and does it purely for the thrill, ordering up odd items from the puzzled clerks. This therapeutic activity soon turns into an addiction, and to help him get away on his next job, he recruits Kim - a young woman with a passion for cars a dreadful driving record. Jack pulls some strings to help her keep her license and now she owes him. The owners of Jack's next target are styling but deadly Eastern European mobsters, and after the job is done, there is no getting away. The resulting events take Jack in a new direction.
