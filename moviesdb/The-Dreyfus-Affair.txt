The Dreyfus Affair

 The seriocomic story of the handsome and spectacularly talented Randy Dreyfus who, at the age of twenty-eight, has a multimillion dollar contract playing shortstop for the Los Angeles Valley Vikings. Married to his college sweetheart, a former Miss California, with whom he has perfect twin daughters and a sprawling home in the suburbs, Dreyfus embodies the American Dream. But during the Vikings' tight race for the pennant, Randy is shocked to discover that he's attracted to - and then falls in love with - his second baseman. When their affair becomes public, Dreyfus' picture-perfect life comes crashing down around him as the resulting scandal rocks the world of organized baseball and quickly becomes the biggest story in the nation, all during one unforgettable World Series championship.
