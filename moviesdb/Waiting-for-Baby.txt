Waiting for Baby

 One of the RKO "Newlyweds" comedy shorts. Newlywed Bob (Robert Neil), is late to work and is told that the best alarm clock is a baby and when wife Betty (Suzi Crandall) telephones him that she has a surprise, he jumps to a hasty conclusion. He joins a class for expectant fathers. When he gets home, Betty shows him the surprise - a new alarm clock which sounds like a baby crying.
