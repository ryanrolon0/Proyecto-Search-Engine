Swept

 Richard and Jamie's lives are spinning out of control. He spends nights at the office while both his wife and boss demand more. She finds herself in a recovery center grappling to unite the voices in her head that fight to preserve and destroy her. When Richard visits Jamie at the center for the first time, this brother and sister begin to break down the barriers. Wavering between denial and confrontation, they struggle to find the path towards healing, support and love.
