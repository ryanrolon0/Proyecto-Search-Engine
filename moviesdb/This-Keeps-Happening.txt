Colin has locked himself in his apartment with one goal in mind has cleared out his room and brought with him 3 packs of cigarettes and just enough pills to kill him in days. Intent on this course of self-punishment, Colin deprives himself of any comfort in his final days. As time slowly progresses Colin begins to lose his grip on reality, He starts to imagine conversations with another version of himself and dark secrets from his past begin to emerge.
