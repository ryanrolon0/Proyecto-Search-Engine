Voll korrekte Jungs

 3 boys and a baby. X-mas is coming and there is a present for 17 years old Frank has left the family years ago. Frank will not do the same. He will not desert his child. So - who's going to help? His best friends - Wolle the nice weirdo and Burhan the young turkish macho - decide children real guys like they are can do it too. And the trouble starts....
