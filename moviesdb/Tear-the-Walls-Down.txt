Tear the Walls Down

 A documentary about 5 amazing people , their obstacles due to 2 members sexual orientation, some personal issues and how these WALLS helped in the creation of one of the greatest local bands of our area. we focused more on the positive and personal growth and less on the drama that lead to how they became the shiz The story of 2 amazing people. It chronicles their meeting , there marriage and their music. Problems they encountered due to there sexual orientation and there life's journey that lead them to the formation of the band "the shiz". the plot echoes there personality ,some ups , some downs...but always optimistic or to quote lilli lewis "crunchy,happy,lovie hippie people"
