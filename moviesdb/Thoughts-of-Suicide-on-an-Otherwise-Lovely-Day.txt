Thoughts of Suicide on an Otherwise Lovely Day

 When a business acquaintance drops dead at 43, Jaycee Damon begins questioning her own existence. Caught up in a fast-paced work life that never seems to slow down, Jaycee struggles to find peace and clarity in a world that feels increasingly more insane. Her journey is one she must walk alone for in the end, it is only ourselves who can decide to find the courage to live.
