Thrust Video Candy Vol. I

 THRUST is a comedic look into the wild and sexy import car lifestyle with amazing car show footage, funny interviews and a flashy visual style. Thrust travels to huge import car shows from Los Angeles to Miami to check out the scene on the top award winning pimped-out cars, gets to know sexy models, amazing break dancers and world champion scratch DJ's. In its signature smart-ass fashion, Thrust messes with models and car guys across the country with hilarious game show-like interviews. But you'll also experience what the exploding import car scene is all about, why these people do what they do and what keeps them going.
