The Shift

 "The Shift" follows a day in the life of Damon Yorke, a paramedic technician for the London Ambulance Service. As he embarks on a particularly stressful night shift with his driver Joe, Damon's anger and frustrations with his personal life come to the surface, and something's got to give. Mixing drama and comedy in equal measure, "The Shift" is a short film about the pressures and the job and facing harsh truths.
