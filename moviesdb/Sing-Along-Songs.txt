Sing Along Songs

 Sing, dance, and play along with your favorite Disney songs! Learning the lyrics is easy because the words appear right on the screen. And for the first time, you can check out cool new dance moves and sing karaoke-style too! So join Disney's most beloved characters in their most memorable musical moments, and turn up the volume on euphonious fun for the entire family! Get set to laugh your antlers off with hosts Rutt and Tuke -- those wisecracking, sidesplitting moose brothers from Disney's BROTHER BEAR. Count on the comic stylings of this wacky duo to keep the hilarity and the harmony going strong from song to song! With dazzling musical numbers from BROTHER BEAR, TREASURE PLANET, 101 DALMATIANS II, STITCH! THE MOVIE, and more, BROTHER BEAR landscape of excitement and entertainment!
