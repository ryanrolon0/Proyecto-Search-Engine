Post Psychotic Girlfriend Trilogy

 Love has many layers. And it manifests itself in an array of colors, shapes, and forms.  Addicted to the highs and lows of the all-powerful drug of love, I've captured three notable variations of the emotion in this trilogy. They embody those that are most prominent in my retrospection.
