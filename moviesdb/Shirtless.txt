Shirtless

 This documentary, shown on the American Movie Classics (AMC) cable channel, is a survey of Hollywood's leading men from silent film star 'Rudolph Valentino' (qv) to today's young stars, such as 'Kerr Smith' (qv). It shows how the various types of leading men have changed through the years, including the ladies' man, man's man, action hero, and antihero.
