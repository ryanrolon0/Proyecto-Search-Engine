Riddles in Rhythm

 This Universal musical short (U-I production number 3651)features The Lancers, Nelson Riddle and His Orchestra, singers Kay Brown and Matt Dennis, and Earl Barton, Jack Costanza, and Augie & Marge. Numbers include "Lison Antigua," "Jazz Ballet," "You Are My Lucky Star," "Barton's Routine," "Brother John" and "We Belong Together."
