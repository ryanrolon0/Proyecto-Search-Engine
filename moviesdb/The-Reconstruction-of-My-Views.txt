The Reconstruction of My Views

 A short film chronicling changes to the filmmakers NYC SOHO landscape. To which she has cast TIME and LIGHT as the main characters. The weather and all other elements that play within the framework act as the supporting characters to the reconstruction of her views. Using time lapse photography and frame animation editing techniques, the film spans a 2.5 year period tracing the environment of what once was an active parking lot to the construction of and completed building of the Crosby Street Hotel.
