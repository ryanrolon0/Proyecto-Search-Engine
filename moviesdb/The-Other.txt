The Other

 Everyone has heard of a love triangle, but what about a love square? Who loves who? Who knows about the scandal? Who wants to kill the enemy? Who will get away with the crime? Who is out for revenge, who wins? Is it him, her or The Other? Drake Films, LLC has another award winning film that takes another step into the unknown. The Other is a silent thriller that will keep you on the edge of your seat throughout the film. You will wonder who is who, what is going on in the minds of the avengers and what do they plan to do next? It's a creepy, exciting look at the silent film genre. Dialogue is not necessary to convey the message in this film!
