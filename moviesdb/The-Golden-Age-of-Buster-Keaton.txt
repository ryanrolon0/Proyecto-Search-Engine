The Golden Age of Buster Keaton

 Compilation of scenes from Buster Keaton's films. The films are Butcher Boy; Fatty at Coney Island; Good Night Nurse!; One Week; The Scarecrow; Neighbors; Cops; The Playhouse; The Boat; The Paleface; My Wife's Relations; The Blacksmith; Day Dreams; Three Ages; Our Hospitality; Sherlock Jr; The Navigator; Seven Chances; Go West; Battling Butler; The General; College; and Steamboat Bill Jr.
