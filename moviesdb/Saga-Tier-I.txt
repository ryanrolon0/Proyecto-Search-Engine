Saga Tier I

 In Westlyn City, Treyman Lewis is a thriving Realtor who is haunted by his past in nightmares. Vonda Lewis, his wife, who is a high powered attorney attempts to help him cope with his dreams, but is unsuccessful. Meanwhile, the city is ran by a corrupt business man and politician, John Grazier, who is becoming uneasy. Lately there is a new string of murders, rumor has it there is a new crime syndicate moving in known as Magi, which is headed by the relentless assassin Gage. In the midst of all of this turmoil, there has been talks of an unknown assassin, the ninja. Whether if this Ninja is a friend or foe is unknown, but one thing is for sure. Life in Westlyn is going to be interesting.
