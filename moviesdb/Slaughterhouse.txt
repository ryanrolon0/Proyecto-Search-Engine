Slaughterhouse

 Grant returns home from the military to the decaying farmhouse where he was brought up. He has come looking for answers into the death of his estranged father. Once there he finds the familiar countryside different - sinister. A strange woman is hiding, terrified, in his deserted, isolated home. As the sun goes down it becomes clear they are not alone of brainwashed Christian cultists ensues and the besieged are forced to unite against the common enemy. Will they survive the night? A battle scarred British Army deserter returns to his isolated family home - to find his father has been murdered and his village over-run by Christian fanatics. Teaming with up with the sole survivor, a terrified female African immigrant, he must hold out until dawn, as the religious psychos return to finish the job. As the battle rages with every weapon available, the ancient farm will become a Slaughterhouse...
