Sooner Magic

 Sooner Magic is a cinematic collage and education into the history surrounding the supernatural power that wouldn't let the Sooners lose. This film, which consists of interviews from the Sooner coaches and players who were part of the games, will highlight the most supernatural games that involved Sooner Magic. The film is written by Emmy Award Winning and Peabody Award Winning writer Michael Strom. He is the one who wrote the groundbreaking ESPN SportsCentury documentary series.
