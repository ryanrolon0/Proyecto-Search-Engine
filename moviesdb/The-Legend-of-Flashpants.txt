The Legend of Flashpants

 One their lunch break three female office workers head to a local male strip club for a diversion. They arrive at a club called 'Fallen Angels' and find it to be depressingly run down. Just as they are about to leave, a waiter mysteriously appears and takes their drink orders. After being questioned by the girls, the waiter tells of the club's glory days and a curious set of events the eventually leads to Fallen Angels's downfall.
