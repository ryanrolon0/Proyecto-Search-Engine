Shon the Piper

 Shon, a scotch duke, refused a woman because she is only after his money and flees in search of adventures into the Scotisch highlands.He falls in love with Madge McIvor,which in turn is to marry the Laird of the Isla.Shon steals her away from the wedding feast and the whole clan follows them.In the end shon is accepted by Madges Family.
