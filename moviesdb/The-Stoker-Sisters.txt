The Stoker Sisters

 Sisters born during the time of Jane Austen, now vampires in the 21st century, battles rogue hunters and ruthless vampires known as Strigoi vampires according to Romanian and Bulgarian vampire myth. Caught in the middle of the battle is the man they both love, and whose secret can destroy them or redeem them. Before they can find out which, a figure from their past has arrived, set on destroying them all.
