Victor Frederick Weisskopf

 May 1992, La Jolla, California; Victor Frederick Weisskopf of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology visits the Institute for Advanced Physics Studies, (Stefan University). In conversation with V. Alexander Stefan, VIKI, (as he is known worldwide), reminiscences on physics and physicists of the 20th century Schroedinger, Werner Heisenberg, Wolfgang Pauli, and others.
