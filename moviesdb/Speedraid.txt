Speedraid

 Speedraid is the world's first documentary about speed-riding Paul G. have been ambitious alpinists and para gliders for a long time. Combining their skills and searching for a true adventure, they are setting off for a journey through the Alps. Their mission is to climb 10 of the most interesting summits in Central Europe and to descend with their speedgliders. On their way, they are facing various challenges which are pushing them to their personal limits.
