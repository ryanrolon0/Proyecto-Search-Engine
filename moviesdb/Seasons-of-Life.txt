Seasons of Life

 Lauren has obtained her childhood dream of a perfect storybook life, living in a peaceful mountain community with her soul mate and two children. Then one day, tragedy strikes. In an instant, her husband and children are taken away. Filled with guilt and despair she spirals down onto the streets of homelessness in Los Angeles. Lost and without hope she meets a stranger; Angela Washington and by her grace Lauren finds hope and strength to continue.
