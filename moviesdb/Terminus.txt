TERMINUS

 Centering on it's two main characters; Jack - a man submerged in his own darkness and haunted by the demons of his troubled past. And Roxanna - A young Polish immigrant who has made Dublin her home and always tries to focus on the light side of life, while she herself harbors demons of her own. Darkness & light collide to bring some kind of color to both their lives but is short lived as the shadow their respective pasts comes ever closer, unfolding a story of pain, violence, passion and vengeance.
