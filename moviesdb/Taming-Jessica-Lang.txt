Taming Jessica Lang

 The World is a crazy place, but so is Jessica. Her life, was a normal life; her mother and father did their best, to give her everything she wanted in life. They never complained because of the cost of her mental issues, she is their child after all. Even the cost of replacing the pets she killed was okay. It's a part of growing up, her father always said. It's now time for Ethan, to fix what her parents did not. He now has the job of, Taming Jessica Lang.
