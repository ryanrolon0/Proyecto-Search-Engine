The Clown

 The Clown is a short film about 2 long-time friends, Deirdre and Alvin. Deirdre stops by Alvin's apartment for a short visit, and when she walks into his living room she finds all his clown outfits and toys have been pulled out of long-buried boxes. As she waits for him to come out she plays with his clown stuff. Alvin is in the midst of reliving his dream of joining the circus and becoming a clown. The two friends connect on the level of their respective dreams for what they want in life, and find that they go about realizing their dreams in different ways. How do any of us bring our secret dreams out to the world? There is a bit of madness about our dreams, yet do we have a choice?
