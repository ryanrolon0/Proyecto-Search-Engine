The Flash

 Wally West is grieving the death of his mentor, Barry Allen, The Flash ... and trying to decide if he wants to continue the heroic legacy as The Fastest Man Alive; when two of Gotham City's most sensational villains, Harley Quinn and Talia al Ghul, come to Kansas to ask for his help at any expense. They are willing to do anything to get his help - even - if it means harming Wally West's girlfriend, Linda Park. Now Wally must choose between hurting over the death of his mentor, or helping these two villains with a shocking request, and in the process determine his own future in The Flash
