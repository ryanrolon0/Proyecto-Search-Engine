Tele kizlar

 Sahin is a courageous inspector investigating prostitution cases. Yusuf is one of the notorious gang leaders. He traps young women and turns them into prostitutes. As one of his girls attempts committing suicide, he gets on thin ice. Yusuf leaves the girl in the woods but another call-girl named Cagla witnesses this. She asks help from Cagla who also is a call-girl and Cagla takes her into her house in order to protect her friend from Yusuf. However he finds her place. As events follow each other Cagla and Sahin fall in love. They work cooperatively to catch Yusuf and his big bosses. In the end, the culprits get the penalty they deserve and Cagla and Sahin will start a brand new life together.
