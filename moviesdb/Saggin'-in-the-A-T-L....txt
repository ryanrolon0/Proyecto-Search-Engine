Saggin' in the A-T-L...

 A short documentary film about the pants sagging phenom and one of the ways Atlanta is trying to combat it. We'll hear from those who are outraged and from those who are outraged by the outraged. From 5 Points, Legend Barber Shop, to Buckhead to the BET Hip Hop awards red-carpet, list to what on the hearts and minds of people addressing, 'Saggin in the A T L'.
