The Man Who Invented the Words

 A man who experiments lost of the one he loves, back home. So home is anymore home like before. The man arrives to a new country to stay with his son but in that huge and extreme new place, he feels even further of everything it was familiar, further from himself . After some years, he decides to enroll to an ESL class, which will be the center of an unique universe in which different ways to understand the world co exits. Through this class, our man finds out that he does not have to try to understand the world he has to try to fit in it.
