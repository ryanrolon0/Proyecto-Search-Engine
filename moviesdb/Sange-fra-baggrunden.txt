Sange fra baggrunden

 Maria is living by herself in a summer house after she has been divorced. But the peace and tranquility she was hoping to find is replaced by a growing paranoia and discomfort about the place and her neighbors. It is as if they're trying to tell her something. Maria starts to have nightmares and the feels that she is being watched. Soon the discomfort of the place turns into horror after receiving weird phone calls and seeing her dead daughter. Finally Maria is looked up by her old friend from high school who makes Maria remember the awful truth about her pas past.
