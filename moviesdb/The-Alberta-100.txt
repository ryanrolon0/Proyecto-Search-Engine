The Alberta 100

 The Alberta 100 is an hour-long documentary celebrating the province's centennial and honoring some of the most notable figures of Alberta's history. Hosted by Ron MacLean, The Alberta 100 counts down cattle barons and oil tycoons, aboriginal leaders and hearty settlers, Innovative visionaries and spirited statesmen. Interviewees include kd lang, Tommy Banks, Ian Tyson, Preston Manning and Mel Hurtig.
