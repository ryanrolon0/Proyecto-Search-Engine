Teed Off Too

 It's skit-comedy and hidden-camera hi-jinx on the links - National Lampoon style. The people who brought you Animal House, Vacation, and Van Wilder invite you to play a round with television and film star David Leisure and his hilarious troupe of not-ready-for-tee-time players as they pull some of the most outrageous pranks ever captured by a hidden camera.
