Rise and Shine

 Around the world soccer is a sport that transcends culture, religion, politics and difference. From the townships of Africa to the streets of Paris, soccer belongs to the people and has become a powerful symbol of hope and passion. Growing up outside of Green Bay, Wisconsin, Jay DeMerit shared the world's love for the game. Rise and Shine the Jay DeMerit story is a true story of a young American's impossible journey - a journey never experienced or heard of in the soccer world. From his small mid-western football town to the streets of London, and on to the greatest stage in sports, follow Jay's rise to the top of the soccer world.
