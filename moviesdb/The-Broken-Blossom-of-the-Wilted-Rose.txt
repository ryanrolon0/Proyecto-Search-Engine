The Broken Blossom of the Wilted Rose

 A man, feeling the anguish of the cruel world, contemplates suicide by jumping off a cliff. A boy soon interrupts the man, and the two engage in a discussion about life and love. Ultimately, the boy leaves the man with his pessimistic thoughts, hoping the man finds hope. The man soaks the aftermath of the discussion and decides to persevere in the journey we call life. The man must overcome the death of his Scottish Terrier.
