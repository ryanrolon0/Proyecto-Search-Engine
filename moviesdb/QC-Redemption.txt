QC Redemption

 In Charlotte, NC, also known as the "Queen City" in 2008 about a powerful drug lord, who seems to be on top of the world. He is ready to get out the game and change his life for the better but fate takes a horrific twist when he is double crossed by his wife and best friend. Now he's back to shut down the street empire he built by any means possible.
