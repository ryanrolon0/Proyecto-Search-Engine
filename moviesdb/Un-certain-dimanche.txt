Un certain dimanche

 Two young girls, Gabrielle and Jeanne, just coming out of adolescence, take off in a car one Sunday afternoon without their parents knowledge. This moment of rebellion allows them their first taste of freedom. Everything can be possible. Can they assume it to the end?
