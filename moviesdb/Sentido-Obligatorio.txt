Sentido Obligatorio

 Ernesto is one of the elements of a complex and dangerous plan that is about to fail, what leads him and his companions to a delicate position. Along with Rosi and Andrey, decides to disappear from the city that very night. In the storm of events, Ernesto meets Laura, with whom he has a small relationship that suddenly seems to open a different horizon in the path of his life. However, some events will rush the outcome and will inevitably lead his footsteps into one of the two only ways he has.
