Screen Snapshots 1860

 This entry of Screen Snapshots travels to Las Vegas, Nevada, and 'Ralph Staub' (qv) visits and talks to many Hollywood notables as they arrive at the famed gambling city and vacation at a dude-ranch resort and spa. Included among the resort guests are 'Roy Rogers (I)' (qv), 'Dale Evans (I)' (qv), Mr. and Mrs. 'Richard Hart (I)' (qv), 'Yvonne De Carlo' (qv), 'Clara Bow (I)' (qv) and her husband, former western-movie star and current Nevada politician, 'Rex Bell (I)' (qv). All of the footage shown was current-1949 footage, so there is no (archive footage) attributes that should be attached to any of the names in this film.
