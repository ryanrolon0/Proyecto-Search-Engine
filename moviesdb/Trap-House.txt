Trap House

 Gavin Kraemer is your average high school graduate; he has a kind past and a possible future law career. However like many teens, he has become bored with normality. in a driven attempt against sobriety, Gavin along with his closest friends Chase, Cameron, and Jimmy venture into the dark world of intoxication at the hands of the mysterious drug dealer Eko. Their lives are plummeted into a river of downfall as they become trapped by the evils that surround them and consumed by the degeneration of our generation.
