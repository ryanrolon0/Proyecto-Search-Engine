The Obolus

 In this fairy tale for adults, ten year old Michael, traveling with his family to visit his father's newly engineered bridge, wakes up in the back seat of the family car alone in the middle of the night. Parked near an old lighthouse, Michael starts his journey to find his parents. Challenged by Bridgeman, the lighthouse keeper, Michael will have to gain strength and courage to find his way out.
