The Friendzone

 When news reaches Earth that an asteroid is heading towards the planet and there are only 3 days left to live, Sam decides to go to the house of his co-worker Lisa and admit his love for her, before the world ends. Unfortunately when he gets there, his co-workers, who share his feelings for Lisa, are also there and he realizes he has to compete for her love. A farcical satire on office romance set at the end of the world.
