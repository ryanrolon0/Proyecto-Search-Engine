Waqt Ki Pukar

 Ajay belongs to a middle-class family, and has to find work to make ends meet. He unfortunately finds employment with a gangster named Ganpat Rai, who wants to use him to further his unlawful gains. On the other hand is Raja, who has a grudge to settle against Ganpat. But Ganpat pits Ajay against Raja, who engage in a bitter battle, not knowing that they are brothers who had been separated years ago. But will they survive long enough to find out about their blood relationship, or will Ganpat get them killed first.
