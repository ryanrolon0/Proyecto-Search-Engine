Roman Nights

 Tennessee Williams and his muse / friend Anna Magnani battle demons -real and imagined- as they make their way through the labyrinth of fame from Rome to Hollywood to New York. Set during the Golden Age and the Dolce Vita era of the 1950s and 1960s. The tumultuous and inspired friendship that Williams and Magnani shared endured 24 years and inspired two Williams masterworks The screenplay is based on the acclaimed play "Roman Nights" by Franco D'Alessandro. The play was produced by Suzanne Corso and premiered Off-Broadway in 2002. "Roman Nights" is opening in various cities throughout Europe from 2004 to 2007.
