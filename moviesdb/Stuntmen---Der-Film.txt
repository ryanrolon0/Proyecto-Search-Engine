Stuntmen - Der Film

 Stuntmen is an action short movie with elements of a behind the scenes feature. The movie centers around a young ambitious stunt team who has to work together with a greedy producer names Günther Von Stein. His perception about how to do stunts does not correspond to the usual working methods of the business. Due to financial restrictions the young team is forced to execute dangerous and risky stunts for the producer's new action movie. Soon, first conflicts arise concerning the accomplishment of some daring and hazardous stunts and because of marketing reasons, the tyrannous producer prefers risk over safety. Will the stunt team still be able to accomplish their goal to shoot a spectacular action movie?
