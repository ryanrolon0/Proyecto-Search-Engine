The Wonder World of K. Gordon Murray

 He was responsible for distributing more than 60 feature films in only two decades. And yet, his own story has been shrouded in both myth and conjecture since his untimely death almost thirty years ago. You are invited on a personal journey across several decades of exploitation film history and pop culture. From the crafty world of early carnivals and sideshows... to the decaying drive-in's and movie palaces of the past, you will learn the true story of K. Gordon Murray... from mouths of those who knew him. Whether you flocked to the theaters to see his presentations or tried desperately to avoid them, few will deny that he is one of film histories neglected pioneers and one the last "true" showman of the 20th century.
