Rasa

 Following the kidnap of his ten year old daughter in Jakarta, Professor Anthony Collins is forced to recover an ancient artifact as a ransom for her life. His path is entangled with a young artist, called Rianti, whose premonitions of people before they die may help Anthony save his daughter's life. Anthony, Rianti and the love of her life are all in grave danger as the kidnappers masked agenda reaches beyond the ransom.
