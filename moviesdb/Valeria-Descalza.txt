Valeria Descalza

 Four characters and two cities. Raul is working on a film in Bilbao and he works at the same time as a travel agent in Cuba. Leonor, his wife, is watching the crisis of their marriage and writes love songs that encourage Salvador, and old architect obsessed in see again Valeria, his daughter, who wants became a singer. Raul just meet Valeria and he cannot forget her. VALERIA DESCALZA is a portrait in present day La Habana.
