The 2003 World Yo-Yo Championships

 Yo-Yo Historian and author of "Lucky's Collectors Guide to 20th Century Yo-Yos" hosts the first documentary on the World Yo-Yo Championships. Filmed during the 2003 World Championships held in Orlando Florida, hundreds of players representing eight different countries vie for world championship titles in five different styles of play. Dr. Lucky Meisenheimer walks us through the different competitions and introduces us to the colorful personalities that make this the premier yo-yo event in the world.
