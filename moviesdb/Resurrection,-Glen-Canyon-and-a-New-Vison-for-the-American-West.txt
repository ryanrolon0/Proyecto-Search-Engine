Resurrection, Glen Canyon and a New Vison for the American West

 Narrator Holter Graham, voice for HBO, tells the story of Glen Canyon in this 26-minute film based on the book Resurrection by Annette McGivney with photography by James Kay. By combining historical video clips of Glen Canyon, before the dam, along with more than 100 stunning images from the book, this inspirational new film showcases the remarkable reemerging canyons of Glen while providing a hopeful message for the future of Western water resources and rivers.
