The New Coach

 Four scenes make up this video. First football player Anthony finds the janitor in the shower stall. He wants to know what the janitor knows about the new coach. Secondly the new coach displays his experience for the principal. Third, scholarship player Hart induces the towel boy to help him out sexually. And lastly, three players discuss how hard the new coach was on them in practice... before he comes and apologizes about it. It seems the principal was being really hard on HIM. They all have sex and then joke about the principal.
