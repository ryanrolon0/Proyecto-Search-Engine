TripFLIX

 Outdoor adventures, thrilling amusement rides, and quirky roadside attractions included, packing up the car and hitting the open road is a longtime tradition of American families! Set a course with comic travelers, Alex and Emily, and embark on the vacation of a lifetime, visiting 25 kid-favorite locations across America. TripFLIX lets you pick your route and create your own adventures packed with exciting locations, fun trivia, interactive activities, and even a few dance lessons. Along with Alex and Emily, you'll train dolphins, fly rockets , rope cattle,  tour a big-league ballpark, and much much more!
