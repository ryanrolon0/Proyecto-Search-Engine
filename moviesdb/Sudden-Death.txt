Sudden Death

 On February 27, 2009 the Fletcher High School Lacrosse Program experienced Sudden Cardiac Arrest first hand when goalie James Hendrick suffered commotio cordis after blunt force trauma to the chest. Experience first hand the incident of James Hendrick through the narrative accounts of the people associated with James, and those who have made it their mission to bring Automated External Defibrillator awareness to public schools in the State of Florida and nationwide.
