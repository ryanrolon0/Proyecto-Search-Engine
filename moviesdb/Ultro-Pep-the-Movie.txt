Ultro-Pep the Movie

 Simon has to overcome his fear of heights and save his girl Haley from an army of energy drinking zombies. The town of Dullworth has been invaded by the living dead. Brought back to life by Ultro-Pep zombies are the perfect worker at the drink plant. Simon is fired and has to use the breath spray to kill all the zombies. Sounds goofy? It is the goofiest 70 minutes of your life!
