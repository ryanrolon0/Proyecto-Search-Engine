The Man from the Moon

 The Man From The Moon is a dark children's fantasy about a lonely young girl who is abused by her mother and one night wishes to be rescued. The one night she wishes an eccentric Dream maker who lives on the moon hears her wish and answers. The Dream Maker and Ella develop a special and innocent bond which climaxes in Ella facing her mother with the help of the Dream Maker. If a door slams in an empty house is it heard? Or if a tree falls in a forest do we hear it? How about if a lonely child cries in the night, and makes a wish on the moon? Does anyone answer the call? The Man From The Moon does!
