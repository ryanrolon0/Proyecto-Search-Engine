Soul Survivor

 The road to recovery can be harsh and daunting, but the personal growth of a survivor is immeasurable. A professional horse trainer with over thirty years experience undergoes twelve hours of reconstructive facial surgery. A story of one woman's courage and determination through personal tragedy. It's a new day and Leslie begins to reclaim her life, identity and passion. Leslie's strength and spirit can be celebrated by many, but her choice to continue working in a profession that almost claimed her life may be her most arduous decision. Leslie is the Soul Survivor.
