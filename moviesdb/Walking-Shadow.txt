Walking Shadow

 Walking Shadow tells the story of a young boy who is haunted by the grandfather he's never met. After Alex is awoken from a strange dream his quest begins. Alex decides to run away from home in search of his grandfather, Sam. A teenage runaway named Allison agrees to help Alex navigate the unknown world of the big city. When Allison leaves him for a moment he is set upon by a group of young bullies who steal his grandfather's pocket watch and lead him deeper into the maze of this unfamiliar world. Alex's journey is not done yet; he is about to have a mystical encounter with his grandfather and what he discovers will change his life forever.
