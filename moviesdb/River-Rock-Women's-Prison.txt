River Rock Women's Prison

 Meet Zerva and Joli, two new arrivals at River Rock Women's Prison. Joli is a first-time offender, naive and afraid. Zerva is a lifetime convict on yet another stint. They are worlds apart. Yet, they share a common need to find their place in this new world, a coal mine turned women's prison. Joli longs for comfort and assurance, which she finds under the protection of lifer Madam...
