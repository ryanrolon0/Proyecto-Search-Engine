The Dead Detective

 SOB Los Angeles detective Martin Stiff has fallen into the hardest murder mystery of his career, his own. Who can it be? His drunken secretary? His scoundrel partner? Maybe his cheating wife? It could even be the paroled mob boss out for vengeance... Join the stiff dic on this hilarious adventure with a long list of suspects who have no problem literally, and gladly, stabbing Martin in the back.
