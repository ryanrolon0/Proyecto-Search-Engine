Screen Snapshots 5854

 This Screen Snapshot short shows Art Linkletter, playing host to a group of children from famous film-land families. Among the stars whose children are seen are Judy Garland, Edgar Bergen, Gregory Peck, Keenan Wynn, Paul Douglas, Virginia Field, Eddie Bracken (with the most), Alan Ladd, Pat O'Brien, Paul Henreid and Don DeFore.
