Vaanmathi

 Sunil (Ajith) belongs to a middle class family, while Vaanmathi (Swathi) is the daughter of a rich business woman who abandoned her husband when Vaanmathi was a baby. Sunil and Vaanmathi play tricks on each other and fight after meeting but eventually fall in love, with Vaanmathi's mother is not happy with. The two lovers then have to overcome many obstacles to be united.
