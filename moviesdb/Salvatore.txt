Salvatore

 The charismatic Salvatore Luciano is spending his last years in a home for the elderly. He passes the days listening in peace to old Italian operas, drinking a glass of good Italian wine and smoking excellent cigars. But his peaceful live abrupt ends as a new attendant is assigned to him and cruel Stefan. The disturbed attendant starts to terrorize him and takes away the only things that gave Salvatores live a meaning. One morning Salvatore is fed up with the attendant and takes a revenge on him that reveals the dark and horrific past of the old man.
