Team A.P.O.C.A.L.Y.P.S.E.

 Set against a post-apocalyptic backdrop, three strangers are kidnapped by a lonely former millionaire and forced on a mission to stop the extinction of the human race. Their mission is to find women, who have all but disappeared from the face of the Earth, to re-populate the planet. During their adventure they encounter a desert nomad who has a strange but limited power that threatens their lives.
