Tall Ships

 The extraordinary story of the tall ship Privateer Lynx and the exciting lives of the young men and women who sail her. A 122-foot Top Sail Schooner inspired by an earlier Lynx built as a war machine in 1812, this modern day sailing ship takes us on an unforgettable adventure at sea, witnessing natural wonders, battling treacherous weather and offering time-tested solutions to the environmental perils facing our planet.
