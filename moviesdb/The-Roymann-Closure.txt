The Roymann Closure

 When Matt Roymann inherits his fathers company, he becomes blinded by obsession to prove he can be a leader. Driven by the memory of his disapproving father, Matt sacrifices everything, including his marriage. As he is about to close a merger that can put Roymann Industries at the top, his wife Lisa gives him a final chance to save their marriage by asking him to step down. Torn between the company and his wife, Matt wants to sign the papers to close the deal. When he wakes up the next day he finds himself in a nightmare that will change everything.
