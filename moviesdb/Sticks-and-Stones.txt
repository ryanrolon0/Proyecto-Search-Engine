Sticks and Stones

 Kim, a young teenage Softball Star, is taking her daily jog through the woods when she falls into a trap. She is stuck in the basement of a Psychopathic murderer. As her own personal scrapbook is finished by the killer and added to the shelf of victims, Kim sits in his basement layer numb to the world. She awakes in the Basement and is faced with the struggle of her life.
