Sachez chasser

 Evelyne's lover has just broken up with her, which is why she is so happy to accept the offer of Karen, her employer, to spend a quiet weekend in her house in the country. Unfortunately, this invitation comes complete with a host of prohibitions worst of it all is the presence of Jonathan, a cheeping bird. And the exasperating winged creature goes as far as faking its own death in order to fly away from its cage. More than ever a lady in distress Evelyne gets help (?) from GErard, an awkward hunter. Their assignment back to its cage...
