The Curse of the Hummingbird

 Billy Wilder, an adult film director, finds himself alone after losing his sight during a bizarre incident on the set of his last movie. Blind and unable to work, Billy spends his days stumbling around the house alone. In a ploy for companionship , Billy posts an ad for a foreign maid. Billy hires Rosa, a good Catholic girl in desperate need of work to avoid eviction.
