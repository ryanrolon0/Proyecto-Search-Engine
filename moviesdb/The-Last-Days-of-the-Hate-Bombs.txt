The Last Days of the Hate Bombs

 At last, the blood and guts fistful of rock and roll that is the Hate Bombs is captured on one film. Follow Orlando's most dedicated garage rock band on their farewell tour (with L.A.'s very own Invisible Men) of the west coast; Canada and their final show at the infamous Kit Kat Club. Included are interviews, insights and all the hits you know and love so well.
