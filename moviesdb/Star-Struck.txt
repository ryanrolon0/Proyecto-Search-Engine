Star Struck

 'Star Struck' is a short film that captures the life of a former child star by the name of Brent Scott. When he was just a boy Brent Scott played the roll of Teddy in the hit television series 'Almost a Family.' He touched audiences everywhere with his signiture dance and cute smile. Now as an adult, Brent faces the hardship of the world still seeing him as Teddy from the show and not as a serious actor in Hollywood.
