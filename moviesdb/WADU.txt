 Everyone talks about saving our planet and being green, but what would animals have to say? Embark on a fantasy based journey that explores how marine mammals keep the faith that they and the planet can thrive. Attend a sermon normally reserved solely for seals and sea lions, but open to the general public through this digital video. This short (WADU Animal Documentaries Unleashed webisode) is part fact, part fantasy, and all edutainment.Shot on location along the coasts of Northern California, Sea Sermon promises to give you a greater appreciation for the planet and all of its inhabitants.
