The Last Turn

 Tandee and Cash are small time drifters living from hustle to hustle on the short con, but Tandee dreams of something more. Together, the hatch a plan to take down a small after hours gambling club for enough money to move away and start fresh. To bankroll the scam and act as a front man they recruit Jimmy, a mob connected street hood. The train Jimmy in the skills of the scam, the rules of the game, and the technique of reading the secret signals that guarantee a winning hand.
