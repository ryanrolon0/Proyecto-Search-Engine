Trout Fishing Afghanistan ll

 In 2007, at the height of the US Taliban conflict, George, a retired US Navy SEAL, disguises himself and sets off for an adventurous trout fishing expedition in Afghanistan. Armed with only a bodyguard and a handy-cam, George stumbles across an opium harvest, granting us access to a passer-by view of the real Afghanistan...a nation suffering the war wounds of Islamic extremism, poverty, and oppressed human rights.
