Runaway Terror

 Two sexy starlets are brutally murdered after auditioning for RUNAWAY TERROR, Sultry Productions' latest film ... Who could possibly want these girls dead? Could it be Jill, the producer's jealous secretary/ex-girlfriend? Or Adam Hill, the shady producer himself? More questions arise, along with the body count, as Lydia, the twin sister of the first victim joins with Detective Luke Brennan to search for the killer ... and finds the most shocking truth of all!
