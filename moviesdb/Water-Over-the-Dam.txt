Water Over the Dam

 Welcome to the world of the Jackson family. A family brought together by the force and strength by an uncommon suspect, aater. Spending most of there year on rivers and rapids they have found a special connection in Freestyle Kayaking. Still not a main stream sport Freestyle Kayaking leaves spectators jaws dropped and stunned. Eric Jackson, the father of this truly unique family, has continuously innovated what the sport is today. Water Over the Dam delves into what makes this family so unique and how they got to where there are today, with a family love and connection rarely seen.
