Straight Outta Puerto Rico

 Explore the burgeoning Reggaeton music scene going back to its roots in Jamaican Reggae and Latin American Soca rhythms, to its current meteoric rise in popularity and the Cuban artists that are dominating this scorching new genre. Featuring top Reggaeton stars Luny Tunes, Daddy Yankee, Tego Calderon, DJ Playero and more!
