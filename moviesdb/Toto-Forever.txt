Toto Forever

 "Toto Forever" tells the impossible love story between a young postmen, that dreams with a new life, and a gangster, that is in deep trouble with a mafia group. Toto will never lose the "hope", despite of the traumatic end of the affair. Filmed in 16mm, Toto Forever is a fable in which feelings and emotions are always fully exposed, with a very poetic and symbolic narrative and aesthetic very close to the Anime Japanese.
