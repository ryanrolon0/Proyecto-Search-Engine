The Scarlet Runner

 A series of 12 2-reel episodes, each a separate and unrelated story, relating the adventures of Christopher Race and his high-powered automobile, The Scarlet Runner. Each episode has a different cast, except for the continuing role of Earle Williams. Episode titles are and His Majesty; #2 Hidden Prince; #5 The Red Whiskered Man; #8 Case; #10 Girl.
