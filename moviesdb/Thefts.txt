Thefts

 A woman, a cop, and a stolen purse... "Thefts," the story of young pick pocket who gets a caught up in a heist bigger than he can imagine after snatching an innocent woman's purse. While being pursed by the cops, Max hides unknowingly in the back of two robber's get away truck, shortly after the robbers return, Max realizes that what started out as a petty crime has turned into an enormously large score.
