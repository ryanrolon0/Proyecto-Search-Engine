The Not Goods Anthology

 A mockumentary by filmmaker Johnny Sanchez which chronicles the cross country search by music biographer Rock Savory for the story behind the legendary New Orleans based band, The Not Goods. With cameo appearances by film and television actors musicians others. The 2 Disc Collector's Edition package includes Revenge of the Not Goods Director's cut and Bonus Features The CD contains original Not Goods songs plus 'A Radio Interview in Four Parts'.
