Still Ramblin'

 Still Ramblin' follows the career of film, television and music star Ramblin' "Doc" Tommy Scott from the foothills of Georgia to stardom through interviews, rare photos and never before seen film footage woth friends Stingbean Akeman, Sunset carson, Carolina Cotton, Col. Tim Mccoy, Bill Monroe, Charlie Monroe, Al "Fuzzy" St. John, Curly Seckler and many more.
