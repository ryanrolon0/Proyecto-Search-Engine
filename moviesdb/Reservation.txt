Reservation

 Charlotte is a 16-year-old who is bullied by a group of girls in her school. She finds sanctuary at her local library where she is close friend with Maggie, the librarian. However one day she meets Will who encourages her to face her fears and leave the library. Unbeknown to Charlotte gaining the courage to leave the library is the difference between life and being consumed by her anxieties.
