Thoondil

 Sriram (Shaam) is an IT professional who works in London. There he meets Pooja (Divya Spandana) who is an upcoming model. They both fall in love. However when Sriram discovers that his love is becoming a hindrance in Pooja's career, he decides to walk away from her. Enraged by this betrayal, Pooja, now a successful model comes back in Sriram's life who is happily married to Anjali (Sandhya) and wrecks havoc in his life.
