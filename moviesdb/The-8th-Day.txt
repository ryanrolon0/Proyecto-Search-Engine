The 8th Day

 Water disappears from Earth. Ex-submarine commander Gary and his 13-year old son coincidentally find a strange plant at the beach, that seems to have to do with the disappearing of water. They find out, it's an extraterrestrial creature, that has already start to work on the extinction of mankind. An 8 day countdown begins and a chase against time, before all the Water on earth will be gone. Gary and Parker connect with the government and help to solve the disaster and Gary reconnects with his ex-wife.
