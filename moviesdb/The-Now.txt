The Now

 When Gil breaks up with his longtime girlfriend Jay, he thinks the worst of his week is behind him. But a series of bizarre and inexplicable events leads him to question the very foundations of his world-view - and to wonder if Jay's secrets go even deeper than he thought. Gil is having a rough week. He's just broken up with his girlfriend Jay for keeping secrets, he's had to move into a new apartment, his psychiatrist is a creep, and to top it all off, he finds himself being chased by a gun-wielding stranger whose motives and identity are bafflingly unclear. As Gil struggles to come to terms with the series of bizarre occurrences that seem to make up his life lately, an unseen hand offers mysterious guidance. But who is shadowing him, and why? To answer these questions, Gil must challenge his most fundamental beliefs about himself and his world, as he discovers that Jay's secrets have a life of their own.
