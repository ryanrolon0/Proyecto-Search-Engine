The Sculptor

 'The Sculptor' is a documentary about artist Lois Teicher. Lois Teicher has been a artist for over 30 years. This is a story about what it take to be a true artist; Focus, determination and just plan drive. From the woman's movement and marching on Washington DC. to the everyday struggle of keeping it going. This is a story for anybody who wants to be, wanted to be or is an artist.
