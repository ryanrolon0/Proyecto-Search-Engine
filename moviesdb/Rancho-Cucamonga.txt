Rancho Cucamonga

 A young girl from a broken family wakes up and realizes that she is pregnant. After making sense of the situation she begins to gather information about what circumstances led to this. As she continues to remember, she comes to the conclusion that she was raped at a gangster party that involved all sorts of drugs. Though she was raped, she was rescued by a friend that night and therefore she begins to put the pieces of the puzzle together.
