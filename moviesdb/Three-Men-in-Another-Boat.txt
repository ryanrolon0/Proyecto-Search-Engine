Three Men in Another Boat

 'Griff Rhys Jones' (qv), 'Dara O'Briain' (qv) and 'Rory McGrath' (qv) reunite for a follow-up journey, this time on board Griff's classic yacht. The trio endeavor to sail from Tower Bridge to the Isle of Wight, followed by participation in a yacht race, with Griff teaching Dara and Rory how to sail.
