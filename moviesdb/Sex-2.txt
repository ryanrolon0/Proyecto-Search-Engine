Sex 2

 Mike Horner is back as the perennial nice-guy loser who can't get the woman for whom he's carrying a serious torch. Nikki Dial is the woman of his dreams, and while she seems to spend most of her time boffing every male in sight into black and blue bliss, Mike just can't seem to get next to her. His best buddy even gives him the shaft by bedding the delectable Ms. Dial. In the end, though, Mike more than gets it made up to him another frustrating date, three women who have secretly had the hots for him show up and insist on some carnal capering on the spot. These include Christy Canyon, Crystal Wilder and his dream girl Nikki.
