The Mezmerist

 The Mezmerist is a story told through the eyes and memories of Tommy Mezmecardo, who 25 years ago made a mini LP with Bill Ward (drummer from Black Sabbath) Bill played drums on the album. You spend 30 minutes listening to Tom tell the stories of recruiting Bill Ward and the other members of the band and how he faded into obscurity and his desire for a comeback. Although the album had no commercial distribution or widespread notoriety, the original vinyl recording continues to be a collectors item for metal enthusiasts. Mezmecardo is re-issuing the original cuts of the album on CD with worldwide distribution. A short documentary told by Tommy Mezmecardo who nearly 25 years ago recorded an album and was able to recruit Bill Ward, from Black Sabbath, who together recorded and independently made 500 copies of the 4 song LP . Tommy tells his story starting briefly in his childhood, through his turbulent teen years, the recording an LP, and the bands hiatus. Tommy describes with great recollection of his performances and his eventual descent into the rock and roll graveyard.
