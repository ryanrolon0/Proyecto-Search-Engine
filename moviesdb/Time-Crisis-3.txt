Time Crisis 3

 Two VSSE agents Alan Dunaway and Wesley Lambert are dispatch to eliminate the threat from the Zagorian Army led by General Giorgio Zott who has taken control of almost the entire island of Astigos and threatens to launch tactical missiles which would not only be a threat to Lukano, but the surrounding nations also. At the same time, Alicia Winston, a member of the Lukano Liberation Army is on a mission to rescue her comrades who were taken hostage by the Zagorian Army. The three join to fight and liberate the island of Astigos and it's only a matter of time to get this done.
