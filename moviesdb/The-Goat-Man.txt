The Goat Man

 A man walking along a beach spots three bathing beauties and tries to get them to have sex with him. They agree to do so only if he inserts himself through a hole in the fence. The girls are then shown backing a goat up against the fence. Satisfied, he gives them money, as agreed. Later, one of the girls stuffs a pillow under her bathing suit to simulate pregnancy and more money is extracted from the man. A man watches three girls frolic at a beach and offers to give them money for sex. One girl agrees, but only if they do it through a hole in a wood fence. He looks like he's getting the best ever, but a look at the other side reveals the three girls back a goat up against the hole. They get the money from and go dancing off down the beach.
