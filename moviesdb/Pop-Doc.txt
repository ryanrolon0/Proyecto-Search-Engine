Pop Doc

 Pop Doc tells the story of Ryan Davies an emerging singer/songwriter. His struggles with self belief, and his journey to find acceptance in his own eyes as well as his mother's. Shot on the Canon 5D MII Pop Doc has a unique cinematic feel, funky graphical transitions and pumping music. Included in the 2010 Sydney Mardi Gras Film Festival touring program - Pop Doc has inspired and excited audiences across Australia.
