Ringside Seats at Victory Point

 This film is set in a fictitious future depicting a totalitarian government that has just made the decision to annihilate itself and it's enemies. Stuck in the middle are two men who's only outlet to the world outside is an old radio which broadcasts their countries dying stations during a nuclear attack. Confused, embittered and frightened the two friends discuss their reasons for living and the choices they face at the end of the world. The film depicts the will to survive against and the acceptance of death.
