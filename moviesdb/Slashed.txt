Slashed

 When Daniel Woodcock starts his new school, little does he know that he will become the prime target of three vicious, vindictive bullies. As the state of his life slowly dawns upon him, he is distracted by the solace he finds in a girl called Lucy but will their love be enough to save him when he is faced with a life or death dilemma? Slashed is the moving tale of the reality of knife crime in urban areas.
