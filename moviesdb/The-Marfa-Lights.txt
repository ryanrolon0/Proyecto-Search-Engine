The Marfa Lights

 Based on the novella LA PETITE FADETTE written by the infamous French author George Sand; Set present day in Marfa Texas, the story revolves around twin brothers, (VAN, who secretly works the border smuggling illegal immigrants, and LANDRY, an Iraq war vet just returning home after three years of service), who fall in love with Fadette, a Mexican "curandera." Conflict ensues between the boys and their ailing father, whose decree is that they continue the legacy of their multi-generational ranch.
