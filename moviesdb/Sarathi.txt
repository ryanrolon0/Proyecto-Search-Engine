Sarathi

 The story explores the universal question, 'Who am I?' and revolves around the mind of Arjun Patel, a young and successful consultant. While riding the waves of success, Arjun rides a limo with an unusual chauffeur. The driver, through intriguing questions, takes Arjun on a journey he is not prepared for, and ultimately brings Arjun back to a familiar place - himself. The story explores the universal question, "Who am I?" and revolves around the mind of Arjun Patel, a young and successful consultant. While riding the waves of success, Arjun rides a limo with an unusual chauffeur. The driver, through intriguing questions, takes Arjun on a journey he is not prepared for, and ultimately brings Arjun back to a familiar place - himself. Arjun arrives at the Austin Bergstrom Airport at 8pm and is received by a limo driver. He needs to be in Nameless, Texas, (about an hour's drive from Austin) for a meeting. The film is a poignant journey, where Arjun sees unusual signs and places that jolt his memory of his past and difficult relationships. As Arjun becomes more reflective, he explores the universal question "Who am I?" and discovers the deeper truths of life.
