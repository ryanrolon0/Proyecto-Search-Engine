Toccatte

 A young lady, a tourist, visit the Donnafugata Castle in Sicily, while a jester distracts continually her attention A mother cry because she lose her child, and ask for help to looking for him. Five children, sitting on the pavement of the castle, sing a rigmarole, and, everyone on his own turn, thinks what they'll be when grow up poet... But a jester takes a child to a labyrinth, and he loses his way, his mother and his future ... Can the tourist help the child?
