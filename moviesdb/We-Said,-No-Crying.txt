We Said, No Crying

 An intimate film, A rare one that aren't about anyone particularly newsworthy and yet tell a story so real and relatable that it sticks with us. We Said, No Crying starts out like many home movies do young couple's decision to start a family. Following the ups and downs of two extremely likeable people, the film showcases not just the high drama of making a new life, it also documents a love affair you'd be lucky to find at the multiplex.
