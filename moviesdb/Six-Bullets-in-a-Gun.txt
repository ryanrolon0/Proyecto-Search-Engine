Six Bullets in a Gun

 Film tell us about eternal opposition between masculinity and feminine. Hero- is real embodiment of masculinity, businessman, who used to make fast and hard solutions. Heroine is full of love and tenderness, but she understand what Hero don't need in its, his soul is dried. Once mysterious Guest is appeared in his flat. He turn of the interior of the Hero, enliven his soul, show him what many of the things in the world are not so as it seems.
