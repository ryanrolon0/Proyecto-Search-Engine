The Bike Courier

 A lovable but not so capable bike courier becomes a hero and finds the love of his life - all in the span of a single delivery. The Bike Courier is a coming of age comedy about Eli, a young courier who desperately wants the chance to ask out the attractive secretary he delivers to every day. His chance finally comes when the two of them find themselves up against two not-so-successful criminals occupying the secretary's office. Eli must find the courage to defeat the criminals and make his move on the secretary.
