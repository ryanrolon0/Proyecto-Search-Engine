Tall in the Saddle

 When a stranger arrives in a western town he finds that the rancher who sent for him has been murdered. Further, most of the townsfolk seem to be at each other's throats, and the newcomer has soon run contrariwise to most of them. Rocklin arrives at a ranch to take over as foreman, but discovers the owner has been murdered. He helps solve the mystery of the murder and a scheme to swindle the ranch away from its rightful owner.
