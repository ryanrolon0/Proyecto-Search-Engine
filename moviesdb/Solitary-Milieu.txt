Solitary Milieu

 Solitary Milieu, pursues a definition or discovery regarding space, particularly that of private space. The project is narrative in design for the purpose of framing a private space. In its final form the video piece will be projected on 3 video screens next to each other in sync from 3 video projectors. The dramatic emphasis will shift from a screen (or character) to another screen (or character) to lead the viewer into the private space of each individual character.
