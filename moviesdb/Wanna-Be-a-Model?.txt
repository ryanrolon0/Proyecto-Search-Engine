Wanna Be a Model?

 This entry in the "Gags and Gals" series featuring Jefferson Machamer, magazine illustrator and creator of the syndicated newspaper comic strip "Gags and Gals"(and husband of actress Pauline Moore), has the model he is working with go on strike and picketing him with a sandwich board. He finds, he thinks, a replacement for her in a lunch wagon where he goes for a sandwich.
