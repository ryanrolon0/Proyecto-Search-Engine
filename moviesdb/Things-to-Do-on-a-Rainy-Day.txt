Things to Do on a Rainy Day

 The story follows a family, in present day New England, who are forced to visit grandmas house, to keep an eye on her. She is grieving over the recent loss of her husband, and has taken to unnatural ways of mourning. She climbs to the top of her roof, she throws shingles at a neighbor, she hides in the basement, things like that. So the family arrives, and almost instantly a huge rainstorm begins. So everyone is forced into the house, where they split up and all go their own ways. Every character is at a different part of their life, different age, different worries, and different excitements. There are two young children, about 8 years old, there is a teenage boy and his teenage girlfriend, there are the mother and father, and the grieving grandmother. The film lasts the length of the rainstorm.
